## Amps (4) — confirmed models (community-confirmed, same units in X)
- **Clean** — Bogner Shiva. Crystal-clear, full, hard to break up; gorgeous pedal-platform clean. Bright switch.
- **Crunch** — modified Marshall Super Lead (JCM800-ish). Edge-of-breakup plexi → mild JCM crunch; great pedal platform/mid-gain. Bright switch, depth circuit, 2 presence.
- **Rhythm** — Peavey 6505/5150 type. The metal rhythm machine; articulate yet saturated noon–7-o'clock.
- **Lead** — Victory Kraken (lunchbox). Hiwatt/Marshall/Orange mashup; ambient crunch low → squishy saturated lead high.

## Pre-effects
- **Compressor** (solid-state) — single Compression + Level + Attack (snappy/smooth). Doubles as clean boost.
- **Overdrive 1** — TS9/808 Tube Screamer. **X adds a Bright toggle** (flat/bright) for extra articulation.
- **Pre Delay** — RARE: a delay BEFORE the amp. Feedback/Mix/Time/Tone (HP/LP), tempo-syncable. Great for slapback OR washed-out ambient/shoegaze leads hitting the dirt.
- **Overdrive 2** — "amp-in-a-box" MOSFET/op-amp drive. Volume/Gain/Treble/Bass. Use as dirt for cleans, roll-off boost for mid amps, or graphic-EQ substitute.

## EQ + Cab
- **9-band graphic EQ** in the loop (between amp & cab). X adds **"Nolly's Mod" EQ** — a SECOND set of notched frequency bands Nolly picked (modern-metal-tuned but useful for ambient/mid-gain); toggle between original & mod bands, decibel moves persist.
- **Cab sim** — 600+ IRs / 4 cabinets, each amp matched. **X doubles speaker options to 4 per cab** (e.g. closed/open Greenback; V30 straight + V30 angled; CB75; etc.). Cabs: Greenback 4x12 (clean), Marshall T75 (crunch), Mesa B30 American (rhythm), Zilla V30 (lead). 4 mics (dynamics + 1 condenser 414 + 1 ribbon 121). **Room Send on every cab (X).** Link/unlink amp↔cab freely. Custom/3rd-party IRs loadable.

## Post-effects
- **Delay 2** (stereo) — modulation, ping-pong, tempo, feedback, time, hi/lo cut (→ warm tape-ish or full digital). Mod can pan the delay.
- **Reverb** — lush washed-out Hall; decay/mix + hi/lo cut. Praised as "incredible/amazing" by reviewers (Secret Weapons). Fairly static character — one specific (very good) flavour.
