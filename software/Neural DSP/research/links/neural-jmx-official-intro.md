Authoritative device/feature breakdown for Archetype: John Mayer X.

## Amplifiers (3, all modelled from Mayer's personal units)
- **Smooth Operator** — 1964 Fender Vibroverb (vibrato channel). Clean/jangle foundation; breaks up early & gnarly. Bass/Treble + Bright switch.
- **Headroom Hero** — Dumble Steel String Singer #002. Most mid-forward; huge headroom until a FET switch → smooth overdrive. 3-band EQ + Mid switch. (Omits the real unit's High/Low filters & Deep/Rock-Jazz switches.)
- **Signature 83** — Two-Rock John Mayer Signature Prototype. Full-range, big headroom (slightly less than the Dumble). 3-band EQ + Presence. (Omits Mid/Deep switches + Contour.)
- **Three-in-One blend** — runs all three amps simultaneously with matched cabs, fixed mic/EQ, gain control, and a **Room Send** for ambience. (Cab block is locked/preset in 3-amp mode; per-amp cab editing only when using ONE amp.)

## Pre-effects (in order)
- **Justa Boost** — clean boost (Keeley Katana); Mayer leaves it on always — "bigger/fatter."
- **Antelope Filter** — envelope filter (EHX Q-Tron+), touch-sensitive.
- **Halfman OD** — Klon Centaur clone (transparent gain).
- **Tealbreaker** — dual-mode OD: Ibanez TS-10 (Tube Screamer) OR Marshall Bluesbreaker MK1, toggle. (Stonesy/open-chord on Bluesbreaker; screamer-y on TS.)
- **Millipede Delay** — slapback delay (Way Huge Aqua Puss). Beefs leads.

## Across-the-top signal chain
- **Gravity Tank** — custom: harmonic tremolo + spring reverb (from a Victoria Reverberato). Sits BEFORE the amp. The '60s Spring is a warm 2-spring tank emulation; can get very drippy.
- **EQ** (4-band semi-parametric w/ high/low cuts) + **Compressor** (Distressor-inspired; only Input/Output exposed — rest preset to Mayer's settings).

## Post-effects
- **Dream Delay** — clean digital delay, tempo-matched, long tails, Ping Pong option, tone-shaping.
- **Studio Verb** — Hall OR Plate modes; decay/predelay/mix. Hall = big all-encompassing room; Plate = tighter.

## Cab block (single-amp mode only)
- Matched cabs: Fender combo w/ 15" CTS · Dumble cab w/ vintage EVM12L · Celestion G12M Greenback (Two-Rock). 2 movable mics (default: ribbon 121 + dynamic 421; also a 57). Drag mics or use Position/Distance dials. **Room Send** for ambience. Custom IR loadable.

## X platform features
- Resizable UI; **Transpose** (±1 octave; tracks single notes well, ~4 semitones before chords get "dark and wobbly"); **Doubler** (one-pass double-track, hard L/R); **Live Tuner**; Metronome (standalone). Free upgrade for existing owners; Quad Cortex compat via future CorOS.
