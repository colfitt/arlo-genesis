# ValhallaRoom — community usage tips & preset lore

## Reputation
- "Big beautiful spaces... non-fatiguing, no ringing, no harsh highs — always smooth." Praised for **per-band decay** (set decay separately for bass/mids/highs). The GUI looks plain but is "conducive to very quickly dialing in a sound." Extremely low CPU; ~$50.
- **Strong for dark ambient** — "a ton of algorithms especially if you're after dark ambient" (the Dark/Alien modes). 
- **Weak at plates** — the one repeated knock; pair with a convolution reverb + plate IRs (or use VintageVerb's plate) when you need that.

## Guitar / ambient placement tips (Gearspace)
- **Subtle realistic room on a guitar track:** insert directly, **Mix ~10%**, Depth 0%, Early Size < 40 ms, modulation off (or low), Diffusion to taste — adds depth + "real recording" feel without an obvious wash. *This is the placement/glue use, not the wall use.*
- A shared community preset "RoomAmbienceForBluedog30" uses very short predelay, minimal decay, small Early Size — same intimate-room intent.

## Preset packs / factory lore
- ValhallaRoom ships with **150+ factory presets** ("minimal tweaking necessary"). v2.0.0 added **Space-focused presets** incl. "SpaceScratchpad."
- **Audiotent "Reflections"** (commercial, 107 presets) is organized by **instrument** (Drums/Bass/Leads/Keys) and by **size** (Small → Very Large) plus **Ambience / Special / Effects** folders — the Special/Effects banks are built by "carefully dialling in specific early and late reflection settings" for unconventional sound design. Useful as a starting-point library if you want size/instrument-sorted recall.

## Pairings the community uses
- **ValhallaShimmer** alongside Room for the ethereal pitch-shifted shimmer Room can't truly do.
- **Audio Damage Eos** (or any reverb with a Hold) for ambient pad textures Room lacks (no Freeze).
- **Convolution + plate IRs** to cover Room's plate weakness.
- For this rig the natural pairings are: **Room (depth) → VintageVerb (color)**, **Big Sky** for true Freeze, **Supermassive** (free) for infinite feedback drones.
