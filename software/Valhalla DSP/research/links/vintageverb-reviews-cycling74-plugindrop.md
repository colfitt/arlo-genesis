## PluginDrop review — most concrete copyable settings
https://plugindrop.net/posts/valhalla-vintageverb-review/
plugindrop.net · 2026-04-24
- **CPU:** 32 instances under ~30% load on mid hardware; Apple Silicon native = "noticeably lower."
- **Vocals:** Plate or Dirty Plate · PreDelay 15–30 ms · Decay 1.0–2.5 s · 1980s color · LowCut to
  kill low-freq mud.
- **Ambient/pads:** "Chorus Room or Sanctuary with Mod Rate turned up." Note: "VintageVerb's
  modulation is unusually musical — it adds movement without introducing obvious pitch wobble."
  → For the drone rig, that means you can push Mod hard without seasick detuning.
- **Color modes:** 1970s = "darker and more diffuse"; 1980s = "brighter with a subtly metallic
  quality"; NOW = flat, reveals the algorithm's natural character.
- **Gotcha:** no built-in post-reverb EQ — "chain a separate EQ after it" for tail shaping.
