kvraudio.com · KVR Effects Forum · "How do you use Valhalla Shimmer?"

Real-user usage (distilled; thread is light on exact numbers — flagged).

- Consensus use: **pads and textural elements**, not as a primary realistic reverb. One
  user's remix has "Shimmer kick in around the 1-minute mark" as a shimmery texture under
  ambient material.
- Mental model that recurs: "a reverb with a pitch shifter in the feedback path" — you
  shape the tail with pitch + feedback, not just reverb controls.
- **"Super easy to use, super easy to overdo."** Standard control moves users mention:
  EQ/filter AFTER the Shimmer, and sometimes **gating or rhythmic gating** the wet to
  rein the wash in.
- Layering approach: add "ambience of a massive momentary reverb spiced with Shimmer +
  delays" — i.e. Shimmer is a layer on top of/alongside a main reverb, not the only verb.
- Reverb tails "evolve over time quite wildly" and need **longer tail settings than a
  normal reverb** to fully bloom into a soundscape.
