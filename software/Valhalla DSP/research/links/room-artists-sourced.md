# ValhallaRoom — artists & producers (sourced, certainty-flagged)

★ **Honest scope note:** sourced ValhallaRoom artist attributions are **thin and skew
EDM / drum&bass / electronic**, NOT ambient/post-rock/shoegaze. I found **no sourced**
ambient or shoegaze artist documented using *Room specifically* (the ambient reputation
is real but word-of-mouth, same situation as VintageVerb). Below are the ones with an
actual source; the rest of the "ambient producers use it" lore is unverified and omitted.

## HIGH certainty (specific source)
- **Noisia (Nik Roos / Thijs / Martijn)** — DnB/electronic. Called ValhallaRoom their
  **favourite reverb plugin** on **Noisia Radio S02E22 (~38:20)**; also referenced
  "reverbs like Valhalla Room" in a Reddit reply and a studio video. The single
  best-sourced Room endorsement found. *(Not ambient, but the most credible "this is the
  one I reach for" quote.)*
- **Rob Swire / Knife Party** — tweeted **"Valhalla Room sounds awesome"** re: favourite
  reverb plugins (quoted on Valhalla's own product page).

## MEDIUM certainty (Equipboard-listed, gear-page sourcing)
- **Martin Garrix** — lists "valhalla room" among favourite plugins/VSTs.
- **3lau**, **A. G. Cook** (PC Music — the most "experimental" of the list), **Cazzette**
  ("we recently invested in Valhalla Room Reverb... that one's really good"),
  **Dzeko & Torres** — all Equipboard-listed users. Equipboard sourcing is crowd-submitted
  gear lists, so treat as MEDIUM, not interview-confirmed.

## What they're documented doing (technique, where stated)
- Electronic/house sound-designers report **dialing a usable space in 10–20 seconds** and
  running **up to ~28 instances** in one track for layered spatial design — i.e. Room used
  as a cheap *per-element* placement tool across a busy arrangement (CPU is a non-issue).
- General consensus framing from these producers: Room = the **clean/realistic** space you
  reach for to "put things in a room," vs VintageVerb for color.

## Certainty summary
- **Safe to cite:** Noisia (favourite reverb, sourced talk), Rob Swire/Knife Party (tweet).
- **Cite as Equipboard-listed (MEDIUM):** Garrix, 3lau, A.G. Cook, Cazzette, Dzeko & Torres.
- **Do NOT claim:** any ambient/post-rock/shoegaze artist on Room specifically — none found
  with a source. The drone-wall use is documented as *technique* (this rig's recipes), not
