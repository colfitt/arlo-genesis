Artist & producer uses of VintageVerb — SOURCED, with certainty flags

Compiled from multiple sources (each artist line cites its origin). VintageVerb is genuinely
ubiquitous; below are the ones with a real paper trail vs. the community-attributed ones.

## STRONG / sourced
- **Tycho** — uses TWO VintageVerb inserts on his synth wall ("Awake"): subtle Fat Plate at 15%,
  then a cranked-modulation Large RHall in 70s color at 30%, with delay AFTER the reverb.
  Source: Reverb Machine breakdown, https://reverbmachine.com/blog/tycho-awake-synth-sound/
  (2017-04-17). HIGH confidence — detailed reconstruction with named presets. ★ Best match for
  the drone/shoegaze-wall aesthetic in this rig.
- **Kendrick Lamar / SZA — "All the Stars"** — VintageVerb among the plugins on the pre-hook
  vocal chain (with Auto-Tune, EQ3, Waves RVox, UAD Galaxy Tape Echo, Soundtoys Little AlterBoy).
  Source: Sound on Sound "Inside Track" interview with producer/engineer Matt Schaeffer, May 2018,
  https://www.soundonsound.com/techniques/inside-track-kendrick-lamar-black-panther-all-stars
  HIGH confidence (named engineer in a print interview). Pop/vocal use, not ambient — but proves
  pro-mix ubiquity.

## COMMUNITY-ATTRIBUTED (lower certainty — verify before quoting as fact)
- **Charlie Puth** — shown using VintageVerb in his "30-Day Songwriting & Production" class.
  Community/marketing-sourced; plausible but not an independent interview. MEDIUM.
- **Calvin Harris** — demonstrated VintageVerb in an effects-chain video. Community-sourced. MEDIUM.

## NOT FOUND (do NOT claim)
- No sourced confirmation that **Stars of the Lid**, **Tame Impala**, or **Bon Iver** specifically
  use VintageVerb turned up. Stars of the Lid predate the plugin's era for much of their catalog.
  Leave these out rather than invent.

## Honest framing
VintageVerb's reputation in ambient/electronic is real and word-of-mouth huge, but most "artist X
uses it" claims online are uncited. The two HIGH-confidence anchors above (Tycho, Kendrick) are
the ones safe to cite. For the rig, **Tycho's two-insert recipe is the directly transferable one.**
