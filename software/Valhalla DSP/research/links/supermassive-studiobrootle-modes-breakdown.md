# Studio Brootle — Supermassive mode-by-mode breakdown (with presets)

Distilled mode characters. (Page lists modes by character, not by attack/decay specs.)

## Modes by character
- **Gemini** — simple delays with feedback (basic echo).
- **Hydra** — two independent delay chains in parallel.
- **Centaurus** — multiple repeating echoes that cycle through the space.
- **Sagittarius** — reverb tail + repeating echo elements (echo-into-reverb).
- **Great Annihilator** — extended reverb + repeating echoes → lush long decay.
- **Andromeda** — *"lots of smaller repeating echoes and long reverb. Great for
  smearing sounds"* into continuous drones. ★ the drone-smear mode.
- **Cassiopeia** — *"lots of feeding-back echoes — great for dub / dub-techno."*
- **Libra** — repeating echoes that **filter out over time** — dub/dub-techno.
- **Leo** (v3.0+) — *"long swelling and dense — really good for background textures."* ★
- **Virgo** (v3.0+) — digital reverb character with feedback and **moving filters.**
- **Pleiades** (v4.0+) — echoes that *"filter out more and more as they feed back."*

## Controls (as summarized here)
- **Warp** — extends delay times relative to base setting.
- **Feedback** — regeneration amount (decay length).
- **Density** — higher = *"smear the sound into a long reverb."*
- **EQ Low** = high-pass; **EQ High** = low-pass.

Honest flag: no numeric factory-preset values quoted on the page — characters only.
