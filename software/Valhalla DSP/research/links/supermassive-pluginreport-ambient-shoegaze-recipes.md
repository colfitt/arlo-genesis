## What it's for
*"Dramatic effects: long evolving tails, modulated echoes, infinite washes, and drones
that drift forever."* Considered the best free reverb/delay plugin; *"still embarrasses
paid plugins."*

## Ambient pad recipe (send on a synth)
- Put Supermassive on a **synth send, Mix 100%.**
- **Bump Feedback + Density**, **slow the Delay time**, gently modulate.
- Hold one note → instant evolving ambient bed (*"hold one note to become an ambient
  artist"*).

## Dream-pop / shoegaze guitar recipe
- **Shorter delay times + moderate feedback + a smidge of Mod Depth + wide stereo
  spread** → *"instant sparkle without shimmery artifacts."* (i.e. lush wash, not a
  cartoon pitch-shimmer.)

## Pro tips (rig-relevant)
- **High-pass the RETURN** with an EQ to keep the mix from turning into fog. (Or use
  Supermassive's own EQ Low Cut — but a dedicated return EQ gives more control.)
- **Automate Delay time + Feedback** with subtle per-section moves so the effect "feels
  alive" (matches MusicRadar's ride-Warp/Delay-live trick).

## On "shimmer"
Supermassive can *fake* shimmer-ish textures, but a true Shimmer's pitch behaviour and
tail feel are *"more musical"* — for literal pitched shimmer, paid Shimmer / H90 win.
Add a modulation/filter plugin AFTER Supermassive for more motion.
