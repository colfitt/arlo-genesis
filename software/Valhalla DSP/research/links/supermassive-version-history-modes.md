# Supermassive — version history & full mode roster (it grew over updates)

Supermassive shipped in 2020 and **gained modes free with each update** — anyone on an
old version is missing modes. **Current = 22 reverb/delay modes** (Valhalla product page).

## Mode additions by version (sourced)
- **v1.0 (2020)** — launch 8: **Gemini, Hydra, Centaurus, Sagittarius, Great
  Annihilator, Andromeda, Lyra, Capricorn** (see supermassive-blog-the-modes.md).
- **then added** (pre-2.0, per v2.0 roster of 16): **Cassiopeia, Cirrus Major, Cirrus
  Minor, Large Magellanic Cloud, Orion, Triangulum.**
- **v2.0 (2022)** — **Aquarius, Pisces** — "EchoVerb" topology, *"reverberant echoes —
  echoes with some reverb mixed in"*; tempo-syncable, density controls echo↔reverb blend.
  - https://musictech.com/news/gear/valhalla-supermassive-2-0-update-aquarius-pisces-echo-reverb/
- **v2.5.0 (2022-11)** — **Scorpio, Libra** (Libra = repeating echoes that filter out
  over time, dub/dub-techno).
  - https://valhalladsp.com/2022/11/21/valhallasupermassive-2-5-0-update-two-new-modes-scorpio-and-libra/
- **v3.0.0 (2023-11)** — **Leo, Virgo.** Leo = *"long swelling and dense — really good
  for background textures"* ★; Virgo = digital-reverb character with feedback + moving
  filters.
  - https://valhalladsp.com/2023/11/21/valhallasupermassive-updated-to-version-3-0-0-two-new-modes-leo-and-virgo/
- **v4.0.0 (2024-11)** — **Pleiades** — echoes that filter out more and more as they feed
  back (MusicRadar's drone/texture pick).
  - https://www.audiopluginguy.com/news-valhalla-dsp-launches-valhalla-supermassive-v4-0-0-featuring-new-pleiades-mode/

## Ambient/drone shortlist among the modes
- **Long-smear drones:** Andromeda (decay into thousands of sec), Great Annihilator,
  Sagittarius, Large Magellanic Cloud (huge), Cirrus Major/Minor (cloud washes).
- **Background-texture swell:** Leo, Centaurus, Pleiades.
- **Clean infinite-hold:** Gemini, Hydra, Lyra, Capricorn (+ Great Annihilator).
- **Echo-forward / dub:** Cassiopeia, Libra, Aquarius, Pisces, Lyra.
