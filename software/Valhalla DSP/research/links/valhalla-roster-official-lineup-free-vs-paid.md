# Valhalla DSP — full lineup, FREE vs PAID

Pricing model: every paid plugin is a flat **$50** (no sales — "$50 all year long").
Free plugins are permanently free, no feature restrictions, no account needed.

## PAID ($50 each)
- **Valhalla VintageVerb** — colored/vintage algorithmic reverb, classic-hardware
  emulations, era color modes. *(INSTALLED — own guide.)*
- **Valhalla Room** — transparent/realistic algorithmic reverb, dual Early/Late engine.
  *(INSTALLED — own guide.)*
- **Valhalla Plate** — warm dense plate reverb, inspired by '60s–'70s plate hardware.
- **Valhalla Shimmer** — ethereal pitch-shifted reverb (the dedicated shimmer machine;
  feedback + pitch shift = rising harmonic clouds).
- **Valhalla Delay** — echo/looping/delay with multiple modes (tape, BBD, ghost, etc.),
  ratios, ducking, diffusion → reverb-ish washes.
- **Valhalla ÜberMod** — chorus / flanging / multitap delay / "warped space" modulation
  + diffusion; experimental modulated-delay textures.
- **Valhalla FutureVerb** — newest engine (2025), does reverb + echo + delay + clean
  spaces in one; "8 years of R&D" flagship.

## FREE
- **Valhalla Supermassive** — massive delay/reverb hybrid, 20+ celestial modes, infinite
  feedback drones, harmonic echoes. ★ THE free ambient/drone wall + fake-shimmer maker.
- **Valhalla Space Modulator** — flanger/doubler: infinite "barber-pole" flanging,
  through-zero flange, doubling. (Modulation, not reverb.)
- **Valhalla Freq Echo** — Bode-style **frequency-shifter + analog-voiced echo**;
  psychedelic/chaotic spiraling echoes (clangorous as feedback climbs). ★ grab it.
