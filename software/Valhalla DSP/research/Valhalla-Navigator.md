# Valhalla DSP — Navigator (reach-for map)

A one-line "what is it / free or paid / reach-for for THIS rig / grab it?" map of the
whole Valhalla lineup. Aesthetic = drone/doom/shoegaze/ambient, sustained walls under
banjo/baritone/synths, degraded/lo-fi, Logic Pro (AU) + Ableton 12 Lite.
Pricing: every paid plugin is a flat **$50** (no sales); frees are permanent, no limits.
`research/links/valhalla-roster-official-lineup-free-vs-paid.md`

## Installed (own deep guides — don't redo)
- **VintageVerb** — PAID. *Colored/vintage* algorithmic reverb (era color modes, Chaotic
  tape modes). **Reach-for:** the degraded vintage character wall; 1970s color = lo-fi
  drone. No Freeze, 70 s ceiling. → `research/VintageVerb-UsageGuide.md`
- **Room** — PAID. *Transparent/realistic* dual-engine (Early+Late+Depth) reverb, decay to
  100 s. **Reach-for:** believable depth/placement + lush dark walls under VintageVerb;
  Dark Room = lo-fi mode. No Freeze, no real shimmer. → `research/Room-UsageGuide.md`

## FREE — grab these now
- **★ Supermassive** — FREE. Massive **delay/reverb hybrid**, 22 celestial modes, Feedback
  to ∞. **Reach-for:** the genuine **infinite/freeze drone** + cascading feedback washes
  VintageVerb/Room can't do; fake-shimmer wall; held synth/banjo beds. **THE free ambient
  wall maker — install first.** → `research/Supermassive-UsageGuide.md`
- **★ Freq Echo** — FREE. Bode-style **frequency-shifter + analog-voiced echo** →
  psychedelic/clangorous spiraling, metallic-detuning repeats as feedback climbs.
  **Reach-for:** broken/"recorded-wrong" delays, atonal drone grit, freq-shift drift on a
  banjo/synth send. Great cheap chaos. **Grab it.**
- **Space Modulator** — FREE. **Flanger/doubler** (infinite "barber-pole" + through-zero
  flange, doubling). **Reach-for:** modulation, not reverb — jet/whoosh motion on a drone
  wall or a widening doubler. Niche-but-free; grab if you want flange motion. (Lowest
  priority of the three frees for this aesthetic.)

## PAID — worth knowing, mostly skippable here
- **Plate** — PAID ($50). Warm dense **plate reverb** ('60s–'70s hardware voiced).
  **Reach-for:** lush bright plate sheen / vocal+snare plates — Room's weak spot. *Maybe*
  if plates become a thing; not core to drone walls.
- **Shimmer** — PAID ($50). Dedicated **pitch-shifted ethereal reverb** (feedback + pitch
  → rising-octave clouds). **Reach-for:** *real* shimmer (the one thing Supermassive
  fakes). **Lower priority — Supermassive's fake-shimmer + VintageVerb Mod cover most of
  it at $0;** buy only if literal rising-octave shimmer becomes essential (else the H90
  pedal already does it). → `research/Shimmer-UsageGuide.md`
- **Delay (ValhallaDelay)** — PAID ($50). Multimode **echo/looping** (tape/BBD/ghost,
  ratios, ducking, diffusion → reverb-ish). **Reach-for:** a deep dedicated ambient delay
  — but EchoBoy already owns this slot in the rig. Skip unless you want Valhalla's specific
  diffusion/ratio washes. → `research/Valhalla-Delay-UsageGuide.md`
- **ÜberMod** — PAID ($50). **Chorus / flange / multitap "warped space"** modulated delay +
  diffusion. **Reach-for:** experimental modulated-delay textures, evolving multitap drones.
  Interesting for this aesthetic but overlaps Microcosm/Blooper hardware; low priority.
- **FutureVerb** — PAID ($50). Newest flagship (2025): reverb + echo + delay + clean spaces
  in one engine, "8 years of R&D." **Reach-for:** an all-in-one modern verb; promising for
  walls but unproven for the rig and overlaps what's owned. Watch, don't rush.

## Buy-order verdict for this rig
1. **Free, today:** Supermassive (★), Freq Echo (★), Space Modulator. Zero reason not to.
2. **Already covered:** Delay → EchoBoy; Shimmer → Supermassive fake-shimmer + H90;
   Plate → VintageVerb plate / convolution.
3. **Only if a gap appears:** Plate (if plates matter), Shimmer (if real shimmer matters),
   FutureVerb/ÜberMod (curiosity). None are urgent — the two installed + three frees cover
   the drone/doom/shoegaze wall.
