synthanatomy.com · Kontakt 8 — what's new at a glance + update history · 2025-08

Verified feature + update history (good for "is it new in K8 vs added later").

## Conflux engine internals (verified)
- Expanded **wavetable engine**: original **PPG wavetables** as sources, **dual
  shapers per wavetable source module**, plus a **modulation oscillator** doing
  **FM, phase, and ring modulation**.
- Architecture: oscillator modules, **multimode filter**, **dual envelopes**,
  **dual LFOs**, a **step animator** (step sequencer), and a **multi-FX
  processor** — EQ, chorus, bit crusher, shimmer reverb, "space," diffusion delay.

## Tools (verified)
- **Chords**: chord generator, fire chord sequences from a single key, with swing.
- **Phrases**: melodic-sequence generator with octave, tempo, swing, and rotation
  controls.

## Leap (verified)
- Sample-manipulation engine inspired by Output Arcade; loops on white keys,
  effects on black keys; **12 factory expansions** (80s New Wave, Afrobeats,
  Latin Trap, etc.).

## UI
- Modernized UI with a **side-panel Navigator** + improved sound browser
  (categories + filters); **Classic/retro view toggle** retained.

## Update history (what came AFTER the Sept-2024 launch — DON'T conflate with launch)
- **8.1** — added stomp effects + additional delay/reverb types.
- **8.3 (Mar 31 2025)** — added **Moog-inspired passband filters** and
  **Japanese Sallen-Key filters**.
- **8.6 (Aug 2025)** — free update adding **six new MIDI Tools**: a simple
  **arpeggiator**, **chord builder (up to 8 voices)**, **humanizer**, **scale
  lock**, **velocity-curve** tool, plus the ability to **stack multiple Tools at
  once**.

So: at LAUNCH (Sept 2024) = Conflux + Leap + Chords + Phrases + new browser +
redesigned Factory Library. The MIDI-tool arsenal and the new filter types are
later free updates.
