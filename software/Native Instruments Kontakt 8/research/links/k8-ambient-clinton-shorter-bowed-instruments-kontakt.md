blog.native-instruments.com · "Clinton Shorter on scoring for film and TV with KONTAKT and LORES" · 2022-04-19

Clinton Shorter — film/TV composer (District 9, The Expanse, Pompeii). Works
surrounded by guitars, modular synths and "unusual instruments" (circle bells,
a UFO happy drum, an actual donkey's jaw). His Kontakt workflow is a near-perfect
blueprint for the drone/doom/shoegaze "make one imperfect source into a wall"
aesthetic.

## The core technique: bowed instrument → Kontakt pad
- Takes an instrument (kamanche, guitar, etc.) and **bows it with a cello bow**,
  recording notes that **waver or have harmonics** — deliberately imperfect.
- Records **2–3 bars** of each note with **slight variations** between passes.
- Loads the passes into Kontakt, then **pans one hard left and one hard right**.
- **Stretches the sample over the entire keyboard** so a single bowed note
  becomes a fully playable pad instrument.
- Adds "a splash of delay, a little bit of reverb" for organic depth.

## Variations as round-robin / velocity layers
- Builds **multiple variations set to different velocity levels**, reachable via
  **key-switching** — so repeated/held notes don't sound mechanically identical.
  (This is the organic-motion move: layered variation, not one looped sample.)

## Stereo width = the "lonely → huge" trick
- Toggling **stereo on/off** is "a big part of taking something that sounds
  really lonely and small and making it sound wider and bigger." A single-voiced
  instrument can be made "as wide and big sounding as an analog pad."

## Motion without notes (perfect for tension/drone beds)
- For tense scenes he avoids adding melody. Instead he modulates **panning and
  volume** (no pitch change) to "undulate sound and keep it boiling without
  introducing additional notes."
- Uses **LFO-based pitch deviation sparingly** for subtle wobble/detune life.

## Aesthetic philosophy (on-brand for this rig)
- The sounds that speak to him are **the imperfect ones**: a rickety/aged piano
  library, big wide analog pads, and unique single-voiced instruments that can be
  stretched as wide as a synth pad. "Imperfect" is the feature, not the bug.

### Why this matters for the rig
This IS the banjo/baritone-into-Kontakt recipe: bow (or e-bow / sustain-pedal-tail)
the banjo, record 2–3 takes with wobble, map across the keyboard, hard-pan the
takes, layer velocity variations, then modulate pan+volume (not pitch) to keep a
drone "boiling." Single owned instrument → sustained wall.
