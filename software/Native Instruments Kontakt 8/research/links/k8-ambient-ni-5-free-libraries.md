blog.native-instruments.com · Native Instruments · "5 free Kontakt libraries for ambient music" · 2024-05-15

NI's own pick of 5 free Kontakt libraries for ambient. Note: most need FULL
Kontakt (which the owner has via Komplete 15 Ultimate) — only the Play Series is
truly free-Player.

## 1. Vapor Keys Sakura — needs full Kontakt
- Three delicate synth patches for ambient pads + melodies; "instant gold."
- Responds well to the built-in reverb, vintage phaser, EQ. Best paired with a
  DAW "hazy delay" (e.g. EchoBoy in tape mode) for ethereal texture.

## 2. Play Series — FREE (Komplete Start) — runs in free Kontakt Player
- Three hybrid instruments to build whole ambient pieces:
  - **Ethereal Earth** — acoustic world instruments + creative processing.
  - **Hybrid Keys** — sampled pianos, mallets, keys.
  - **Analog Dreams** — vintage synth sounds.
- "Soft and otherworldly" — pads, drifting melodies, synthetic atmospheres.
  (The owner already has these via Komplete; great fast-grab ambient front end.)

## 3. EMP Mark II — needs full Kontakt
- Vintage electro-mechanical piano, "melancholic ambient keys." Six spatial
  reverbs (spring recommended). Gentle melodic / emotional depth over a drone bed.

## 4. Soniccouture Tape Choir — needs full Kontakt
- Layered voice samples → an **"infinite choir"** with vintage tape character.
  Swelling chords, "unearthly melodies." Adventurous reverbs for experimental
  beds. (Tape-degraded choir = squarely on-aesthetic.)

## 5. Signal (free version) — needs full Kontakt
- A **pulse engine**: piano/guitar/synth arranged into overlapping rhythmic loops.
  "Soothing, hypnotic movement" via pattern controls + onboard FX — "mesmerizing
  repeats" for meditative/generative ambient. (Feed your own banjo loop, get a
  hypnotic moving bed.)
