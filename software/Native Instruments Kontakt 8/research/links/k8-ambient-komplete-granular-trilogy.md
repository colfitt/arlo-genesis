## The Light Trilogy — three cinematic GRANULAR Kontakt instruments
Shared engine = **dual-layer**: a **GRAIN layer** (explores hidden timbres in the
specially-recorded sample content via a **tempo-synced grain cursor** for timed
risers/sweeps/transitions) + a **SAMPLE layer** (one-shots, arpeggiated patterns,
multisampled instruments). Intuitive modulation for "scoring shifting
intensities — from edge-of-seat sweeps to introspective keys to world-shaking
walls of sound." Hundreds of presets, or build your own dual-layer textures.

- **STRAYLIGHT** — sci-fi soundscapes: tempo-synced transitions, **fragile pads**,
  musical tones, harmonic plucks. Post-apocalyptic / gritty-thriller beds.
- **PHARLIGHT** — granular **human voice**: multisampled choirs, beatboxers,
  singers, noise. Vocal-to-abstract-cloud pads (best "at edge of recognizability").
- **ASHLIGHT** — "the **darker side of granular**": pulsating textures, muted
  keys, colder atmospheres. (v1.5+ added a **Shaper module** + new presets.)
  The doom/dark-ambient pick of the three.

## Verified Komplete 15 ULTIMATE ambient/cinematic Kontakt roster
(from NI included-products + SOS K15 review + NI library pages — cross-checked)
- **Granular:** Straylight, Pharlight, Ashlight.
- **Cinematic/textural:** **Arkhis** (orchestral→granular pads, 4-layer),
  **Thrill** (dissonant string-cluster tension), **Mysteria** (vocal/choir
  textures), **Rise & Hit** (risers/impacts toolkit), **Sequis** (evolving
  acoustic patterns), **Lores** (lo-fi/retro cinematic), **Conflux** (new hybrid
  sampling+synthesis/wavetable), **Fables** (NEW in K15 — 57 GB orchestral +
  choirs + synths for soundscapes), **Damage** (cinematic percussion/tension).
- **Sequenced soundscapes:** **Schema: Dark** / **Schema: Light** (pattern engines).
- **Designed-texture:** **Kinetic Toys** / **Kinetic Metal**, **Mallet Flux**,
  **Scene: Saffron**, **Vocal Colors**, **Piano Colors**.
- **Free Play Series (also in NI ecosystem):** Ethereal Earth, Hybrid Keys,
  Analog Dreams — fast ambient front end, run in free Kontakt Player.

## Reach-for map (this aesthetic, what's already owned)
- **Dark drone wall** → Ashlight (grain) or Arkhis (4-layer stack).
- **Sci-fi fragile pad** → Straylight.
- **Choir/vocal cloud** → Pharlight / Mysteria / Vocal Colors.
- **String dissonance/tension** → Thrill, Damage.
- **Generative/sequenced motion** → Schema Dark, Sequis, Conflux.
- **Your OWN sample as granular source** → drop into Straylight/Ashlight's sample
  layer (they accept user content) for personal field recordings → granular pad.
