Curated list of Kontakt ambient/pad/drone libraries. Bold = part of NI's
ecosystem (so likely already owned or adjacent via Komplete 15 Ultimate — verify
exact bundle tier on the drive).

## 1. NI Cloud Supply  — NI
- Granular processing + spectral manipulation; minute-scale evolution for
  long-form atmospheres. Set a preset and let it breathe; no manual automation.

## 2. Heavyocity Mosaic Pads — 3rd party (NOT in Komplete)
- Orchestral + hybrid electronic + vocal textures, 350+ presets by mood/intensity.
- Tip: slow-attack envelopes let textures "materialize out of silence."

## 3. NI Arkhis — NI (in Komplete 15 Ultimate cinematic set)
- Orchestral source run through granular + spectral processing — strips
  instrumental identity but keeps harmonic warmth. ~250 presets.
- Tip: **four-layer architecture** — stack processed sources at independent
  levels for dense beds. (One of the workhorses for this aesthetic.)

## 4. Pharlight — NI (in Komplete 15 Ultimate)
- Granular vocal/choir → pad textures, recognizably-vocal to abstract clouds.
  ~300 presets.
- Tip: best when vocal character sits "at the edge of recognizability."

## 5. NI Glaze 2 — NI
- Hybrid synthesis (wavetable + granular + spectral) for continuous non-repeating
  pad/drone evolution. 400+ presets.
- Tip: modulation rates extend to **minute-scale** to kill the mechanical loop feel.

## 6. Ethereal Earth — NI (FREE, Play Series)
- Processes organic/natural field recordings into pad/drone content with a
  grounded, physical character.
- Tip: best as a **foundation layer** under more harmonically-defined libs.

## 7. Vocal Colors — NI (in Komplete 15 Ultimate)
- Keeps recognizable vocal character in pads (vs. obscuring it). ~200 presets.
- Tip: velocity/expression meaningfully alter texture → "played rather than
  triggered."

### Takeaway
"Texture itself is the composition" — favor libs with long-form (minute-scale)
evolution. For THIS rig the Komplete-resident ones (Arkhis, Pharlight, Vocal
Colors; plus Cloud Supply/Glaze 2 if owned) cover most of the wall-of-texture
need without buying anything.
