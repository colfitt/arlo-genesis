## What ships in Discover (confirmed by the official walkthrough)

"An entire symphony orchestra at your fingertips," in four grouped sections:
- **Strings** — a large group: violins, violas, cellos, basses (played together and separately).
- **Woodwind** — flutes, oboes, clarinets, bassoons.
- **Brass** — trumpets, trombones, horns, and the tuba.
- **Percussion** — harp, snare drum, glockenspiel, "kettle drums" (timpani), triangle, etc.

## Headline facts

- Runs inside Spitfire's **free, easy-to-use plugin**, "compatible with any major music creation software" (i.e. native AU/VST — loads directly in Logic).
- **Only 200 MB.**
- Framed as the entry point to the BBC SO ecosystem (interchangeable up to Core/Pro via mode-switching — see the editions notes).
