# Originals — Rare Flutes (owned, dedicated plugin)

**What it is.** A budget "Originals"-range library of **unusual/ethnic flutes** — bansuri, ocarina, shakuhachi and other non-orchestral flutes. Recorded **dry** at Spitfire's London studio for control.

**Format.** Spitfire's streamlined **Originals dedicated plugin** (AU/VST/VST3) — installed, native in Logic. Lightweight, fast to load.

**Content.** **47 presets** — single articulations + layered textures: long sustains, shorts, **flutters**, expressive **bends**. Three signals: **Close / Room / Super Reverb** (warmth, sharpness, or wash).

**For drone/doom/shoegaze.** The **breathy/overblown/fluttery** flute tones + the **Super Reverb** signal = instant "mystique"/ethnic-ambient colour. Breathy sustained bansuri/shakuhachi under banjo, pushed into EchoBoy/Valhalla, is a strong on-aesthetic move. "Adds instant mystique to any score" and "blends easily with other instruments." A flavour/topline-colour library, not a wall-builder.
