# Resonate — vendor product page

**Pitch.** "Experimental instrument library." Built with **Dame Evelyn Glennie** — "unlocks the other-worldly mystery, magic, and tension of orchestral percussion," focusing on the **reverberations and resonances that follow the initial strike**.

**Content.** Tuned + untuned instruments "collide across **fourteen signals**, then spin out toward new frontiers of texture, tone, and dimension." Four main instruments: **Barrel, Timpani, Water Tank, Thundersheet**. Over **45 articulations, 14 signal paths, six after-effects**.

**Warp features.** 5 individual FX chains add character per sample, "ranging from a gritty, textural, yet organic sound, to an endlessly resonating reverb."

**Positioning (reviewers).** "Relatively unique in the sample market — if you want a percussion library, this is not what you need." Ideal for composers/producers in **media, film, post-production special effects**. Described as "a sound design masterpiece crammed full of both beautiful and spine-tingling resonances which are full of life and character." Requires Spitfire's **dedicated plugin player**.
