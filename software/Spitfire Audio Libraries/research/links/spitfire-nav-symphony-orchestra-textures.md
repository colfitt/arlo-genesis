# Spitfire Symphony Orchestra (SSO) — the big Kontakt orchestra, textural angle

**Format.** Runs in **Kontakt** (full or free **Kontakt Player 7.5.2+**). The drive-resident "everything" library: seven libraries in one **345 GB** collection — Symphonic Strings, Brass, Woodwinds, Percussion, Harp, Orchestral Grand Piano, Curated Ensembles. AIR Lyndhurst Hall, multiple mics.

**Textural / ambient capabilities (the only reason it touches this rig):**
- Strings: "luxuriant and expansive three-dynamic **sustains**" and "gorgeous breathy **flautandos**"; advanced **sul tasto, harmonics, con sordino blends** — for atmospheric pads and evolving soundscapes.
- Woodwinds: **legato sustains** across 11 solo instruments; new Performance Legato.
- Percussion "wallows in the amazing reverb of the galleried Lyndhurst Hall" — marimba/tubular bells as **textural** layers, not rhythm.

**Reach-for note.** SSO is the realistic, do-everything orchestra — usually overkill for drone/doom. But its **super-sul-tasto / flautando / harmonics / con-sordino** sustains, pulled way back and drenched in Valhalla, give a *real-orchestra* ghost colour the dedicated texture libs can't fake. Reach for it when you want recognisable orchestral instruments behaving ethereally — sparingly.
