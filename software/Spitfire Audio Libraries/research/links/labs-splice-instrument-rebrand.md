# THE BIG 2025 REBRAND — LABS → Splice INSTRUMENT (read this first)

As of late 2025, **LABS is becoming part of "Splice INSTRUMENT"** — a new sample-based virtual instrument built by the LABS team together with Splice. The free LABS website (labs.spitfireaudio.com) now **301-redirects to splice.com/instrument**, so the old per-pack URLs are dead. This matters for THIS rig because the user already has the standalone LABS plugin and packs installed on the external drive — those keep working.

## What stays true for an existing LABS owner (the important part)
- **The old LABS / LABS+ plugin is NOT going away.** It "will be supported until 2026 and you can continue to use it as usual."
- **Installed LABS packs keep loading.** INSTRUMENT's migration "scans for installed LABS packs and points at them in place" rather than asking for a re-download.
- **Existing projects keep playing.** Tracks using the LABS plugin "will still play back just fine and respond to automation" — even after cancelling a LABS+ subscription.
- So: nothing the user already owns/installed breaks. The change is about where NEW free content lives and how you browse/download it.

## What changed
- Fresh interface, easier browsing, individual-preset access (not full-pack download).
- All LABS + LABS+ sounds are reimagined inside INSTRUMENT, tagged under "LABS" and "Spitfire" labels.
- **Free monthly drops** return — but inside INSTRUMENT, not as standalone free LABS packs. "No new free LABS content" for the old plugin; new freebies appear as INSTRUMENT "Free Drops."
- INSTRUMENT launches with **500+ free presets**.

## Pricing tiers (2025/26, as published)
- **Free:** all free instruments from Splice + LABS; can also buy individual LABS+ instruments (yours forever, no sub) — coming, no firm date at launch.
- **INSTRUMENT $12.99/mo:** all premium instruments incl. LABS+ releases.
- **Creator $19.99/mo · Creator+ $39.99/mo:** premium instruments + 200–500 Splice library credits.
- LABS+ and INSTRUMENT are **separate** services — content/subscription does not transfer between them.

## Community reaction (BPB, Oct 2025)
Skepticism remains after the earlier LABS+ subscription move "generated a massive shift in public opinion" against Spitfire. The new INSTRUMENT browsing is "significantly improved" over the "controversial LABS+ interface that frustrated users," but BPB questions whether the returning monthly freebies will match the original LABS "character, quirkiness, and story."

## Practical takeaway
For this rig: keep using the installed standalone **LABS** plugin and the packs already on the drive — that's the workflow this guide documents, and it's stable into 2026. If grabbing NEW free sounds, that now happens through the Splice INSTRUMENT plugin/site instead of the old free LABS downloads. Don't pay a subscription for the drone/ambient use case — the free catalogue (esp. the textural/experimental packs) already covers it.
