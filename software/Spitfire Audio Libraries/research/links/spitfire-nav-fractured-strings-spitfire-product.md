# Fractured Strings — vendor product page

**Pitch.** "Experimental string texture library." An innovative approach to articulations — intervallic performances and new modal colours for depth and realism. Eight-piece string ensemble + two soloists, recorded at AIR Lyndhurst Hall, **15 signal paths**.

**Aesthetic fit (vendor's own words).** With its emotive musical gestures and **Scale Mode technology**, the library "lends itself perfectly to **cellular, ambient, and neoclassical composing** — reflecting the intricacies, patterns and contrasts found in natural phenomena." This is the exact drone/ambient/neoclassical lane.

**Plug-in.** Hosted in Spitfire's dedicated plug-in with **12 mic + 3 macro mix** options; includes the **Evo Grid** with classic features like **randomise** "to find beautiful special accidents."

**Scale Mode.** Select any of the seven diatonic modes or **build your own scale** by clicking the notes to include.

**Usage trick (vendor guidance).** "Use it WITH other libraries — it needs to duck and hide within other arrangements, just poking its head out every now and again to add flavour and interest." Designed to add **colour / seasoning / spice**, not carry a part.
