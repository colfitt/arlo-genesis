# Spitfire Albion Loegria (Albion II) — chamber Albion (Kontakt)

**What it is.** A **chamber-sized** orchestral library: strings, woods, brass, cinematic drums, **eclectic loops/percussion**, plus "an inspiring organic synth." Recorded at AIR Lyndhurst Hall. (Albion's chamber sibling — intimate where Albion ONE is epic.)

**Format.** **NCW → Kontakt only**, free Kontakt Player included. So in Logic it loads inside the **Kontakt 8 AU**.

**Character (reviews).** The strings have "a delightful airiness," ideal for **lighter compositions where a full string section is too thick." High+low string patches combine into an "elegant and stately timbre with a luxuriant texture" — sounds bigger/more symphonic than a true chamber group. The strings cover **extended techniques**: tremolando, trills, **harmonics, flautando, light col legno** — the airy/ghostly end that suits ambient.

**For this rig.** Loegria's value here is the **organic synth + loops + airy extended-technique strings** — odd organic colours and small-room texture to layer under banjo, rather than a big orchestral statement. Niche but on-aesthetic for the degraded/intimate side.
