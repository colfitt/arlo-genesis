# BBC SO — the textural/contemporary articulations (the ambient ghost colours)

These are the patches that make BBC SO worth reaching for over Discover-tier sustains
for atmospheric work. **All are Core/Pro tier (NOT in free Discover).** Direct review
quotes — these are the patch names to type into the BBC SO search box.

## The textural long-note set
- **Super sul tasto** — Spitfire's trademark; **"a quiet, beautifully tentative and
  delicate timbre."** The single most ghostly string colour in the library — bow way
  over the fingerboard, almost no bite. This is THE ambient-ghost patch.
- **Sul tasto, con sordino (muted), flautando long notes** — "offer **lush layering
  opportunities**." These four (super sul tasto / sul tasto / sordino / flautando) are
  the textural family; layering two or three across sections = a breathy evolving bed.
- Violas: **"sumptuous, breathy sul tastos."** Solo cello: **"yearning, gently
  undulating sul tasto performances sound great when played chordally"** → i.e. hold a
  CHORD on the solo-cello sul tasto for a drone, not single lines.
- **Double bass sordino long notes** — reviewer got goosebumps from "**low fifths** on
  their sordino long notes: a great, rich sound." → A held **low fifth (root+5th) on
  bass sordino** is a ready-made doom/drone foundation under banjo/baritone.
- Also in the set: **Short + Long Harmonics, Con Sordino Spiccato.** Harmonics = the
  glassy, pitched-air top layer over the sul-tasto body.

## Recording / mic character for ambience
- Maida Vale gives "size, depth and a unique combination of warmth and brightness."
- **20 signals = 11 mic positions + stereo mixes + 5 spill signals.** Paul Thomson's
  design intent: "all of the mics open all of the time" like a live recording; "the
  **spill** from these open mics adds a subtle but telling ambience… the sense of being
  in the room." → The **spill mics are a free ambience layer** that still sounds dry-ish
  (it's Maida Vale spill, not a cathedral) — useful when you want a touch of room
  without committing to the Ambient/Balcony hall mics that ring under Valhalla.
- The 11 positions "traverse from dry, close-miked articulations to distant, ambient
  perspectives" — full dry↔distant range in the mic mixer.

## Reach-for rule for the rig
For the believable faint-orchestra ghost: **Super Sul Tasto** (or viola sul tasto /
bass sordino low fifth), **Close + a little Leader/spill** for dryness, CC1 way down,
held chord → into Valhalla Room/VintageVerb + EchoBoy + Decapitator. Layer
sordino+flautando+harmonics across sections for a breathing wall.
