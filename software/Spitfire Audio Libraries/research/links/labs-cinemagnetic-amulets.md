# Cinemagnetic — the single most on-aesthetic LABS pack for this rig

**Cinemagnetic** (a 2025 LABS+ release; now in Splice INSTRUMENT) is a deep dive into the sound world of **Amulets** — the solo project of Portland artist **Randall Taylor**, a master of **cassette tape loops, live-processed guitar, and decaying analogue textures**. If the user's aesthetic is "drone/doom/shoegaze, degraded/recorded-wrong, sustained walls, tape/lo-fi," this pack is the bullseye — it's literally sampled from a tape-loop drone artist's rig.

## What it is / how it was made
Captures Amulets' stage + studio setup: **handmade tape loops, boutique synths, FX pedals, and even a stylophone.** Spitfire describe it as:
- "Lush synths that drift from fragile leads to guttural basses, alongside immersive drones that evolve, glitch, and dissolve."
- "Ambient music with grit and grain" — a "texture-first sound design" approach that's "raw, imperfect, and endlessly playable."
- It captures "the beauty of sound breaking apart in real time."

## What it's for
"Crafting cinematic soundscapes, experimental textures, or adding lo-fi weight to electronic productions." The evolving/decaying nature is built for drone and ambient walls. (Note: this is a LABS+/INSTRUMENT premium pack, not one of the always-free ones — but it's the closest thing to a purpose-built drone-doom-tape texture instrument in the whole LABS catalogue.)

## Who Amulets is (why this matters)
Randall Taylor / Amulets uses handmade cassette tape loops + live-processed guitar loops to build "live, lush soundscapes and immersive drones," blurring ambient/drone/noise/electronic. His technique: a **4-track recorder used as a mixer** for multiple incoming tape signals (samples, loops, a microcassette), the tape itself "played like an instrument," parts pre-recorded to handmade tape loops and **layered in dense layers**. The classic loops are ~5-second handmade blank tape loops cut from found source material — each tape unique. This is the same philosophy as the user's hardware tape/lo-fi rig (CB Big Time, Generation-Loss-style degradation, Octatrack loop mangling) — Cinemagnetic is a software shortcut to that exact decaying-loop drone palette.

## Rig fit
- Pair Cinemagnetic drones with the banjo/baritone lead; push the dynamics/mod-wheel for evolving fullness.
- Route into ValhallaVintageVerb/Supermassive + EchoBoy for the wall, Decapitator/SketchCassette for extra grit (though it already arrives gritty).
- Conceptually identical to what the user does on hardware — so it layers naturally with Octatrack/Digitakt tape loops rather than fighting them.
