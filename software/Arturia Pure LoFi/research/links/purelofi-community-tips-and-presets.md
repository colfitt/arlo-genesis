## Concrete workflow tips (Arturia forum)
- **Noise/texture-bed bounce trick (the rig-relevant one):** "Turn off oscillator one and two so no notes are played — then the other sources (noise/texture) play **constantly**. Bounce that out to an audio file so you can ride the volume up and down to get the desired level of background noise." → Pure LoFi as a **standalone lo-fi noise/texture generator** for an ambient bed (exactly the SketchCassette "hiss-only texture track" move, but with Pure LoFi's 190+ curated noises + tape processor on top).
- **UI gotchas:** in the **Noise** section you **double-click** a sample to load it (differs from other areas — single-arrow auditions). In the **Advanced Effects** menu, the **Power icon** toggles module visibility, and Advanced-Effects modules can **show greyed-out even when powered on** (contradicts the in-app tutorial). For some generators, browse via the **samples browser**, not the preset browser.
- Users note the **built-in tutorial lags the shipping feature set** (OP: "Was that function just removed from the released software and not the tutorial?" — tutorial/software drift at launch); they recommend the video walkthroughs (CatSynth / Sound Test Room / Woody Piano Shack / Molten) over the in-app docs. (A full PDF manual exists — large, ~295–309 MB.)
- General sentiment: "having a great time with Pure LoFi"; community discovering workarounds by experiment rather than established best practice (it's new).
