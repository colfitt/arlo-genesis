# Auto-Tune Unlimited — what's installed + the creative reach-for map (distilled)

Auto-Tune Unlimited is Antares' everything-subscription: every Auto-Tune tier + every AVOX
module + the standalone creative tools, all updated to current versions. From CONTENTS.md the
machine actually has 22 plugins + 2 apps (AU + VST3). Grouped by what they're FOR — and flagged
by relevance to a drone/doom/shoegaze sound-design rig (★ = reach-for creatively, · = vocal-mix
utility, mostly skip for this rig).

## Pitch / tuning engines (Auto-Tune family)
- ★ **Auto-Tune Pro X** (`Auto-Tune Pro`) — the flagship. Auto + Graph modes, Classic Mode,
  Flex-Tune, Humanize. THE hard-retune effect box (Retune Speed 0). Also the deepest for
  designed/Graph-drawn alien melodies. Works on mono instruments.
- ★ **Auto-Tune EFX+** — the lightweight "effect-first" version: built for the robotic sound +
  has its own throw/FX. Fastest path to the T-Pain sound if Pro X feels heavy.
- · Auto-Tune Artist / Access — slimmer tuning tiers (Access = entry, Artist = streamlined
  pro). Same engine, fewer knobs.
- ★ **Auto-Tune Slice** — sampler/beat tool that slices & pitches audio to a playable
  instrument; can turn a recorded banjo/vocal phrase into a tuned, MIDI-triggerable chop kit
  (creative resampling angle).
- · **Auto-Tune Hybrid** — (subscription tier; not separately in CONTENTS) low-latency tracking
  version for live.
- **Auto-Key** (app + plugin) — detects song key/scale and pushes it to the Auto-Tune
  plugins. Handy, not creative.

## ★ AVOX — the sound-design suite (the real prize here)
- ★★ **MUTATOR** — ring-mod + throat-length/width + alienize (reverse-chop). The alien/monster/
  inhuman texture maker. #1 for this rig.
- ★★ **THROAT** — physical-model vocal-tract reshaping, beyond-anatomy formants. Designed timbre.
- ★★ **CHOIR** — 1 → up to 32 unison voices. The choir/drone wall.
- ★ **DUO** — single automatic double-tracker (tight widening).
- ★ **ARTICULATOR** — 2-input vocoder (older talkbox-style; Vocodist is deeper).
- · **ASPIRE** — breath/aspiration add-subtract (subtle texture).
- · **PUNCH** — vocal compressor.
- · **WARM** — tube/tape colour (Velvet preamp / Crunch valve-amp sat) — usable saturation on
  any source.

## ★ Standalone creative tools
- ★★ **Harmony Engine 4x** — 4-voice harmony generator + per-voice Choir multiplier + throat
  modelling + Freeze. Works on a mono **instrument** too → instant choral/drone beds, MIDI-
  playable clusters.
- ★★ **Vocodist** (`Auto-Tune Vocodist`) — modern 20-model vocoder + internal/side-chain
  carrier synth + sat/ring-mod/chorus/noise. Synthetic talking-instrument textures.
- ★ **Mic Mod** — mic-modelling: takes the modelled "source mic" off and applies a "target
  mic" curve. Creative coloration: model a cheap/weird mic onto a clean source, or A/B
  characters as an EQ-with-attitude. (In Unlimited; if not separately listed it's folded into
  the suite.)

## · Vocal mix utilities (mostly skip for this rig)
- Vocal Compressor, Vocal EQ, **De-Esser**, **Vocal Reverb** (`Auto-Tune SoundSoap` = noise
  removal; `Vocal Prep` app = a chain front-end). Competent, generic; the rig already has
  Valhalla/Decapitator/etc. SoundSoap's *denoise* could double as a creative "scrub the life
  out of it" tool but isn't a headline.

|---|---|

| Mic-character coloration on a clean source | **Mic Mod** |
| Saturation/tube colour | **WARM** |

## Honesty
~90% of all Antares documentation, presets, and tutorials assume a **pop/rap lead-vocal**
context. The creative/non-vocal uses above are real and supported by the engines (input-range
"vocal OR instrument" controls, MIDI playability, external carriers, beyond-anatomy formant
ranges), but are **under-documented** — almost no published drone/doom/shoegaze artist credits
or genre-specific tutorials exist. Relevance here is by *technique + capability*, not by a
genre-peer endorsement.
