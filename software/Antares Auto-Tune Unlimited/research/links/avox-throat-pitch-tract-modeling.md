# THROAT Evo — control set + the formant/pitch-shift behaviour (spec triangulation)

Antares' own product pages are JS-rendered; the control set below is cross-confirmed from
Antares product copy, Sweetwater/PluginBoutique listings, and the Sound On Sound AVOX review,
and matches the live UI in the captured voice-actors walkthrough.

## What Throat is
"Process your vocals through a meticulously crafted physical model of the human vocal tract, so
you can literally **design your own vocal sound**." It models a real tract and lets you reshape
it past natural limits.

## Control set (confirmed)
- **Modeled-throat globals:** globally **stretch / shorten / widen / constrict** the modelled
  tract. (Length & Width knobs that re-scale the graph.)
- **5-point graphical Throat-Shaping display:** individually adjust **position + width of five
  points** — vocal cords → throat → mouth → lips. (Walkthrough: node 1 = cords, 2-3-4 = throat
  passageway, 5 = mouth/lips.) This is where the "physically impossible" timbres live.
- **Glottal waveform** edit — reshape the excitation source (the buzz/thickness).
- **Breathiness** — adds **variable-frequency noise**: subtle breath → raspiness → full whisper.
  (Walkthrough confirms a high-pass-freq control under it; higher = less original, more modelled.)
- **Pitch shift** — shift the voice **up or down as much as an octave** to create alternative
  vocal characters. (Evo addition vs. the old AVOX-2 Throat.)
- **Throat Precision** (Subtle / Medium / Extreme) — overall depth of change; "like Retune Speed,
  but for the throat." **Vocal Range** + **Glottal voice-type** source selectors; **Level
  Matching**; Output/Bypass; Reset.

## The KEY physical-modelling behaviour (why it's good on sustained sources)
Vocal formants are **resonances of the tract** that, in a real singer, **stay roughly constant
as pitch changes**. A naïve pitch-shifter drags formants with the fundamental → "chipmunk"/
unnatural. Throat models the tract separately, so it **corrects/holds formants while you pitch-
shift** — OR lets you set them deliberately WRONG. That decoupling (pitch here, formants there)
is the whole sound-design lever: hold a banjo/baritone/synth drone and morph ONLY the formants
for a vowel-locked, hollow, gender-wrong colour while pitch stays put.

## Stated creative uses (Antares + retailer copy)
- Subtle: refine timbre, add breathiness/raspiness, gentle gender shift (male↔female).
- Extreme: **"take vocals to physically impossible places"**, character voices for post, and
  **"weird, sci-fi and comical sounds… if applied with extreme settings."**
- Pairs with Mutator (Pitch Shift + Throat + Mutation) to "turn a vocalist into a demon."
