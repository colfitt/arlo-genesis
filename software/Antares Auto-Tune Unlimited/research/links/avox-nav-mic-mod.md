# Mic Mod — microphone modeling as creative coloration

## What it does
Mic Mod (a.k.a. Mic Mod EFX) holds digital models of **125+ classic/exotic microphones** built with
Antares' patented Spectral Shaping Tool tech. Workflow: tell it (a) **what mic you actually used**
(the "source" model) and (b) **what mic you want it to sound like** (the "target" model). It removes
the source mic's characteristic curve and applies the target's. Controls: **Modeled Mic** menu,
**Low Cut**, **Pattern** (pickup pattern), **Proximity** (models the proximity-effect bass bump at
different mic distances per modelled capsule), and **Tube Saturation** (the same engine as AVOX WARM).

## The honest limitation
Antares themselves say don't expect to turn an SM57 into a U87 — cheap-source → expensive-target
doesn't conjure detail that was never captured. The useful direction is the **other way**: take a
clean/neutral source and dial in a coloured, characterful, or deliberately cheap/weird mic curve.

## Reach-for in THIS rig
**SITUATIONAL creative EQ-with-attitude.** Because it's really a fancy, mic-shaped EQ + proximity +
tube stage, you can run it on ANY clean source (banjo DI, synth, drone, even a sampled part), not
just a vocal:
- Model a **cheap/lo-fi/ribbon-y** mic onto a too-clean banjo or synth = instant "recorded-wrong,"
  dark, boxy, vintage character that fits the degraded aesthetic.
- Crank **Proximity** for an exaggerated, woofy, too-close low-bump on a baritone/drone.
- Add **Tube Saturation** for warmth (it's the WARM engine) without loading WARM separately.
- A/B mic models as a fast "give this source a different voice" coloration pass.
Caveat: it's a coloration EQ, not a headline texture generator — and the rig's tape/lo-fi tools
(RC-20, SketchCassette) plus Decapitator/WARM cover most "make it sound worse/warmer" needs. Reach
for Mic Mod specifically when you want a *mic-shaped resonance/proximity* character that an EQ alone
won't give. Thinner coverage than the flagship AVOX modules — included by capability, low-priority.
