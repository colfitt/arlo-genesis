# AVOX Mutator (Evo) — Usage Guide

Mutator is the bundle's **destroyer** — ring modulation, throat reshaping, and a real-time
reverse-chop ("Alienize") that turn any mono source into robotic, metallic, monster, or
backwards-glitch textures. For a degrade/doom rig it's the #1 alien-texture tool: feed it the
banjo, baritone, a synth drone, or a vocal, then push it further through Decapitator / RC-20 /
SketchCassette.

---

## 1. The three sections

- **Voice Design** — **Shift Pitch** (±24 st / ±2 oct), **Throat Length**, **Throat Width**.
- **Mutate** — **24 pitch-tracking ring-mod mutations** + **Mutant Mix** (wet/dry).
- **Alienize** — real-time **reverse-chop**; **Dialect** = segment length (tempo-syncable).
[links/avox-mutator-product-and-musicradar.md; transcripts/avox-mutator-synth-textures-jamie-blake.md]

---

## 2. The two rules that make it usable

- **Odd/even Mutation rule** (manual, confirmed on the product page): **ODD-numbered mutation
  = inharmonic / atonal / gritty-growly**; **EVEN-numbered = more harmonic / melodic /
  pleasant.** Odd for destroyed/metallic doom; even for broken-but-musical synth doubles.
- **Voice-Design feel map** (Jamie Blake): Throat Length on **stretch = rubbery/gooey/soft**;
  Throat Width **wide = grittier/brighter**, **narrow = softer/rounder**. Pitch-up + sculpt =
  a layer that "follows the pitch like an analog synth lead."
[transcripts/avox-mutator-synth-textures-jamie-blake.md; links/avox-mutator-product-and-musicradar.md]

---

## 3. Routing & the Alienize gotcha

- Runs as **insert OR send/return**. The best workflow: blend a mutated layer **under** the
  dry via a return, and **automate the send** for varied texture.
- **Alienize is a separate path:** pulling **Mutant Mix wet down still passes the Alienize
  reverse audio** — so you can blend pitched-reverse vs un-pitched-reverse independently.
- **Alienize fast** = a reversed slap-delay / granular texture.
[transcripts/avox-mutator-synth-textures-jamie-blake.md]

---

## 4. Rig-specific recipes

- **Destroyed vocal / banjo creature:** odd Mutation + Pitch down + Throat Length stretch →
  growl → into **Decapitator / RC-20 / SketchCassette** to degrade further.
- **Alien drone bed:** low Mutant Mix under a sustained banjo/synth + Alienize ON,
  tempo-synced, short Dialect → backwards-glitch wall.
- **Bendy broken-synth double:** even Mutation + slight Pitch on a **send**, blended low under
  the dry.
- **Logic AU:** mono insert or send; **print/freeze** (ring-mod + pitch-track is heavy).

## 6. Common pitfalls / gotchas
- **Mono input only** — comp a clean single-note take.
- **CPU** — ring-mod + pitch-track → print/freeze. Subscription auth. AU (Logic-fine) + VST3;
  bounce heavy returns in Ableton Lite.
