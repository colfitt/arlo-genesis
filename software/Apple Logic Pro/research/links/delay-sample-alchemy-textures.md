Delay + granular texture moves for evolving drone beds.

## Delay Designer (multitap, up to 26 taps)
- Per-tap controls: delay time, level, pan, **HP/LP filter, pitch-shift, feedback** — sculpt anything from ADT to cascading custom patterns.
- Sound-design use: pitch-shift individual taps for a chord-of-echoes; pan taps across the field for a spreading cloud; feed a couple of taps back for self-building texture. This is the stock answer to a multi-head/cascading delay.

## Tape Delay (ambient/drone)
- Make a **mono** Tape Delay on an aux; preset **"1/4 Note Flutter"**; **Feedback 0%**; **0% dry / 100% wet**.
- Add **pitch modulation** (flutter) for tape-drift detune.
- Chain into **Space Designer with an unconventional IR** for "intriguing" hybrid delay-verbs.

## Sample Alchemy / Alchemy (granular)
- Drag **field recordings or vocal chops** into the granular engine; use the performance controls (XY pad, motion) for subtle drift → evolving ambient pads, drones, glitchy/noisy soundscapes, rhythmic plucks.
- Alchemy is explicitly a strong **pad source**, especially for "glitchy/noisy" textures.

## Rig fit
- Sample Alchemy is the natural place to drop **rig recordings** (a banjo drone, OP-1 loop, Octatrack stem, a degraded Big-Time/Generation-Loss pass) and granulate them into a moving bed — the in-Logic complement to the hardware samplers.
- Delay Designer's per-tap pitch is a stock way to fake the harmonized/octave cascades the time-pedals do, when staying in the box.
