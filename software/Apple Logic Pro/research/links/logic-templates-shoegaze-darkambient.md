Two off-the-shelf full-session templates in the rig's genre space. Useful as reference structures to reverse-engineer / steal a bus layout from, even if not bought.

## COMP — Shoegaze template
- Genre: shoegaze ("ethereal mixture of Choir, guitar distortion and effects, feedback, and overwhelming volume").
- Specs: **127 BPM**, key **D major**, 2:56, **21 MIDI channels**, ~230 MB.
- Built around the dense, effects-heavy, layered-instrument shoegaze approach (choir + distorted/feedback guitars + volume). The product page does NOT publish the internal track/bus/plugin chain — you'd have to open it to see the channel strips. So this is a *structure reference*, not a settings source.

## Asylum — Dark Ambient / Electronic template
- Composed by film/TV composer **Jon Brooks**.
- "Mysterious, suspenseful, dark cinematic ambient electronica drone" with experimental/industrial influences.
- Same caveat: marketed by mood/genre, internal chains not published.

## Honesty flag
These are paid commercial templates; the public pages give genre/specs but not the channel-strip detail. They confirm that "shoegaze wall" and "dark-ambient drone" are recognized template categories in Logic, and give a credible tempo/key/track-count starting point — but they are not a free, copyable settings source. Treat as "structures worth stealing the *shape* of," not verified recipes.
