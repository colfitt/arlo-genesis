# Live 12 Lite — official spec sheet (authoritative)

The vendor's own feature page. Settles the conflicting forum numbers (some
posts quote old 8-scene/16-track figures from Live 9/10/11 Lite — Live 12 Lite
is different).

## Track / routing caps (the real ceiling)
- **8 audio and MIDI tracks** (total, any combination)
- **16 scenes**
- **2 send and return tracks** (so only 2 aux/FX sends — a real constraint for
  a parallel-heavy ambient workflow)
- **8 mono audio input channels**
- **8 mono audio output channels**

## Instruments included (5)
- Drum Rack
- Impulse
- Instrument Rack
- Simpler
- **Drift** (the subtractive synth — this is the one genuinely useful sound
  source in Lite for pads/drones)

> NOT in Lite: Operator, Wavetable, Sampler, Analog, Tension, Electric,
> Collision, Granulator — all the deeper synths/samplers live in Standard/Suite.

## Effects
- **16 audio effects** (incl. Compressor, Reverb, Delay, EQ Eight/Three, Auto
  Filter, Saturator, etc.)
- **10 MIDI effects** (incl. Arpeggiator, Chord, Scale)

## Included library
- 925 instrument + drum racks
- 348 MIDI clips
- 493 presets
- 3,309 samples (loops/drum hits/multisamples)
- 4 templates
- Packs: "Beat Tools" + "Build and Drop"

## Not present (by omission — confirmed against compare-editions)
- No Max for Live
- No audio-to-MIDI conversion
- No audio slicing / Slice-to-MIDI
- No Convolution/Hybrid Reverb, no Sampler/Wavetable/Operator
- No Comping (linked-track editing), no modulation, no Stem Separation
