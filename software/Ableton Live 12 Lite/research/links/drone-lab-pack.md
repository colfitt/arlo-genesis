# Drone Lab pack — partly Lite-usable, with caveats

Official Ableton pack purpose-built for sustained/drone work. Most rack-relevant
shared content for this aesthetic, BUT read the Lite caveats.

## Contents
- 600+ samples, 70+ Instrument & Drum Racks, **20 Audio Effect Racks**, 45+ MIDI
  clips, 4 Improvisation Sets, **1 Max for Live device** ("Harmonic Drone
  Generator" — an 8-voice M4L synth).
- Price USD 59 (free/included with Push 3). Min edition stated = **Live 12 Intro 12.0+**.

## Lite reality check (the important part)
- The pack's stated minimum is **Intro**, not Lite — and Intro ≠ Lite. Packs that
  target Intro can still contain devices/instruments absent from Lite. Expect SOME
  of the 70 instrument racks / 20 FX racks to throw missing-device errors in Lite
  if they lean on Wavetable/Operator/Echo/Spectral/EQ Eight etc.
- The **Harmonic Drone Generator (M4L)** will NOT run in Lite (no Max for Live).
  That's the pack's signature instrument — losing it is a real hit.
- What DOES survive in Lite: the raw **samples** (drop onto Simpler/audio tracks),
  the **MIDI clips**, and any **Audio Effect Racks built only from core devices**
  (Reverb/Delay/Auto Filter/Erosion/Redux/Saturator/Chorus/Phaser/Utility).

## Verdict
Useful as a SAMPLE + MIDI source and for the core-device racks, but do NOT assume
the whole pack loads in Lite. Buy it for the samples/improv sets, not the M4L synth.
Honest: the marquee drone instrument is Suite/M4L-gated.

## Certainty
HIGH on contents and the M4L gate (official page). The "some racks won't load in
Lite" warning is inference from the confirmed Lite device list (high confidence,
not individually tested rack-by-rack).
