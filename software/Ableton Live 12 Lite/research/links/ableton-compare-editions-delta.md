# Live 12 editions delta — Lite/Intro vs Standard vs Suite (authoritative)

Note: the compare-editions table itself only shows Intro/Standard/Suite. Lite
sits a notch BELOW Intro (8 tracks vs Intro's 16). Lite's own numbers come from
the live-lite features page; the cross-tier feature gates below come from this
comparison.

## Tracks / scenes / routing
| | Lite | Intro | Standard | Suite |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Audio+MIDI tracks | **8** | 16 | Unlimited | Unlimited |
| Scenes | 16 | 16 | Unlimited | Unlimited |
| Send/return tracks | **2** | 2 | 12 | 12 |
| Mono in/out | 8/8 | 8/8 | 256/256 | 256/256 |
| Instruments | 5 | 8 | 12 | 21 |
| Audio effects | 16 | 27 | 36 | 59 |
| MIDI effects | 10 | 12 | 13 | 14 |

## Feature gates — what unlocks where

**Exclusive to Standard & Suite (i.e. the first real upgrade from Lite/Intro
buys these):**
- Audio-to-MIDI (Melody/Harmony/Drums to MIDI)
- Audio slicing (Slice to New MIDI Track)
- Linked-track editing / **Comping** (take lanes)
- REX file support
- POW-r dithering
- **Device/clip modulation**
- 17 additional MIDI tools/transformations
- Complex/Complex Pro warp modes (Standard+)

**Exclusive to Suite only (the big jump):**
- **Max for Live** (no M4L devices at all below Suite — this kills a huge swath
  of ambient/generative community devices for Lite users)
- Sampler, Wavetable, Operator, Analog, Tension, Electric, Collision
- Convolution Reverb, Hybrid Reverb
- Granulator III, Corpus (full), Echo (some), Drum Sampler
- Stem Separation (Music.AI algorithm)
- The full Suite sound library (70+ GB)

## Takeaway for a hardware-front rig
The two limits that actually bite a drone/ambient performer in Lite:
1. **8 tracks + 2 sends** — caps how many simultaneous parallel layers/aux-reverb
   chains you can run live.
2. **No Max for Live** — locks out the community's generative/ambient M4L
   ecosystem (which is Suite-only anyway, not a Standard feature).

Everything else (warping basics, Session view, clip launch, recording hardware,
Link, MIDI clock) is fully present in Lite.
