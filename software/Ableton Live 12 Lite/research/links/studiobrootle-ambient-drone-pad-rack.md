# Studio Brootle "Ambient Drone / Pad Maker" — free Audio Effect Rack (verify in Lite)

A FREE downloadable Audio Effect Rack designed to be fed an external source — exactly
the pedalboard/banjo-front use case. Worth trying, with a Lite caveat.

## What it is
- An **Audio Effect Rack** (makes no sound itself — "run a sample or synth through
  it"). → Drop it on an audio track receiving the VG-800 / baritone / OP-1 and it
  becomes an instant ambient processor.
- 9 macros: **Drive 1, Drive 2, Reverb (wet/dry), 3x Pitch Wobble** (per echo unit),
  **2x filter freq (HiShelf, LowShelf).**
- Internals: multiple delay units w/ feedback, fully-wet Reverb, overdrive/drive
  stages, tape-wobble modules, hi/lo-shelf filters.

## Lite compatibility — UNVERIFIED, likely partial
- The page does NOT state stock-only. The "overdrive" stage may be the **Overdrive**
  device (Suite — not in Lite) and "tape wobble" may use **Grain Delay** (not in Lite)
  or an M4L module.
- **Action:** download and open in Lite; if it errors, the broken devices will be
  flagged — swap Overdrive→Saturator, Grain-Delay-wobble→Chorus-Ensemble or a Delay
  with modulated time, and the rest (Delay/Reverb/filters) should survive.
- Reverb wet/dry, the delay/feedback core, and shelf filters ARE reproducible in Lite
  even if this exact rack needs surgery.

## Verdict
A good free starting point AND a good Lite test case — but treat as "may need device
swaps," not guaranteed-loadable. Honest: untested on Lite here.
