Review with usable settings guidance.

- Primary use case for this reviewer = VOCALS; also good on hip-hop, pop, synthwave, anything 80s/90s-flavored.
- **Age = the crucial knob**: "determines how warped and worn the tape sound is." Treat Age as the master degradation control and the tape Type/Quality as the tonal voicing.
- Encourages trying ALL the tape types/qualities — differences between some adjacent profiles are subtle (may need headphones to A/B), but real when you tweak.
- Lots of presets (old tape machines, saturation models, flutter, chorus modes, distortion amps) — good map of the factory categories.
- Install is simple (VST3 + AAX named here; AU also exists per the site/manual). 5/5, "great plugin at a great price."
