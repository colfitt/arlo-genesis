# Which AD2 kits / ADpaks suit moody / vintage / dry / ambient drums

The aesthetic wants DRY, dampened, vintage, room-controllable kits — not bright modern
metal kits. Best matches:

## Dead & Dry Collection (top pick for doom/slowcore/desert/psych)
- Recorded at **Ingrid Studios, Stockholm**. Every piece **dampened** with the old
  tricks — "tea cloths, towels, blankets, tape, moon gel." That's the dead thud you
  want for slow/heavy and lo-fi.
- **Vintage Dead** kit: toms converted to **concert toms (no resonant heads)** —
  serious thump, "a boatload of mojo." XLN pitches it for **Tame Impala, Khruangbin,
  Glass Beams** (psych indie) and **QOTSA / Death From Above 1979** (heavy). Excellent
  doom/desert/psych base.
- **Vintage Dry** kit: **1968 Ludwig Silver Sparkle**, '70s vibe; 30 mix-ready
  presets from soft early-'70s folk to punchy disco. Forum consensus also flags
  **Vintage Dry as THE indie sound** (Men I Trust, Mac DeMarco vibe).

## Indie ADpak (warm/analog, tape-tracked)
- Handcrafted kit through **rare mics, analog preamps, and 2″ tape**; blue-sparkle
  ICE Custom Shop kit, vintage Slingerland hardware, **Premier Keith Moon snare**.
  Warm analog tone out of the box — least work to get a vintage indie sound.

## Boutique Mallets (ambient / soft / post-rock washes)
- **Felt mallets** + 22 production-ready presets aimed at **ambient, organic,
  cinematic, experimental** (also includes regular sticks). This is your soft-mallet,
  cinematic-swell, slowcore-intro kit. Pair with the Delerb Swirl reverb.

## From the DrumSpy review (kit picks by character)
- **Vintage/organic:** **Fairfax Vol. 1 & 2** (tracked at **Sound City**) — roomy,
  classic-rock, great for layering.
- **Clean/versatile:** **United Pop** (Leedy & Ludwig at United Recording) — the
  "do-anything" natural kit; good clean source to then degrade.
- **AVOID for this aesthetic:** "**Studio Pop**" and "**Studio Rock**" — reviewer
  calls them "very dated"/over-produced; and the default modern kits' bright cymbals
  fight the washed sound.

## Genre-targeted 3rd-party preset packs (community)
- **Prod Shadow "DIY Drums"** — 30 presets + loops + MIDI specifically for
  **shoegaze / indie / post-punk / slowcore** (real recorded samples). Closest
  off-the-shelf match to the owner's aesthetic. (prodshadow.com / yt 4zBgM9bjF3Q —
  video is the other agent's, product page noted here.)
