## LoFi Weekly free packs — 3 named presets with module recipes (the useful part)
- **LoFi Light** — "subtle vinyl warmth + sampler-style aliasing." → light **Noise** (vinyl) + a touch of **Digital** (rate reduction for the sampler aliasing). The gentle everyday color.
- **Tunnel Vision** — "wide, ambient feel with radio-style coloration." → **Space** (reverb, wide/stereo) + band-limited **EQ/Focus** (the AM-radio bandpass) + **Width**. The ambient-wash recipe.
- **Feeling Lost** — "deeper, spacey textures using FLUTTER, REVERB, and 8-BIT NOISE for a worn-out atmospheric vibe." → **Magnetic flutter/wear** + **Space** + **Digital** (8-bit) + **Noise**. The drone/atmosphere recipe — closest to this rig.
- **LoFi Tapestry** (separate free preset) — "instant warmth, wobble, texture for melodies; nostalgic tape-saturated feel." → **Wobble** + **Distort** + **Noise**.
