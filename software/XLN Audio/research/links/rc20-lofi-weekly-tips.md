Lo-fi-producer-focused tips. Distilled from the accessible snippet:

- RC-20 = "an in-depth lofi and vintage FX plugin" with the **6 modules: Noise, Wobble,
  Distort, Digital, Space, Magnetic**.
- **Wobble** — add warble/pitch modulation for retro character.
- **Noise** — vinyl crackle + tape hiss for an instant vintage floor.
- **Magnitude trick** — "Use this control to **lower the intensity of all modules at once,**
  allowing you to capture the sound of the preset you are using but make it more **subtle**."
  (The standard fix for heavy-handed factory presets: load a preset, pull Magnitude back to
  taste — see Pitfalls.)
- **Compression / Ducking** — "Lofi hip hop loves over-compression, whether the **ducking**
  feel that is nostalgic for J Dilla beats or that **SP-404 vinyl-sim compression** everyone
  tries to emulate." (Noise module **Duck**, plus the Magnetic/Wear motion, is the route.)
