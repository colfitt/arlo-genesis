KVR Audio · "XLN RC-20 Retro Color" Effects Forum megathread (multi-page; notes from pp.15–16)

The long-running owner thread — strengths, limits, and the most rig-relevant artist namecheck.

## Strengths
- "**Amazing assortment of effects. It really has enough to cover ~95% of all the audio
  destruction effects**." The one-box argument.
- **MIDI automation** praised for creative control.
- The **Magnetic dropout** effect is called out as something competitors (Sonitex) lacked.
- **Rig-relevant artist use-case (sourced as user opinion):** users say RC-20 alone is enough
  to emulate **The Caretaker** and **William Basinski** "without requiring additional
  plugins." (Decay/loop-degradation drone aesthetic — exactly this rig. Flag: it's a forum
  *capability* claim about emulating those artists, **not** evidence either artist actually
  uses RC-20.)

## Weaknesses / wishes
- **Wants deeper tape character:** "I'd like it to have **more of the tape sound, so I won't
  need to combine it with a specific tape plugin**." (The exact gap SketchCassette/ChowTape
  fill.)
- **Module depth:** "Each module could be more advanced imo. Maybe some extra features hidden
  behind the 'hatch' of each module." (No hidden deep-edit pages — what you see is it.)
- **Slow updates** from XLN; some **installer/plugin-manager** friction moving VST2 files into
  subfolders; users delete the 32-bit build before launching the DAW.

## Context
- Competed historically with **Sonitex STX-1260** (and later VYBZ, ~2021). For *realistic*
  tape, the thread points to **Aberrant DSP SketchCassette** (variable tape speeds/formulas)
  as the better pick.
