Cross-list of the strongest free User-Library ensembles for the drone/dark-ambient/degraded aesthetic (need full Reaktor 6). Entry numbers are the User Library `entry/show/<N>` IDs.

**Drone / dark-ambient:**
- **SpaceDrone MOD+++** — variable filter modes, counters, pitch + wave modulators; "epic waves of noise," "warped modulations of reverberated discords and cacophonies." → 13079. (Modded SpaceDrone; closest to "emulate Loscil / Ben Frost.")
- **Contemplative Soundscapes** — Eser Karaca. "Eery, drone-like soundscapes and spheres with hisses and noise effects." → 7071.
- **Nebula – Clouds Synthesizer** — impulse generator + six resonators → "otherworldly dub-like pad sounds, and abstract ambient textures." → 13065.
- **Illusions of Sound 2.6** — three oscillators, MS-20-style filter, LFO, flange/delay/reverb; "dream-like, smooth, beautiful sounds," spatial sound design. → 12583.

**Granular / texture / generative:**
- **Drone-E** — drone via **granular synthesis** + pitch-shift + reverb + sub generator. 344 Audio: "one of our favourite methods for creating drones in software ever." (KVR dark-ambient thread also rates it top.) [User Library]
- **GRIP / Grip Cloud Synth** — Uwe Hoenig. Simplified granular UI; "launch your samples into the cloud and soften them into tender droplets of granularity"; shimmering 4-D textures; can **granularise your own live input**; Maschine-friendly. [User Library]
- **Magma** — "hidden gem" for convincing "liquidy textures" — volcano/lava sound design, works in busy mixes. [User Library]
- **lifeforms03** — "insect-like chirping, airy swooshes, **granular glitches**" + **randomize** button. → 3607.
- **Metaphysical Scenery / Metaphysical Fabrications** — generative drone reskins of Metaphysical Function: "generative drone synth has provided hours of endless fun," "alien planet soundscape goodness." Fabrications adds Resochord comb filters, chord progressions, sampling. → Fabrications 9813.
- **Sylvan** — Steven Bateman. "Gated noises sent through morph filters → dreamlike sound textures." Ambient + generative. → 11707.
- **Space and Time Generator** — three sound generators, ADSR, filters, stereo-field generator, reverb. Ambient/generative. → 6597.

**Degrade / lo-fi FX ensembles (for processing your own sound — the "recorded-wrong" tools):**
- **VHS Audio Degradation Suite** — James Peck. "Subtle lo-fi'esque esthetics to ultra-damaged 80s cassette tapes." #1 most-downloaded 2018. → 11003.
- **Grungelator** — vintage tape + vinyl degradation. → 11027.
- **Tape Mate** — Stephen Reid. Tape warmth/saturation. → 10131.
- **FM Radio** — vintage radio emulation effect (freq/noise/filter), "warts and all." [User Library]

**Blocks noise-makers:** **Diadem of Alien Wizardry II** (Blocks, atonal oscillators + random mod, → 12881), **Super Genius** (Blocks, multiple filters + randomization, "cosmic soundscapes and swathes of noise," → 10057), **aL / Alien Landscape** (4 knobs + randomize, 2001 stargate vibe, → 2611).
