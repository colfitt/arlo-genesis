# RAUM reverb — the design + the ambient/drone moves

RAUM is the **single most rig-relevant NI effect** for this aesthetic. It's also frequently **free** (NI holiday giveaways), so even Standard owners likely have it.

## Three algorithms
- **Grounded** — room paradigm: strong early reflections + dense tail. Feedback-delay-network design (early reflections baked into the network, not separate). Tiny ambiences → large chambers. "Dense" maximizes resonance density for smoother tails.
- **Airy** — hall paradigm: naturally dispersed early reflections, smooth tail. Medium chambers → cathedrals and beyond.
- **Cosmic** — NOT a classic reverb. **Cascaded all-pass filters in a feedback loop** that progressively blur/diffuse each echo from the pre-delay → abstract spaces, ambient soundscapes, granular textures. This is the doom/shoegaze mode.

## The creative engine
- **Freeze** — "an interactive performance element." Infinite drones, live manipulation. Catch a chord/wall → freeze → it sustains forever while you play over it. The core ambient move.
- **Pre-delay as resonator / comb filter / looper** — with feedback added it's a **lossless sample-quantized delay**: crystal-clear filtering (comb), echo, resonator, and **four-bar looping with auto-overdub** (adaptive-limiter overdub behaviour). One reverb doubles as a looper.
- **Size** — tight spaces → "almost like a granular effect" at extremes.
- **Modulation** — subtle echo-breaking → chorusing → "weird, uncanny territory" with **detuned, dissonant** tails. Add a shifting/detuned feel to the tail or go full Cosmic for "wonky atmospheric soundscapes and unique granular textures."

## Rig recipes (drone/doom/shoegaze)
- **Infinite drone bed:** Cosmic mode, play a chord on a pad/banjo → **Freeze** → wall sustains; modulate Size/Mod live.
- **Detuned shimmer wall:** Airy, big Size, crank Modulation into the detuned zone → seasick cathedral.
- **Resonator/comb on a tail:** pre-delay + feedback as a tuned comb filter on a sustained note.
- **One-knob automation** — every param is automation-friendly and "playable"; ride Size or Mod under a held note.
