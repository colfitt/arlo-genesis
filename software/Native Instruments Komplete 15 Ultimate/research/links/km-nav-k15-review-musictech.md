# Komplete 15 review (MusicTech) — distilled

- Three new genre-focused **Select** bundles (Beats, Band, Electronic) at reduced price (~£89 vs £179) sit *below* Standard.
- New **Session** instruments = "standard sample playback with ready-made rhythmic and melodic patterns": **Session Percussionist**, **Acoustic Sunburst Deluxe**, **Icon Bass**.
- Headline flagships across the bundle: **Massive X, Guitar Rig 7 Pro, Reaktor 6, Kontakt 8.**
- **For textural work** the reviewer specifically flags **Conflux** and **Vocal Colours**, plus **Kithara** — "an idiosyncratic sampler" that "smooshes together multiple string samples to create a single envelope of sound" (CE-only).
- **Ultimate tier targets media composers:** Symphony Essentials orchestral, the **Action** series scoring tools (pattern-based, ensemble version of the Session approach), **Brainworx** mixing tools, iZotope **Ozone** mastering.
- Tier ladder (£): Select £89 · Standard £539 · Ultimate £1,079 · Collector's £1,619.
- CE = 165+ instruments, expanded Symphony Series + Valves Pro.
- Reviewer caveat: higher tiers are "almost too complete for any one person."
