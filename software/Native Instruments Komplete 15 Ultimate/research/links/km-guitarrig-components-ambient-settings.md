Native Instruments Blog — Guitar Rig components + concrete ambient settings

The best source of copyable GR settings for evolving ambient/drone textures, plus the effect roster.

## The key delays (for walls/ambience)
- **Replika GR** — multi-algorithm powerhouse: wide delays, ping-pong, multiple saturation characters.
- **Psych Delay** — "diffuse delays... reversed and pitched for dreamy soundscapes"; stereo; atmospheric ambient echoes + **reverse / backwards-tape (1960s)** effects. **Ambient setting: delay time 300–500 ms, feedback ~40%, low dry/wet mix** (underlying layer, not centre). Modulate delay time with an **LFO at ~0.1 Hz** for organic, non-repetitive echoes.
- **Grain Delay** — glitchy, repeats short snippets you can manipulate (granular textures).
- **Tape Echo** — warm/warped (vocal throws). **Delay Man** — classic analog pedal.

## The key reverbs
- **Replika Shimmer** — octavized shimmer (Electro-Harmonix style); add reverb tail at pitch intervals **−12 to +12 semitones**, with Time + Feedback control. The shimmer/wall maker.
- **Ice Verb** — cold, **Radiohead-style modulation**.
- **Studio Reverb** — atmospheric "place" reverb. **Setting: pre-delay ~80 ms, mix ~40%; automate pre-delay** to evolve a rhythmic feel without percussion.
- **Spring Reverb** (big stereo tank), **Vintage Verb** (mono-spring retro), **RC 24 / RC 48** (famous digital reverbs).

## Lo-fi / degrade
- **Tape Wobble** — wow, flutter, speed, **Age** (rolls top off + adds hiss), **Scrape** (degrades, crunchy). For lo-fi on sustained notes/pads/melodies.
- **Noise Machine** — hiss/crackle + hum models (50/60 Hz, transformer, power, amp), vinyl noise.
- **Vintage Vibrato** — organ vibrato/chorus, 6 intensity modes.
- **Color / Kolor** — 10 distortion algorithms (several from iZotope **Trash**), flexible EQ + **Bass Saver**.
- **Bite** — bitcrusher (also shipped as its own plugin in Komplete).

## Copyable ambient-texture recipes (NI's own settings)
1. **Multi-modulation space**: **Choral → Phaser 9** (phaser rate low, depth ~40%); automate Choral Rate + Amount **0.3–0.7 Hz** for evolving width.
2. **Stereo expansion**: **Split Mix** width ~60–70%; use **Crossover** to process bands separately.
3. **Granular evolving echo**: **Psych Delay** 300–500 ms / fb ~40% / low mix, LFO on delay-time ~0.1 Hz.
4. **Subtle frequency-shift drift**: **Freak** (freq shifter) ~0.1 Hz rate, 50–100 Hz shift, "imperceptible"; automate Type/Contour in sparse sections.
5. **Warm amp bed**: **Cool Plex** at low gain (saturation without distortion); use Presence for air.
6. **Rhythmic-without-drums**: **Analog Sequencer** modulating a reverb's wet 30–60% per step; delay before/after to smooth jumps.
7. **Harmonic enrichment**: **Pitch Pedal** ~20% mix, +7 semitones, blend into reverb tails.
8. **Vowel movement**: **Formant Filter** + LFO ~0.2 Hz on the Vowel control.
Philosophy: "treat reverb and delay as instrumental elements... something is always shifting in the background."
