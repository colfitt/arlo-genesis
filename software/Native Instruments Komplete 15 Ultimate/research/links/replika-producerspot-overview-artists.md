# Replika XT — overview + artist presets (distilled)

## Modes & combining
Five modes (Vintage Digital / Modern / Analogue / Tape / Diffusion) "which can be combined to
create classic mixing tricks" (→ via Dual mode Serial/Parallel, you combine e.g. Tape + Diffusion).

## Engine notes
- **Pattern + per-tap panning** — "rhythmic repeats that shift organically… dial in the perfect
  stereo image with panning for each delay and the dry signal."
- **Ducking** — reduces delay when transients hit → "keep percussive hits nice and tight."
- **7 time-based effects** — "can be combined with the delay modes OR used on their own" → Replika
  XT doubles as a standalone modulation/FX rack (chorus/phaser/etc.) even with delay near-zero.

## Artist presets (the documented "notable users")
- **Richard Devine** and **Funkstörung** contributed factory presets (+ other sound designers).
- Character: "from **hyper-modulated echoes** to subtle widening effects." Devine/Funkstörung =
  experimental/IDM pedigree → the hyper-modulated/self-oscillating/inharmonic presets are the
  doom/experimental starting points (browse those first). FLAG: no preset *names* documented; the
  artist credit is real (corroborated by NI product page + Devine's own social post) but specific
  patch-by-patch breakdowns aren't published — treat as "browse the Devine/Funkstörung bank."

## Use
Works on single instruments and full mixes; "ambient textures and rhythmic processing."
