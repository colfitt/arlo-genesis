blog.native-instruments.com · NI Blog · "Download our top 5 free drone synths for REAKTOR"

Curated NI list of free **drone** ensembles for the Reaktor User Library (require full Reaktor 6, not Player). All are one-key-hold drone machines — ideal beds to layer under banjo.

1. **4x4 Drone Thing V2** — John Squibb. "Hauntingly ethereal." Combines sine + sawtooth banks through HP/LP filters with sine/tri/pulse LFO modulators. Controls: 2×4 sine banks, 2×4 saw banks, HP/LP filters, LFOs. → User Library entry 13991.
2. **Space Modulator** — Colin Chabot. "Absorbingly ambient" **self-playing** ensemble — "beautifully evolving textures and tones," celestial soundscapes. Scale + note programming for custom interstellar compositions. (This is the souped-up cousin of SpaceDrone — "SpaceDrone on steroids" in other NI write-ups.) → entry 6427.
3. **Britta Harmonic Drone Synthesizer** — Robert Sigmuntowski. **Additive** synth, "dreamily meditative drone sounds" from harmonic sine tones (healing/meditation frequencies). Controls: harmonic on/off switches, speed knobs for panning/swells, root note, fine-tune. → entry 7983.
4. **Droplets 4** — TTQ. Four-voice drone synth for "cinematic soundscapes and tense, atonal atmospheres," inspired by **LYRA-8** and Stereo Field. Interconnected controls, two-step sequencer per voice. → entry 13511.
5. **CRUDE** — Bolle. "Crushingly heavy" drone generator, "deliciously diabolical." Twin distortion, bit crusher, two LP filters, two wavetable oscillators, three LFOs, chorus, **randomize button**. → entry 14515. (See the dedicated Synth Anatomy capture — adds Krell randomization.)
