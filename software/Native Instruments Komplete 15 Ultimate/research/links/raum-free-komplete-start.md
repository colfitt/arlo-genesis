# RAUM is free — Komplete Start (relevant for the rig)

- RAUM first shipped to everyone as the **NI Christmas 2019/2020 free giveaway** (the tutorial in
  `transcripts/raum-ni-walkthrough.md` is from that era, "free until 31 Jan 2020").
- It is **now part of the free Komplete Start bundle** — confirmed current as of May 2026. So the
  full RAUM is available even to non-Komplete owners; you don't need Ultimate to use it.
- KVR/MusicTech framing: "one of NI's best reverbs… sculpt sounds from small spaces to huge,
  granular textures." 403/triangulated on the NI product page (auto-fetch blocked); availability
  corroborated across Komplete Start page summary + MusicTech giveaway news + KVR product page.
- Practical: the rig already owns it inside K15 Ultimate; the free angle just means presets/patches
  shared by other RAUM users (free-tier) are all compatible. FLAG: Replika XT is NOT free — the
  free **Replika** is the older single-mode version; **Replika XT** (5 modes + FX) is Komplete-only.
