**KVR "Reaktor 6 Dark Ambient" thread — community reach-for list:**
- **Metaphysical Function** (factory) — "dark, it's ambient, it straddles the line between composition and sound design." (Ficciones)
- **Drone-E** — "amazing, especially geared toward ambiances, soundscapes." (Neon Breath)
- **A Memory of Light** (Blocks ensemble) — "dark droney stuff," whale-themed presets. (GaryG)
- **Everything Turns** — "not free, but worth it IMHO." (bharris22)
- **Continuum** — wavetable monosynth with 2D wavetable surface + wavetable randomization, "exotic click patterns." (cron)
- **Lleisiau – Breath of Stars** + **Lliwiaeth** — dave dove's dark-ambient instruments.
- Advice (repeated): **learn the factory library deeply before chasing User-Library uploads** — "stick for just a few ones, learn them and use them." Blocks are well-suited to drone (V0RT3X). Use **slow LFOs + morphing timbres** for evolving soundscapes.

**Self-running Blocks drone recipe** (from the BoBSwanS "Distorted Drone Experiment #1" video description — a hand-wired self-generating patch, downloadable at User Library entry 11455):
- Core reverb: **Cal Scott's DOOM VERB** (from the "Avant Blocks" collection — a community Blocks pack worth grabbing for doom/dark work).
- **Modded Kodiak Seq** (entry 11315) for the sequence.
- **RScale** (entry 10689) scale quantizer.
- **SimpleMan Synth** by Matthew Friedrichs (entry 11419) as a voice.
- Re-skinned **Complex Oscillator** + a ported-to-Block delay.
→ Pattern: sequencer + scale-quantizer → simple synth voice → DOOM VERB + delay, left self-running = distorted generative drone.

**Ambient-loop machine (Reaktor Tips):** team **Fast FX** with a Sound Generator — here **Metaphysical Function** ("complex, evolving walls of sound that need no accompaniment"). Fast FX adds rhythmic structure on top of the generative wall so it slots into rhythmic productions = "an ambient loop generation machine."
