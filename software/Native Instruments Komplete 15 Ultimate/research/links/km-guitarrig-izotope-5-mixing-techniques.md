iZotope (Native Instruments) Learn · "5 techniques for mixing with Guitar Rig 7 Pro" · 2023/2024

The definitive "GR7 as a full FX/mixing rack on non-guitar sources" article. Frames GR7 as "a multi-effects rack with cutting-edge circuit modeling... its effects span crucial mixing tools including EQ, compression, reverbs, distortion, and more" — usable on vocals, drums, keyboards, strings, synths.

## 1. Augment the mix with delays & reverbs
GR7's delays: **Tape Echo** (warm/warped, for vocal throws), **Delay Man** (classic pedal emulation), **Psych Delay** (diffuse delays you can **reverse and pitch** for dreamy soundscapes), **Grain Delay** (glitchy, snippet-based), **Replika GR** (multiple algorithms — wide delays, ping-pong, saturation).
Reverbs: **Spring Reverb** (larger-than-life stereo tank), **Vintage Verb** (mono-spring retro character), **Studio Reverb** (atmospheric "place" without obvious reverb), **Ice Verb** ("cold, **Radiohead-style modulation**"), **Replika Shimmer** (octavized/shimmer, Electro-Harmonix-inspired), **RC 48 / RC 24** (the famous digital reverbs for period effects). Applied to vocal throws/leads and synth lines.

## 2. Top-notch compressors (drum bus)
- Room mics: **VC 2A** (optical, smashed). Overheads: **Supercharger** with **parallel compression via the Split Mix** module ("tube-like, glassy character"). Toms: **Solid Dynamics** (gating) + **VC76** all-buttons-in. Snare: **VC 160** serial (the "thwack") + **Fast Comp** parallel via Split Mix. Kick/snare bus: **Tube Comp** parallel. Drum bus: **mid/side** compression, blend ~10% of the compressed signal to avoid smearing the stereo image.

## 3. Dynamic soundscapes with Modifiers
**Split Mix** splits the path into A and B, each with its own stereo pan + a blend control. Assign an **LFO Modifier** to the Split Mix blend = a custom **auto-panner** (A hard left, B hard right, LFO sweeps the blend across the stereo field). General principle: "you can use **LFOs, envelopes, sequencers, and input signals** to change any module control." Demoed on a percussion/tom loop.

## 4. Re-amp DI bass
**Bass Invader** amp favoring "room" on the cab slider; dual-track with a second track on **Ampeg B15** emulation, **flip polarity on one** track (preserve low end), blend → "more growling, mid-forward tone." Directly relevant to re-amping a DI bass/baritone.

## 5. Guitar tones
Distorted lead = fuzz pedal → amp → **second amp in series**. Clean DI on a separate track = **Plex** amp barely overdriven.

## Architecture takeaways
- Modular plugin with searchable modules; the **Signal Flow** chart visualizes the path.
- "**Container-style design allowing creative chaining and parallel routing**" — supports an FX-rack methodology for multi-stage processing.
- **Split Mix** is the core parallel/blend tool (no aux send needed — like Decapitator's Mix knob but for whole sub-chains).
