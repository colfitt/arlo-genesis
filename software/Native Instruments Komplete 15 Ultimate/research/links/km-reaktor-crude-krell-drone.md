synthanatomy.com · Synth Anatomy · Feb 2024 · "CRUDE, a fabulous free drone synth with Krell randomization for Reaktor 6"

**CRUDE** by **Bolle** — free experimental **drone** synth for Reaktor 6 (full version, **not** Player), free in the User Library, NKS-compatible. Continuously refined with community input.

**Krell randomization:** two **Krell buttons (1 and 2)** turn CRUDE into "a random sound generator" — algorithmic, self-running drone creation without manual tweaking, after the **Krell** patch idea (the self-generating Buchla patch from the TRON soundtrack; the canonical "no two moments alike" generative-drone technique).

**Signal path:**
- Two **wavetable oscillators** — 84 selectable waves; octave/semitone/fine tune.
- **Bit crusher** + **distortion** — switchable pre- or post-filter.
- Two **lowpass filters** per oscillator — selectable 12 or 24 dB/oct.
- Three **LFOs** — control filters and output amplitude; waveform, pulse, sync options.
- **Multi-FX:** chorus, stereo **Lunar Delay** (CV-controllable width), reverb.
- **Randomize button** — instant new sound, randomizes everything *except* the volume control.

**Character:** purpose-built for drone synthesis, "lots of sound possibilities," modulation-rich, can go crushing/heavy — perfect for the doom/degraded end of the rig.

Rig note: CRUDE + the two Krell buttons = a hands-off generative doom-drone. Bitcrush + distortion onboard means it already self-degrades before it even hits SketchCassette or the tape pedals.
