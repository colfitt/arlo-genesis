# Replika XT — the 5 styles & their per-style controls (official manual)

Each style swaps in its OWN unique controls. This is the real depth the nav one-liner skipped.

## Modern — clean/defined, 1–2000 ms (or 1/64–2/1 synced)
- **Saturation** — tube-like, at the delay INPUT; bypass → overdrive.
- **Depth / Rate / Stereo|Mono** — built-in modulation (Stereo offsets L/R for width).
- **Hi Cut / Low Cut** — in the feedback path.
- Use: the precise/transparent delay; cleanest self-oscillation.

## Analogue — dark/hazy BBD, 20–2000 ms
- **BBD Type** — **Clean / Warm / Dark / Grunge** (subtle filtering → heavily degraded).
- ★ *"As delay time increases, repeats become darker with more noise and distortion."* → the
  **dub-decay** style: long time + Dark/Grunge = each echo dissolves darker into the drone.
- Depth/Rate/Stereo|Mono modulation + Hi/Low Cut.

## Tape — textured/lively tape echo, 20–2000 ms
- **Saturation** — clean → overdrive.
- **Tape Age** — limited HF response + hiss of aging tape.
- **Noise On/Off** — tape hiss (level tracks Age).
- **Wow & Flutter** — pitch variation over time (mechanical wobble). ★ the lo-fi/seasick pick.
- Hi/Low Cut. Use: short slap with flutter into a wall; the rig's lo-fi tape-echo move.

## Vintage Digital — warm/crunchy early-digital, 20–2000 ms
- **Quality** — **High** (bright/subtle) / **Medium** / **Low** (darker, grittier) / **Crunch**
  (bright + digital artifacts). Longer time = more artifacts.
- Depth/Rate/Stereo|Mono + Hi/Low Cut. Use: gritty 8-bit-ish repeats; degrade flavour.

## Diffusion — clean delay + unique reverb, 1–2000 ms  ★ the ambient mode
- **Amount** — how much diffusion is applied → turns repeats into a reverb wash.
- **Size** — swell, reflection pattern AND decay (space impression).
- **Dense / Sparse** — Dense = washed-out smear; **Sparse = granular quality** (doom/shoegaze).
- **Movement** — depth + speed of modulation of the reverb reflections.
- Hi/Low Cut. Use: the "delay-into-reverb-wash" trick; smears banjo/baritone into a cloud.

## Universal note
All five share Hi/Low Cut in the feedback path → roll Hi Cut down for darkening dub repeats in
ANY style. Diffusion's Sparse+big-Size == Replika's analogue of RAUM's granular Cosmic.
