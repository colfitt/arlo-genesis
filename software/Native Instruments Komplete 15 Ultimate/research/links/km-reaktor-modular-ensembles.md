blog.native-instruments.com · NI Blog · "7 free modular ensembles for REAKTOR"

Free **Blocks-based / modular** ensembles (User Library; need full Reaktor 6). Generative ones flagged.

1. **Lizard's Logic** — Jan Ola Korte. Drone machine: four VCOs with sync + FM through Logic Mix modules → wide stereo. **Generative** ("beatings and lively disturbances"). → entry 8715.
2. **DevineWaveTable Synth** — Richard Devine. Wavetable texture generator with sequenced delay/reverb/filter; "otherworldly textures." Needs sequencing (not self-running). → entry 9823.
3. **Cloudlab 200t V2** — Trevor Gavilan. **Buchla 200/200e-inspired** modular system (homage, not a clone). Flexible/generative. (One of the most-downloaded ensembles of 2018.) → entry 11077.
4. **MoMo Mk2** — hands-on modular with four external inputs (sequencers, audio, modulators). ADSR, filter, VCA, LFO; accepts external CV/audio. → entry 11273.
5. **FM Modular** — Doron Sadja. Two FM synths feeding back into each other (Nord Modular-based). **Self-sustaining feedback** = generative. → entry 8049.
6. **RUHR** — herw. Polyphonic modular flagship (poly = the digital advantage over analog Eurorack). Voltage-controlled, "endless sonic possibilities." → entry 7329.
7. **VoltageCntrl-We Rise** — Shiro Fujioka. **Ambient Blocks patch** with pitch/gate outputs to drive external Eurorack; "instant sonic dreamland," **10 snapshots**, generative. → entry 10297. (Same author as the Polyrhythmic Blocks video transcript captured here.)

Bonus: NI **modular building template for Blocks** → entry 8705 (starting scaffold for your own patches).
