# Mod Pack — Phasis + Choral + Flair (the modulation trio)

Three reimagined classic modulation effects (the predecessor to Crush Pack). Included in Komplete; clean 8–9-control UIs.

## PHASIS (phaser)
- Classic phasing + new tricks. **ULTRA** mode raises max modulation speed from **8.37 kHz to 477.3 kHz** → extreme **FM-style** effects never heard from a phaser. From subtle movement to metallic sci-fi resonance.

## CHORAL (chorus)
- Modeled on 40 years of prized choruses. Modes: **Synth** (vintage polysynth chorus), **Ensemble** (string-synth chorus), **Dimension** (early-'80s rack), **Universal** (modern clean), plus a new feedback mode **Scatter**. Width, sheen, shimmer.

## FLAIR (flanger)
- Four comb filters = **Voices**. New **Voices mode** tunes the combs to **harmonic intervals** → flanger acts as a **tuned/chord-generating resonator** (pitched flanging, harmonic resonances). The most distinctive of the three.

## Rig relevance (shoegaze/drone)
- **Choral (Dimension/Ensemble)** = the classic shoegaze widener on a guitar/banjo/pad — that lush '80s shimmer.
- **Flair Voices** tuned to a chord interval on a drone → harmonic, jet-plane resonant wall.
- **Phasis ULTRA** for slow seasick movement under a sustained pad, or FM-grit at the extreme.
- Pairs naturally before RAUM/Replika XT in a chain (modulate → delay → infinite reverb).
