# Komplete 15 tier differences (what Ultimate actually adds)

- **Standard**: 95+ instruments/effects, 50+ expansions, ~50,000 sounds. Includes Kontakt 8, Massive X, Guitar Rig 7 Pro, Ozone 11 Standard.
- **Ultimate**: 150+ instruments/effects, 80+ expansions, ~100,000 sounds. Adds the high-end versions of NI ensemble/orchestral instruments, Symphony **Essentials** series, Action scoring series, Brainworx mixing tools, Schema Dark/Light, Valves, Vocal Colors, more Session instruments.
- **Collector's Edition**: 165+ instruments/effects, 120–125+ expansions, ~150,000 sounds (1.1 TB compressed DL). Adds full **Symphony Series** (not Essentials), **Valves Pro**, **Fables**, **Kithara**, and the most film/media-composer-oriented high-end orchestral libs.

All three tiers share the flagship engines: **Kontakt 8, Massive X, Reaktor 6, Guitar Rig 7 Pro, iZotope Ozone 11 Standard.**

The jump Standard→Ultimate is mostly **more (and higher-end) sampled acoustic/orchestral content + mixing/mastering tools**, not new synth engines. For a drone/doom/shoegaze rig the Ultimate-only wins worth knowing: **Ashlight** (dark granular — confirmed Ultimate), **Schema Dark/Light**, **Thrill**, **Arkhis**-class cinematic textures, **Action Woodwinds/Strings** for swells (all run in Kontakt — cross-ref Kontakt 8 guide §8).
