The Reaktor-built **effect** ensembles that turn the banjo/pedalboard into texture. All run in **Reaktor 6 or the free Reaktor 6 Player** (licensed separately; bundled in higher Komplete tiers).

**MOLEKULAR** — modular **multi-effects** system for Reaktor. Four effect modules, each with its own library of processors, roughly classed as: **spectral (filters), temporal (delays), modulation, distortion.** "Limitless routing" (matrix you can patch freely) + four assignable **modulators** and a performance **morph/XY** section for live macro moves. This is the deepest Reaktor FX box — drop the banjo in, route through spectral + delay + a modulated distortion, and automate the morph for evolving walls. Runs in Reaktor or free Reaktor Player.

**FLESH** (Tim Exile) — a **resynthesis instrument** powered by Reaktor 6. Load up to **12 samples**; Flesh analyzes the transients and spectral profile, turns them into curves + triggers, and sends them to **four audio engines** that re-synthesize new material. "Transforms your input signal into chords." Granular/resonating character — feed it a banjo phrase and it spits back evolving harmonic/granular beds keyed to the banjo's rhythm. Runs in Reaktor 6 or free Reaktor 6 Player.

**THE FINGER** (Tim Exile / Native Instruments) — keyboard-triggered **glitch/stutter/gating effect** ensemble: each key fires a different real-time mangle (stutter, scratch, tape-stop, gate, filter sweep) and you can chain them. Classic for chopping a sustained source into rhythmic chaos. (Reaktor-built; runs in Reaktor / Player when licensed.)

**Third-party Reaktor FX worth knowing (User Library / commercial):**
- **Blinksonic LIQUIFI** (spectral effects) + **IGNITA** (granular synth) — experimental granular/spectral toolbox for Reaktor 6 (commercial, Blinksonic).
- **CATALIZ°** (Blinksonic) — playable granular synth + beat-looper for Reaktor 6.

Rig note: this is the "process your own sound" lane. Reamp the pedalboard or record the banjo dry → into Molekular (spectral + delay + modulated distortion) or Flesh (resynth into chords) → bounce → Valhalla/EchoBoy. Molekular's free-Player support means it works even without full Reaktor.
