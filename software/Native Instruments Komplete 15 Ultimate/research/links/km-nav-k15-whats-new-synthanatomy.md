# Komplete 15 — what's new + tier breakdown

Release date: **September 23, 2024**.

## Tiers / pricing (full price · update price)
| Tier | Instr/FX | Expansions | Sounds | Full | Update |
|------|----------|-----------|--------|------|--------|
| Standard | 95+ | 50+ | 50,000 | €599 | €199 |
| Ultimate | 150+ | 80+ | 100,000 | €1,199 | €399 |
| Collector's Edition | 150–165+ | 125+ | 150,000 | €1,799 | €499 |

## New across ALL tiers (core tools)
- **Kontakt 8** — sampler with new Tools layer (Chords/Phrases MIDI FX), **Leap** loop framework, **Conflux** wavetable+sample instrument (covered in the dedicated Kontakt 8 guide).
- **Guitar Rig 7 Pro** — loop machine, four new virtual amps (own deep agent).
- **Conflux** — experimental hybrid wavetable instrument.

## New effects / plugins
- iZotope **Trash** (distortion with X/Y morphing)
- iZotope **Ozone 11 Standard** (mastering)
- iZotope **VocalSynth 2**

## New instruments (across tiers)
Scene, Saffron, **Bazzazian Tapes**, Bouquet, Feel It, **Glaze 2**, Nacht, Sway, Utopia, Karriem Riggins Drums, Rudiments, **Session Percussionist**, Electric Keys Diamond/Phoenix.

## Ultimate-tier additions
Action Woodwinds, **Valves**, **Schema: Dark / Light**, **Vocal Colors**, Alicia's Electric Keys, Session Bassist variants, Session Guitarist, Session Ukulele, Symphony Essentials orchestral, Brainworx mixing tools.

## Collector's-Edition-only additions
Valves Pro, **Fables**, **Kithara** (flagship — high-quality guitars/plucked strings), expanded Symphony Series.

Early-bird bonus: free iZotope Neutron 4 with pre-order.

> Note: synthanatomy lists CE instr count "150+"; MusicRadar/MusicTech peg CE at "165+ / 150,000 sounds." Triangulated: Standard 95+, Ultimate 150+, CE 165+.
