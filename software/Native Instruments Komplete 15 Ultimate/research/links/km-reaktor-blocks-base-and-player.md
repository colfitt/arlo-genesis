**What Blocks is:** a framework that turns Reaktor 6 into a **rack-style modular synth**. **30+ Blocks** ship with Reaktor 6 — oscillators, filters, effects, modulators, sequencers, util. Patches range "from generative sound machines and classic subtractive synths to experimental devices only possible on a modular system." You patch on the front panel (since the 6.3 update added **front-panel patching**) or on the structure level.

**Blocks Base (the free tier):** a **24-module** starter set introduced in the free **Komplete Start** bundle — runs in the **free Reaktor 6 Player**. Tutorials build a working synth from: **Oscillator, Filter, LFO, VCA, ADSR, Step Sequencer**. Blocks Base also includes a flexible **8-step sequencer, a clock divider, and a pitch quantizer** — modulate the offset controls in the sequencer + quantizer and you get "hypnotic morphing sequences that walk the line between order and chaos."

**Blocks Primes (paid, free with full Reaktor 6):** the premium Block set. NOT in the free Player.

**Player vs full Reaktor 6 (the important distinction for this rig):**
- **Reaktor Player** = free engine/wrapper. Runs NI Komplete instruments built on Reaktor and the **Blocks ecosystem** (all NI + partner Blocks releases can be patched and played in Player). Runs commercial ensembles like **Molekular, Flesh, Razor, Monark, Rounds, Prism** when licensed.
- **Player CANNOT:** build/edit ensembles from scratch, open/run **User Library** content (the huge free community drone/granular resource — owners of full Reaktor 6 only), or use **Blocks Primes**.
- **Full Reaktor 6** (in Komplete 15 Ultimate): **70+ factory instruments/effects**, the full edit/build environment, **User Library access**, Blocks Primes, and the two-layer build system — **Primary** (high-level modules: oscillators, filters, envelopes — fast patching) vs **Core** (low-level DSP under the hood, for building modules sample-by-sample). You almost never need Core for ambient work; Primary + Blocks covers it.

**Why it matters here:** the user has **full Reaktor 6** via Komplete 15 Ultimate → the entire User Library (every drone/granular ensemble in these notes) and Blocks Primes are all available, plus the ability to wire custom generative Blocks patches.
