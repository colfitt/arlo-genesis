The full Reaktor 6 Factory Library = **70+ ready-to-use instruments/effects** — synthesizers, legacy effects, groove boxes, **sample transformers**, sequenced synths, and sequencers. The ones relevant to drone/ambient/granular:

**Drone / ambient / generative self-runners:**
- **SpaceDrone** — the flagship self-playing drone (96 voices; Geiger-counter random triggering). THE factory drone. (See deep-dive capture.)
- **Metaphysical Function** — "complex, evolving walls of sound"; dark, between composition and sound design. Generative; iorie's "cheat code" (the A&B variant). Pair with Fast FX = ambient-loop machine.
- **Newscool** — idiosyncratic generative cellular-automaton groovebox/synth (~20 yrs old, still musical; guest patches by Âme).

**Granular / sample transformers:**
- **Travelizer** — Sample Transformer built on the **Grain Cloud** module; load your own samples (double-click sample window → Sample Map). The factory granular workhorse.
- **Grain States / The Mouth / other sample-transformer ensembles** — additional granular/transform tools in the Sample Transformers folder.

**Other factory synths usable for pads/texture:**
- **Photone** — lush evolving pad/atmosphere synth.
- **Carbon 2** — additive/spectral synth.
- **Steampipe** (physical-modelling wind/pipe), **Titan**, **SubHarmonic**, **Limelite**, **Gaugear**.

**Bundled Reaktor-built commercial instruments** (often shipped with Komplete, run in Reaktor / Player): **Razor** (additive synth — great for shimmering pads/drones), **Monark** (Minimoog), **Rounds**, **Prism** (modal/physical-modelling — bell/metallic drones), **Molekular** (FX), **Flesh** (resynth), **The Finger** (glitch FX).

**Blocks** ships separately as the modular framework: **Blocks Base** (24 free modules, runs in free Player) + **Blocks Primes** (premium, free with full Reaktor 6) + **30+ Blocks total** with Reaktor 6.

Practical reach-for order for this rig: **SpaceDrone** (instant self-drone) → **Travelizer** (granular your banjo) → **Metaphysical Function** (generative wall, iorie method) → **CRUDE/System Flow** (User Library) → build your own in **Blocks**.
