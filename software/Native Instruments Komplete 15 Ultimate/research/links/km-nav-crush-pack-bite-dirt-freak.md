# Crush Pack — Bite + Dirt + Freak (the degrade trio)

Successor to MOD PACK. Three plug-ins covering bit-crushing, stompbox distortion, ring mod, AM, and frequency shifting. **Bite ships inside Komplete (confirmed installed in CONTENTS.md as a .component/.vst/.vst3); Dirt + Freak are the rest of the pack.**

## BITE (bit + sample-rate reduction — INSTALLED)
- **Anti-aliased** sample-rate AND bit-depth reduction in one plug-in.
- **Freq** = resample frequency **0.1 kHz → 44.1 kHz** (lo-fi as you drop it).
- **Bits** = bit-depth reduction.
- **Crunch** = level into the bit-reduction stage — a **smooth, step-free** way to dial resolution (no zipper artifacts).
- **Jitter** = clock jitter → fluctuates the sample rate → noisier, more unstable (great for "broken tape" instability).
- **Dither** = added noise to mask quantization distortion — but can be pushed **creatively** for grit.
- **Pre / Post filters** = sculpt before and after the crush (tame the aliasing fizz post-crush).
- Auto in/out gain compensation. "Authentic organic crunch of 80s digital sample-rate reduction." $69 standalone; bundled in Komplete.

## DIRT (distortion stompbox)
- **Two circuit-modeled diode clipping stages (A, B)**, series or parallel via **Routing**.
- **Drive** + **Amount**: past center, **Amount** folds the signal back into itself → **wavefolding** distortion with strong harmonic overtones.
- Blends dry → distortion → wavefold for dynamic transitions. The "warm-to-savage" saturation in the pack.

## FREAK (ring mod / AM / frequency shifter)
- Models an **analog diode ring circuit**. Three modes — **Oscillator, Radio, Sidechain** — that re-task the three left knobs.
- **Type** knob morphs **Amplitude → Ring → Frequency-shifting** continuously.
- Output: "inharmonic noises, sci-fi burbles, detuned sweeps, crazy frequency wanderings," AM-radio warble, tempo-locked AM, subtle stereo widening.
- Reviewers: **best for versatile modulation, less for classic distortion.**

## Rig relevance (degrade/ambient)
- **Bite** = the workhorse lo-fi/degrade insert (own automation sweep recipes in km-nav-bite-bitcrushing-blog.md).
- **Freak** in Frequency-shift mode on a drone → slowly detuning, inharmonic metallic wall (very doom). Sidechain mode = rhythmic AM gating.
- **Dirt** wavefold on a sine sub or pad → adds harmonic teeth without a full amp sim.
