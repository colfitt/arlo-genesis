# DIRT — official NI walkthrough (distilled)

Dirt = a two-stage diode-clipping distortion stompbox with wavefolding. The "warm-to-savage" saturation unit of Crush Pack. **NOT confirmed installed on disk (Crush Pack referenced, only Bite verified).**

## Architecture
- **Two analog-modeled channels (A, B)** with flexible routing.
- **Routing:** A→B serial, B→A serial, or A+B parallel. "Single mode" = use just one stage by setting the other's Amount to 0.
- **Mix:** dry/wet of the effect. In **parallel**, the same knob becomes a **Blend** between A and B outputs.

## Per-channel controls
- **Drive:** input gain into the stage → "increases the intensity of the distortion."
- **Amount:** the key control. "Smooth transition from clean signals to saturated ones and beyond saturation into wavefold distortion for more extreme sounds." → **first half of range = saturation, second half = wavefolding.** Wavefolding "folds the waveform back into itself" instead of clipping → cleaner distortion with less clipping, complex harmonics even from a sine.
- **Mode (three colors per channel):**
  - **I** = most subtle; "presence and warmth to any kind of signal."
  - **II** = default; "well-balanced distortion, the brightest of the three."
  - **III** = most extreme; "dark, crushed sound."

## Tone-shaping
- **Tilt:** "shift the distortion to the lower end for dark tones, or the higher end for brighter sounds." (Tone/spectral tilt at input.)
- **Bias:** "introduces asymmetry to the circuit" → generates **even harmonics**; subtle; fixes a thin/hollow distortion → warmer.
- **FX Trim:** wet-signal output level (dry not affected). Range about −18 dB to +6 dB.
- **Safety switch (next to Drive):** turn OFF for higher gain — "allows you to drive a stronger signal from one channel to the other → more extreme distortions." (Disables gain compensation.)

## Rig relevance
- **Amount past noon = wavefolding** → harmonic teeth on a sine sub / pad / banjo without a full amp sim.
- **Mode III + low Mix** parallel-blended = dark crushed grit under a clean drone, kept controllable.
- **B→A vs A→B** routing genuinely changes the tone (drive into the second stage harder).
- **Bias** is the fix when wavefold distortion sounds thin/hollow — adds body via even harmonics.
