# CHORAL — official NI walkthrough (distilled)

Choral = chorus "that adds reverb-like capabilities to the standard chorus feature set." The shoegaze/ambient widener of Mod Pack. **NOT confirmed installed on disk.**

## The four modes (heard side-by-side at Amount 100%)
Start from Monark's default simple pulse: "simply adding Choral takes this mono sound and makes it stereo, adding movement."
- **Synth** (default): "a dark, vintage sound, ideal for rich synth tones, e.g. bass sounds."
- **Ensemble:** "more string-machine feel — Amount at full gives a queasy detuned effect, but lower values produce warm, lush results." (The classic shoegaze/string-synth lush.)
- **Dimension:** "bright and transparent, ideal for making something sound bigger without overly obvious modulation." (The invisible-widener — early-'80s rack vibe.)
- **Universal:** "contemporary, clean and modern" — but turn Voices up for "a little chaos."

## Key controls
- **Amount:** strength of the effect (drives the 100% A/B mode comparison).
- **Voices:** 1→3 (per video, max creates ear-catching effects with feedback). [Manual lists 1→3.]
- **Feedback:** with Voices at max, high feedback = "really ear-catching effects" / a fluttery effect at default Delay time.
- **Rate:** speed of the fluttery feedback effect.
- **Delay:** spatial depth; longer = bigger Scatter sound.
- **Scatter (the unique feature):** "changes the feedback routing of the voices → a reverb-like effect," avoiding the metallic quality of normal high-feedback chorus. Bigger with more Delay; results differ per mode. **Turning Amount DOWN removes the fluttery modulation but RETAINS the reverb-like effect** → a clean ambient bloom.

## Rig relevance (shoegaze/ambient)
- **Ensemble at LOW-to-mid Amount** = the lush, warm shoegaze string-synth widener on banjo/guitar/pad (full Amount = intentional seasick detune).
- **Dimension** = make a drone "bigger" with no obvious modulation — set it and forget it.
- **Scatter ON + Amount DOWN + long Delay** = a chorus that behaves like a soft reverb — a pre-RAUM ambient widener/bloom that won't go metallic.
- **Synth mode** for thickening sub/bass drones without brightening.
