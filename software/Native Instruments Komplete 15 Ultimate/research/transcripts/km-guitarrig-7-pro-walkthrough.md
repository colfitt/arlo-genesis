[Presenter: Sam Luce, NI. The release walkthrough — concise overview of the v7 additions and the "creative effects playground" framing.]

## Framing
"Cover the basics... and check out how you can use it as a creative effects playground for your productions." GR7 builds on GR6 with 4 new amps + 5 new effects based on ICM machine-learning modeling. "So much more than just realistic guitar tones — an effect playground that sparks your creativity." **The theme of this release is Lo-Fi**: four new lo-fi effects + 150 new rack presets, "From High Gain patches to Lo-Fi chains for guitar and beyond."

## Getting started
- Left column = color palette of processing options; double-click to load. Amps load with **matched cabinets**. Reorder units freely. Either pick a curated preset, or jump to the **components** section to choose any module directly.
- Use it **standalone** (ideas board / jamming; plug guitar into interface, set audio input) or as a **DAW insert effect** (gain DAW automation to change parameters mid-track).

## New features
- **Signal Flow** (right side): see the whole rig at once; freely move modules to experiment with chains; click a module to jump to it. "Especially useful for A/B splits — gives a nice representation of which modules are in which chains."
- **Cabinet IR loader**: blend up to four IRs, independent level + pan; pick from the bundled IRs or load your own to capture the sound of your speakers.
- New **Loop Machine Pro**; context help on hover for every parameter.

## Matched Cabinet Pro
Blend mic'd cabinet sound + room tone — "great for giving real depth to lead lines and adding to the sense of realism."

## Lo-fi effects on non-guitar (demoed on a keyboard track)
"It's not just guitars these are useful for." On a keyboard track: **Tape Wobble, Vintage Vibrato, and Color** together "to give it a real Lo-Fi vibe."
- **Ozone Maximizer** module — borrowed from iZotope's mastering suite; tames peaks without pumping, keeps dynamics/clarity; uses the IRC-LL algorithm for low latency in the rack.
- **Color** — 10 distortion algorithms (many from iZotope **Trash 2**); a **Bass Saver** control adjusts how strongly distortion hits bass frequencies.
- **Tape Wobble** — wow/flutter + saturation of an analog tape machine; "age a track... vintage vibe with grit, saturation, character."
- **Vintage Vibrato** — vibrato/chorus of a classic vintage organ; six modes of increasing intensity for a lo-fi edge.

## New stompboxes
Seattle Fuzz (grunge fuzz + diode clipping), Chainsaw (1980s Swedish-death-metal buzz distortion), Screamer Deluxe (mid-forward SRV blues), IVP Stomp (rack-preamp tone with bass/treble *before* the drive circuit).
