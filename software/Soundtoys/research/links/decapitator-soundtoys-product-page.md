# Decapitator — official framing + artist endorsements

- Billed as an **"Analog Saturation Modeler"** — "subtle to extreme hardware-
  modeled saturation," "the secret weapon of top mix engineers." Soundtoys
  "collected and analyzed both vintage and modern hardware — consoles, preamps,
  input channels, EQs, compressors and studio distortion units" for the 5 models.
- **Punish** = "kicks in an extra chunk of gain to push Decapitator over the edge."
- **Mix knob** = "parallel processing without the need for routing and sub-mixes."
- **Tone** = analog-modeled, to shape the saturated sound.

## Artist endorsements (quoted on Soundtoys' own page — sourced, not invented)
- **Tchad Blake** (Radiohead, Black Keys, Tom Waits — the dark/saturated/"recorded-
  wrong" mixer, also an EchoBoy user): **"I like it on everything."**
- **Fabrice "Fab" Dupont:** Decapitator is **"instavibe-in-a-box."**
- **Ryan Hewitt** (Red Hot Chili Peppers, Avett Brothers): **"Watch out! This thing

Note: Tchad Blake is the single most aesthetically-aligned name for this rig — same
name surfaced as an EchoBoy user. Genre crossover (dark, degraded, saturated) is by
