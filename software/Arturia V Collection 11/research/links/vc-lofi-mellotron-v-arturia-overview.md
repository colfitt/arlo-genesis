Arturia's official feature page for Mellotron V, modeling the tape-replay
Mellotron (MKI/MKII/M300/M400) at component level via reverse-engineering of the
actual tape transport + heads.

## The tape banks
- **65 original tape racks** sourced from MKI / MKII / M300 / M400 Mellotrons,
  recorded directly from the actual tapes ("utterly authentic").
- Instrument categories on the tapes: **flutes, strings, brass, organs, choirs,
  mandolins** (the classic Mellotron palette). The famous "3 violins" string
  tape and choir tapes are the shoegaze/prog staples.
- **61 presets** modeled on famous tracks.

## Track selector (A / B / C / All)
- Original M400 tape frames held **three sounds (A/B/C)**; you pick one with the
  Track Selector.
- Arturia adds **intermediate positions** = variable mix/fade between A↔B and
  B↔C, plus an **"All" position** (all three tapes at once — impossible on
  hardware) for thick combo/stacked sounds.

## Tape modeling controls (the lo-fi character)
- **Flutter** — tape-spin wow/flutter; adds vibration/instability to pitch.
- **Tape Saturation** — warm/thick saturation, slight distortion.
- **Mechanics** — loudness of the Mellotron's signature mechanical noise (the
  machine being "played").
- Plus noise/hiss floor, Pitch knob (tape-speed simulation), Tone, Volume.
- Velocity-sensitive tapes; **aftertouch can be routed to flutter** for
  expressive, wobbling pitch instability — and to volume.
- A simple **ADSR envelope** added (not on hardware).

## FX / Advanced additions
- **Four effect slots** choosing from ~10 effects (chorus, flanger, compressor,
  phaser, delay, distortion, reverb, etc.).
- **Amp simulator**: Fender-Twin-style amp **and** a Leslie rotary cabinet.
- **State-of-the-art convolution reverb / ambience panel** (no need to leave the
  plug-in).
- LFO, noise generator, VCA, mixer, external audio input.

## Import-your-own-samples (key for the lo-fi rig)
- Mellotron V lets you **import your own samples and play them back as if
  recorded on vintage tape** through the original instrument.
- Use **up to 8 samples** to build a custom tape track; blend tracks live with
  the mod wheel (DAW-automatable) for evolving sounds.
- Per-sample controls: pan, gain, transpose, **loop points**, sample ADSR,
  **time-stretching**.
