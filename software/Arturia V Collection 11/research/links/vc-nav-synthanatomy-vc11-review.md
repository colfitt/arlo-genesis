synthanatomy.com · Synth Anatomy review · Sept 2025

"45 plugins — the best upgrade in a long time." Bundle expanded from 39 to 45.

NEW: Jup-8000 V (Roland JP-8000), Pure LoFi (lo-fi synth), Synthx V (Elka
Synthex), MiniBrute V, + Augmented Yangtze & Augmented Mallets.

MAJOR UPDATES:
- SEM V3 — complete engine rebuild; up to 8-voice polyphony (the original SEM V
  was monophonic). The poly expansion is the big deal: "lush chords" and
  "evolving textures" now possible on the Oberheim SEM.
- Augmented Series 2 — enhanced modular design, dynamic visual displays,
  improved morphing curves, updated arpeggiator with randomization.

ARCHITECTURE / SOUND DESIGN:
- Expanded modulation via function generators + flexible randomization.
- A multi-arpeggiator and sequencer appears selectively across instruments
  (some still lack a sequencer).

AMBIENT / TEXTURE CALLOUTS (directly relevant to drone/shoegaze):
- Pure LoFi "excels" for atmospheric sound with six vintage character modes
  incl. "tape flutter and cassette wear."
- SEM V3's poly expansion → lush chords + evolving textures.
- Jup-8000 V's FEEDBACK OSCILLATOR creates "unique, harsh-sounding sounds"
  beyond typical supersaw use → useful for noise/drone.

PERFORMANCE: reviewer flags "relatively high CPU usage" as a lingering concern
months post-release.
