THE NEW FLAGSHIP SYNTH OF VC11 — and why it matters for shoegaze walls.

Jup-8000 V = Roland JP-8000 recreation, reverse-engineered circuitry, 7 OSC
modes on Osc 1: SUPERSAW, Triangle Mod, FEEDBACK, NOISE, Square, Saw, Triangle.
- SUPERSAW = stacked detuned saws → "definitively raw and wide" → the classic
  shoegaze/big-pad WALL in one oscillator. Crank detune + unison for the smear.
- FEEDBACK + NOISE modes = the harsh/drone side (per SoundOnSound, can be pushed
  into "unique, harsh-sounding" territory beyond trance use).
- Captures the JP's "lush pianos, phased pads, percussive attacks."

MODULATION (modern Advanced-panel style): envelopes, random LFOs, step
modulators, and an "infinitely shapeable" FUNCTION GENERATOR — route anywhere
for evolving pads / unpredictable shifts.

FX: supercharged rack with 18 FX engines (incl. new Trance Gate) — the 4-slot
shared FX concept, expanded here.

REALITY CHECK (Gearspace/KVR): authentic emulation (a 10-yr JP-8000 owner called
it "dead-on"), but for pure modern supersaw firepower some prefer Icarus/Spire/
Avenger. For THIS rig the value is the authentic-but-degradable wide pad you then
push through Decapitator/SketchCassette/Valhalla — not EDM-grade supersaw.

CAVEAT (SoundOnSound): a constant, non-random phase offset between the dual osc
can cause cancellation across voices on certain patches.
