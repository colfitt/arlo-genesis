arturia.com · Prophet-5 V official overview · current

The warm-analog pad/drone workhorse — and the "high strings" layer in the Blade Runner
recreation (per Reverb Machine).

**Oscillators:** two modeled SSM VCOs, 3 waveforms + noise — "beautifully rich, buttery
sound," with hard sync.

**Filter:** Dave Rossum SSM 2040 low-pass — warmth + bounce; a no-nonsense resonant LP
that "tames the oscillators for delicate ambient drones." This is the soft, rounded
character that makes it ideal for pads.

**Poly-Mod:** routes the Filter Envelope and OSC 2 frequency as mod sources → "curious
movements and wild detuned warbles" that respond in real time. The pad-character secret
weapon for evolving warble.

**Pad-enhancing extras:** Voice Dispersion (analog drift/detune), Unison detune (lush
layering), LFO for movement, 16 built-in effects (incl. reverb/spatial), built-in chorus.

**Pad factory presets to start from:**
- **"Folded Synthwaves"** — echoed, undulating wave pad with brassy overtones
- **"Deja Vu"** — rich pad with built-in chorus
- **"Mambo Pad"** — smooth pad

**The Vangelis high-string trick (from Reverb Machine):** apply LFO + mod-wheel vibrato to
**only ONE of the two oscillators** → the shimmering, slightly-detuned high string layer
over the CS-80 brass.

**Pad polysynth reach-for map (where Prophet-5 sits):**
- **CS-80 V4** — expressive brass/string PAD, ribbon + poly-AT, the Vangelis wall. THE flagship.
- **Prophet-VS V** — evolving/morphing pads via vector synthesis (joystick + mixer-env path).
- **Matrix-12 V2** — deepest, most complex evolving textures (40 routings, 5 LFOs/envs).
- **Prophet-5 V** — warm analog pad + ambient drones; buttery, simple, fast.
- **OP-Xa V** (= OB-Xa) — lush warm Oberheim pads, stereo spread, cross-mod, chimes.
- **SEM V3** — state-variable filter, smooth evolving pad → cutting; single-oscillator-ish, raw.
- **Jup-8 V4** — bright lush Jupiter pads, unison, the built-in chorus shimmer.
- **Solina V2** — string-machine ensemble bed (not a polysynth proper, but the strings layer).
