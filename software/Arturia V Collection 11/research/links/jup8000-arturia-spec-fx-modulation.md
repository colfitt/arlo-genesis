## Oscillators
- **OSC 1: 7 waveforms** — Supersaw, Triangle Mod, Feedback, Noise, Square, Saw, Triangle —
  each with its own tweakable controls. Supersaw = stacked detuned saws → "definitively raw
  and wide."
- **OSC 2:** Pulse, Saw, Triangle, with **sync**, wide tuning, and **PWM**.
- **Ring Mod + Cross-Mod (X-Mod)** for harmonic movement / "noisy or FM-like stuff."
- **16-voice polyphony.**

## FX rack — 4 slots, 18 effect types
Named: **Trance Gate** (rhythmic chop, up to **32 steps, 5 envelope shapes**), **Super
Unison**, **Multi-Band Compressor**, **JUN-6 Chorus**, **Delay**, **Phaser**, + more (the
shared VC11 rack incl. reverb, lo-fi/bitcrush, tape — same one Analog Lab exposes). Slots
reorderable; the on-panel slot mirrors the rack.

## Modulation (Advanced panel)
Drag-and-drop routing: **2 ADSR envelopes** (filter & amp), **2 LFOs** (LFO1 syncable, poly/
mono modes), an **"infinitely shapeable" Function Generator**, **step modulators/sequencers**,
plus velocity / aftertouch / mod-wheel / **MPE**. This is the native replacement for the
hardware's Motion Control — park a slow Function/Random here for self-evolving pads.

## Sequencing
- **Multi-Arp: up to 4 independent arpeggiator layers.**
- **64-step polyphonic sequencer** (piano roll, velocity lanes, real-time param edits).

## Arturia sound-design framing (their designers)
"The supersaw oscillator and the reproduction of its limitations made Trance and EDM what it
is today"; the **feedback oscillator** called "an absolute gem" for experimental sounds; X-Mod
"for noisy or FM-like stuff"; MultiArp "complex patterns in no time." Named factory presets
incl. "Bellish Super Saw" (Drew Schlesinger), "Violin On Acid" (DJ Hidden), "Formant
Multisequence" (Foco). 240+ mix-ready presets total.
