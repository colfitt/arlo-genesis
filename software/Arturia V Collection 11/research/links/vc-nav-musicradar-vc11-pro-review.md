musicradar.com · MusicRadar review ("Still the collection to beat") · 2025

Four headline additions:
1. Jup-8000 V — 1990s Roland JP-8000, the supersaw; covers "lush and atmospheric
   pads and strings" plus "bendy and squealing bass."
2. Pure Lofi — non-emulation synth that adds lo-fi character via hardware
   emulations, distortion, noise sources (tape, percussion, nature) + FX chains.
3. Synthx V — Elka Synthex polysynth, dual DCOs + multimode filter; "slightly
   off-the-wall sound compared to standard analogue."
4. SEM V3 — complete rewrite of the 1974 Oberheim SEM; improved engine, reworked
   filter, 8-voice polyphony.
Plus MiniBrute V + Augmented Yangtze & Mallets.

COLLECTION SCALE / TAXONOMY (useful for the navigator):
~45 instruments = 17 analogue synths, 9 digital synths, 5 keyboards, 5 pianos,
7 Augmented instruments, plus Analog Lab Pro (the browser).

PRICING: Pro €699/$699 (~£539); Intro €199/$199 (ten instruments).
