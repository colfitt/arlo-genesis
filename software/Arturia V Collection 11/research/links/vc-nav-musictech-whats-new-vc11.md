WHAT'S NEW IN V COLLECTION 11 (released April 24, 2025; 45 instruments total).

The seven "additions" vs V Collection X — but only ~4 are genuinely new:
TRULY NEW / EXCLUSIVE to VC11:
- Jup-8000 V — Roland JP-8000 recreation, virtual-analogue, with the famous
  "supersaw"; described as having seven synthesis engines.
- Pure LoFi — original Arturia lo-fi instrument with multiple hardware "modes"
  (tape flutter, cassette wear etc.); combines sample-based + analogue-modelling
  + wavetable. (Note: in THIS rig Pure LoFi is filed separately under
  Software/Arturia Pure LoFi/ — it was a scanner-misfiling catch, already
  researched.)
- Augmented Mallets — new Augmented Series instrument (marimba/vibraphone/
  mallet hybrids).
- Augmented Yangtze — new Augmented Series instrument (Chinese instruments).

PREVIOUSLY RELEASED AS STANDALONE, NOW BUNDLED IN VC11:
- MiniBrute V — released May 2024 as a €199 standalone first.
- Synthx V — Elka Synthex emulation, released standalone before VC11.

MAJOR OVERHAULS:
- SEM V → completely "remodelled from the ground up" (sleeker UI, new engine);
  this jump is why the file shows "SEM V3" in some reviews.
- Augmented Series 2 — the ENTIRE Augmented lineup got a new interface +
  workflow, new features, and new sampled content/factory banks (esp. for
  Augmented Strings and Voices).

IMPORTANT CLARIFICATION (do not conflate with VC10/VC X):
- Acid V and MiniFreak V are NOT new in VC11 — both arrived in V Collection X
  (December 2023). They are just part of the full bundle.

PRICING:
- V Collection 11 Pro = €699 / $699 (a $100 increase over V Collection X).
- V Collection Intro (new tier) = €199 / $199 — 10 curated plugins: Analog Lab
  Pro, Mini V, Jun-6 V, Prophet-5 V, DX7 V, Pure Lofi, MiniFreak V, Stage-73 V,
  Augmented Grand Piano, Augmented Strings.
