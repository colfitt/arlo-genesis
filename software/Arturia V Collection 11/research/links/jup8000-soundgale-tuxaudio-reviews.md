Independent (non-Arturia) confirmation of the Jup-8000 V's architecture + a quality verdict.

## Confirmed specs (matches Arturia + Nu-Trix)
- Supersaw = "**seven sawtooth waves that are slightly distorted up and down in pitch**" —
  i.e. 7 detuned saws (Szabo's finding, independently restated).
- OSC 1 = **7 wave types**: Supersaw, Triangle Mod, Noise, Feedback, Square, Saw, Triangle.
- FX: **up to 4 simultaneous, 17–18 effect types** (frequency / dynamic / spatial).

## Verdict
Sound quality "**top-notch**"; the synth is "interesting not only for its excellent graphics
but also for its advanced features." Treated as one of the standout NEW instruments in VC11.

## Reality-check (carried from prior nav source + community)
A real-JP-8000 owner on community boards called the emulation "dead-on," but for pure modern
supersaw firepower some producers still prefer Serum/Spire/Avenger. **For THIS rig the value
is the authentic, slightly-thinned, degradable wide pad** you then push through Decapitator /
SketchCassette / Valhalla — not EDM-grade brightness. No split/layer mode (single-timbral) —
load two instances for bass+lead splits.
