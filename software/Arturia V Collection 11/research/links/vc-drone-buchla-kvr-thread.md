kvraudio.com · KVR Audio Instruments Forum · "Arturia Buchla Easel V" thread · (ongoing)

User-reported tips & character:
- "A combination of fun and frustration to program" — captures the authentic West-Coast
  workflow; rewards exploration over targeted patching.
- "It takes you in places you haven't been before. I've created patches for it and they are
  totally unique!" — confirms its value as a texture/idea generator.
- The dual low-pass gates (LPGs) are singled out by users as particularly excellent — the
  plucky/organic "bongo" envelope behaviour is the heart of its sound.
- 4-voice polyphony vs. the original's monophony is a real practical advantage (stack a chord
  drone).
- Learning resource users point to: Todd Barton's website / videos for understanding Buchla
  synthesis principles (he's the definitive Music Easel educator).
- Pricing: wait for a sale — frequently far below the $199 list.
- Alternatives debated (Aalto, Reaktor Blocks, ARP 2600 V) but defenders stress it's about
  workflow/inspiration, not raw spec.
