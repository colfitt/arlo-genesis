## What it is
- **"Stairway To Mello"** = a **FREE soundbank for Arturia Mellotron V** (also an
  Analog Lab V edition) with **20 Mellotron presets**.
- Homage to '60s/'70s Mellotron use — Led-Zeppelin-style sounds + Mellotron
  orchestral tones; aimed at prog/vintage but invites genre-bending.

## Practical notes
- **Playable key range = G2–F5** (Mellotron tapes only cover a limited range —
  important when programming/mapping; above/below = no sound).
- Designed with **Mellotron V 1.8.0.3420 (x64)**; Analog Lab V edition needs
  Analog Lab V 5.7.2.3703+.
- Free, drag-into-Mellotron-V soundbank — a quick way to expand the factory
  61-preset library with more roadworn/orchestral tape patches.
