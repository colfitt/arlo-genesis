Sound-designer notes (Arturia's own pull-quotes) + features that matter for drone/noise:
- Character: "fat and clear at the same time with lots of harmonics"; great for "heavy basses
  and weird SFX." Raw, rich oscillators that make complex textures even un-modulated.
- PATCH MATRIX is "the principal strength" — the modular routing brain; everything (osc, noise,
  filter, ring mod, envelope, joystick, FX) cross-connects here.
- FUNCTION GENERATORS (Arturia addition) let you "go further and faster in terms of sound
  exploration" — loopable multi-stage envelopes/LFOs for slow evolving motion.
- JOYSTICK: e.g. "this joystick that slowly detunes OSC2 and opens the VCF" — assign X/Y to
  detune + cutoff and you have an instant hand-played drone-swell controller; animate+loop it
  for hands-free evolution.
- RING MODULATION is called one of "Synthi V's secret weapons" — for metallic/inharmonic and
  unnatural textures (great noise-bed source).
- The self-resonating 18 dB/oct FILTER reproduces the original's "soft distortion" — push
  resonance to self-oscillation for a pure sine drone tone, or just under for grit.
- Output REVERB (spring-model) makes sounds "real"; chorus + stereo delay also onboard.
