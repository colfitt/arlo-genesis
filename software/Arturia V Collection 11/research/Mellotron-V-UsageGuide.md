# Mellotron V — Usage Guide

Arturia's component-model of the M400 tape-replay machine — **the** shoegaze
strings/choir/flute source. For this rig it's the tape-degraded wall under banjo: the
famous "3 violins" string tape + choir, pushed through Flutter/Saturation/Mechanics and
into Valhalla/EchoBoy.

## 1. What it is + the tape character

**65 original tape racks** (MKI/MKII/M300/M400) — flutes, **strings (the "3 violins")**,
brass, organs, **choirs**, mandolins; **61 factory presets**. The lo-fi controls live in
the Advanced panel: `research/transcripts/vc-lofi-mellotron-v-overview-arturia.md`, `research/links/vc-lofi-mellotron-v-arturia-overview.md`
- **Flutter** (wow/flutter pitch wobble), **Tape Saturation** (warm thickening + slight
  distortion), **Mechanics** (the machine's mechanical noise) + a hiss floor + a Pitch
  (tape-speed) knob.
- **Velocity-sensitive; aftertouch → flutter** (press harder = more pitch seasickness)
  and volume.
- The original **~8-second tape limit is modeled** — but **defeat it with sample looping**
  for infinite sustain.

**The A/B/C/All track selector:** M400 frames hold 3 sounds; Arturia adds **intermediate
fade positions (A↔B, B↔C)** and an **"All" position** (all three tapes at once — impossible
on hardware) for thick stacked walls. Blend live with the mod wheel (DAW-automatable).

**Arturia additions:** 4 FX slots (~10 effects), a **Fender-Twin amp + Leslie**, a
**convolution reverb** panel, ADSR, LFO, external input, and **import up to 8 of your own
samples** → played back *as if recorded to vintage tape* (so you can run a banjo through
the Mellotron's tape model + Leslie + convolution). `research/links/vc-lofi-mellotron-v-arturia-overview.md`

## 3. Rig-specific recipes

- **★ Shoegaze string wall:** 3-violins strings, **"All"** or two tapes panned L/R,
  **Flutter ~40%, Tape Saturation up, Mechanics low-mid**, loop for sustain → internal
  convolution reverb → **Valhalla VintageVerb** (long) + **Decapitator** for the wall.
  This is the My Bloody Valentine *Loveless* "Touched" / Radiohead string-tape move. `research/links/vc-lofi-mellotron-shoegaze-radiohead-context.md`
- **Run your banjo through it:** import a banjo sample (up to 8) → the tape model + Leslie
  + convolution = banjo "recorded onto a Mellotron tape."
- **Choir bed:** Big Stereo Choir recipe, buried low under the banjo lead.

## 5. Pitfalls / gotchas

- **The 8-second tape limit is modeled** — loop samples if you need sustained pads.
- **It's a character instrument** — the Flutter/Saturation/Mechanics ARE the sound; clean
  = pointless. Lean into the wobble.
- Playable range is limited per tape (the originals were ~35-note keyboards).
