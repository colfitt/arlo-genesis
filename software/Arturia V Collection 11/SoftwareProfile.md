# Arturia V Collection 11

**Summary** — V Collection **11** (April 2025): 44 vintage instrument emulations + Analog
Lab V. The synth/texture-source bench for the rig.

## Why I have it
The deep well of **evolving-pad / drone / lo-fi-sampling / tape-string** sources to layer
under banjo/baritone into Valhalla/EchoBoy/Decapitator. Research filed as a **navigator +
5 flagship guides** (deep 4-facet dive): → `research/Arturia-Navigator.md`.

## Signature uses
- **Lush walls:** **CS-80 V4** (Vangelis brass/pad, ribbon + per-voice poly-AT). →
  `research/CS-80-V-UsageGuide.md`
- **Shoegaze strings/choir:** **Mellotron V** (3-violins tape, Flutter/Saturation, "All"
  position, loop for sustain). → `research/Mellotron-V-UsageGuide.md`
- **Drone/noise:** **Synthi V** (self-oscillating filter, pin-matrix, looped function
  generators) + **Buchla Easel V** (random-voltage → Period, Gravity). →
  `research/Synthi-V-UsageGuide.md`, `research/Buchla-Easel-V-UsageGuide.md`
- **Lo-fi sampling of your OWN gear:** **Emulator II V** (DAC=Vintage 8-bit, set root note)
  + **CMI V** (drop bit/rate) + **Synclavier V** (Timbre Slices = 2–5 min morphing drone).
  → `research/Emulator-II-CMI-UsageGuide.md`
- **Fast browse:** **Analog Lab V** Type tiles + the 4 macros (Delay+Release → instant wash).
- **Universal evolving trick:** park 2–3 Advanced-panel **Function/Random modulators at
  unsynced rates** on any synth → the pad never repeats.
- **Deepest evolving texture:** **Matrix-12 V2** (matrix self-modulation, keyboard-tracked
  self-osc filter, 24-osc wall, single-note drone-chord trick). → `research/Matrix-12-V2-UsageGuide.md`
- **Supersaw walls:** **Jup-8000 V** (Detune low third + Mix high, Feedback-osc drone) →
  Decapitator/Valhalla. → `research/Jup-8000-V-UsageGuide.md`
- **Rig integration / processed banjo:** **KORG MS-20 V** — self-oscillating-filter drones +
  the ESP audio-in. **⚠️ MS-20 V's AU may not expose sidechain in Logic — use the free
  Filter MS-20 effect to crunch the banjo instead.** → `research/KORG-MS-20-V-UsageGuide.md`
- **Modern texture/choir walls:** **Augmented STRINGS / VOICES** (Additive/Custom Morph;
  **freeze early — worst CPU in VC**). → `research/Augmented-Strings-Voices-UsageGuide.md`

## Notes
- **Logic AU-only** → the `.component` loads under AU Instruments → Arturia. **CPU is
  heavy** (Augmented Strings/Voices are the worst; CS-80/Matrix-12/Modular unison too) →
  **freeze/bounce pad & drone washes early.**
- **New in VC11:** Jup-8000 V (supersaw walls), Augmented Mallets/Yangtze, SEM V3 (now
  8-voice poly), MiniBrute V + Synthx V added. (Acid V / MiniFreak V are NOT new — VC X.)
- **Pure LoFi is a separate Arturia product**, filed at `Software/Arturia Pure LoFi/` (not
  in this 44-instrument bundle).
- Deep usage research → navigator + **9 flagship guides** in `research/` (first pass: 4-facet
  deep dive, 61 sources; **2nd pass 2026-06-11: +4 flagships — Matrix-12 V2 / Jup-8000 V /
  KORG MS-20 V / Augmented Strings+Voices, +20 sources = ~81 total**). The remaining ~36
  instruments stay navigator-one-liner level (aesthetic-filtered-deep policy).
