# Reverb Machine Sample Packs

**Summary** — Atmospheric sample packs, Ableton racks, and synth-preset banks from
Reverb Machine (Dan Tindall). A *small* unit where the **free blog of sound-design
deconstructions is worth more than the paid packs.** Three product types: synth-preset
banks (need the target synth — Arturia/Analog Lab ones load here), Ableton-native
sample/rack packs (Motions, drum sets), and multi-format sampler packs (VP-30 / PS-48,
which ship a Kontakt version). Owned packs live on the **offline `PlaySomeGodDamnMusic`
drive** — formats below are from the vendor site; verify on disk when mounted.

## Why I have it
Texture/ambient source material for the drone-shoegaze workflow, plus — the real draw —
Dan Tindall's blog as a learning library: reverse-engineered Eno / Aphex Twin / Vangelis
/ Boards of Canada records, several of which use plugins already in this rig (CS-80 V4,
Synthi V, Prophet-5 V, Stage-73 V2, Valhalla VintageVerb).

## Signature uses
- **VP-30 / PS-48 → Kontakt 8 inside Logic** (Kontakt version needs full Kontakt 6+; have
  Kontakt 8). VP-330 choir/strings = sustained pads under a banjo wall.
- **Motions ambient pack in Live 12 Lite** (vendor-confirmed Lite-compatible, no Max for
  Live) → resample/bounce textures to WAV → into Logic.
- **Extract a pack's WAV → map a playable drone instrument in Kontakt** (long loop +
  long attack/release).
- **Blog recipes built with owned gear:** Eno incommensurable-loops walls, Discreet-Music
  long-delay-on-a-send generative drone (Synthi V), Vangelis CS-80 brass + one big
  VintageVerb send, BoC perfect-fifth + ±30-cent-detune nostalgia.

## Notes
- **Most "packs" are synth presets, not samples** — useless without the matching synth.
  Safe ones here = the **Analog Lab / V Collection** packs (Chroma Campfire = Boards of
  Canada ambient, even loads in free Analog Lab Play; Europa; Seminal).
- **Logic has no `.adg` import** — Ableton-rack packs (Motions, drum sets) run in Live 12
  Lite, then bounce/resample to WAV for Logic.
- **Lite ceiling:** Motions itself is Lite-safe; the paid **Remake Projects** (full Live
  11/12 sets) may not open intact in Lite — use their bundled **stems + MIDI** in Logic.
- **Kontakt packs need full Kontakt 6+** (have 8); Decent Sampler version = free fallback.
- **Community usage is thin** — Motions has 0 KVR reviews; the YouTube videos are
  caption-less music playthroughs. The **blog articles are the documentation.**
- Drive must be mounted (`requires_drive_mounted: true`).
- Deep usage notes + copyable recipes → `research/Reverb-Machine-Packs-UsageGuide.md`.
