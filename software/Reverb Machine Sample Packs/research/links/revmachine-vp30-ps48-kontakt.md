# VP-30 & PS-48 — the Kontakt-ready sample packs (directly loadable in Logic)

The two Reverb Machine packs that ship a **Kontakt** version = the only ones that drop
straight into the user's Logic-via-Kontakt-8 workflow without Ableton.

## VP-30 (Roland VP-330 strings/choir/vocoder)
- **1,050 samples** of the VP-330: Strings, Male Choir, Female Choir, Mixed Choir, Vocoder.
- Three formats: **Kontakt**, Decent Sampler (100% free, cross-platform), Ableton Live
  (7 instrument racks with macros).
- **Kontakt version requires FULL Kontakt 6 or higher** — the user has **Kontakt 8**, so
  this loads natively. (Free Kontakt Player will NOT open it.)
- $30.

## PS-48 (Yamaha PSS-480 FM "Phoenix-style")
- Sampled FM keyboard sounds → same three formats: Kontakt, Decent Sampler, Ableton Live.
- Also requires full Kontakt for the Kontakt version.

## Rig application
- These are the cleanest Reverb-Machine → Logic path: load the `.nki` in Kontakt 8 inside
  Logic. The VP-330 choir/strings are perfect sustained-pad fodder to layer under banjo
  walls. Decent Sampler version = a free fallback if Kontakt is busy/CPU-bound.
- General principle: the **Kontakt/Decent-Sampler-format** Reverb Machine packs (VP-30,
  PS-48) are the ones that suit Logic; the **Ableton-rack-format** packs (Motions, the drum
  packs) live in Live 12 Lite and get bounced/resampled into Logic.
