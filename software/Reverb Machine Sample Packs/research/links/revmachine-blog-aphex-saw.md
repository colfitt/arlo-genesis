# How Aphex Twin Created "Selected Ambient Works 85-92" — the lo-fi ambient blueprint

Reverb Machine's most-shared article (covered by MusicRadar, Sonic State, Reverb.com).
Includes downloadable presets + full track remakes.

## Gear / signal philosophy
- **Alesis Quadraverb** reverb on EVERY track (one box, consistent space — like Vangelis's
  one-big-send idea).
- Core synths: **Roland SH-101, Korg MS-20, Yamaha DX7**.
- Everything **mixed to standard cassette** → the warm, slightly-degraded lo-fi character.
- Drums: not a real TR-808 — a **Roland R-8 with an 808 expansion card** (this is exactly
  what Reverb Machine's DrumVerse R8 pack samples).

## Copyable sound-design
- **Pluck patch (SH-101 style)**: square wave (osc 1), filter wide open, **VCA decay ~73 ms,
  zero sustain** → very short plucky notes.
- **Sampling found material**: the "Xtal" chords/vocal are sampled from a 1986 Chappell
  library-music LP — i.e. build pads from sampled sources, not just synths.
- The cassette mixdown is the throughline: track-bus → tape → back in.

## Rig application
- The cassette-master move = SketchCassette II / RC-20 / the CB tape pedals / MXNHLT
  PORTA424 on the master or stems. This is the user's whole "recorded-wrong" aesthetic.
- The 73 ms-decay pluck recipe works on any owned mono synth (Arturia Mini V, MS-20-ish
  patches in Massive X).
- DrumVerse R8 pack = the literal R-8 drum source Aphex used, in Ableton-rack form.
