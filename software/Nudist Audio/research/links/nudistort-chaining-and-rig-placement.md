Synthesis of placement/stacking advice for Nudistort, tied to a doom/drone/shoegaze
degraded rig (Logic Pro AU host; neighbors = Decapitator, RC-20, Valhalla, hardware
fuzzes Hizumitas/MXR 108, JHS Colour Box). Where a claim is a general principle vs a
Nudistort-specific source, it's marked.

## Nudistort-specific placement (from WaveInformer hands-on + the two video demos)
- **On Reverb/Delay SENDS** (WaveInformer's explicit rec) — put Nudistort on an aux
  send feeding (or fed by) a reverb to get "alien formants / ripping distortion" and
  ambient texture rather than dirtying the dry source. Ideal for shoegaze wash.
- **Pre-EQ INTO the drive matters** (Noise Kitchen demo): he moves a dark low-pass
  filter BEFORE Nudistort "so it doesn't cut off the top of it" — the distortion
  reacts to what you feed it, so shape tone pre-drive. (Nudistort's own **Tone** tilt
  is pre-distortion, consistent with this.)
- **Damping = a tone/EQ move, not just a transient tamer** (Boring Gear Reviews): "just
  by controlling the damping control the EQ changed totally… I landed on this throaty
  aggressive sound." Use Damping to dial the distortion's voicing.
- **Mix Lock** (Noise Kitchen): lock the wet/dry so you can audition Types/Modes/presets
  at a FIXED parallel blend — the practical way to use it as parallel grit under a wall.
- **The delay self-destructs by design**: high Feedback + high Abnormality "can quickly
  spiral" (WaveInformer) → that runaway is a *feature* for drone/noise beds, but
  automate Feedback down or gate it. Sustained "droning effects" called out as a
  use case in the Boring Gear demo.
- **Per-source distortion** (WaveInformer): the reviewer ran each guitar string through
  its own Nudistort via a hexaphonic pickup — directly transferable to a GK-5/VG-800
  hexaphonic banjo/baritone rig (one Nudistort per string = polyphonic-aware fuzz).

## General principles (NOT Nudistort-specific — marked)
- **Distortion BEFORE reverb** = crunch/harmonics that the reverb then smears (organic
  shoegaze wall). **Reverb BEFORE distortion** = more lo-fi, "recorded-wrong," upfront,
  the reverb tail itself gets mangled (audiotent). Both are valid; pick by intent.
- Drone/doom chain order that matches the rest of this rig's research: distortion
  EARLY (harmonic bed) → modulation → time/reverb (Valhalla). Nudistort can be the
  early harmonic bed OR the late send-mangler, depending on which weirdness you want.

## How it sits next to the rig's other dirt (the "which when" map)
- **vs Decapitator** — Decapitator = predictable analog harmonic *color/warmth* (its
  community verdict literally is "shines when you DON'T use it for distortion").
  Nudistort = unpredictable nonlinear *destruction/texture* + a weird delay. Reach for
  Decapitator to glue/warm; reach for Nudistort to mangle/transform/generate texture.
  Zero overlap — they're complementary, can even stack (Decapitator color → Nudistort
  chaos, or Nudistort into a clean source then Decapitator to re-glue).
- **vs RC-20 Retro Color** — RC-20 = lo-fi *degradation motion* (wobble/noise/dropout)
  + mild Distort module. Nudistort's distortion is far more aggressive/alien and adds a
  delay engine RC-20 lacks; RC-20's wobble is gentler/tape-ier. Use RC-20 for
  "old/broken tape" vibe, Nudistort for "broken in a way you've never heard."
- **vs hardware fuzzes (Hizumitas / MXR 108) + JHS Colour Box V2** — those are
  committed, played-in front-end character you PRINT (and the fuzzes are
  pick-dynamic/feedback-interactive in a way only real-time playing gives). Nudistort
  = recallable, automatable, in-the-box, and does things no fuzz can (ring-mod, the
  abnormal delay, Species note-resampling). So: fuzz for the committed wall you perform;
  Nudistort for recallable studio mangling, sends, and sound-design textures. Don't try
  to replace the fuzzes with it — use it for what they CAN'T do.

## Avoiding mud (general + inferred)
- It's a single-band, full-range mangler with NO internal multiband split (despite the
  brief's premise) — so on bass/low sources blend with **Mix / Mix-Lock** (parallel) or
  high-pass into it / low-pass the wet to keep lows clean, the same discipline used with
  Decapitator. Stacking many aggressive distortions = mud; pick ONE lead dirt per source.
