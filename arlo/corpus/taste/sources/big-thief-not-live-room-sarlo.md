# Big Thief "Not" / Two Hands — raw live-room capture (Andrew Sarlo)

Distilled concrete info:

- *Two Hands* recorded mostly **live in the studio with largely no overdubs**; vocals on all but
  two songs are **completely live takes**. Recorded at **Sonic Ranch**, Tornillo TX, July 2018.
  Producer **Andrew Sarlo** (produced every Big Thief album).
- *Two Hands* is the "earth twin" to *U.F.O.F.*'s "celestial twin": **pointedly raw, eschews
  U.F.O.F.'s spectral overdubs in favour of almost entirely live recordings.** The band-in-a-room
  energy, finger noise, amp bleed and imperfection are the aesthetic.
- "Not" = the song that **"led the way" for the record** — its raw guitar-solo build captured live.

Rig takeaway: this is the LEAST-processed anchor — the lesson is restraint. The rig emulates "Not"
not with a wall of FX but with a present, close, slightly-degraded dry source (CB Clean always-on,
a touch of Chroma CASSETTE/SWEETEN) and the texture pedals barely on. When the cluster DOES build a
wall, it's the U.F.O.F. "spectral overdub" side (reverse/granular swells) — keep the two modes
distinct.
