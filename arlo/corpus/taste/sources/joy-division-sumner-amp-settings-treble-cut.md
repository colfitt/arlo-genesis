# Bernard Sumner / Joy Division — concrete guitar amp settings + the treble-cut "cold" character

THE AMP + REPRESENTATIVE SETTINGS (the headline concrete data):
- Amp: **Vox AC30** (AC30C2X = modern equiv of the discontinued JMI Vox UL730); the original produced "grittiness and natural overdrive."
- "She's Lost Control" representative dial: **Gain 3 · Volume 5 · Bass 8 · Middle 4 · Treble 2 · Presence 1.**
  → i.e. **bass UP, treble + presence cut HARD, gain LOW.** This is the cold/dark/percussive rhythmic Joy Division voice on the guitar itself.

GUITARS / SYNTHS:
- Bernard Sumner: Shergold Masquerader (dual humbuckers, maple neck). Ian Curtis sometimes Vox Phantom Special VI.
- Synths/strings: **Powertran Transcendent 2000** (mono analog, valve in signal path — warm/edgy) + **ARP Omni-2** for the "icy synthesizer" string/brass textures on Unknown Pleasures (Hannett + Sumner together).

THE EQ APPROACH (the single most translatable move):
- Use **an EQ pedal to take away most of the guitar's treble** → the "dark, percussive, rhythmic-oriented" tone. Guitar plays a RHYTHMIC role, not lead.
- Huge reverb sits OVER this dark, treble-cut source (Hannett's cold artificial space — see hannett-joy-division-cold-gated-space.md). "Melancholic, dark, pulsating."

DISTILLED FOR THE RIG:
- The character is **DARK + TREBLE-CUT + LOW-GAIN + RHYTHMIC**, then the cold space arrives over it.
- The user's units are downstream of the source, but the *voicing target* maps:
  - Dark/treble-cut source → Longsword EQ with **High pulled hard down + Low up + Mid moderate** (the active Baxandall EQ is the rig's "EQ pedal that takes away treble"), Drive low.
  - Cold separated space over it → Dark Star V3 **Lag (predelay) + HPF Filter + clean Crush** (cold/clinical, not warm hall) — see hannett sources.
  - This is a banjo-friendly target: the banjo's piercing 2–4 kHz is exactly the treble these settings cut.

PROVENANCE: the amp dial is a guitar-tone GUIDE's representative reconstruction (MusicStrive), not Sumner's own published numbers — cite as a reference, NOT an artist setting. None of the user's pedals were used by Joy Division (Vox AC30 + Hannett's AMS rack). All rig patches = 🟣 designed-to-emulate the RESULT (dark treble-cut rhythmic guitar under cold predelayed space).
