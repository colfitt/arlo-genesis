# Martin Hannett — the cold, separated, cavernous Joy Division space ("Disorder"/"Atmosphere")

THE SIGNATURE SOUND: "reverbed drums, echoing vocals, icy synthesizers, clanking/crashing sound effects, prominent bass, and effected guitars." Hannett mixed alone, discovered sounds by extended trial-and-error.

KEY GEAR:
- **AMS 15-80S digital delay** — the famous unit. On "Disorder" the delay was "set to some minute period of time" — a tiny delay on the drums for a subtle, hardened, separated character.
- AMS digital reverbs; Rebis delays + **noise gates**; MXR delays + harmonisers; Roland chorus; various analogue delays.

HOW THE COLD/SEPARATED/SPACIOUS SOUND IS MADE:
- **Record each drum SEPARATELY**, one at a time, to kill all bleed → total separation (the "cold," clinical isolation).
- **Drum prep**: front bass-drum skin removed, lower tom skins removed, pillows/cloth gaffer-taped on → deadened, dry resonance so the reverb is the ONLY space.
- **Very fast delay on the snare** = "punchy" — short slap that hardens the transient.
- **Re-amped reverb trick**: drum sounds routed through an Auratone speaker on a toilet in the basement bathroom, re-recorded through a single mic with "spectral reverb" → an unnatural, otherworldly room.
- **Extreme panning**: snare (normally center) panned across the whole field; delayed releases panned to the OPPOSITE side → "cavernous."
- **Backwards guitar** opens "New Dawn Fades."
- Bass forward and trebly (Hook), guitars set BACK behind reverb (the famous "separated, spacious" mix balance).
- Synare drum-synth (Stephen Morris) on "She's Lost Control."

DISTILLED FOR THE RIG (dry-source → big artificial space, predelay/separation, cold):
- The Joy Division reverb is a LONG, COLD, ARTIFICIAL space with PREDELAY (the source stays dry/separated, then the space arrives after a gap). Predelay = "Lag" on the Dark Star; cold/non-lush = HPF filter + low Crush (clean digital), not warm hall.
- Gated/short slap = the fast-delay snare; a noise gate on a long verb = the gated-reverb cousin.
- Backwards guitar = reverse grain (QI) / Dark Star pitch tricks.

RIG TRANSLATION: all 🟣 designed-to-emulate — none of the user's pedals were used by Hannett (he used AMS/Rebis/MXR studio rack). The rig chases the RESULT: dry brittle source + cold predelayed artificial space + reverse + extreme stereo width.
