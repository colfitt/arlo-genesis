## TMS LDN Production Trio

TMS LDN — Tom 'Froe' Barnes, Ben Kohn, and Peter 'Merf' Kelleher — formed as The Musical Shed in 2002. They created "Someone You Loved" with Lewis Capaldi during an afternoon writing session.

## Studio: State Of The Ark

Moved to State Of The Ark studio in London (formerly Terry Britten's). Years of incremental development preceded this, including:

- Original garden shed with inadequate soundproofing
- Chiswick's RDS studio
- Grand Prix House (converted call centre, 12 years)
- State Of The Ark (current)

## Technical Setup

- **DAW:** Apple Logic
- **Interface/cards:** UAD interfaces and expansion cards
- **Monitoring:** Barefoot Sound MicroMain 27s (primary), Yamaha NS10s, Auratones
- **Plugins:** Waves, Softube, FabFilter, iZotope, Soundtoys

Stated workflow principle: **"simplicity and quick workflow are essential"** — applies to plugin selection and DAW familiarity.

## Weekly Methodology

TMS operates as **rotating pair-and-solo configurations**, not as a complete trio. Five-day weeks split as:

- **3 days writing**
- **2 days production**

Different member pairings rotate weekly to prevent creative stagnation.

## "Starters" Pattern

The team creates **starters** — 15-second musical sketches featuring keyboard parts, drums, or vocal elements. Each member individually scores these. Selected options presented to the visiting artist or topliner.

The rationale: balance preparation with spontaneity, avoid intimidating the artist with a completely finished backing track.

## Writing Room Design

Writing room mimics a living-room environment, not a traditional studio:

- Unconventional monitor placement prioritizes mood over acoustic precision
- Lyrics displayed on a projector screen throughout sessions
- No vocal booth — everyone in the same space to enhance communication

Kohn: production sessions are *"like therapy, a lot of the time"* — extended conversations establish emotional core before musical development.

## "Someone You Loved" Session

- Lewis Capaldi visited the studio multiple times before the August 2018 writing session
- Final version emerged largely from **one three-hour afternoon session** following completion of another track that day
- Capaldi explored piano chords; team recorded on iPad to preserve musical ideas
- Capaldi developed verse, pre-chorus melody, and initial lyrics
- Production team assisted with chorus development and lyric refinement
- Barnes on the role: Capaldi contributed substantial lyrical material, team's role was *"catching them and knitting them together"*
- Verse and chorus completed that day
- **Middle eight and second verse developed during a subsequent session**
