# Cassette Demo Fatten + Wobble

## Concept
A designed-to-emulate build for Car Seat Headrest's bedroom-cassette warm degrade — lo-fi tape glue on the source. Cassette voice adds grit, low end and compression; a bouncy wobble double layers warped-tape movement. Mirrors the Lo-Fi Crush Wall intent but pointed at demo-warm rather than the doom-wall end. The bedroom-cassette warmth + wobble on the source, before the End-board Gen Loss / PORTA424 ruin, reads as intentional.

## How to play it
1. Engage Tape Saturation and Doubletracker. Voice cassette.
2. Saturation ~12–1:00 (set fairly high; ride guitar/VG-800 volume for the drive amount), Tone ~11:00 (dark-ish).
3. Hold Tape Saturation ON, turn TONE to set Low Trim up ~10:00 (keep the bottom tight).
4. Doubletracker: Type bounce (most warbly/lo-fi), Lag Time ~12–1:00 (slapback ~50–100 ms), Blend ~11:00 (density not echo), Wobble ~12–1:00 (warped-tape movement).
5. Hold Tape Saturation ON, turn BLEND to set Doubletracker Boost/Cut to 12:00.

## Notes
- Designed-to-emulate. The actual destruction is the End-Board's job — Deco stays well-behaved.
- bounce = most-warble per Gueringer's ranking.
