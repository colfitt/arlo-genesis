# LS-PP1 — BC,NR Op-Amp Sabbath Slab

## Concept
Designed-to-emulate (Ref: BC,NR 'Basketball Shoes' — the late op-amp distortion Sabbath slab). The Longsword (op-amp) is the rig's most accurate unit for op-amp distortion; it sits late, off until the climax. Silicon for the squared brick, Drive high, Low boosted for slab weight, High cut, SHIFT down (300 Hz) for Sabbath body. Stack after Hizumitas HZ-PP2.

## How to play it
1. DIODE down (silicon), Drive ~2-3:00 (high), SHIFT down (300 Hz).
2. Low ~1:00 (boosted), High ~10:00 (cut), Mid at noon.
3. Rebalance Level (~2:00) — silicon is loud.
4. Kick the Boost footswitch ON as the 'kick the door in' stomp at the climax.

## Notes
- APPROXIMATION: voicing chases the Sabbath-class scooped slab, not BC,NR's exact pedal (Maxon/ZVex).
- Pull Low toward noon if the bottom flubs.
- Designed-to-emulate; knob positions are starting points — dial by ear.
