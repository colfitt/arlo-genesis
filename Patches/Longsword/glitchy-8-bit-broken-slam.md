# Glitchy 8-Bit Broken Slam

## Concept
Designed-to-emulate (LSW-LF2, Ref: Car Seat Headrest, layered-crunchy lo-fi warts; broken/gated sputter). The existing Glitchy 8-Bit Slam recipe voiced for the layered-wall lens — Toledo stacking blown-out passes to "make it all as big as possible." Level MAX, Drive MAX, Boost knob MAX with footswitch ON, silicon for the most squared/gated result. Broken, gated, 8-bit sputter = the layered-lo-fi-warts-as-feature aesthetic.

## How to play it
1. Level, Drive, and Boost knob all MAX; Boost footswitch ON; DIODE down (silicon).
2. EQ to taste.
3. Feed the maxed output into a downstream drive (Oxford) or the 424 driven hard until it overloads and sputters.
