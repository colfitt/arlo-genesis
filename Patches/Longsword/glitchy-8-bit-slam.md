# Glitchy 8-Bit Slam

## Concept
Inverts the rig's usual placement — the Longsword becomes the FRONT slammer and the Oxford is the "another overdrive" it overloads. Level MAX, Drive MAX, Boost knob MAX with the footswitch ON, silicon for the most squared/gated result. Feed the maxed output into the Oxford driven hard until it overloads and sputters into a broken, gated, "8-bit"-fuzz texture.

## How to play it
1. Level, Drive, and Boost knob all MAX; Boost footswitch ON; DIODE down (silicon).
2. EQ to taste.
3. Feed the maxed output into the Oxford driven hard until it overloads and sputters.

## Notes
- On-aesthetic for the "recorded-wrong"/degraded lens (gated, broken, sputtering).
- **WATCH OUTPUT LEVEL — this is the loudest patch on the sheet.**
