# Anduril

## Concept
EAE's "classy, refined gain" voice — the polite end of the pedal. A low-gain, high-output boost with smoother highs, used to push the amp or the next stage with the Level knob rather than added grit.

## How to play it
1. Run Drive at noon and bring Level up to ~2-3:00 for the output push.
2. DIODE up (MOSFET) for softer compression; SHIFT up (1 kHz).
3. Use it as a clean-ish platform to drive the downstream texture/time boards, or to push the Oxford harder.

## Notes
- Least on-aesthetic for drone/doom, but the essential "use it as a boost/EQ" voice.
- MOSFET (DIODE up) gives the softer compression that keeps it polite.
- Knob positions are approximate (read off the manual diagrams) — dial by ear.
