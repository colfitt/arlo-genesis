# Finesse Weapon

## Concept
The opposite of Berserker — a focused, mid-forward voice for cutting a lead through density. Mid boosted hard while pulling BOTH Low and High back (cutting both = the effective mid-boost), DIODE up (MOSFET), moderate Drive. EAE's "full bore mid boost… for pushing amps and other pedals."

## How to play it
1. Boost the Mid hard (~2-3:00), then pull both Low and High back.
2. DIODE up (MOSFET), moderate Drive, rebalance Level by ear.
3. Try BOTH SHIFT positions: up (1 kHz) for a piercing cut, down (300 Hz) for body.

## Notes
- Both SHIFT positions are useful for banjo-as-lead riding on top of the fuzz wall — SHIFT up pierces, SHIFT down carries body.
- Cut-both-Low+High = effective mid-boost (verbatim console move from the manual).
