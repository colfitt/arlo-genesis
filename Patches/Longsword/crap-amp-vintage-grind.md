# Crap-Amp Vintage Grind (Stormbringer-lean)

## Concept
Designed-to-emulate (LSW-LF1, Ref: Car Seat Headrest / Modest Mouse, "old distortion pedal into a practice amp" demo grind). The existing factory Stormbringer voice — EAE's own "Very Cvlt" framing — re-pointed at the CSH/Modest Mouse demo-crunch lineage. Drive plus Boost engaged, silicon clipping, the 1 kHz mid pushed for the mid-forward crap-amp honk. The Longsword has no documented signature artist; this is not an artist setting.

## How to play it
1. Drive ~1-2:00, Boost knob ~1:00 with Boost footswitch ON.
2. DIODE down (silicon), SHIFT up (1 kHz), Mid boosted ~1:00.
3. Low and High at noon, Level ~1:00.
4. Keep it DRY; into PORTA424/424 for the recorded-wrong finish.

## Notes
- Designed-to-emulate; no artist setting for this box.
- Knob positions are starting points — dial by ear.
