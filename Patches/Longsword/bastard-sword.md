# Bastard Sword

## Concept
The closest factory preset to the rig's standalone doom/baritone wall — heavy, raw, "swinging a big chunk of steel… very potent on bass." Silicon clipping squares it into a brick; SHIFT-down (300 Hz) plus boosted Low gives the baritone/VG-800 low crush its mass.

## How to play it
1. Run fuzz OFF — this is the rig's own distortion voice, not a stacker here.
2. Drive near max (~3:00), DIODE down (silicon) for max compression / squared brick, SHIFT down (300 Hz).
3. Boost Low (~2:00), cut High (~10:00), Mid at noon.
4. Rebalance Level (~2:00) by ear — silicon at high Drive is loud.

## Notes
- Maps directly to the baritone/VG-800 low crush.
- Pull Low back toward noon if the bottom flubs.
- Signature-fit: post-punk/Swans drone-wall + baritone weight.
- Knob positions are approximate (read off the manual diagrams) — dial by ear.
