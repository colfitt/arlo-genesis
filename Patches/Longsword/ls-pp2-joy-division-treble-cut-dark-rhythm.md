# LS-PP2 — Joy Division Treble-Cut Dark Rhythm (the EQ move)

## Concept
Designed-to-emulate (Ref: Joy Division 'Disorder' — 'use an EQ pedal to take away most of the treble'). The most literal Joy Division translation in the rig — the Longsword's active Baxandall EQ IS the 'EQ pedal that takes away the treble.' Dark, treble-cut, low-gain, rhythmic; then drop the cold Dark Star DS-PP1 space over it. Also the best banjo-darkening move.

## How to play it
1. DIODE up (MOSFET, soft), Drive ~10:00 (low — JD is low-gain), SHIFT up (1 kHz).
2. Low ~1-2:00 (boosted), High ~9:00 (cut HARD), Mid at noon.
3. Level ~noon, Boost footswitch OFF.
4. Layer the cold Dark Star DS-PP1 space over it.

## Notes
- APPROXIMATION: JD's tone is a Vox AC30 + a separate EQ pedal cutting treble; the Longsword does the cut AND the grit in one box.
- Kills the banjo's 2-4 kHz pierce.
- Designed-to-emulate; knob positions are starting points — dial by ear.
