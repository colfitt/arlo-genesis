# Great Weapon Fighting

## Concept
EAE's max-dynamics sustaining body voice — DIODE center (no diodes), SHIFT down (300 Hz) with the Mid cranked, moderate Drive. The boosted low-mid adds exactly the mass a bright banjo physically lacks, making this the factory basis for the rig's banjo-lead body. Sturdy sustain, desert/stoner.

## How to play it
1. DIODE center (no diodes, max dynamics), SHIFT down (300 Hz).
2. Crank/boost the Mid (~2-3:00) — the 300 Hz mid-boost body move.
3. Moderate Drive; Low/High to taste; rebalance Level by ear.

## Notes
- The single most flattering dirt move in the rig for a bright source that lacks low-mid body.
- Center keeps the pluck dynamic; switch to MOSFET (DIODE up) for evened attack/decay on sustained lines.
- Signature-fit: doom/stoner sustain + banjo-as-lead.
