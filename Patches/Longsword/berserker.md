# Berserker

## Concept
A scooped slab positioned by EAE as a fuzz-pedal replacement ("might kick a fuzz off your board"). Boosting BOTH Low and High produces an effective mid-scoop AND overloads the output stage for more aggression, while the Mid is pulled hard down for the full scoop.

## How to play it
1. Mid pulled hard DOWN (full mid cut), then boost both Low and High.
2. Drive up to ~2-3:00, DIODE up (MOSFET), Boost footswitch ON.
3. Run with the fuzz OFF — this is a standalone scooped wall.

## Notes
- The boost-both-Low+High trick = mid-scoop + a hotter, dirtier output stage (verbatim console move from the manual).
- Honest: this is more "aggressive metal scoop" than drone — treat it as a brittle/scooped post-punk contrast option, not the daily wall.
- Knob positions are approximate (read off the manual diagrams) — dial by ear.
