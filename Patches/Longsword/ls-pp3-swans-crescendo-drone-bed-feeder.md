# LS-PP3 — Swans Crescendo Drone-Bed Feeder

## Concept
Designed-to-emulate (Ref: Swans 'Bring the Sun' — sustained drone bed that swells from near-silence). A near-clone of the Drone-Bed Feeder re-aimed at the Swans crescendo: a dense, stable, sustained source you swell up from near-silence (CB Clean dynamic-swell / volume pedal) and that the Dark Star/QI freeze layers into 'four church choirs.' DIODE center for max sustain/dynamics, Drive backed off to a smooth bloom, Low/High softened for downstream definition, Mid SHIFT-down (300 Hz) for body.

## How to play it
1. DIODE center (no diodes — max sustain/dynamics), Drive ~10:00 (smooth bloom, not fizz).
2. Level ~1:00, Low ~11:00 (cut), High ~11:00 (softened), Mid SHIFT-down (300 Hz) ~1:00.
3. Boost footswitch OFF — steady bed.
4. Swell it up from near-silence with the CB Clean dynamic-swell / volume pedal; let the Dark Star/QI freeze layer it.

## Notes
- APPROXIMATION: Swans build with volume + sustain + repetition on a loud amp; this manufactures a stable bed for the freeze engines to layer.
- Designed-to-emulate; knob positions are starting points — dial by ear.
