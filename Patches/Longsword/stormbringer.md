# Stormbringer

## Concept
EAE's "old distortion pedal into a practice amp. Very Cvlt" voice — Drive and Boost both engaged with the 1 kHz mid pushed for a small/crap-amp vintage grind. Silicon clipping squares it; the pushed 1 kHz mid gives the mid-forward honk.

## How to play it
1. Drive up (~1-2:00), Boost knob ~1:00 and Boost footswitch ON.
2. DIODE down (silicon), SHIFT up (1 kHz), Mid boosted (~1:00).
3. Low and High at noon; Level ~1:00.

## Notes
- Signature-fit lo-fi cluster (Car Seat Headrest/Modest Mouse demo-crunch / "recorded-wrong").
- EAE's own "Very Cvlt" framing leans the right way.
- Knob positions are approximate (read off the manual diagrams) — dial by ear.
