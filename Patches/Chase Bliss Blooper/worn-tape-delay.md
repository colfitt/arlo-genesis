# Worn tape delay

## Concept

A worn tape-echo voice with concrete starting knob positions. REPEATS and STABILITY both around 10 o'clock give an "excellent tape echo sound" — this is the manual's Tape Machine patch with numbers attached.

## How to play it

1. Build a delay-style base — tap the RECORD footswitch into the overdub state, Mode ADD.
2. Set REPEATS ≈ 10 o'clock and STABILITY ≈ 10 o'clock for an excellent tape echo sound.
3. Push REPEATS fully CW (infinity symbol) for an infinite frozen loop.
4. Pull REPEATS CCW (only effective while recording) for faster decay.
5. STABILITY fully CCW = bypassed/clean.
