# Glitchprint

## Concept

Imprint stuttering glitches into a loop at chosen spots. Record short bursts of Stepped Trimmer in Additive mode and the glitches print exactly where you place them.

## How to play it

1. In ADD mode, set MOD A to Stepped Trimmer (A3).
2. Record short bursts of Trimmer — use one-shot or punch-in to place them precisely.
3. Each burst imprints a stuttering glitch into the loop.

## Notes

- Trimmer CCW shortens/abrades ("crumbs"); right-of-noon CW introduces a recordable manual time-stretch as you scan the loop.
- Pair with Punch-in for clean placement.
