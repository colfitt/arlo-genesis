# Stability-off-the-rails chaos

## Concept

Broken-tape runaway. Loop a wall, then let heavy Stability plus a maxed Dropper and a CW Scrambler tear it apart over passes. A more extreme, hand-set sibling of Repair Me that needs no ramp dips.

## How to play it

1. In ADD mode, set STABILITY 2–3 o'clock (heavy, volatile).
2. MOD A = Dropper (A2) at max for granular crumble.
3. MOD B = Scrambler (B5) CW for repeating chop.
4. REPEATS ~11 o'clock (fading), VOLUME 12.
5. Expect feedback and self-oscillation — ride VOLUME by holding the RIGHT footswitch.

## Notes

- Clock-face positions are designed; the underlying concept is the manual's "Repair Me" (p.45).
