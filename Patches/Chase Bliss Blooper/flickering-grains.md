# Flickering grains

## Concept

A fluttering granular feel dominated by silence with pieces of audio poking through. Turn Dropper to either extreme and the loop becomes a sparse grain cloud. The drop pattern recalculates each time the modifier is toggled on, so you can tap on/off to audition patterns.

## How to play it

1. Set MOD A to Dropper (A2), knob to either extreme — CW for consistent, CCW for random; at max it goes granular.
2. Tap the modifier on/off to re-roll and audition new drop patterns.
3. Variant: turn REPEATS down for a dissolving granular delay.

## Notes

- A mono-fine texture; great Blooper-source material to feed the Chroma Console / H90 for stereo grain clouds.
