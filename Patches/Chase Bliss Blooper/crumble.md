# Crumble

## Concept

A loop that slowly falls apart over time — an alternative to a Repeats fade. Leave Dropper engaged in Additive mode and the loop progressively crumbles. New layers recorded on top of a crumbled loop are NOT crumbled, so you can rebuild over the rot.

## How to play it

1. In ADD mode, set MOD A to Dropper (A2), mild.
2. Leave it engaged so the loop slowly disintegrates.
3. Dial the Dropper knob: CW for consistent/repeating drops, CCW for random, max for granular.
4. Record new layers on top to rebuild over the crumbled bed.

## Notes

- Pairs beautifully with Accumulator/Stability for cascading decay.
