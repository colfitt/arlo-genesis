# Glacier

## Concept

A sustained banjo or baritone phrase captured, then transformed into a slowly shifting drone texture by setting the Smooth Speed modifier close to noon so the loop is almost stopped. This is the drone cornerstone for the whole rig — a near-frozen bed that drifts under a fresh live lead.

## How to play it

1. Audition in NORM, then commit to ADD.
2. Set MOD A to Smooth Speed (slot A1), knob close to noon so the loop is almost stopped.
3. Engage the arcade button to latch the modifier.
4. Keep the other knobs at the Clean-drone defaults: VOLUME at 12, LAYERS full, REPEATS max.
5. Play a fresh live banjo lead over the top of the frozen bed.

## Notes

- Mono is fine here — the Chroma Console and H90 re-spread it to stereo downstream.
- Pairs under a fresh live banjo lead; this is the neutral drone foundation other patches deviate from.
