# Mystery looping

## Concept

Structured surprise — overdubs land in unpredictable spots across the full loop. Trimmer shrinks the loop to a small play window; record overdubs into that window, then turn Trimmer off and the whole loop and the overdubs could be anywhere. Generative/chance arrangement with no ramping.

## How to play it

1. In NORM mode, record a loop.
2. Turn on MOD A Trimmer (A3) — the loop shrinks to a small play area.
3. Record overdubs into that small window.
4. Turn Trimmer off — the whole loop and the overdubs could now be anywhere.

## Notes

- Great for found-texture banjo fragments.
