# Punch-in

## Concept

Place a unique moment or blip at a precise spot in the loop without timing stress. Audio only changes while the modifier is engaged, so your start/stop timing can be relaxed — great for placing unique moments and blips into your loop.

## How to play it

1. In ADD mode, start overdubbing.
2. Briefly engage a modifier where you want the blip.
3. Stop overdubbing.
4. Audio only changes while the modifier is engaged, so timing can be relaxed.
5. One-shot variant: briefly HOLD the LEFT footswitch to overdub exactly one loop length, then it auto-stops and disengages the modifier — the clean way to print one pass.

## Notes

- Pair with Additive Assist ON (see Setup) for predictable prints.
