# Rolling Clouds — Swapper swelling/fading ambient bed

## Concept

Use the right (FADE-mode) side of Swapper in Additive mode to create a soft landscape of swelling and fading sounds — an ambient bed builder that gently breathes parts of the loop in and out. Swapper is an interactive/envelope-controlled mute-and-swap modifier: its right side gives the fade/landscape behavior, while noon/left is the interactive muting used elsewhere. This is the factory cheat-sheet's named "Rolling Clouds" recipe.

## How to play it

1. Engage the BANK B dip so the alt bank's Swapper is available on MOD B (or load Swapper into a Mod slot via BLIP).
2. Set the Swapper knob (MOD B) to its right (FADE-mode) side.
3. Enter ADD mode and let it run while overdubbing — parts of the loop swell and fade into a soft landscape.
4. Layer sustained baritone/banjo for a continuously breathing ambient wall.

## Notes

- Swapper is an interactive/envelope-controlled mute-and-swap modifier; the right side is the fade/landscape behavior, the noon/left side is interactive muting (see Cut It Out / Twitchy Swapper).
- Requires the BANK B dip (or a BLIP load) — physical-only setup, not a per-song recall.
- Exact knob position beyond "right side" is not published; treat the other knobs as a dialable recipe rather than sourced values.
