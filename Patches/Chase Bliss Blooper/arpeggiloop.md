# Arpeggiloop

## Concept

Turn a held banjo or baritone tone into a synth-style arpeggiated sequence. A ramped Stepped Speed with WIGGLE keeps the pitch range tight and controlled, while SYNC keeps the movement consistent and locked to the loop — a single root note becomes an arpeggiated sequence, hands-free.

## How to play it

1. Set MOD B to Stepped Speed (B4).
2. Set the ramping dips to MOD B + BOUNCE + SYNC + WIGGLE.
3. WIGGLE keeps the pitch range tight/controlled; SYNC keeps the movement consistent and locked to the loop.
4. Hold a banjo root tone and let it arpeggiate.

## Notes

- Single banjo root becomes an arpeggiated sequence, hands-free.
- Sync to the Digitakt for tempo-locked arps.
- Dip switches are physical-only.
