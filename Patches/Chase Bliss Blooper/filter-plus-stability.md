# Filter + Stability

## Concept

Intensify the vintage/lo-fi effect by narrowing the frequency range and adding resonance. Filter narrows the band and adds resonance while Stability adds wow, flutter, noise, and roll-off — together they compound into deep lo-fi. In ADD, Filter gradually introduces resonant chime and can self-oscillate into ringing resonance.

## How to play it

1. Set MOD B to Filter (B6) and engage it — CCW for low-pass, CW for high-pass, noon for none.
2. Engage STABILITY for wow/flutter/noise/roll-off.
3. Together they narrow the band and add resonance for compounding lo-fi.
4. In ADD, watch for self-oscillation as Filter introduces resonant chime per pass.

## Notes

- Stack under the Chroma Console's degradation for layered lo-fi.
- Watch self-oscillation in ADD mode.
