# Time Machine — Sequencing

## Concept

A synced, rhythmic roll of layers in and out — hands-free generative motion. The ramp rolls layers in and out in a synchronized pattern; quick ramps create unique glitches while slower ramps produce remixes of your loop. Take a rest — the loop arranges itself.

## How to play it

1. Set the ramping dips to LAYERS + BOUNCE + SYNC.
2. Set RAMP speed: quicker for unique glitches, slower for remixes of your loop.
3. Variation: swap SYNC for RANDOM to randomize how many layers play at once, for free-running constant change.

## Notes

- Tempo-lock it to the Digitakt clock.
- Dip switches are physical-only, not MIDI-addressable.
