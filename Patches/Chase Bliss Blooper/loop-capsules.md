# Loop Capsules

## Concept

Capture distinct ideas (a C section vs a G section) on separate layers and navigate or shuffle between them with the LAYERS knob — generative arrangement with no modifiers needed.

## How to play it

1. Record a loop.
2. Turn REPEATS all the way down.
3. Engage overdubbing.
4. Once the audio has faded, turn REPEATS all the way up.
5. Record new sounds.
6. Go into playback, then use the LAYERS knob to move between the two totally distinct layers.
7. Auto-shuffle: ramp the LAYERS knob (RANDOM).

## Notes

- You can only peel from the TOP of the stack (8 levels of undo/redo); navigating back and recording forks the loop and erases layers above.
