# Clean drone loop

## Concept

A pristine, song-length capture of a banjo or baritone wall to layer live leads over. This is the neutral baseline before any modifier — the honest starting point every other patch deviates from. No degradation, deliberately; add it later in ADD.

## How to play it

1. In NORM mode with STABILITY off, capture a sustained wall.
2. Keep VOLUME 12, LAYERS full (all layers in), REPEATS max (infinite, no fade).
3. Leave both MOD A and MOD B arcade buttons up (off).
4. Drop to playback and overdub live leads on top.
5. Let the Chroma Console re-stereo it downstream.

## Notes

- The honest baseline — no degradation is deliberate. Add character later in ADD mode.
