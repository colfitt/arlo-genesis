# Transparent Enhancer

## Concept
The maker's quoted use: "Lead lines or a versatile all-around starter." A subtle ~2:1 compression that thickens, focuses, and extends — versatile all-around starter for banjo or baritone single-note work where you want thickening without squash. A slower Attack means the pick attack escapes uncompressed, so it preserves the instrument's energy and attack.

## How to play it
1. **Set Sensitivity by ear to the left LED first**, per instrument.
2. Dynamics ~10–11:00 into the ~2:1 zone.
3. Attack SLOW (CW, ~1:00) so the pick transient stays uncompressed.
4. Release toggle RIGHT (Slow 1.5 s); Wet noon–1:00; Dry off; EQ noon.

## Notes
- The cleanest banjo-as-lead base — preserves instrument energy/attack.
- A good "live"/MIDDLE base to build on before adding the banjo hidden-tweaks of Patch 5 (Banjo Transient Tamer).
- All dips OFF.
