# Tight & Lively

## Concept
The maker's quoted use: "Chords or fast and dense playing (e.g. funk)." A medium ~4:1 compression with swift attack and release that absorbs everything except the first bit of pick attack, then releases quickly to avoid an overly squished/static sound. The bread-and-butter everyday comp — good for chords, fast dense playing, tightening the baritone before the Colour Box, and rhythmic banjo strumming.

## How to play it
1. **Set Sensitivity by ear to the left LED first**, per instrument.
2. Dynamics ~noon-ish into the ~4:1 zone (just below noon).
3. Attack FAST (CCW); Release toggle LEFT (Fast 50 ms).
4. Wet ~noon (unity); Dry off; EQ noon.
