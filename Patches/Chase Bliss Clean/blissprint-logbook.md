# Blissprint Logbook (preset tracking)

## Concept
Not a sound — a slot/recall strategy. Clean has only 2 onboard preset slots plus a middle "live" position, and the single-LED, detent-free interface means every other recipe must be remembered "by feel." This patch is the community workaround: commit the three keepers to slots and log everything else's knob/toggle/dip positions in a preset logbook (Blissprint or any tool).

## How to play it
1. Commit the 3 keepers to slots:
   - **LEFT = Home Base (Patch 1)**
   - **RIGHT = Dusty Pre-Dirt (Patch 8)**
   - **MIDDLE/live = a creative keeper** (Motion Pad / Sag / banjo-set, your call)
2. **Save** = hold the target footswitch ~3 s, then add the other footswitch ~3 s.
3. Log every other patch's knob/toggle/dip positions in Blissprint (or any preset logbook) — the pedal has no recallable detents and Sensitivity is re-set per instrument anyway.
4. MIDI ch.2 PC recalls the onboard slot inside a whole-stack CB scene.

## Notes
- A fresh web pass found **no new dial recipes** — the community dials Clean "by feel" and uses logbook tools to remember settings.
- Treat your own logged numbers as the real "presets."
- Slot/Bank: N/A — this *is* the slot strategy.
