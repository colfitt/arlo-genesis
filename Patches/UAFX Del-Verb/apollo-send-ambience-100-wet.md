# Apollo Send Ambience (100% Wet)

## Concept
The Del-Verb's quiet studio superpower. Fed from an Apollo aux/parallel send (NOT in the main series chain), both wet knobs at full clockwise = kill-dry: a pure 100%-wet stereo return, with the dry left untouched on the console. Track the dry into the x8, monitor wet through the pedal, then extend/automate in the box with the matching UAD twin.

## How to play it
1. Feed from an Apollo aux/parallel send — not the main series board.
2. Set both wet knobs to full clockwise: Reverb knob full CW, delay Mix full CW (kill-dry = 100% wet).
3. Return the two TS outputs to two Apollo line inputs (Output 2 = stereo R) for a true-stereo wet capture.
4. Sync delay Time to MIDI Beat Clock (or set dotted-eighth) and dial Feedback to taste.
5. Extend / automate in the box afterward with the matching UAD twin: Hall 224 → Lexicon 224, Plate → EMT 140 (these HAVE the decay/tone/pre-delay the pedal's hardware lacks).

## Notes
- Not signature-fit — pure studio utility.
- **CAUTION:** on a normal SERIES board, full-CW Mix/Reverb mutes your dry tone. Kill-dry is only desirable on a parallel send.
