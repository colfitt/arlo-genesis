# Carry-on Shimmer Wash (Shoegaze)

## Concept
Same patch family as the Doom version of the travel wash — swap only the Hall voicing (bright "Cascade 224" instead of dark "Dark N Dusty Trail") and nudge Color brighter. The result is a huge, sparkling bloom over a chord wall: the shoegaze making-aesthetic rather than the doom one.

## How to play it
1. Pre-assign in the app: Reverb toggle "Cascade 224" on Hall 224, Delay toggle "Stereo Ping Pong Hi-Cut" on Precision.
2. Run the reverb as the always-on wash (Reverb knob 1:00).
3. Left foot drops the delay; right foot taps the dotted-eighth to tempo.
4. Strum and let the bright hall bloom over the top.

## Notes
- **You can only hold ONE Hall voicing at a time** — a voicing rides with the toggle and is stored in the pedal. Pick Doom (Dark N Dusty Trail) or Shoegaze (Cascade 224) before the gig; they cannot both be live.
- Keep Mix and Reverb below full CW on a series board (full CW = kill-dry).
