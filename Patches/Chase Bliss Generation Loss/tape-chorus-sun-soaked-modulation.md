# Tape Chorus / "Sun-Soaked" Modulation

## Concept
A wide, vintage, random-rubbery chorus on banjo/strings without dropout or destruction. "Wow is your key to unique chorus… random and rubbery" (manual pg.17). The **Unity-dry** setting is the load-bearing requirement — at None/Small there's no clean signal to chorus against.

## How to play it
1. Set the **Dry toggle = Unity** so the clean path blends in for chorusing.
2. Turn **Wow up** (it drives the random-rubbery chorus); keep Flutter low.
3. Keep Failure off and Saturate low; pick a Model to taste.

## Notes
- Not signature-fit — it's the pretty end of the pedal — but an essential classic voice.
- Dry **Unity** is non-negotiable here: without a clean blend there's nothing to chorus.
- Store as the chorus scene.
