# Filtered Lo-Fi / "AM Radio" Small-Speaker Bed

## Concept
A dark, narrow, mid-forward wash under everything. The dictaphone/toy models (7–9) roll off both extremes for an AM/telephone/small-speaker voice — the closest thing to an "AM radio" recipe (built from band-limiting models, not a named preset). Mid-forward and narrow, it sits *under* a mix rather than over it, and lets the H90 reverb bloom on top.

## How to play it
1. Pick **Model 7–8** (Dictatron-EX/IN) or **9** (Fishy 60 toy).
2. Keep Wow/Flutter/Saturate low and Failure low — the band-limiting is doing the work.
3. Set Dry **Small** and run it as a pad/ambient bed, with a big reverb downstream.

## Notes
- Pad/ambient bed material; pairs with a big reverb downstream (H90).
- Store as the ambient-bed scene.
