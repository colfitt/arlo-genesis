# Brittle Mid-Forward AM-Jangle Bed

## Concept
Modest Mouse brittle mid-forward jangle, narrow/small-speaker character. The band-limiting models (7–9) roll off both extremes → a thin, mid-forward, small-speaker voice that keeps the jangle BRITTLE and lets a light reverb bloom on top. Mid-forward + narrow = sits under a mix, the broken-radio jangle bed.

## How to play it
1. Pick **Model 7–8** (Dictatron-EX/IN) or **9** (Fishy 60 toy).
2. Wow ~9:00, Flutter ~8:00, Saturate ~10:00, Failure low, Dry **Small**.
3. Let a light reverb bloom on top.
