# Dying Cassette / Seasick Wow-Flutter

## Concept
A drunken, half-speed melted-tape drone — let chords ring and the warble does the rest. Model 5's half-speed character is especially evocative for slow drone banjo. Dry set to **None** means full wet, so the tape voice dominates entirely. This is the doom-ambient move into a Big Time long delay + H90 hall.

## How to play it
1. Recall onboard preset **R** (the "destroyed" counterpart to slot L).
2. Hold chords and let them ring — the seasick wow/flutter does the work.
3. Feed it into Big Time on a long delay and an H90 hall for the doom-ambient wall.

## Notes
- The pitch-warp can get nauseating — use this as a section, not a whole song.
- Dry **None** is load-bearing here: it removes the clean blend so the melted tape is all you hear.
- Store to onboard preset **R** (the destroyed counterpart to the "Subtle Tape Age" home base in L).
