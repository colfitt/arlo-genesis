# Baritone Drone — Low-Retention

## Concept
Baritone weight kept intact while degraded. The VHS models (1–3) hold more low end for a heavy, worn drone bed under the doom wall; M-PEX (12) is the cleanest/biggest "tape" when you want low-end size over destruction. VHS gives more color and dropout potential.

## How to play it
1. Pick **Model 1–3** (VHS) for color and dropout, or **Model 12** (M-PEX) for clean low-end size.
2. Keep **Saturate modest** — bass-heavy material flubs if pushed.
3. Set Dry to **Small** or **Unity** to preserve the fundamental, and add a touch of Wow/Flutter/Failure.
