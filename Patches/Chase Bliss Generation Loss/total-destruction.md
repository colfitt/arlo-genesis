# Total Destruction

## Concept
A nauseating-on-purpose full meltdown as a momentary build-climax — NOT a tone you sit on. Everything cranked, VHS models, hiss/noise on. The manual has no such page; this synthesizes the dossier's §3–4 destruction cautions.

## How to play it
1. Crank Wow and Flutter to 2:00+, Saturate and Failure to max, pick a VHS model (1–3).
2. Turn the **Noise toggle on** for hiss.
3. Use it as a single climax moment, then pull back — don't sit on it.
