# Andy Othling (Lowercase Noises) — texture generator into delay soup

## Concept
Gen Loss as a **texture GENERATOR** (not a tone-shaper) on a parallel send — a degraded source feeding two or three stacked delays for "a big soupy sound." Board-as-DAW with parallel chains and a volume pedal for swells. Loves the "very non-guitar, even non-musical" textural side.

## How to play it
1. **No published numeric settings** — this is a method/role.
2. Push **Model/Failure** for character.
3. Put Gen Loss on a parallel texture send → stacked delays (Big Time + others).
4. Bury it in the delay stack and **swell it in with a volume pedal**, engaged for ambient moments.

## Notes
- 🟢 *for the cited credit only* — the method (parallel texture send into stacked delays, swelled with a volume pedal) is directly portable to this rig's Big Time + Blooper/MOOD downstream.
