# Worn VHS / Dropout Chaos

## Concept
Skipping, dropping, stereo-scattered VHS destruction as a moment effect. Failure drops/snags fire and pan; the SPREAD dip reads Failure, so drops/snags scatter L↔R (best heard on headphones). With Aux=Stop you can punctuate with a tape-halt that the downstream Big Time catches and repeats.

## How to play it
1. Set **SPREAD dip = on** so Failure scatters across the stereo field.
2. Engage and let the VHS drops/snags fire — they're non-repeatable by design.
3. Use the **Aux=Stop** footswitch for tape-halt punctuation; the Big Time catches the punch-ins and repeats them.

## Notes
- **Non-repeatable by design, so record it** if you want the take.
- SPREAD is best appreciated on headphones to hear the L↔R scatter.
- Store as an onboard preset (it's a moment effect, not an always-on tone).
