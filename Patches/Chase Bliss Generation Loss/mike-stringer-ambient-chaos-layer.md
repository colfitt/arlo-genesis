# Mike Stringer (Spiritbox) — over-the-top ambient chaos layer

## Concept
A degraded ambient wash deployed **sparingly but heavily when on** — Gen Loss + MOOD + Microcosm on the "textures & ambience" top row, used over a drum-solo break (song "Crystal Roses"), NOT always-on. Structured ambient parts come from Microcosm/DL4; Gen Loss adds the chaos *over the top*. Convergent with Othling/St. Vincent: degraded texture + granular, engaged for moments.

## How to play it
1. **No published numeric settings** — this is a role + pairing map.
2. Build the sound from "Worn VHS / Dropout Chaos" (#7) or "Total Destruction" (#9).
3. Fire it into the loop trio (MOOD micro-loop + Microcosm granular) for moments, not as an always-on tone.
4. Recall a "chaos" onboard preset with the MOOD/Microcosm scene.
