# Maintenance Phase (ever-changing malfunction)

## Concept
An ever-evolving, never-repeating "broken" texture. Bouncing Failure means the dropout/snag character morphs continuously — captured into a looper it becomes a living degraded bed. The ideal moving source to feed Blooper/MOOD loops.

## How to play it
1. Enable the **FAILURE** ramp-target dip.
2. Enable the **BOUNCE** dip.
3. The **Volume knob = ramp speed** while ramping.
4. **Save to a preset** so the moving patch recalls.

## Notes
- Captured into a looper it becomes a living degraded bed.
- The preset saves the *moving* patch (evolving-texture scene).
