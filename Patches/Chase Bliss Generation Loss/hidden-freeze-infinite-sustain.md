# Hidden FREEZE — infinite sustain you play OVER

## Concept
Hold the last sound indefinitely while you **play your wet signal OVER the frozen note** — the MkII uniquely allows this. It's a drone-and-solo bed, and the ideal source to seed the MOOD micro-loop / Microcosm granular (the Stringer trio) without playing. With the right dip combo (CLASSIC + DROP BYP + SNAG BYP) and Aux=Fail, the left footswitch fires Freeze instead of its normal job.

## How to play it
1. Set **Aux toggle = Fail**.
2. Set **CLASSIC dip = on**, **DROP BYP dip = on**, **SNAG BYP dip = on** — the left footswitch now fires **Freeze** (holds the current sound).
3. Set the hidden **Aux Onset** (Wow knob in the hidden menu) for how fast it grabs.
4. Seed a drone, freeze it, then solo banjo over the held bed.
