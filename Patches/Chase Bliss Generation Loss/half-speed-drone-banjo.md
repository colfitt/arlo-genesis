# Half-Speed Drone Banjo

## Concept
Banjo-as-lead rendered "recorded-wrong." The cassette models (4–5) roll off the piercing top, and the wow/flutter smears the staccato pluck attack into sustain. Band-limiting models 4–8 hit the percussive attack hardest. Into Big Time, the lead line blurs into its own echoes — the signature banjo-lead-in-a-wall texture.

## How to play it
1. Pick **Model 4** (Portamax-RT), or **5** (half-speed) for a slower drone.
2. **First** keep Wow/Flutter low so you can hear the model's EQ rolloff bite the attack.
3. **Then** push Wow (~10–11:00) and add light Flutter for the smear.
4. Recall slot L and nudge Wow up live as an alternative to a dedicated preset.
