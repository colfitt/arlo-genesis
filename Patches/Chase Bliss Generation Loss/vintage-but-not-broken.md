# Vintage But Not Broken

## Concept
The manual's named "secret ingredient" (pg.19) — "a little Flutter with Wow off" gives tape quiver without the wobble. A barely-there liveness under an otherwise clean tone: vintage warmth, no warble, no dropout. This is the candidate to leave on beside CB Clean when you want air, not degrade.

## How to play it
1. Turn **Wow OFF** and nudge **Flutter** just a hair up.
2. Keep Saturate/Failure off or low; pick a Model to taste or leave it OFF.
3. Set Dry to **Small** or **Unity** and leave it running under your clean tone.

## Notes
- Not signature-fit — this is the clean end of the pedal — but it's the essential subtle move.
- Store as a global "subtle" base or an onboard preset.
