# Synth Self-Osc Glitch (Zachari Smith)

## Concept
The one-knob-move path to chaos for guitar and VG-800 synth patches. Pure noon + square = near-self-oscillating synth glitch — "it starts to modulate and almost self-oscillate a bit... synth-like effects... bleep bloop," his go-to "if I just want something a bit crazy."

## How to play it
1. All four knobs at 12:00 — same as True North.
2. Flip WAVE to square (the only move from the noon start) — TONE Bright.
3. It modulates and nearly self-oscillates into synth-like bleep-bloop.
4. Stab HAVOC to push the chaos further.

## Notes
- For the rig: route a VG-800 synth patch through it for sequenced/Electronic-cluster glitch, or stutter at phrase-ends into the CE-2W/Deco split.
- The whole patch is True North with WAVE flipped to square.
