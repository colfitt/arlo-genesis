# Personal Data Sold Online (glitch malfunction)

## Concept
A square-wave glitch/malfunction voice for guitar and VG-800 synth patches, used as phrase-end punctuation. The glitch comes from SQUARE + the OFFSET/MIX interaction, NOT from cranked depth — note the LOW DEPTH (the chart shows the needle near full-CCW). Factory row: "I guess this is what my personal data being sold online sounded like inside the fiberoptic cable."

## How to play it
1. MIX ~1:30 (mostly wet), OFFSET ~10:30.
2. Keep DEPTH LOW at ~7:30 (near full-CCW) — this is the push-and-pull rule in action.
3. SPEED ~12:00, WAVE square, TONE Bright.
4. Stab HAVOC for ray-gun bursts; use as a phrase-end stutter into the Deco.

## Notes
- LOW DEPTH is the key — the glitch is from square + OFFSET/MIX, not from cranked depth.
- Fits the Radiohead glitch-rhythm lean as a phrase-end stutter into the Deco.
