# Broken Cassette Vibrato (Guitar Chalk)

## Concept
A "worn-out tape deck" warble for guitar or baritone (lo-fi). Square + Dark + high DEPTH = the warble, mapping directly onto the rig's cassette-degrade goal. Grid scale: 5 ≈ noon, 10 ≈ max.

## How to play it
1. MIX ~3:00 (near-wet, 8/10), OFFSET ~8:00 (low, 2/10).
2. DEPTH ~4:00 (9/10), SPEED ~2:00 (7/10).
3. WAVE square, TONE Dark.
4. Verify by ear — this is an audition-only recipe.
