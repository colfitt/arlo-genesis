# Baritone Doom-Vibrato

## Concept
A post-punk drone-wall for sustained baritone drones (VG-800). A woozy Leslie-ish wobble under sustained baritone — lose the dry anchor on purpose and let it seesaw slowly. Mono Somersault feeding the stereo CE-2W = the widest "underwater" image.

## How to play it
1. MIX full-CW (5:00) — 100% wet drops the anchor for slow seesaw.
2. OFFSET ~10:00 (moderate) — keep it moderate on the lowest notes so the wobble doesn't disorient against the fundamental.
3. DEPTH ~1:00 (moderate-high), SPEED ~8:30 (very slow).
4. WAVE triangle, TONE Dark. Stab HAVOC for an occasional rotary lift.
