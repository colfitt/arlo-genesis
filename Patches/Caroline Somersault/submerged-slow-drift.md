# Submerged Slow Drift

## Concept
A shoegaze/dreampop bed for guitar and VG-800 pad — the closest published "underwater dream" recipe, a slow/deep/dark blend. Keep MIX a *blend* (chorus, not vibrato) so chords don't seesaw. A dreamwash bed under the wall. Grid scale: 5 ≈ noon.

## How to play it
1. MIX ~1:00 (blend, 6/10) — keep it a blend so chords stay anchored.
2. OFFSET ~11:00 (4/10), DEPTH ~3:30 (8/10).
3. SPEED ~8:00 (very slow, 2/10) for the drift.
4. WAVE triangle, TONE Dark for the underwater darkness.
