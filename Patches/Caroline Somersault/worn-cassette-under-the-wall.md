# Worn Cassette Under the Wall

## Concept
The "recorded-wrong" home turf for baritone/banjo drone (VG-800) into CE-2W → Deco V2. A mostly-wet seasick chorus, darkened, run into the Deco's tape saturation = pitch instability + tape grit compounding. Lo-fi + art-rock clusters (cassette degrade, tape flutter/wow).

## How to play it
1. MIX ~1:00 (mostly-wet blend), OFFSET ~3:00 (high), DEPTH ~12:00.
2. Deliberately set SPEED ~9:00 slower than the Deco's wow/flutter so the two LFOs drift/beat apart.
3. WAVE triangle, TONE Dark.
4. Stab HAVOC for tape-flutter bursts.

## Notes
- DESIGNED (not found).
- Pulling SPEED slower than the Deco wobble lets the LFOs drift apart for an organic, non-mechanical waver.
- Mono Somersault → stereo CE-2W → Deco V2 is the intended signal path.
