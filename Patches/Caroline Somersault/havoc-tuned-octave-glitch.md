# HAVOC Tuned-Octave Glitch

## Concept
A phrase-end fill gesture for guitar and VG-800 synth. The in-tune octave-down square artifact is REAL per AndyDemos ("a setting which gives me a lower octave despite where I have the SPEED set"), built on square + high DEPTH + low OFFSET. HAVOC is the only foot control: stab it for ray-gun/rotary bursts at phrase-ends; into the Deco it reads as glitchy tape-flutter. Safe — won't self-oscillate. Square + Dark sells the bleep-bloop.

## How to play it
1. MIX ~3:00 (mostly wet), OFFSET ~8:00 (low base), DEPTH ~4:30 (high), SPEED ~9:00 (slow base).
2. WAVE square, TONE Dark.
3. Experiment around square + DEPTH/OFFSET to land the in-tune octave-down artifact.
4. Hold HAVOC for the instant ray-gun/rotary speed-slam, release back to the slow base.
