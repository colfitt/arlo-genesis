# Default Adjustable Chorus (Zachari Smith)

## Concept
Same noon start as True North (demoed on a G&L ASAT), but with the documented Dark-toggle workflow. At noon with Bright TONE it's "a really great chorus"; Zachari Smith's frequent move is to flip TONE to Dark for a subtler, supportive chorus that sits under the mix.

## How to play it
1. All four knobs at 12:00, triangle WAVE, Bright TONE → a great general chorus.
2. When the chorus has to sit under a vocal or wall, flip TONE to Dark.
3. Reach for Dark whenever the chorus must stay underneath.

## Notes
- Dark is the chorus he reaches for most when supporting a vocal or wall.
- Identical knob start to True North — the difference is the TONE workflow.
