# Pretend It's the 1980s

## Concept
The under-quiet-passages chorus for guitar and VG-800 modeled cleans. Low OFFSET keeps it tasteful and tight — remember OFFSET fully CCW is the fastest delay / least warp. The essential classic, included for completeness.

## How to play it
1. MIX ~12:00 for a clear dry+wet blend, OFFSET pulled low to ~10:00 to stay tight.
2. DEPTH ~11:00, SPEED ~12:00, triangle WAVE, Bright TONE.
3. Use it under quiet passages where you want width without spectacle.

## Notes
- Low OFFSET keeps it tasteful (OFFSET fully CCW = fastest delay / least warp).
- In this rig, a subtle CE-2W behind it adds width without piling on character.
