# Vibrato / Leslie (AndyDemos method)

## Concept
The clean, documented vibrato for guitar or baritone. Counterintuitive but documented — you do NOT need high OFFSET for vibrato. Full-wet MIX is what makes it vibrato; keeping OFFSET CCW removes the laggy/disorienting feel. AndyDemos: "increase the MIX to 100%... I like to keep the OFFSET at its quickest setting to reduce any latency."

## How to play it
1. MIX full-CW (5:00) — 100% wet drops the dry anchor, so the pitch seesaws into true vibrato.
2. OFFSET full-CCW (7:00) to minimize latency and keep it clean.
3. DEPTH ~12:00 (raise for more), SPEED ~12:00, triangle WAVE.
4. TONE Bright (or Dark for darker). Stab HAVOC for rotary swells.
