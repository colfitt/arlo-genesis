# Seasick Warble (Yvette Young feel)

## Concept
A woozy doom/post-punk texture for guitar or banjo, built on OFFSET as the seasick/danger knob. Cranking OFFSET toward 2:00–3:00 gets "on a boat about to throw up"; pull back to ~9:00 for a subtle warble. MIX at 100% wet drops the dry anchor so the pitch material seesaws.

## How to play it
1. MIX full-CW (5:00) for full-wet vibrato.
2. Sweep OFFSET: ~2:00–3:00 for the strongest seasick warp, back to ~9:00 for subtle.
3. DEPTH ~12:00, SPEED ~11:00, triangle WAVE (her stated default).
4. TONE Bright or Dark to taste. HAVOC optional.

## Notes
- HONEST: this is a store DEMO placement, NOT a signature endorsement — the Somersault is not on her touring rig. Captured for her on-camera OFFSET-as-seasick-knob guidance only.
- The clock positions are DOUG-interpolated onto her by-feel description (she gave no numbers).
- The verified, attributable part is the OFFSET-sweep behavior: "you can make it a subtle warble, but you can also make it like you're on a boat about to throw up."
