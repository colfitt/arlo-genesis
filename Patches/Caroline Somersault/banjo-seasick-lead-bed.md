# Banjo Seasick Lead Bed

## Concept
A lead voice over a drone for banjo via GK-5 → VG-800 (indie-folk). Triangle + Dark hi-cut is essential on banjo — square + bright makes the fast banjo attack percussive/harsh. A wet-leaning blend gives a seasick waver while leaving enough pluck for the banjo to read as a lead inside the wall. Full-wet vibrato on a banjo drone is genuinely novel.

## How to play it
1. MIX ~2:00 (wet-leaning blend) — keeps some pluck reference so it reads as a lead.
2. OFFSET ~11:00, DEPTH ~11:00, SPEED ~10:00.
3. WAVE triangle (square gets choppy/harsh on banjo transients).
4. TONE Dark to tame the bright banjo top end. Stab HAVOC for a "ticking-clock" rhythmic spin.
