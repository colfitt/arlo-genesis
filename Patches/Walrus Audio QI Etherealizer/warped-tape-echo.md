# Warped Tape Echo (Guitar Chalk)

## Concept
Vintage, drifting repeats with subtle grain smear — a "warped tape" feel for the recorded-wrong aesthetic. The Qi delay is a clean digital mono line with NO tape/BBD voicing, so this recipe approximates "warped tape" via x0.5 grain + chorus, not via real tape character.

## How to play it
1. Dial the 0–10 settings above with Freeze OFF and Delay subdivision on QUARTER.
2. Play sustained phrases and let the x0.5 octave-down grain + chorus blur the repeats.

## Notes
- ⚠️ Suggested-not-verified — Guitar Chalk 0–10 recipe (Bobby Kittleberger).
- The Qi delay is a **CLEAN digital mono line with NO tape/BBD voicing** — this approximates "warped tape" via x0.5 grain + chorus, NOT via real tape character (the pedal has none).
- x0.5 octave-down is baritone-friendly.
