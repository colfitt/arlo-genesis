# Delay Self-Oscillation Snowball (Tap/Osc hold)

## Concept
Press-and-hold the Tap/Osc footswitch to drive Delay Feedback to maximum — the delay self-oscillates into a saturated snowball that builds over several seconds. This is the DELAY freeze gesture, distinct from grain and reverb freeze. It fits the sustained-crescendo aesthetic.

## How to play it
1. **Press-and-hold the Tap/Osc footswitch** = drives Delay Feedback to maximum.
2. The delay self-oscillates into a saturated snowball that builds over several seconds.
3. Release to let it decay.
4. Combine over a frozen grain + reverb for the galaxy-wash crescendo.

## Notes
- The DELAY freeze gesture (distinct from grain/reverb freeze).
- Momentary footswitch, not a knob runaway.
- Fits the sustained-crescendo making aesthetic.
