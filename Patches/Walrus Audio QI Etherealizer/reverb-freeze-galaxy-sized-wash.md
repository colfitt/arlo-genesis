# Reverb Freeze (long-press) — Galaxy-Sized Wash

## Concept
Long-press the center Freeze footswitch (over an existing grain freeze) to ramp the Space reverb to 100% (LED green), layering a frozen grain loop under a maxed wash. This is the "galaxy-sized stereo wash" — Reverb Freeze, gesture 3 of 3.

## How to play it
1. **Long-press the center Freeze footswitch** (over an existing grain freeze) = ramps Space reverb to 100% (LED green).
2. **Long-press AGAIN** releases the reverb ramp while the grain keeps looping.
3. A **short press** releases both.
4. You can go straight to the long-press by holding from off.

## Notes
- Reverb Freeze (gesture 3 of 3) — the "galaxy-sized stereo wash."
- Into the Dark Star, use deliberately: two maxed reverbs in series can mud.
