# Series Wet Wall (full-takeover ambient)

## Concept
A maxed all-wet ambient wall for when you want the Qi to BE the sound, not sit behind dry — then hand it to the tape stage. The fixed Series order (Chorus → Delay → Grain → Reverb → Tone) lets the Space swallow everything, which is the point here. The opposite of the Parallel default.

## How to play it
1. Set Flow to **Series** ("Forward").
2. Set Mix/Dry to **full wet**.
3. Dial Chorus noon (Tri), Delay noon (Feedback 1–2:00), Grain 1:00 (Grain Cloud, REV or x.5), Space 1–2:00.
4. Let the Space swallow everything; hand the wall to the tape stage.

## Notes
- The fixed Series order (Chorus → Delay → Grain → Reverb → Tone) lets the Space swallow everything — which is the point here.
- Use only when the Qi is the featured wall, not when stacking into the Dark Star (two big reverbs = mud).
