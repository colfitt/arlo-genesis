# Parallel Dry-Defined Bass/Baritone Grain

## Concept
Keep a defined low end (bass or baritone) dry at unity while layering wet grain/space on top — so the fundamental stays articulate under the texture. The Qi has no dedicated dry-bass preservation beyond Mix/Dry; Parallel-at-unity IS the trick.

## How to play it
1. Set Flow to **Parallel**.
2. Set **Mix/Dry near max** (dry at unity).
3. Layer Grain Mix ~noon (Grain Cloud, x1 or x.5 for weight).
4. Keep Space modest (9–10:00) and Tone open for definition.

## Notes
- The Qi has no dedicated dry-bass preservation beyond Mix/Dry — Parallel-at-unity IS the trick.
- Watch that Space doesn't turn low notes to wash; keep Tone open for definition.
