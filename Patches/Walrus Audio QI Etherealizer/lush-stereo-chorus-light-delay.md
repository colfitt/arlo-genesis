# Lush Stereo Chorus + Light Delay (rig "down-to-earth")

## Concept
Shimmer behind clean baritone arpeggios before the dirt boards engage — the useful everyday tone. The clean pole of the rig: off-aesthetic for drone/doom but documented as the rig's "useful" tone.

## How to play it
1. Set Flow to **Series**.
2. Play clean baritone arpeggios under the stereo chorus.
3. Light delay (dotted-eighth, low feedback) adds motion without dominating.
