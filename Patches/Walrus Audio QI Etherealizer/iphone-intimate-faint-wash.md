# iPhone-Intimate Faint Wash

## Concept
The Carrie & Lowell close/dry voice balance — texture sits faintly BEHIND a present source (halo, not hall). The restraint patch: keep the dry voice forward with a faint grain wash and a small Space, matching the documented C&L "close, dry, space-added-faintly" balance (Ref: Sufjan 'Carrie & Lowell' intimate voice; Bon Iver close-mic philosophy).

## How to play it
1. Set Flow to **Parallel**, Mix/Dry HIGH (mostly dry — voice stays close/present).
2. Add faint grain (Mix ~10:00, Grain Cloud, x1) and low chorus (~9:00).
3. Set Space to ~9:00 (a halo, not a hall); Tone ~11:00.
4. The texture sits behind a dry-forward source.

## Notes
- 🟣⭐ The halo-not-hall move; keeps the dry voice forward (matches Bon Iver/Sufjan/Big Thief intimacy).
