# Grain Freeze (short-press) — Hypnotic Texture Loop

## Concept
Short-press the center Freeze footswitch to freeze the grain buffer; a captured slice loops indefinitely (LED blue) — "instantly creating a hypnotic loop of pure texture." This is Grain Freeze, gesture 2 of 3, and the core mechanic behind RED/BLUE and the rig freeze pads.

## How to play it
1. **Short-press the center Freeze footswitch** = freezes the grain buffer; the captured slice loops indefinitely (LED blue).
2. Play over it.
3. Change the **Playback** mode (x1/x2/x.5/REV/RNDM) LIVE while frozen for evolving motion.
4. Short-press again releases.
5. Long-press **Bypass** clears a stale grain buffer.
