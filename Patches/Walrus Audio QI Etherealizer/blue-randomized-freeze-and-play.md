# BLUE — Randomized Freeze-and-Play

## Concept
Freeze an unpredictable/randomized grain moment and improvise over it — the preset designed to "take the control away from you" and generate surprises. RNDM reverse/octave grains make a strong "recorded-wrong" character source. Verbatim manual description: "Great for randomized moments to freeze and play over."

## How to play it
1. Set Flow to **Parallel**.
2. Play with the Playback rotary on **RNDM** so grains randomize reverse/octave moments.
3. **Short-press Freeze** on a randomized grain moment you like.
4. Improvise over the frozen result.

## Notes
- RNDM reverse/octave grains = a strong "recorded-wrong" character source.
- Don't run its RNDM/REV against the Microcosm's glitch simultaneously (mud).
