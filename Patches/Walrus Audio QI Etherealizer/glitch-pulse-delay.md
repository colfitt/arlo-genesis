# Glitch Pulse Delay (Guitar Chalk)

## Concept
More aggressive, rhythmic grain stutters with synced delay pulses. A third-party suggested starting point for tempo-married glitch motion.

## How to play it
1. Dial the 0–10 settings above with Freeze OFF and Delay subdivision on EIGHTH.
2. To MIDI-clock the grains to the rig, switch grain to **Phrase Sample** and set **X to minimum** (X-min locks Phrase Sample to Delay time).
3. Let the Microcosm own rhythm and keep the Qi for texture — OR run this WITHOUT the Microcosm to avoid double-glitch mud.

## Notes
- ⚠️ Suggested-not-verified — Guitar Chalk 0–10 recipe (Bobby Kittleberger).
- X-min trick: in Phrase Sample, X at minimum locks grains to the Delay time.
- Don't double the Microcosm's glitch.
