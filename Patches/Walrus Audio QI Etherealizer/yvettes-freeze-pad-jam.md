# Yvette's Freeze-Pad Jam (designer workflow)

## Concept
Yvette Young's documented core technique: build a granular texture, capture it, and improvise a lead over the self-sustaining bed. In Parallel your live playing stays unaffected on top of the frozen wall. Best anchored by the RED preset. Her words: "an instant vibe and mood machine that would... generate pads and things you could jam over by yourself."

## How to play it
1. Build a granular texture (**Grain Cloud** or **Phrase Sample**).
2. **Short-press Freeze** to latch the grain buffer (LED blue).
3. Improvise a tapped/fingerstyle lead over the looping pad.
4. In **Parallel**, your live playing stays unaffected on top of the frozen wall.
5. Optional: long-press Freeze ramps Space to 100% under the loop.
