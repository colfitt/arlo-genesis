# Soft-Source Granulation (baritone/sine-friendly)

## Concept
Granulating softer, fewer-harmonic tones — sine/triangle-leaning material, baritone drones, banjo with Tone rolled back — which a community user theorized granulate especially nicely. The grain engine alone sounds a bit weird; combining it with delay or reverb is what unleashes its true beauty. Directly relevant to baritone-drone material.

## How to play it
1. Feed a soft/low-harmonic source: baritone drone, sine/triangle synth, or banjo with Tone rolled back.
2. Set grain mode to **Grain Cloud** or **Phrase Sample**, Grain Mix to taste.
3. Blend into **Space** — never solo the grain.
4. Use **Tone** low-pass to strip "guitar-ness."
5. Keep Flow on **Parallel**.
