# Glitch-Cloud Texture (over the Microcosm's rhythm)

## Concept
Sprinkle reverse/octave grains over the Microcosm's rhythmic granular motion without doubling the glitch. A division-of-labor patch: the Microcosm drives the rhythm/motion, and the Qi sprinkles freeze/smear reverse-octave grains over it.

## How to play it
1. Set Flow to **Parallel**.
2. Let the Microcosm drive the rhythm.
3. The Qi sprinkles sparse, surprising reverse/octave grains over it (Playback RNDM, X ~1:00).

## Notes
- Division-of-labor patch (Microcosm = motion, Qi = freeze/smear).
- Don't run both engines hot or it muds.
