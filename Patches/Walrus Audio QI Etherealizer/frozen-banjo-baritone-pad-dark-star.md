# Frozen Banjo/Baritone Pad → Dark Star

## Concept
Turn a banjo or baritone chord into a sustained source that the Dark Star V3 then turns into space — this rig's headline use of the Qi (slot 4 of Board 2). Keep Space modest so the Dark Star owns the actual reverb; the Qi just makes the held, evolving pad.

## How to play it
1. Set Flow to **Parallel**.
2. Hit a banjo or baritone chord.
3. **Short-press Freeze** to loop the grain buffer.
4. Play a banjo lead over the loop while feeding the held pad into the Dark Star.

## Notes
- Use the **Tone** LPF to tame the banjo 2–4 kHz spike before it grains.
- Banjo's fast bright transients are ideal **Phrase Sample** peak-fodder if you switch grain modes.
- Space kept at 10–11:00 (modest) so the Dark Star owns the space — don't stack two big reverbs.
- RIG-CRITICAL: pad/reamp the VG-800 line level into the Qi (no level switch — it clips hot).
- Serves "banjo-as-lead over sustained walls" directly.
