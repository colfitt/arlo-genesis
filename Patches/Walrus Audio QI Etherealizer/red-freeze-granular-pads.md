# RED — Freeze / Granular Pads

## Concept
The headline freeze-pad-and-jam workflow: dial a chord into a granular cloud, latch it with the Freeze switch, and improvise a lead over the self-sustaining loop. This is the anchor for the frozen-banjo/baritone pad and the rig's bread-and-butter feed into the Dark Star. Verbatim manual description: "Great for freezing & making granular pads."

## How to play it
1. Set Flow to **Parallel** so your live playing rides on top of the frozen wall.
2. Dial a chord with the grain engine in **Grain Cloud**.
3. **Short-press Freeze** to loop the grain buffer (LED blue).
4. Play a lead over the looping pad.

## Notes
- The anchor preset for frozen banjo/baritone pads.
- Restored by factory reset (hold Bypass+Freeze on power-up).
- ⚠️ Knob positions are read from the manual diagram (approximate clock-face); the text descriptions are verbatim manual quotes, but the numbers are diagram-read, not Walrus-published.
