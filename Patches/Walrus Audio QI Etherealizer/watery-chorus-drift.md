# Watery Chorus Drift (Guitar Chalk)

## Concept
Clean, floating chorus with subtle granular wandering — a third-party suggested tone. Deep chorus (Depth 8) with grains barely present; the clean/utility lane and the rig's "down-to-earth" pole.

## How to play it
1. Dial the 0–10 settings above with Freeze OFF.
2. Play clean chords and let the deep chorus float, with grains barely present (RNDM, Grain Mix 3).

## Notes
- ⚠️ Suggested-not-verified — Guitar Chalk 0–10 recipe (Bobby Kittleberger).
- Deep chorus (Depth 8) with grains barely present — the clean/utility lane; lower drone/doom fit (the "down-to-earth" pole).
