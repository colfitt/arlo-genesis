# Everything-at-Noon + 2x (owner quick-start)

## Concept
The fastest hands-off way to a usable ambient sound — the canonical owner starting point. Put every knob at noon, set grain playback to x2 for a +1-octave shimmer, tap your BPM, and dial to taste.

## How to play it
1. Put EVERY knob at **NOON** (12 o'clock).
2. Set Grain Playback to **x2** (+1-octave shimmer).
3. Tap-tempo to your BPM and dial to taste.
4. **Pull Space back** from noon if it's mushing the delay.

## Notes
- Widely repeated owner recipe (re-encountered in Sweetwater owner reviews).
- Generic, not aesthetic-specific — with all engines hot it WILL mud; back Space and Grain off for the rig.
