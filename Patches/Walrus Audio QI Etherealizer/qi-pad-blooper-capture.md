# QI Pad → Blooper Capture (End-Board layering)

## Concept
Use a frozen Qi grain pad as a sustained source for the Blooper on Board 3 to loop and layer — the drone/loop aesthetic. The Qi makes the source; the Blooper/End Board prints it.

## How to play it
1. On the Qi, build any freeze-pad patch (e.g. Frozen Banjo/Baritone Pad).
2. **Short-press Freeze** to hold a grain pad (Space modest).
3. Feed the held pad downstream to the Blooper.
4. Capture it into the Blooper loop and layer.

## Notes
- The Qi makes the *source*; the Blooper/End Board *prints* it.
- Serves the layered-drone making aesthetic.
