# GREEN — "THE Chorus Tone"

## Concept
Yvette's flagship/favorite chorus — the "down-to-earth, always useful" lush voicing with the grain engine essentially off. Her stated target is the Roland JC-40 / JC-120. The chorus is the dominant engine here; this is the clean/utility pole of the three factory presets. Verbatim manual description: "THE Chorus tone."

## How to play it
1. Set Flow to **Parallel**.
2. Play clean chords or arpeggios — the lush Tri chorus carries the voicing.
3. Tap tempo does NOT affect chorus rate (intentional), so set Rate by ear.

## Notes
- The clean/utility pole — lowest drone/doom fit of the three, but documented as Yvette's signed-off favorite.
- Tap tempo does NOT affect chorus rate (intentional).
