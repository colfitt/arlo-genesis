# QI-PP3 — BC,NR Sustained Droning-Intro Freeze Pad

## Concept
A frozen chamber-rock bed for the BC,NR minute-long droning intro — fills melodic space (Luke's "filling out another melodic space") under a restrained brittle texture (108-PP2), before the slab kicks in (Ref: BC,NR 'Sunglasses' 2021 minute-long droning intro).

## How to play it
1. Set Flow to **Parallel**, grain to **Grain Cloud** (Mix ~1:00, Playback x.5 or x1).
2. Add subtle Tri chorus (~10:00); Delay off or ~9:00; Space ~11:00 (modest); Tone ~12:00.
3. Hit a sustained chord → **short-press Freeze** → hold the looping pad as the droning intro.

## Notes
- 🟣 Distinct from the Dark Star's octave-down drone (DS-PP3) — run one or the other, or layer them quietly.
- RIG: pad/reamp the VG-800 line into the Qi.
- APPROXIMATION: BC,NR's intro is a sustained guitar drone into Orange amps; this freezes a grain pad to occupy that space.
