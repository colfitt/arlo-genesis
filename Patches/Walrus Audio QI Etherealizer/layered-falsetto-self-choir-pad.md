# Layered-Falsetto Self-Choir Pad

## Concept
Emulate Bon Iver's stacked-SM7 falsetto self-choir — one source thickened into "many voices." The unison-x1 grain plus heavy chorus is the literal stand-in for "stack falsetto layers": looping unison-grain + heavy chorus = a self-choir of one source (Ref: Bon Iver stacked-SM7/SM57 layered falsetto, For Emma / 22 A Million).

## How to play it
1. Set Flow to **Parallel**, grain to **Grain Cloud** (Mix noon, Playback x1 = unison thickening, NOT an interval).
2. Push the Tri chorus dominant (Mix ~1:00, Rate ~10, Depth ~1:00 for lush detune).
3. **Freeze a sung/banjo chord** → looping unison-grain + heavy chorus = a self-choir of one source.
