# Freeze Pad Texture (Guitar Chalk)

## Concept
Long, lush pads built from the Freeze switch with dense space and smooth grain clouds — the closest community analogue to the RED factory pad. Reverse grains + Freeze + a sustained source is the biggest drone-wall fit, ideal as a Blooper/tape-stage feed.

## How to play it
1. Dial the 0–10 settings above with Freeze ON.
2. Hold a sustained source and let the reverse grain cloud loop.
3. Hand the wall downstream to the Blooper or tape stage.

## Notes
- ⚠️ Suggested-not-verified — Guitar Chalk recipes are third-party SUGGESTED on a 0–10 scale, NOT hardware-verified (Bobby Kittleberger / guitarchalk.com).
- CAUTION: **Space 10 is full-tilt** — drop Space hard (~10–11 o'clock equivalent) if feeding the Dark Star so you don't stack two huge reverbs into featureless cloud.
- Biggest drone-wall fit; reverse grains + Freeze + sustained source = ideal Blooper/tape-stage feed.
