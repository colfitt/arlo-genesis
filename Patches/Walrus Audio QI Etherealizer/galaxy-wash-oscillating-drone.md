# Galaxy Wash / Oscillating Drone ("let the Qi take over")

## Concept
Max ambient: a frozen grain under a maxed reverb wash with an oscillating delay — the post-punk/doom sustained-crescendo move. This is the "let the Qi take over" pole. Print the result to the End Board. The x.5 (−1 oct) grain serves baritone weight, and Tone-by-hand is the expression substitute since there's no EXP jack.

## How to play it
1. Set Flow to **Series**.
2. **Long-press Freeze** to ramp Space to 100% over a frozen grain.
3. **Hold Tap/Osc** to oscillate the delay (saturated snowball).
4. Control with proximity and the **Tone** knob by hand.
5. Hand the result to the End Board.

## Notes
- The "let the Qi take over" pole.
- x.5 (−1 oct) grain serves baritone weight.
- Tone-by-hand is the expression substitute (no EXP jack).
