# QI-PP1 — Swans Galaxy-Wash Crescendo (freeze + oscillate)

## Concept
The Swans "rise from near-silence to a pulverising crescendo" — capture a drone, freeze it, ramp Space to 100%, then oscillate the delay into a saturated snowball that builds over several seconds. The "let the Qi take over" pole; hand the result to the End Board (Ref: Swans 'Bring the Sun'/'The Seer').

## How to play it
1. Set Flow to **Series** (Grain ~2:00 Phrase Sample, Playback x.5, Delay noon, Feedback ~2:00, Space ~2–3:00, Mix/Dry full wet).
2. **Short-press Freeze** (grain loop, the barely-audible start).
3. **Long-press Freeze** (Space to 100%).
4. **Hold Tap/Osc** (saturated snowball crescendo).
5. Control with the Tone knob by hand + proximity.
