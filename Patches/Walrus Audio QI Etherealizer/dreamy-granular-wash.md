# Dreamy Granular Wash (Guitar Chalk)

## Concept
A smooth ambient pad with drifting grains and soft chorus — a third-party suggested starting point. REVERSE grains plus high Space make for a strong shoegaze fit.

## How to play it
1. Dial the 0–10 settings above with Freeze OFF.
2. Play sustained chords and let the reverse grains drift under the soft chorus.
3. For this rig into the Dark Star, pull Space back so the reverb doesn't dominate.

## Notes
- ⚠️ Suggested-not-verified, not a factory preset — Guitar Chalk 0–10 recipe (Bobby Kittleberger).
