# Tone-Sweep Movement on a Frozen Pad (hands-free expression)

## Concept
Add movement to an endlessly looping ambience without an expression pedal — the Qi has no EXP jack. Freeze a grain pad, then sweep the resonant Tone low-pass by hand for dramatic shifts in and out of darkness over the looping bed. Tone is a resonant low-pass on the WET path only.

## How to play it
1. Freeze a grain pad (**short-press Freeze**).
2. Start **Tone** fully OPEN.
3. Sweep/yank Tone DOWN by hand for dramatic shifts in/out of darkness over the looping bed.
4. Sweep back open for motion.

## Notes
- guitar.com: Tone "yanked down for dramatic shifts... particularly effective when you want to add some movement to an endlessly looping ambience."
- The hands-free expression workaround; continuous control otherwise = MIDI CC only.
- Tone is a resonant low-pass on the WET path only.
