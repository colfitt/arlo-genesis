# Dance-Yrself-Clean Cued-Explosion Session

## Concept
Staging the LCD Soundsystem withhold-then-explosion — a sparse quiet vamp held for minutes, then a cued drop slams the full groove in on the downbeat. The explosion is arrangement, not a filter sweep: a sparse Part holds for ~3 minutes, then a cued Session/pattern switch brings the full drop in on the boundary. SL is clock master driving the Elektrons/MPC.

## How to play it
1. Standalone Session.
2. Part 1 = the sparse quiet vamp (held organ/baritone drone + a tapped-percussion pattern, low density, sustained ~3 min).
3. Part 2 = the full drop (distorted VG-800 saw arp + full kick groove).
4. Use Sequence Transpose / a cued Session or pattern switch at the boundary to slam Part 2 in on the downbeat (the explosion is arrangement, not a filter sweep).
5. SL = clock master driving the Elektrons/MPC.

## Notes
- 61SL-E2.
- Sessions = 8 Parts x 8 patterns; Save-Lock the ones you keep.
