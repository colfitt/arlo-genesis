# Vintage CE-2 Match

## Concept
Dials the CE-2W to mimic an original black-label CE-2 — the documented A/B sweet spot for the warm vintage Standard voicing. The reviewer found the reissue needs DEPTH ~2:00 to equal an original CE-2 sitting at 1:00, so dial a hair higher than you'd expect to match.

## How to play it
1. Set MODE to Standard (switch left, CE-2 voicing).
2. RATE to 11:00, DEPTH to 2:00.
3. Use OUTPUT A only for the authentic mono CE-2, or A+B to add the stereo the original never had.

## Notes
- The DEPTH ~2:00 figure is the community A/B match — dial a hair higher than expected to equal an original CE-2 at 1:00.
- Community-cited A/B, not artist. Essential vintage reference; lower signature-fit.
