# Banjo Wide-Spread (Banjo-as-Lead)

## Concept
Widens the bright, transient-heavy banjo (via VG-800) without exaggerating the attack, so it spreads across the stereo field once the Microcosm/Dark Star get hold of it downstream. Banjo carries the lead line inside the wall. Standard mode widens without exaggerating the transient.

## How to play it
1. Set MODE to Standard (switch left, CE-2 voicing).
2. RATE to 9:30 (slow, doesn't fight the pluck), DEPTH to 11:00 (subtle so the attack stays clear).
3. Route OUTPUT A+B wet/dry split feeding stereo Board 2.

## Notes
- ⭐ Signature: indie-folk banjo-bed / banjo-as-lead.
- DESIGNED — no published banjo-chorus settings.
- CE-1 Vibrato on banjo gets seasick fast — keep that for drone passages only, not rhythmic banjo.
