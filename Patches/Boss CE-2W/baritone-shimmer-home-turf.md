# Baritone Shimmer (Home Turf)

## Concept
Adds classic shimmer to the baritone (via VG-800) without thinning the low end — the gentle BBD top-roll flatters the darker voice. Modest DEPTH so the lows don't pitch-warble into mud. The dry B leg is the low-end anchor — the same logic that makes the wet/dry split better than a fully-wet chorus on bass.

## How to play it
1. Set MODE to Standard (switch left, CE-2 voicing).
2. RATE to 10:00, DEPTH to 11:00 (kept modest to protect the fundamental).
3. Route OUTPUT A+B wet/dry split (OUTPUT B keeps a dry low-end anchor under the modulation).
4. If lows still thin in Standard, switch to CE-1 Chorus (documented as having less low-end loss) at the same dials.
