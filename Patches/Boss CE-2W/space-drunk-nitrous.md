# Space-Drunk Nitrous (Subtle Pitch Shimmer)

## Concept
Barely-there pitch shimmer — a "space-drunk, nitrous" detune that sits close to a second chorus voice without obvious wobble. A subtler alternative to Deep Vibrato for ambient/indie-folk beds where the motion should be felt, not heard.

## How to play it
1. Set MODE to CE-1 Vibrato (switch right).
2. RATE to 8:00 (low), DEPTH to 9:00 (low).
3. Use OUTPUT A only for wet-only mono shimmer, or A+B for the wide wet/dry spread.

## Notes
- ⭐ Signature: indie-folk/art-rock subliminal detune under the wall.
- DESIGNED — Premier Guitar names the "space-drunk, nitrous" texture but gives no numbers; the low/low clock positions are DOUG's inference.
