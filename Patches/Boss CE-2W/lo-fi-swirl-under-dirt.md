# Lo-Fi Swirl Under Dirt

## Concept
Thicker chorus with enough movement to sit under gain — '90s alt/grunge swirl that survives a saturated wall. For the lo-fi cluster (Car Seat Headrest / Modest Mouse) with Hizumitas/Colour Box dirt upstream. Standard mode keeps movement legible under saturation where CE-1's deeper mod would smear into mud.

## How to play it
1. Set MODE to Standard (switch left, CE-2 voicing).
2. RATE to 10:00, DEPTH to 2:00.
3. Use OUTPUT A only into the dirt for mono thickness, or A+B for stereo width post-wall.

## Notes
- ⭐ Signature: lo-fi / "recorded-wrong" lane.
- Clock positions translate Guitar Chalk's "Mid-Gain Grunge/Alt Rock Swirl" (RATE 4 / DEPTH 7 on 0–10 → 10:00 / 2:00).
