# 100% Wet (Underwater)

## Concept
Pure modulated chorus with no dry mixed back in — much more dramatic/"underwater" for a wet-only patch into the Deco. The fully-wet voice when you want all modulation, no anchor.

## How to play it
1. Set MODE to CE-1 Chorus (middle) for wet chorus, or CE-1 Vibrato (right) for inherently dry-free pitch.
2. RATE to 10:00, DEPTH to 2:00.
3. Route OUTPUT A → Deco (wet only) with a dead/dummy plug in OUTPUT B to force OUTPUT A to 100% wet. (In the rig, B already feeds Deco R, so A is already pure-wet.)

## Notes
- ⭐ Signature: drone/shoegaze fully-wet wall.
- The dummy-plug routing is the load-bearing "control" here — plugging anything into OUTPUT B flips A from mixed to wet-only.
- CE-1 Vibrato needs no trick (dry already removed).
