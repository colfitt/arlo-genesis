# Icy Undulation (Fast + Deep)

## Concept
Fast RATE + high DEPTH for icy, regular undulations — a colder, more agitated modulation for brittle post-punk texture or a tense build. The opposite pole from the slow oceanic beds.

## How to play it
1. Set MODE to Standard (switch left) for icy clarity, or CE-1 Chorus (middle) for darker intensity.
2. RATE to 2:30 (fast), DEPTH to 2:30 (high).
3. Use OUTPUT A+B split.

## Notes
- ⭐ Signature: post-punk brittle-texture / dynamic-build lane.
- DESIGNED — Premier Guitar names the "icy/regular undulation" character but gives no numbers; the fast/deep clock positions are DOUG's inference.
- Trim before the signature beds if a hard 12-cap is needed.
