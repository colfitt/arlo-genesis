# CE-1 Wall of Sound (Two-Amp Studio)

## Concept
Boss's own "living wall of sound" two-amp stereo recording technique — deeper pitch modulation, slightly darker character, hard-pan the wet/dry legs, leave a dry track up the middle. The drone/shoegaze foundation feeding the Deco + Board 2. The divergent wet/dry pair feeds the Deco's tape sim two different inputs → free degraded asymmetry (the channels evolve differently under the Deco's wobble/saturation).

## How to play it
1. Set MODE to CE-1 Chorus (middle).
2. RATE to 10:00, DEPTH to 2:00.
3. Route OUTPUT A (wet) → amp/Deco L and OUTPUT B (dry) → amp/Deco R, hard-panned.
4. Optionally add a 3rd dry track up the middle.
