# Pretenders Jangle (80s Chorus)

## Concept
Bright, pronounced '80s jangle-pop chorus for rhythmic arpeggios that cut through — the Pretenders flavor of the classic compact CE-2. Clean, mid-forward, rhythmic. Use the A+B split for the wide '80s stereo spread.

## How to play it
1. Set MODE to Standard (switch left, CE-2 voicing).
2. RATE to 1:00, DEPTH to 1:00.
3. Use OUTPUT A+B for the wide '80s stereo spread.
4. Play rhythmic arpeggios — the bright, pronounced chorus cuts through.

## Notes
- DESIGNED — NOT an artist-attributed setting. James Honeyman-Scott (The Pretenders) is a verified CE-2 user (Guitar World) but published no dials.
- The numbers are Guitar Chalk's "'80s Jangle Pop" preset (RATE 6 / DEPTH 6 on a 0–10 scale → 1:00/1:00 clock); the artist is inspiration only.
- Essential classic-era flavor, but off-lens (not drone/doom) — trim first if a hard 12-cap is needed.
