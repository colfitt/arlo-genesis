# Cavernous Blackhole Drone

## Concept
The drone workhorse: a single chord held forever, oceanic and bottomless. Feed a sustained Hizumitas/baritone fuzz chord into Blackhole sitting near Infinite, then freeze it and play over the frozen tail. Preset B adds optional early-reflection body via a low-Mix Hall or Room, or stays bypassed to leave a free slot.

## How to play it
1. Set Routing to Series with B after A.
2. Hold a sustained chord into Blackhole (Mix 60-80%, Size 90+, Gravity right, Feedback near Infinite).
3. Hit **HotSwitch 1** to push Blackhole Feedback to Freeze — the wall locks into an instant pad.
4. Solo over the frozen tail.

## Notes
- Fully numeric but **inferred** — not a found or cited setting. Treat the values as a designed starting point, never as an artist/factory capture.
- For banjo-as-lead, pull Blackhole Mix back so the wall sits behind the line.
- Keep Lo Level slightly down to keep the bottom clear under the baritone.
- Print the frozen tail to the downstream PORTA424 / JHS 424 tape stage.
