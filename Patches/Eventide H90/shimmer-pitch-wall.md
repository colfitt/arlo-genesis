# Shimmer / Pitch Wall

## Concept
A heavenly octave-up cathedral drone/doom shimmer wall. Shimmer climbs an octave above the input; Pitch Freeze locks the climb into an infinite cathedral. Preset B adds octave-stacked body via a low-Mix Polyphony (Percussive mode if banjo-fed) or stays bypassed.

## How to play it
1. Set Routing to Series.
2. Engage Shimmer on Preset A with the octave pitch and high Decay above.
3. Hold a chord and use **Pitch Freeze** (or a HotSwitch mapped to Shimmer Pitch+verb Freeze) to lock the climb.
4. Solo over the frozen pad.
