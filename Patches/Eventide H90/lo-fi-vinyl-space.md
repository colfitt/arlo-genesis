# Lo-fi Vinyl Space (LoFi -> Blackhole, series)

## Concept
A pre-degraded ambient wall that overlaps the rig's tape/lo-fi thesis even before the PORTA424 stage. LoFi (12-bit, tape hiss, flutter) crunches the signal, then Blackhole blooms it into a degraded space. The cleanest single-Program statement of the rig's "recorded-wrong" thesis.

## How to play it
1. Set Routing to Series, LoFi -> Blackhole.
2. Engage LoFi on A (12-bit, Tape Hiss 40%, Flutter 60%, Mix 70%).
3. Engage Blackhole on B (Gravity -25, Feedback 70%, Size 90, Mix 65%).
4. Map an expression pedal to control both for live swells.
