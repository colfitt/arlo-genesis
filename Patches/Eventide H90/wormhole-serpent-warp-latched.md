# Wormhole Serpent (factory, Warp-latched)

## Concept
The factory "Wormhole Serpent" preset, untouched except for one mapping: Warp assigned to a latching footswitch so you can hold it and let the reverb ring out. The workflow is to noodle ambient, then engage Warp for the swell.

## How to play it
1. Load the factory "Wormhole Serpent" preset on Preset A.
2. Assign a footswitch to control **Warp as a latching switch**.
3. Noodle ambient, then kick Warp for the held hyperspace swell and let it ring out.

## Notes
- Named factory preset — the captured "setting" is the Warp-to-latching-footswitch mapping. On-aesthetic.
