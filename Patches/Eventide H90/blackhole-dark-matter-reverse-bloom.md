# Blackhole — Dark Matter Reverse Bloom

## Concept
Backwards, blooming swells built on the deep Inverse side of Blackhole — the most "recorded-wrong" Blackhole on disk. Low Gravity flips Blackhole into its reverse-swell character; dark Hi EQ keeps the bloom murky. A short Plate on B can seat the bloom in a room.

## How to play it
1. Set Blackhole on Preset A with Gravity low (2) to engage the Inverse / reverse side.
2. Pair each note or chord with a **volume-swell attack** for the backwards entrance.
3. Let the bloom rise and fall; add a short Plate on B if it needs grounding.

## Notes
- Low Gravity number = the Inverse/reverse side on the H90.
- Knob positions are Blackhole-pedal 1-10 values — dial by ear.
