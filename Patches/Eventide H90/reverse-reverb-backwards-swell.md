# Reverse Reverb Backwards-Swell

## Concept
A backwards/blooming swell entrance — "instant shoegaze." Reverse Reverb paired with a volume-swell attack gives the backwards, blooming entrance. Eventide Space factory presets "Fluttering" and "Ghosts" are good reverse starting points (they take a while to dial in).

## How to play it
1. Engage Reverse Reverb on Preset A.
2. Pair each entrance with a **volume-swell attack** for the backwards/blooming effect.
3. Use "Fluttering" or "Ghosts" as starting points; seat with a short Plate on B if needed.

## Notes
- Lightly sourced (offsetguitars / Space). "Reverse Reverb = instant shoegaze" (UsageGuide §2).
- Named presets are Eventide's and transfer to the H90 reverse algorithm. No numeric values.
