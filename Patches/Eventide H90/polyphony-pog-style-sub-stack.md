# Polyphony POG-style Sub Stack

## Concept
A POG-style octave stack for sub-weight under a banjo/baritone line. An octave-down voice plus a little octave-up with detune; Percussive inst type preserves the banjo's transients. A low-Mix reverb on B seats it.

## How to play it
1. Engage Polyphony on A with an octave-down voice plus a little octave-up (with Detune).
2. Set Inst Type = Percussive for banjo.
3. Add a low-Mix reverb on B to seat it, or leave B bypassed.

## Notes
- Leon Todd. Octave-down sub weight is on-aesthetic for baritone/doom.
