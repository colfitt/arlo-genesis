# Posen "Filter Sound" (ModFilter)

## Concept
Ariel Posen's ModFilter, tweaked "warmer and touch-sensitive, really dynamic when you hit it." A variant adds reverb. Heard on "Heart by Heart" / "Carry Me Home" / "What Are We Doing Here." Clean/studio, kept for completeness. Real artist attribution on H9 hardware (algorithm identical on the H90).

## How to play it
1. Engage the ModFilter algorithm.
2. Tweak it warmer and touch-sensitive so it responds dynamically to pick attack.
3. For the variant, add reverb.

## Notes
- Real artist attribution on H9 hardware; algorithm identical on the H90. Clean/studio, non-signature.
- No numeric values were documented — verbal only.
