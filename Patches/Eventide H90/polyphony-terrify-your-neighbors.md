# Polyphony Terrify Your Neighbors (tritone chaos)

## Concept
A dissonant, chaotic self-oscillating pitch wall (post-punk / atonal texture). Both voice intervals set to a tritone, with lots of delay feedback, the Feedback switch IN, and long delays. Demoed with diminished chords.

## How to play it
1. Engage Polyphony on A.
2. Set both Voice intervals to a TRITONE.
3. Add lots of delay Feedback, flip the Feedback switch to IN, and use long Delays.
4. Demoed with diminished chords for maximum chaos.

## Notes
- Leon Todd. UsageGuide §2's "chaos = tritone both voices + feedback IN."
- On-aesthetic atonal/post-punk.
