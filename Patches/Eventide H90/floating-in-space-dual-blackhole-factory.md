# Floating in Space — Dual Blackhole Stack (factory)

## Concept
The headline demonstration of the H90's dual-algorithm power: two Blackhole reverbs at different Size/Gravity settings stacked into a single Program for a deep, layered ambient wash. Make one slot a tighter/darker space and the other huge and long, then run them parallel for layering or series for a reverb-into-reverb cascade. Optionally push one slot's Gravity to the reverse side for a swelling pre-bloom on attacks. This is one of the best factory drone starting points to load and dissect for real Blackhole values.

## How to play it
1. Load the factory "Floating in Space" / "Floating Space" Program (Factory list on the pedal or in H90 Control).
2. Make slot A a tighter/darker space, slot B huge and long.
3. Run in parallel and blend the two slots; or set one Gravity to the reverse side for swelling attacks.
4. Dissect the differing Size/Gravity/Feedback per slot in H90 Control to learn the algorithm.
