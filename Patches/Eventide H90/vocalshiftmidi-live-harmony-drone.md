# VocalShiftMIDI Live-Harmony Drone (61SL-played)

## Concept
Play harmony intervals live from the Novation 61SL MkIII over a held banjo/baritone note. VocalShiftMIDI gives 4 MIDI-played voices; map Glide / Hold / Freeze to footswitches for pitch-rise swells and frozen vowel-drones.

## How to play it
1. Engage VocalShiftMIDI on Preset A and set it to receive from the 61SL MkIII.
2. Hold a banjo/baritone note; play harmony intervals live on the 61SL.
3. Map Glide/Hold/Freeze to footswitches for pitch-rise swells and frozen vowel-drones.
4. Use **Hold = Off** so voices sound only while a MIDI note is held.
