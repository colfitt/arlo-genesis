# Blackhole — Low Orbit Resonance

## Concept
A dark, sub-heavy ambient tail tuned to sit under the baritone. Lo EQ 8 / Hi EQ 4 keeps the wall living under the fundamental without clouding the banjo's mids.

## How to play it
1. Engage Blackhole on Preset A with the sub-heavy EQ above.
2. Feed it the baritone; let the dense, dark tail pool under the chord.
3. Leave Preset B bypassed.

## Notes
- Lo EQ 8 / Hi EQ 4 keeps the wall under the fundamental without muddying the mids.
- Knob positions are Blackhole-pedal 1-10 values — dial by ear.
