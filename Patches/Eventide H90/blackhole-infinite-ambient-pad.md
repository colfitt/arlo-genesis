# Blackhole — Infinite Ambient Pad

## Concept
The everyday "lock a wall" Blackhole: Feedback near max pushes the tail into freeze territory, giving an infinite ambient pad to solo over. Preset B is left bypassed so you can add a Hall or a pitch voice later.

## How to play it
1. Engage Blackhole on Preset A with the values above.
2. Hold a chord and let Feedback at 10 carry it into a near-infinite freeze pad.
3. Play over the locked wall.

## Notes
- Feedback at max = the freeze pad.
- Values are Blackhole-**pedal** knob positions (1-10), which map to the same 6 controls as the H90 Blackhole algorithm. Dial by ear.
