# Blackhole — Swirling Modulation Wash

## Concept
A dream-pop/shoegaze wash where the reverb becomes the instrument. Bright Hi EQ plus lower Gravity gives a shimmery, swirling wall; a low-Mix Chorus or TriceraChorus on Preset B adds wobble to the tail.

## How to play it
1. Engage Blackhole on Preset A with the bright EQ above.
2. Optionally add a low-Mix Chorus/TriceraChorus on Preset B to wobble the tail.
3. Let the wash bloom and let it carry the texture.

## Notes
- Bright Hi EQ + lower Gravity = a shimmery shoegaze wall.
- Knob positions are Blackhole-pedal 1-10 values — dial by ear.
