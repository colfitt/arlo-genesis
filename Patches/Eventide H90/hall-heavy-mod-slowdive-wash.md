# Hall Heavy-Mod Slowdive Wash

## Concept
An alternative Slowdive wash where the reverb sits under and around the note. The general washy principle: long Decay + heavy modulation + rolled-off highs + moderate Mix, with Freeze/Infinite for a sustained bed.

## How to play it
1. Engage Hall on A with heavy modulation and a long Decay.
2. Roll off the highs and keep Mix moderate so it sits under the note.
3. Engage Freeze/Infinite for the sustained wash.
