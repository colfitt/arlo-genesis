# Shimmer Subtle Octave Wash

## Concept
A subtle "makes everything bigger" octave wash for single-note or cleaner passages. Shimmer pitches +1 octave and -1 octave simultaneously, kept low in the mix so it widens rather than dominates. Push Mix higher and freeze for a heavier cathedral wall.

## How to play it
1. Engage Shimmer with +1 and -1 octave simultaneously, Decay ~4-5 s, Mix ~25%.
2. Play single notes or cleaner passages — the wash just makes everything bigger.
3. For the heavier wall, push Mix up and use **Pitch Freeze** or **Pitch+verb Freeze** to lock a chord into an infinite cathedral.
