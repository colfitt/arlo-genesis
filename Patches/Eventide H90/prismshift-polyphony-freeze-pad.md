# PrismShift / Polyphony Freeze Pad (play-over)

## Concept
A frozen pitched pad to solo over. Polyphony and PrismShift each have a dedicated Freeze Performance Parameter (PolySynth has Freeze too: "freeze/sustain the synth then solo on top"). The unique PrismShift trick: the High and Low voices freeze but the **Mid voice does not** — so a frozen PrismShift pad stays playable. Freeze the bed, keep playing the melody through it.

## How to play it
1. Engage Polyphony or PrismShift on Preset A.
2. Assign **Freeze** to a footswitch (latching, or momentary "M").
3. Hold a chord, hit Freeze to lock the bed.
4. With PrismShift, keep playing — the Mid voice stays live for the melody.
