# Polyphony Polyrhythm 5th-up/oct-down

## Concept
A rhythmic, wide pitched bed — Todd's "might be the most inspiring sound in here." A modification of the factory "Polyrhythm": Voice 1 a 5th up, Voice 2 an octave down, rhythmic delays, feedback added, voices panned hard L/R, no detune, Mix at 50%.

## How to play it
1. Load factory "Polyrhythm" on Preset A.
2. Set Voice 1 = 5th UP, Voice 2 = octave DOWN.
3. Apply rhythmic delay values, add Feedback, pan the voices hard L/R, leave detune off.
4. Set Mix to 50%.

## Notes
- Leon Todd modification of a factory preset.
- One of the few patches with an explicit Mix (50%) and pan (hard L/R).
