# Wormhole Hyperspace Wall (long-warp)

## Concept
A sustained unstable-pitch reverb wall over a held baritone/fuzz chord. Built off the factory "Wormhole Serpent," with Stability dropped for more pitch-wobble and Length up for a long decay. Kick Warp (on a latching footswitch) for a rising/falling hyperspace swell that you hold and ring out.

## How to play it
1. Engage Wormhole on A with the warp values above and Stability DOWN, Length UP.
2. Assign **Warp to a latching footswitch** (Perform / HotSwitch).
3. Hold a baritone/fuzz chord, then kick Warp to hold and ring out the hyperspace swell.
