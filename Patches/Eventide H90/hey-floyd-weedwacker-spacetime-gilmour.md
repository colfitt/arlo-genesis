# Hey Floyd — WeedWacker + SpaceTime (Gilmour Wall, factory)

## Concept
A factory Program that emulates David Gilmour's Big Muff + Electric Mistress sound from The Wall: a WeedWacker-style drive (TS-style 2-stage OD / Muff-ish dirt) running into SpaceTime's "Outer Limits" preset — modulation plus two delays and a reverb. The result is a fat fuzz lead flowing into liquid flange and space. SpaceTime's Verb Lvl knob sets how its delays and reverb route (series or parallel), so that one control governs the balance of echo vs. wash around the lead.

## How to play it
1. Load the factory "hey floyd" Program (WeedWacker into SpaceTime "Outer Limits").
2. Play sustained lead lines for the Big Muff + Electric Mistress wall.
3. Use the SpaceTime block for the surrounding mod/delay/reverb space; dissect the Verb Lvl balance to taste.
