# Shimmer Pad Wash (Blackhole + PitchFuzz, parallel)

## Concept
A sustained shimmer pad wall you can swell in and out with an expression pedal. Blackhole (Size 90, Inverse Gravity) provides the wall; an octave-up PitchFuzz running in parallel adds the bright shimmer overtone with some grit.

## How to play it
1. Set Routing to Parallel.
2. Engage Blackhole on A and PitchFuzz (+1 octave, low gain, mellow tone) on B.
3. Map an **expression pedal to reverb Mix (30-80%)** and swell the wall in and out under your playing.
