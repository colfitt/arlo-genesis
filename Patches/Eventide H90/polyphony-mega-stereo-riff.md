# Polyphony Mega Stereo Riff (into distortion)

## Concept
A huge stereo tone for big riffs and chords into distortion — width and girth for doom walls. Voice 1 octave up and Voice 2 octave down, each with a little detune, panned slightly L/R, then driven into distortion.

## How to play it
1. Engage Polyphony on A with Voice 1 = octave UP, Voice 2 = octave DOWN.
2. Add a little Detune on each and pan the voices slightly L/R for width.
3. Pair with distortion via the wet-only routing trick into a distortion algo, or run into external fuzz.

## Notes
- Leon Todd. On-aesthetic for doom (octave weight + width + distortion).
- Detune is given verbally ("a little") — dial by ear.
