# ModEchoVerb Shoegaze Wall ("shoegaze for days")

## Concept
Slowdive-style "everything blurred together" washy shoegaze wall — the most on-aesthetic concrete recipe on disk. ModEchoVerb with Chorus modulation, a 12.75 s decay, and high EQ rolled off (-5) for dark haze. Pair Preset B with a long Hall or Blackhole for depth and add a HotSwitch -> Freeze.

## How to play it
1. Engage ModEchoVerb on A with Mod = Chorus and the values above.
2. Pair B with a long Hall or Blackhole for depth.
3. Add a HotSwitch mapped to Freeze to lock the wall when wanted.
