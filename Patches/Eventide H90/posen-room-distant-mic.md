# Posen "Room" Distant-Mic

## Concept
Ariel Posen's Room algorithm tweaked to emulate a distant room mic — "the sound of the space." Used on the solo of "What Are We Doing Here" (with extra fuzz). A clean/studio sound, not a drone wall, kept for completeness. Real artist attribution on H9 hardware (algorithm identical on the H90).

## How to play it
1. Engage the Room algorithm.
2. Tweak it toward a distant room-mic character rather than a tight near-field room.
3. Stack extra fuzz upstream for the solo tone Posen used.

## Notes
- Real artist attribution on H9 hardware; algorithm identical on the H90. Clean/studio, non-signature.
- No numeric values were documented — verbal only. Posen names presets by function for fast live recall.
