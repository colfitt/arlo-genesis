# ModEchoVerb Shoegaze Wall (staff direction)

## Concept
Eventide staff's own shoegaze/ambient direction for ModEchoVerb — the qualitative complement to the numeric "shoegaze for days" recipe. Low Mod Rate with higher Mod Depth smooths the top end; Hall 2 at a large Size with Diffusion experimented on the lower end of its range.

## How to play it
1. Engage ModEchoVerb on A with reverb mode = Hall 2, large Size.
2. Set Mod Rate low and Mod Depth higher to smooth the top end.
3. Experiment with Diffusion on the lower end of its range.
4. Use this as the dial-by-ear scaffold, then layer the numeric recipe's values on top.
