# Posen "Hall Reverb" + "Leslie effect"

## Concept
Ariel Posen's pair of utility/studio sounds: a straightforward Hall for general ambience, and a modified stock Rotary/Leslie preset tuned for a natural Leslie tone. He also keeps a spare slap delay as a utility failover (the "Big Sky as failover" logic). Clean/studio, kept for completeness. Real artist attribution on H9 hardware (algorithm identical on the H90).

## How to play it
1. Use the Hall algorithm for general, straightforward ambience.
2. For the Leslie tone, modify a stock Rotary/Leslie preset toward a natural rotating-speaker sound.
3. Keep a spare slap delay slot as a failover.

## Notes
- Real artist attribution on H9 hardware; algorithm identical on the H90. Clean/studio, non-signature.
- No numeric values were documented — verbal only. Posen keeps failover copies of other pedals' sounds.
