# Sustain Pad / Breathing Lead

## Concept
Single-note leads that breathe — banjo or baritone melodic lines over a bed. Neutral Tone keeps the note's pitch legible for melodic playing, and the swell technique sidesteps the hard fuzz attack. The sustain holds long enough that reverb alone can carry it.

## How to play it
1. Volume 11:00, Sustain 1:00, Tone 12:00 (neutral).
2. Use the instrument volume knob to swell INTO notes (sidesteps the hard fuzz attack).
3. Let notes hang — reverb alone can carry them; add modulated delay (CE-2W / Big Time) after.
