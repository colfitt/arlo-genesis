# Plumes-Front Metal Stack — go-to high-gain

## Concept
The Distortion Principle's demonstrated "go-to metal setting": an EQD Plumes (low-gain overdrive) kicked on IN FRONT of the Hizumitas to slam the Sustain stage into tight, brutal high-gain. The Hizumitas itself stays at its quoted base setting — Volume 9–10 o'clock, everything else at 1 o'clock — and the Plumes supplies the tightening up front. Demonstrated working into both a broken-up Orange TH30 and a clean EVH 5150.

## How to play it
1. **Hizumitas:** Volume 9:00–10:00, Sustain ~1:00, Tone ~1:00 (the demonstrator's quoted base setting).
2. Place an EQD Plumes (or any low-gain OD/boost) IN FRONT of the Hizumitas.
3. Kick the Plumes on to tighten and slam the Sustain stage — the "go-to metal setting."
4. Works into both a broken-up amp (Orange TH30) and a clean amp (EVH 5150).

## Notes
- The Hizumitas positions ARE sourced — from The Distortion Principle's spoken summary (Volume 9–10 o'clock, everything else at 1 o'clock).
- **No published values for the Plumes:** its knob settings are not published. The front-drive is a dialable low-gain boost — set to taste.
