# Granular/Glitch Capture — sustained source for grains

## Concept
The Hizumitas as a granular/glitch *source* rather than a destination. Its long sustain gives a granular sampler or glitch pedal a continuous, unbroken signal to chew on — so the grains land as intentional texture instead of choppy artifacts. Hold a chord and the Hizumitas keeps the source alive long after the pick attack, which is exactly what granular engines (Microcosm, Chroma Console) and capture loopers (Mood, Blooper) want. The Wata-adjacent move: run a Mood (V1/II) mod looper against sustained Hizumitas chords, or capture a Hizumitas drone into a Chase Bliss Blooper and layer modulations on top.

## How to play it
1. Set the Hizumitas for a long-sustaining drone voice — Sustain up, Tone to taste.
2. Hold a chord/note so the downstream granular/glitch pedal has a continuous source to sample.
3. Run a granular pedal after it (Microcosm, Chroma Console) — the grains sound intentional, not choppy.
4. Wata-adjacent: run a Mood (V1 or II) mod looper against the sustained chords, or capture the drone into a Blooper and layer modulations.
