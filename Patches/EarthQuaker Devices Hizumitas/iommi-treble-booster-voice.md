# Iommi Treble-Booster Voice — CCW grind/bark

## Concept
Mixdown's / High on Technology's CCW Tone zone: turning Tone fully counter-clockwise gives "the whole Iommi treble-booster sound" — a bright, mid/treble-forward grind with chewy attack and bark that approaches near-RAT territory. This is the bright, biting end of the pedal's range for cutting leads and grindy rhythm — the opposite pole from the fat doom walls.

## How to play it
1. Turn the Tone knob fully counter-clockwise (treble side).
2. Sustain around noon keeps it grindy and articulate; raise for singing leads. Start Volume near unity.
3. Play single-note leads or grindy rhythm for the bright, biting, chewy "treble-booster" voice; the bridge pickup amplifies the bark.
4. Tame any harshness / ice-pick with the guitar tone knob rather than adding Tone-side bass.

## Notes
- CCW = "the whole Iommi treble-booster sound" (Mixdown) / "Iommi-style treble booster; chewy attack, bark" (High on Technology); corroborates EQD's "near RAT territory" CCW description.
- Honesty flag: only the Tone direction (full CCW) is sourced. Sustain and Volume are a dialable recipe, not published values.
- Gotcha: high-treble CCW settings can sound harsh or excessively noisy to some players (Premier Guitar) — back off slightly if it gets ice-picky.
- Distinct from Controlled Crunch (Sustain min + CCW): this seed is the CCW Tone VOICE itself, sustain-agnostic, named for the Iommi / treble-booster character.
