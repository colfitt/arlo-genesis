# Balanced Mid-Forward Lead/Rhythm

## Concept
The mid voice that survives a busy board — the best single setting when the banjo needs to read AS a lead line inside the wall. Articulate and mid-forward, it punches through a dense downstream chain and survives the Brothers AM / Longsword stack.

## How to play it
1. Volume ~10:00–12:00, Sustain 12:00, Tone ~10:00 (just treble side of noon).
2. Play baritone leads or chord work — the mids stay forward and legible.

## Notes
- The best single setting for cut-through when the lead line must read inside a wall.
- Tone just treble-side of noon keeps articulation without going harsh.
