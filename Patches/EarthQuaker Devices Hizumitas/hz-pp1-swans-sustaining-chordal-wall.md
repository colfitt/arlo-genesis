# HZ-PP1 — Swans Sustaining Chordal Wall

## Concept
The daily Swans crescendo wall — a sustained chord that blooms *as a chord* (not a single oceanic note), like Westberg's "vast sustaining chordal sound." This is the peak of a dynamics build, feeding the Dark Star DS-PP2 freeze + Big Time. Sustain is deliberately not maxed (vs the Doom Wall patch) so the voicing rings as a chord.

## How to play it
1. Volume ~1:00 (hot into the next stage), Sustain ~2:00 (just shy of max), Tone ~1:00 (bass side).
2. Neck pickup — hold a chord and let the overtone/feedback bloom at volume.
3. Feed Dark Star DS-PP2 freeze + Big Time for the sustained chordal crescendo.

## Notes
- 🟣 **APPROXIMATION:** Swans use NO fuzz — this manufactures the sustained-overtone result a pedal-free loud-amp wall gets by physics. Not an artist setting.
- Sustain deliberately not maxed (vs Doom Wall #3 / doom-wall-max-heavy) so the voicing rings as a chord.
- Ref: Swans "The Seer"/"Bring the Sun" — Gira/Westberg "vast sustaining chordal" crescendo wall.
