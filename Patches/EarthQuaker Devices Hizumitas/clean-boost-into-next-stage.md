# Clean Boost Into Next Stage — Volume past unity

## Concept
Using the Hizumitas's hot output as a boost: unity gain sits around 9–10 o'clock, so everything past that pushes a clean boost into the next pedal or amp. Park the Volume well above unity to slam a downstream drive/amp harder without raising the noise floor — the mid-forward voicing keeps it quiet. A utility patch for driving the rest of the chain.

## How to play it
1. Note unity gain sits around 9–10 o'clock on Volume.
2. Push the Volume well past that to use the pedal as a clean boost into the next stage.
3. Set Sustain & Tone to your preferred fuzz voice.
4. Use it to slam a downstream drive or amp harder; the mid-forward voicing keeps the noise floor low.

## Notes
- Sourced: unity gain ~9–10:00; "everything past that is a clean boost into the next pedal/amp."
- **Honesty flag:** only the unity reference is published — the boost amount and other knobs (Sustain/Tone) are a dialable recipe, not sourced values.
- Output is HOT — Premier Guitar / EQD confirm unity ~9–10:00 and a massive output past it.
- Quiet for a high-gain Muff — the mid-forward voicing has less hiss-band content than a scooped Muff.
