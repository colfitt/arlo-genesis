# Controlled Crunch / Low-Gain

## Concept
The lo-fi gateway sound — articulate rhythm, palm-mutes, and chord stabs. With Sustain at minimum the Hizumitas becomes a genuinely nice overdrive that cleans up via the guitar's volume knob (especially on a treble-bleed baritone). The lo-fi-cluster demo-crunch that backs off without bypassing.

## How to play it
1. Volume ~12:00, Sustain fully CCW (minimum — still a fuzzy overdrive, no clean floor), Tone fully CCW (treble / RAT-ish bright).
2. Play palm-mutes, chord stabs, and articulate rhythm.
3. Roll the instrument volume down to 6–8 to clean it up further.

## Notes
- Sustain min = "genuinely nice overdrive" (Andy Bassist) — useful when you want grit that responds to the guitar's volume rather than bypassing.
- No clean floor: even at minimum Sustain this is still a fuzzy overdrive.
- Ref: Lo-fi cluster (Car Seat Headrest / Modest Mouse demo crunch).
