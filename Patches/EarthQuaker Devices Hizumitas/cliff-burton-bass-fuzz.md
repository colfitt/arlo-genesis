# Cliff-Burton Bass Fuzz

## Concept
Sustained low fuzz on bass or the VG-800 baritone model — recording-preamp-style distorted low end. Volume straight up, Sustain around three-quarters, and Tone on the treble side so the low strings stay defined. Low feedback even at this gain, with only slight hiss. Maps onto the VG-800 baritone model for baritone-weight drone.

## How to play it
1. Volume 12:00 (straight up), Sustain ~3:00 (≈¾), Tone ~10:00 (treble side).
2. Play sustained low notes — low strings stay defined, feedback stays low.
3. Run a compressor after to tame it (Andy used a Cali76).

## Notes
- Maps onto the VG-800 baritone model for baritone-weight drone.
- "Cliff Burton vibes" is the *demonstrator's descriptor* for the sound — NOT a claim of Burton's actual settings.
- Ref: Cliff-Burton-style sustained bass fuzz (Andy Bassist demo descriptor).
