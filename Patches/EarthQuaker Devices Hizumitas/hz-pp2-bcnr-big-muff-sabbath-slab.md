# HZ-PP2 — BC,NR Big-Muff Sabbath Slab

## Concept
The late, sudden distortion-slab climax — the Black Country, New Road "kick the door in" Op-Amp-Big-Muff slab. Everything maxed with Tone full bass-side for the scooped Op-Amp-Muff slab voice. Park it off until the second-half climax; kick it on as the drop, not through the verse. Pairs with Longsword LS-PP1 after for the op-amp edge.

## How to play it
1. Volume max, Sustain max, Tone ~2:00 (full bass side).
2. Keep it OFF through the verse.
3. Kick it on as the drop / second-half climax — the "kick the door in" slab.
4. Pair with Longsword LS-PP1 after for the op-amp edge.

## Notes
- 🟣 **APPROXIMATION:** BC,NR's slab is an Op Amp Big Muff (a different fuzz); the Hizumitas chases the scooped-wall result, not the exact pedal.
- Ref: BC,NR "Basketball Shoes"/"Sunglasses" — late Op-Amp-Big-Muff distortion slab.
