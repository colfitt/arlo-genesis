# Synth-Filter Decay Sweep

## Concept
A sustained baritone/banjo drone whose voicing MORPHS as it rings — the embers "burn and change shape." Start Tone fat (1:00), then physically sweep it CW→CCW through the note's decay so the wall morphs fat/dark → mid-forward as it rings. The Tone knob becomes an almost synth-like filter control on sustained notes — a frequency sweep like a ProCo RAT filter. The most expressive single gesture on the pedal.

## How to play it
1. Volume 11:00, Sustain 12:00, Tone START at 1:00 (fat).
2. Hit a sustained note/chord and let it ring at amp volume.
3. Physically SWEEP the Tone knob CW→CCW through the decay so the voicing morphs fat/dark → mid-forward.
4. Pair with long verb/granular after (H90, Microcosm, Big Time) — it's the ideal source so they sound intentional, not choppy.
