# Max Chaos / Runaway (recorded-wrong)

## Concept
Broken, gated, ripping decay tails; controlled feedback near the amp; a degraded "recorded-wrong" aesthetic; VG-800 poly-synth artifacting. Volume boosts hard into the next stage, Sustain is maxed, and the treble-side Tone brings out the gated/bit-crushed clamp on the tail. This deliberately courts the Sustain-max + CCW-Tone instability the gotchas warn about — that instability IS the patch.

## How to play it
1. Volume 2:00 (boosting hard into the next stage), Sustain max (CW), Tone ~10:00 (treble side).
2. Push VG-800 poly-guitar-synth patches in for heavy artifacting (a *feature* here).
3. Court feedback near the amp; control it via amp proximity.
4. Use a gate (BF-3) downstream if you need silence between bursts.
