# HZ-PP3 — Joy Division Dark Treble-Cut Rhythm

## Concept
Joy Division's dark, treble-cut, low-gain rhythmic guitar — palm-muted chord stabs, the least-fuzzy Hizumitas patch (a dark grit, not a wall). The cold Dark Star DS-PP1 space does the work over it. Sustain stays low because JD guitar is low-gain and rhythmic, and Tone goes deep bass-side to take away most of the treble (the EQ-pedal move).

## How to play it
1. Volume ~12:00, Sustain ~10:00 (low), Tone ~2:00 (deep bass side).
2. Play palm-muted rhythmic chord stabs.
3. Let the cold Dark Star DS-PP1 space do the atmospheric work over the top.

## Notes
- 🟣 **APPROXIMATION:** JD's guitar is a Vox AC30 (Gain 3 / Bass 8 / Mid 4 / Treble 2 / Presence 1) with treble cut by an EQ pedal; the Hizumitas darkens via its bass-side Tone instead.
- Brittle texture lives on the 108/Longsword; the SPACE does the cold work.
- Ref: Joy Division "Disorder"/"Atmosphere" — dark, treble-cut rhythmic guitar.
