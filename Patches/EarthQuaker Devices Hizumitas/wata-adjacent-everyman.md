# Wata-Adjacent Everyman (Boris core)

## Concept
Wata of Boris's main fuzz-wall voice — the closest documented approximation of her own setting, and the post-punk anchor patch. Volume sits at unity (9–10 o'clock), with Sustain and Tone slightly bass-side of noon. For Wata's *own* recollected setting, nudge Sustain and Tone back to ~12:00. Hit a loud amp for the feedback bloom.

## How to play it
1. Volume 9:00–10:00 (unity), Sustain ~1:00, Tone ~1:00 (slightly bass side of noon).
2. For Wata's *own* (recollected) setting, move Sustain & Tone back to ~12:00.
3. Hold a chord into a loud amp and let the feedback/sustain bloom arrive.

## Notes
- **Honesty caveat:** Wata's exact positions are not published by her. The noon / 1-o'clock figures are The Distortion Principle's on-camera recollection of a Wata video — *not invented*, but secondhand.
- Wata tours the Hizumitas as her main fuzz (*"not inferior to my ELK"*).
- Ref: Wata / Boris — post-2022 fuzz wall (song "Feedbacker" = original Elk; post-2022 audible fuzz wall = Hizumitas).
