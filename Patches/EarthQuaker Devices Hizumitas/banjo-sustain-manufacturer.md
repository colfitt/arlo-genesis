# Banjo Sustain Manufacturer

## Concept
The single highest-leverage rig use of this pedal. Banjo decays in ~1.5 s; the Hizumitas extends and evens that into a guitar-like sustained lead voice (GK-5 → VG-800). Tone is deliberately *bass-side* because the banjo source is the opposite problem from a dark guitar — the CW sweep rolls off the piercing 2–4 kHz via the 3n3 HPF cap. The gentle compression also evens banjo's fast attack/decay so notes behave like guitar notes.

## How to play it
1. Volume ~11:00, Sustain 12:00, Tone 1:00–2:00 (well into the bass-boost CW side).
2. Play melodic banjo lines — notes now sustain and even out like guitar notes.
3. If still too bright, feed it a darker VG-800 amp model (Tweed/AC, treble pulled back).
