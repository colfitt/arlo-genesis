# Rhythmic Granular Arp — clock-face starting point

## Concept
Pulsing, sequenced banjo-as-lead locked to the board's clock — a companion to the Arp A and Arp B patches. Engine Arp A/B or Mosaic D with a full clock-face chart; Filter at noon tames banjo's 2–4 kHz brittleness. Pluck banjo rolls and the engine chains onsets into sustaining arpeggiated runs locked to the board.

## How to play it
1. Select engine Arp A/B (or Mosaic D).
2. Dial: Activity 12:00–2:00 · Repeats 11:00 · Time synced to MIDI clock (or tap) · Filter 12:00 · Space 11:00 · Mix 12:00.
3. Pluck banjo rolls; the engine chains onsets into sustaining arpeggiated runs locked to the board.
