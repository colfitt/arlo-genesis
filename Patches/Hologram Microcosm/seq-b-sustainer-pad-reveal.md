# Seq B Sustainer-Pad Reveal

## Concept
A soft sustainer pad sitting under a sequenced octave-bounce bed. Seq B alternates samples between normal and half speed (octave-down), with Activity setting the bounce rate. Turning one knob fully clockwise introduces a soft SUSTAINER PAD layer underneath the sequence.

## How to play it
1. Select engine SEQ (Micro Loop) variation B.
2. Set Activity for the bounce rate between the normal and half-speed samples.
3. Turn one knob fully clockwise to introduce the soft sustainer-pad layer under the sequence.

## Notes
- Documented hidden behavior (Whirrings + Starsky).
