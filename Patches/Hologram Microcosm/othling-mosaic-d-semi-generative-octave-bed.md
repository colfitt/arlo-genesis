# Othling Mosaic-D Semi-Generative Octave Bed

## Concept
A hands-free, self-modulating evolving pad — the most concrete real-world calibration on disk. Mosaic D adds ±2 octaves (two up and two down). With a very long Time, the octave content drifts in slowly ("it'll take a while for us to hear"). A slow EXP ramper on Filter at full Mix opens to let the high twinkles through, then "filters itself out again." Print it to the Apollo.

## How to play it
1. Select engine MOSAIC (Micro Loop) variation D (±2 octaves).
2. Set Time very long so the octave content drifts in slowly.
3. Set Mix to ~2 o'clock (NOT noon — "noon doesn't read as 50/50; about 2 o'clock is unity to my ear").
4. Assign a slow EXP ramper to FILTER at full Mix so the filter opens for the twinkles then filters itself out again.
5. Rolling Filter further back also lets a little dry back into the mix.

## Notes
- REAL artist settings — Mix ~2 o'clock = unity is the single most reusable number from any source.
- Pairs with the EXP-to-Filter generative trick (see the EXP-to-Filter setup note).
- Power-on trap: an EXP pedal plugged in at power-on auto-binds to Filter at heel-down = filter closed = no wet (looks broken; toe-down or reassign).
