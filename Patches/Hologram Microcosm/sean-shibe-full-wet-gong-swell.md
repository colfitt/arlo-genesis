# Sean Shibe Full-Wet Gong Swell

## Concept
Run the pedal 100% wet so it becomes its own instrument: Space and Mix cranked (Mix fully CW, Space toward 100% wet). Play a swell and let the pedal generate the wash. The cleanest documented "Microcosm as a 100%-wet wall" precedent — a wall that stands on its own with no dry signal.

## How to play it
1. Set Mix fully clockwise and push Space toward 100% wet.
2. Pick a Granules or Tunnel engine for gong character; dial Activity/engine to taste.
3. Play a swell and let the pedal generate the wash on its own.

## Notes
- REAL verified user, directly-quoted use — the only "settings" on disk are "full wet / Space + Mix cranked." No knob numbers published; dial Activity/engine to taste.
