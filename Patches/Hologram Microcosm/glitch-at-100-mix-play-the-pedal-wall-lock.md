# Glitch-at-100%-Mix "Play the Pedal" Wall-Lock

## Concept
The headline rig move — feed the Board-1 fuzz wall in and let the Microcosm re-sequence it. With any Glitch engine (or Tunnel) at Mix 100% (dry fully removed), everything you play comes out re-sequenced. The output always has a regular internal rhythm/pulse — "lock onto the pulse of what the pedal is doing and hang on to it for dear life," then play to that pulse.

## How to play it
1. Select any GLITCH engine (or Tunnel).
2. Set Mix to 100% (dry fully removed).
3. Set Effect Volume (Shift+Mix / CC 16) so engaging it on a hot fuzz wall doesn't jump the board level.
4. Feed the fuzz wall in; lock onto the pedal's internal pulse and play to that pulse.
