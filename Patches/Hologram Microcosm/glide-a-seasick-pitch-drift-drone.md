# Glide A Seasick Pitch-Drift Drone

## Concept
A slow, seasick pitch-drift bed on a sustained baritone/fuzz source. Glide A "glides between audio at half and normal speed," and with a triangle Shape (fade in/fade out) and a very slow subdivision it becomes a gently drifting drone. The slower the glide, the nicer. Best on a sustained source.

## How to play it
1. Select engine GLIDE (Micro Loop) variation A.
2. Set Shape to triangle (fade in / fade out).
3. Set Time to a very slow subdivision — the slower the glide, the nicer.
4. Use Activity to change the glide time.
5. Feed it a sustained source.

## Notes
- REAL demo settings (engine + mode + Shape=triangle + slow subdivision all stated).
- Glide D ("glides both directions at once, beautifully spacey") is the wider-drift alternative.
