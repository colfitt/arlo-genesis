# Banjo Fingerpick Onset-Halo (Strum/Mosaic)

## Concept
Chain fingerpicked banjo plucks into a cloud of pitched copies — a granular stand-in for the intertwining harmony "echoes" (Sufjan fingerpicked banjo → layered harmony echoes; Bon Iver self-choir impulse). Strum B overlaps onsets into phasing/cascading runs, or Mosaic A gives octave-up harmony loops for the brighter prism-up colour.

## How to play it
1. Select engine Strum (Granules) variation B (onset chains) OR Mosaic A (octave-up harmony loops).
2. Set Filter to noon (tame 2–4 kHz) and Mix to noon (dry banjo lead stays present).
3. Feed fingerpicked banjo.
