# Frozen Drone Bed — clock-face starting point

## Concept
A dial-it-now starting point for the Hold-Sampler frozen drone — sustained, oceanic, hold-and-evolve. Engine Tunnel B (sub-octave drone with filter sweeps) or Haze A, with a full clock-face chart. Feed a Hizumitas/VG-800 pad and freeze it into a continuous bed.

## How to play it
1. Select engine Tunnel B (or Haze A).
2. Dial: Activity 1:00–2:00 · Repeats 12:00 · Shape 12:00 · Filter 1:00 (slight roll-off) · Space 2:00–3:00 · Mix 1:00–2:00.
3. Confirm Global Config: Stereo in, Buffered + Trails.
4. Feed a Hizumitas/VG-800 pad, play a chord, hit HOLD to freeze.
5. Twist Activity over the drone; play lead on top with dry mixed in.
