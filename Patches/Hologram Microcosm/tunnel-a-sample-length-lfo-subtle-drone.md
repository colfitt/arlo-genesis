# Tunnel A Sample-Length-LFO Subtle Drone

## Concept
A gentler, subtler drone than Tunnel B — a slowly breathing sustained bed. In Tunnel A the drone sample length compresses and lengthens (a sample-length LFO), "really quite a nice subtle effect." Activity sets the depth of the modifier.

## How to play it
1. Select engine TUNNEL (Granules) variation A.
2. Use Activity to set the depth of the sample-length LFO.
3. Feed a sustained source for the slowly breathing bed.

## Notes
- Documented Tunnel A/C/D modifier map. The whole Tunnel family is the dossier's named drone engine.
- Watch the neighbors: Tunnel C = resonant bandpass ("a lot of resonance, be careful — can be fast, slow it down"); Tunnel D = envelope LFO, more bit-crush/noisy.
