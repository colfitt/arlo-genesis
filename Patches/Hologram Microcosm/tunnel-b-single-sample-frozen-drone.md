# Tunnel B Single-Sample Frozen Drone

## Concept
The cleanest sustained sub-octave drone on the pedal. Tunnel B "only processes the most recent sample regardless of where Activity is," so engaging HOLD sustains a single most-recent sample as a continuous drone — unlike Arp A, which arpeggiates multiple buffered notes. The dossier's single most on-aesthetic engine for baritone drone work.

## How to play it
1. Select engine TUNNEL (Granules) variation B (sub-octave drone with filter sweeps).
2. Play a baritone/fuzz-wall note or chord, then engage HOLD.
3. The freeze sustains the single most-recent sample as a continuous drone.
4. Roll Filter back to tame highs.
5. Bring Space up (Shift+Space → C Large Hall or D Ambient) for an oceanic bed.
