# Self-Replacing Infinite Slapback

## Concept
Build evolving sustained walls without a looper — play a phrase, get an infinite slapback of it; play something new and it ditches the old and repeats the new. Set up like a slapback delay with effectively infinite repeats, but content-replacing (Tunnel/Strum territory — "newest phrase wins").

## How to play it
1. Set up like a slapback delay with effectively infinite repeats, but in content-replacing territory (Tunnel/Strum).
2. Play a phrase to get an infinite slapback of it.
3. Play something new and the engine ditches the old and repeats the new.
