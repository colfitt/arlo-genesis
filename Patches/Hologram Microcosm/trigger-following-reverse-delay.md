# Trigger-Following Reverse Delay

## Concept
Reverse swells that follow your phrasing — each pluck triggers the reverse at the note onset instead of cycling on a fixed grid. With Reverse engaged on an envelope-triggered engine, the reverse delays "behave how a normal delay would" and follow your phrasing. A shoegaze owner's documented move for banjo-as-lead reverse swells and shoegaze walls.

## How to play it
1. Pick any envelope-triggered engine (e.g. Multidelay PATTERN A linear delay, or a Granules engine).
2. Engage REVERSE (press the selector knob in / CC 47).
3. Play INTO the pedal so each pluck triggers the reverse at the moment of onset.
4. Optionally add pitch-mod via Shift+Shape (rate) and Shift+Repeats (depth) for shimmer on the swells.

## Notes
- Control-state recipe: source gives engine class + Reverse ON + technique, NOT Activity/Time/Mix positions — dial those to taste.
- Concrete on the Reverse control and playing technique only.
