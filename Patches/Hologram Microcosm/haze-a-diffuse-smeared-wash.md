# Haze A Diffuse Smeared Wash

## Concept
The Board-2 smear sound — a diffuse, floaty wash from a sustained source. Haze A produces a short diffuse effect from stretching overlapping samples; engaging Reverse and reducing the playback speed pushes it into a slowed, smeared wash. Roll Filter back and bring Space up to push it floaty. Feeds the Etherealizer / Dark Star diffuser stage.

## How to play it
1. Select engine HAZE (Granules) variation A.
2. Engage REVERSE (press selector in / CC 47).
3. Reduce playback Speed (slowed).
4. Roll Filter back and bring Space up to push it floaty.
5. Feed it into QI Etherealizer + Dark Star V3.

## Notes
- REAL demo (Haze A + Reverse + changed playback speed all stated).
- Pull Dark Star back so it diffuses rather than double-granulates (avoid granular-into-granular mud).
