# Haze Grain-Wash Map (A/B/C/D)

## Concept
Glacial granular-reverb beds from a VG-800 pad — the cleanest "huge ambient bed." Haze with Activity setting grain density and spread:
- **A** = short diffused (stretch/overlap)
- **B** = many simultaneous randomized grains ("random diffuse," smoother)
- **C** = mix of normal + double-speed grains
- **D** = mix of normal + half-speed (octave down), "very much a grain wash," behaves like a granular delay/reverb

Haze B/D are the glacial-wash modes for the smear stage (Haze B is the §13c smear engine).

## How to play it
1. Select engine HAZE (Granules) and pick a variation A/B/C/D.
2. Use Activity to set grain density and spread.
3. On D especially, cycle Shape to find an envelope you like.
