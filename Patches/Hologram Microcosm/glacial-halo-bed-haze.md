# Glacial Halo Bed (Haze)

## Concept
An orchestral/electronic glacial wash AROUND a single close voice (the U.F.O.F. spectral side) — the Microcosm standing in for the U.F.O.F. spectral orchestral-electronic halo. Haze B or D (D = +half-speed octave-down body) with Mix modest so the wash sits behind the dry voice — halo, not hall. Hand it to the QI / Dark Star downstream.

## How to play it
1. Confirm Global Config baseline (Stereo in, Line, Buffered + Trails).
2. Select engine Haze (Granules) variation B or D (D adds half-speed octave-down body).
3. Dial: Activity ~1:00 · Shape ~1:00 (slow envelope) · Filter 11:00–12:00 · Space ~2:00 (mostly wet) · Mix ~11:00.
4. Keep Mix modest so the bed sits behind the dry voice.
