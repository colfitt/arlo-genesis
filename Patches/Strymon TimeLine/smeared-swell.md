# Smeared Swell

## Concept
A fast-rise, high-smear swell with no high-pass — a washy, full-range bloom. The washy end of the swell range.

## How to play it
1. Load 04B.
2. Play sustained material and let the fast rise bloom into a wash.
3. Add HP if the baritone gets muddy.

## Notes
- No HP = full-range bloom; add HIPASS if baritone gets muddy.
- SMEAR is a bar-graph (high), not a number.
