# Infinite Swell

## Concept
An auto-volume-swell pad where plucked notes "bow in" — the high-Smear ambient swell. Matching the dossier banjo-Swell note, Rise matched to delay time turns plucked banjo into bowed pads.

## How to play it
1. Load 26A.
2. Pick longer notes and leave space so each swell can bloom.
3. Let plucked banjo notes "bow in" via the rise.

## Notes
- RISE 0.50 is matched to the delay time for the bowed effect.
- SMEAR is a bar-graph (high), not a number.
