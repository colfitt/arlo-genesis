# Intercom

## Concept
A broken-transmission, "recorded-wrong" repeat built on the apartment-intercom gadget filter. It's a cleaner factory cousin of Corrupted Flowers — degraded but more controlled, sitting mostly dry so the texture sits under the part instead of taking over.

## How to play it
1. Load 25B.
2. Bring MIX up for a more obviously degraded repeat.
3. Use as a textured echo behind sustained notes/chords.

## Notes
- FILTER 8 is the apartment-intercom gadget filter — the load-bearing character.
- VINYL Off here; engage "S" (constant crackle) or "D" (crackle in repeats only) for record-player grit (see 8Bit Vinyl).
