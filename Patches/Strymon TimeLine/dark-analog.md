# Dark Analog

## Concept
Dark double-chip analog repeats — warm, baritone-friendly decay that won't fight the lows. The warmer alternative to a dTape wall when tape smear gets muddy under baritone.

## How to play it
1. Load 32A.
2. Nudge GRIT (Bucket Loss) up for more degradation.
3. Use under baritone where a dTape wall would muddy.

## Notes
- GRIT = Bucket Loss on the dBucket machine.
- RANGE Double engages the double-chip (darker, longer) path.
