# Debris

## Concept
A high-smear reverse — a diffuse backwards wash. The smear turns the backwards swells into a cloud rather than discrete reversed notes. On-aesthetic for the wall.

## How to play it
1. Load 20A.
2. Play sustained material so the smeared reverse builds into a cloud.

## Notes
- SMEAR is a bar-graph (high), not a number.
- The smeared counterpart to "Turn Around" (15B, SMEAR Off).
