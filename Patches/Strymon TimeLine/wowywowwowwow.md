# Wowywowwowwow

## Concept
The seasick end of the dTape range — extreme wow & flutter warble that turns a held chord into a queasy, smeared pad. The tape pitch is constantly drifting, so sustained material becomes its own warbling texture.

## How to play it
1. Load 03B.
2. Drop the banjo's fast decay into it and let the warble do the sustaining.
3. Hold chords/notes and let the wow/flutter modulate the pitch into the queasy pad.

## Notes
- TP SPD Fast pushes the warble rate up — this is the seasick extreme of the tape range.
- LO END is a bar-graph on the factory card (read "toward bass"), not a number.
- Pairs with Bokeh Bells, which pushes the same wow/flutter idea even further.
