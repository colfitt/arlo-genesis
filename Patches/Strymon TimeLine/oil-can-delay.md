# Oil Can Delay

## Concept
The on-aesthetic Filter-machine preset — a degraded, pitch-warbling vintage oil-can voice, a "wrong-machine" wobble under banjo/baritone. The filter-cutoff LFO is off; instead the delay pitch mod (Speed & Depth) is set to approximate the natural pitch vibrato of an oil-can delay.

## How to play it
1. Import the free `.syx` via Nixie 2 (no `.syx` on disk; transcribed setting).
2. Keep LFO DEPTH OFF (no filter-cutoff modulation).
3. Set SPEED & DEPTH to approximate the oil-can pitch vibrato.
4. Play banjo/baritone through it for the seasick wobble.

## Notes
- Near dTape's Wow & Flutter, but on the Filter engine — a different flavor of seasick.
- TIME / MIX / REPEATS / filter-cutoff are image/.syx-only and not captured.
