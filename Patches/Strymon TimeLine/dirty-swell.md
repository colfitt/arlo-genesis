# Dirty Swell

## Concept
A gritty auto volume-swell pad — the dirty cousin of Infinite/Smeared Swell. In Gilardini's chain it's paired with a long reverb and a wah. Add SMEAR + HIPASS to it for a bowed banjo-as-lead pad with dirt.

## How to play it
1. Import the free `.syx` via Nixie 2 (no `.syx` on disk; transcribed setting).
2. Crank GRIT a bit for the crunchy/creamy character.
3. Add SMEAR + HIPASS for a bowed banjo-as-lead pad.
4. Pair with a long reverb (and optionally a wah) downstream.

## Notes
- RISE / SMEAR / HIPASS / TIME are image/.syx-only and not captured — GRIT is the documented load-bearing move.
