# Silent-loop Ice drone method

## Concept
Turn the TimeLine into a self-contained drone machine. Build a silent loop, volume-swell smeared Ice repeats into it so a bed accumulates from nothing, then hold the loop running and play live over the top. The canonical drone method for this aesthetic — pairs with the Frippertronics dTape layer as the over-layer.

## How to play it
1. Set the looper globals: **LP LOC = Post** (loop includes the wet tail), **Loop Exit = Play** (keeps running on exit).
2. Load Ice (+5th, very smeared — see "Melt Away / Fizifths").
3. Enter the looper → Record, count 8 beats of silence.
4. Overdub onto the silence → volume-swell the Ice repeats in (play longer than one loop so layers blend).
5. Set Play → hold TAP to exit with the loop still running.
6. Switch to the dTape Frippertronics preset and play over the held bed.

## Notes
- Played on baritone in the video.
- LP LOC = Post is what lets the wet Ice tail get captured into the loop.
