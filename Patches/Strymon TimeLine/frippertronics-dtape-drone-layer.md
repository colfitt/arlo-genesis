# Frippertronics dTape drone layer

## Concept
A grungy, Frippertronics-style tape layer played on top of a held loop: longest delay time, lots of repeats, and heavy tape decay/fuzz/grunge so each pass smears further into a fuzzy tape wall. This is the canonical drone-wall move for the rig — the over-layer you play across the silent-loop Ice drone.

## How to play it
1. Set TIME to its longest (~2.5 s) and push REPEATS up; dial in heavy tape decay/grunge.
2. Run an overdrive in front (the video chain: baritone → comp → fuzzy Sunset → volume → TimeLine).
3. Play this over the silent-loop Ice drone (see "Silent-loop Ice drone method").

## Notes
- Built to sit on top of a held loop, not as a standalone delay — the loop sustains, this layer fuzzes over it.
- Pairs directly with the silent-loop Ice drone method as the over-layer.
