# Low Voltage

## Concept
A long double-chip "dying battery" analog decay — the longest, darkest factory dBucket. It sags and degrades as it trails, which is exactly what you want under a drone.

## How to play it
1. Load 27B.
2. Hold sustained chords/notes under a drone and let the repeats sag and darken.

## Notes
- The longest, darkest factory dBucket; RANGE Double for the extended dying-battery trail.
