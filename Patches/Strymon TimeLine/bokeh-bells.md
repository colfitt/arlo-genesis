# Bokeh Bells

## Concept
Strymon's official free dTape preset, described by Ethan Tufts as "a machine in desperate need of service, with a warbled wash of echoes." It's the Degraded wall recipe pushed to the wow/flutter extreme — built around a bell-like synth in the demo, but a baritone chord dropped in turns the warble into a sustained seasick pad.

## How to play it
1. Import the free `.syx` via Nixie 2 (no `.syx` files are stored on disk; this is a transcribed setting).
2. Drop a baritone chord in and let the warble become a sustained, seasick pad.
3. Ride REPEATS to thicken the wash.

## Notes
- Wow & Flutter (DEPTH) maxed + Tape Crinkle (SPEED) near-max + Tape Age (FILTER) ~2:00 is the copyable core; exact TIME and the remaining bar-graphs are image/.syx-only on the Strymon blog and were not captured.
- Sits next to Wowywowwowwow (03B) as the deliberately-broken tape voice.
