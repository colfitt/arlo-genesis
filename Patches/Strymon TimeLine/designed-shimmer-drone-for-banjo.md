# Designed — Shimmer drone for banjo

## Concept
Ice dialed from scratch for banjo-as-lead pads: swell into notes with guitar volume so the Ice floats in as the repeats regenerate. The HP at 160 Hz is the fix that keeps the bright banjo from ice-picking.

## How to play it
1. Set INTRVL +Octave (or +Oct & 5th), SLICE Medium, BLEND toward Dry.
2. REPEATS 3:00, MIX 11–12:00, DEPTH 9–10:00 for movement, HP 160 Hz.
3. Swell into each note with guitar volume so the Ice blooms in as repeats build.
