# Turn Around

## Concept
A long backwards swell for big chords and slow ambient. Best on sustained chords — blend a washy reverb downstream and the reversed swells smear beautifully (Rhett Shull's reverse tip).

## How to play it
1. Load 15B.
2. Play sustained chords and let each note swell in backwards.
3. Run a washy reverb after it to smear the reversed swells.

## Notes
- SMEAR Off keeps the reversed notes more discrete; see "Debris" (20A) for the high-smear cloud version.
