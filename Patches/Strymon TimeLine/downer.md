# Downer

## Concept
The inverse of the octave-up shimmer: −octave Ice for darker, sub-octave pitched repeats that add doom weight. Sits great under a baritone drone.

## How to play it
1. Load 44A.
2. Play sustained chords/notes so the sub-octave repeats build weight underneath.
3. For a sinking pitch, see Joel Gilardini's "Descent" (slow downward Ice drift).

## Notes
- BLEND is a bar-graph (toward dry), not a number.
- Cousin of Gilardini's "Descent" if you want the pitch to sink over time.
