# Da Funk Portamento Mono Lead

## Concept

Daft Punk "Da Funk" — the distorted, band-passed, glided mono lead (the overdriven-guitar-like synth line, originally an MS-20/Juno into a Fat Cat distortion). A designed-to-emulate patch translating the move onto the Field's engines by ear, since no factory/artist OP-1 patches exist for it. Print to **Porta** tape for the degrade lean; the banjo (VG-E1) can carry the same line.

## How to play it

1. Use **Voltage** (AM/biting) or **Digital** for the band-passed squelch.
2. Turn **portamento / GLIDE ON** — the Da Funk slide moves in perfect fourths.
3. Set a short filter-env "wah" attack.
4. FX **nitro** on for overdriven-guitar grit; **value LFO** for a touch of movement; **Octave 0**.
5. Play mono. Print to **Porta** tape for the degrade lean.

## Notes

- Engine/octave/FX/LFO are op1.fun-granularity; per-knob arrays dial by ear.
- Designed-to-emulate (no factory/artist OP-1 patch exists). Banjo (VG-E1) can carry the same line.
