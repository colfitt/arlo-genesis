# Opines — slot_piano (chiptune sparkle lead)

## Concept

A chiptune lead-piano voice; the reference delay + random-LFO sparkle patch in Cuckoo's "Opines" pack. A REAL Cuckoo patch (op1.fun-verified; per-knob numbers not on disk). It exemplifies the pack's pattern: delay is the default FX, leads sit at +1, basses at −2.

## How to play it

1. Load slot_piano (Dr Wave, Octave +1, delay, random LFO).
2. Play sparkly lead-piano lines — the random LFO scatters the sparkle.
3. The delay is the pack-default FX; keep it for the chiptune echo feel.
