# Around-the-World Acid-Pluck Bass

## Concept

Daft Punk "Around the World" — the bouncing sawtooth acid-pluck bass, a short repeating figure whose filter opens over the build. A designed-to-emulate patch translating the move onto the Field's engines by ear. The part doesn't change; the filter does.

## How to play it

1. Use **Cluster** or **Dr Wave** at **Octave −1/−2** for the saw character.
2. Set a short **DECAY / no SUSTAIN** envelope (the "subtle pluck").
3. Filter **cutoff low + resonance high** (the acid signature).
4. FX **filter** or **nitro**.
5. Sequence a short repeating bass figure; ride the filter **open** over a build.

## Notes

- The recreation ADSR/filter numbers come from a Logic/TAL tutorial (sawtooth, low cutoff, high reso, attack 1 / decay 6 / no sustain pluck) — translate by ear on the OP-1.
- Designed-to-emulate; no factory/artist OP-1 patch exists.
