# celestial — evolving celestial ambient lead

## Concept

An evolving celestial ambient lead/pad voice. Voltage (the AM / biting engine) is "surprisingly good for evolving sounds," and the Punch FX gives it lo-fi / SP-303-ish grit. 18.1k downloads.

## How to play it

1. Load celestial (Voltage, Octave +1, Punch on).
2. Play sustained, slowly-evolving lead/pad lines.
3. Turn on the **random LFO** for shimmer.

## Notes

- Voltage is the AM/biting engine; Punch FX = lo-fi SP-303-ish grit.
- The random LFO is present but off by default — engage it for the evolving shimmer.
