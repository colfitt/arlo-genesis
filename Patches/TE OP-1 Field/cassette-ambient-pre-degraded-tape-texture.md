# Cassette Ambient — pre-degraded tape texture

## Concept

A drop-in "recorded-wrong" bed with baked-in cassette degradation, played chromatically — instant lo-fi. The sample (44.1 kHz, 6.0 s) was resampled off a Sony Walkman plus an analog synth, so the cassette character is already in the audio. Punch + tremolo add motion. 24k downloads. Car Seat Headrest / Modest Mouse demo-crunch fit.

## How to play it

1. Load Cassette Ambient (Sampler, Octave +1, Punch on, tremolo on).
2. Play chromatically — the cassette wow/flutter and hiss come for free.
3. Use as an instant lo-fi bed under a drone or lead.

## Notes

- A sampler patch carrying real cassette degradation — the degradation is in the sample, not an FX.
- Punch FX + tremolo LFO add motion on top.
