# Bon Iver — FM radio capture (found-sound)

## Concept

Sampling FM radio for found-sound texture — native on the Field thanks to its built-in FM receiver. Raw material to chop and mangle. Bon Iver layered a muffled callsign from 105.7 FM WCFW on *22, A Million*. Art-rock studio-as-instrument / Postal Service found-sound territory.

## How to play it

1. In the sampling menu, set the sample source to **RADIO** (BLUE encoder).
2. Tune a station with the BLUE encoder; **press the radio encoder IN to auto-seek**.
3. If the bare RX is weak, **plug a cable into REC IN as an antenna**.
4. Sample, then chop/mangle the capture.
