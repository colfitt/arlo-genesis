# Opines — bass_buzzer

## Concept

A clean sub/bass voice following Cuckoo's "basses at oct −2" convention. A dry foundation to sit under a busier drone. A REAL Cuckoo "Opines" patch — it establishes the pack's "basses at −2, leads at +1" rule.

## How to play it

1. Load bass_buzzer (Dr Wave, Octave −2, no FX, no LFO).
2. Play clean bass lines, or hold a low note as a dry foundation.
3. Layer a busier drone or lead on top.

## Notes

- REAL Cuckoo "Opines" patch; dry by design (no FX, no LFO).
- The clean low-end anchor of the pack.
