# Fazer — hidden phaser (Close Encounters unlock)

## Concept

Firmware 1.6 hides a phaser effect, **Fazer**, unlocked by playing the five-note Close Encounters of the Third Kind motif. Once unlocked it's a lush swirling master-FX phaser for pads and electric-piano patches — a flavor the standard master-FX list doesn't otherwise offer. It is distinct from the amp-engine T3 phaser: Fazer is a master-FX easter egg, not an input-engine effect.

## How to play it

1. On firmware **1.6+**, play the motif: **G → A → (next octave) G → A → D**.
2. Watch for the **alien animation** confirming Fazer is unlocked.
3. Apply **Fazer** to a pad or electric-piano patch.
4. Set a **slow rate** and **moderate depth** for a classic swirling sweep; add feedback to taste.

## Notes

- The unlock motif is the famous five-tone phrase from *Close Encounters of the Third Kind*.
- Distinct from the amp-engine T3 phaser — Fazer is a master-FX easter egg, not an input-engine effect.
- Requires firmware **1.6+**; once unlocked the effect persists.
- **No published values.** The unlock sequence is published, but Fazer's rate/depth/feedback positions are not — treat the per-knob values above as a dialable recipe, not sourced numbers.
