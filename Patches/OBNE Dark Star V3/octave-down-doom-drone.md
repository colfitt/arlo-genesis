# Octave-down doom drone (interstellar grumbles)

## Concept
Subterranean doom-pad weight, especially on the baritone Jazzmaster. "Double down on lower pitches and hear the interstellar grumbles." Both shifters octave-down, high Decay and Multiply build a dense, evolving texture, with the Filter rolled toward LPF to control the low-end build.

## How to play it
1. Set Pitch 1 and Pitch 2 both CCW (octave-down).
2. Set Decay high and Multiply high for a dense, evolving texture.
3. Roll Filter toward LPF (below noon) to control the low-end build.
4. Watch for self-oscillation — high Multiply + high Decay tips into it.

## Notes
- russomusic explicitly flags self-oscillation at high Multiply + Decay.
- Pair with a sustained-fuzz wall upstream (Hizumitas / Longsword, Board 1).
- Home turf for this rig; suggested onboard slot as a baritone doom recall.
