# OBNE SOUND 2 — octave-down + Lag + HPF cleanup

## Concept
OBNE's own demonstrated V3 build: keep the octave-down character but lose the weight, with predelay separation. Pitch 1 at -1 octave, Lag introduced for space between playing and reverb, and the Filter set to HPF (above noon) to clear the mud — you keep the octave-down character but not the build-up. Spread moves the pitches in the field.

## How to play it
1. Set Pitch 1 to -1 octave.
2. Introduce Lag for predelay separation between playing and reverb.
3. Set Filter to HPF (above noon) to clear the mud.
4. Use Spread to move the pitches in the stereo field.

## Notes
- The HPF-on-octave-down combo is a documented OBNE move to keep low pitch without mud.
- Manufacturer build — directional, not numeric.
