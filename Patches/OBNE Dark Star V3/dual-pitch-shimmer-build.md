# Dual-pitch shimmer build (Multiply spiral)

## Concept
Ascending shimmer with a chorusy "drop": both shifters set to +1 octave with a slight detune *between* the two for the chorusy quality, plus a little Multiply (regeneration) so the octaves spiral upward. The LPF side of the Filter keeps the regenerating pitches from escaping.

## How to play it
1. Set Pitch 1 to +1 oct (L) and Pitch 2 to +1 oct (R), detuned a hair from each other for the chorusy drop.
2. Add a little Multiply so the octaves regenerate and spiral upward.
3. Roll the Filter to the LPF side (below noon) to tame runaway pitches.
4. Keep Multiply moderate — too much and it escapes into self-oscillation.

## Notes
- Octave-up content ice-picks over banjo — favor LPF and keep Multiply moderate.
- Fits the shoegaze lane; on banjo, lean on the LPF.
