# Stereo Mayhem (±2-oct extreme cluster)

## Concept
The most extreme voice on disk: a 4-octave split orchestral/seasick cluster, the degraded-wall lane pushed to chaos. Pitch 1 at +2 octaves (full CW) and Pitch 2 at -2 octaves (full CCW), Spread maxed. High Decay plus some Multiply intensifies the cluster.

## How to play it
1. Set Pitch 1 to +2 octaves (full CW), Pitch 2 to -2 octaves (full CCW).
2. Max the Spread.
3. Dial Decay, Multiply, Crush, Filter, Mix to taste — high Decay + some Multiply intensifies the cluster.
4. Tame the ice-pick with the LPF side of Filter; watch Multiply for runaway self-oscillation.

## Notes
- The +2-oct side is ice-picky on banjo — tame with the LPF side of Filter.
- Watch Multiply for runaway self-oscillation.
- A performance extreme; live mode. On-aesthetic for the orchestral/seasick wall.
