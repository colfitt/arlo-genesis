# Ambient Rescue (named infinite-trail freeze)

## Concept
The freeze move stated as a named retailer patch: drop a banjo roll, freeze into an infinite-trail bed, then solo over the static pad. Lag low for a short predelay, Multiply at mid to keep it from self-oscillating, Mix high. Set Decay/Pitch/Crush/Filter to your desired bed voicing *before* freezing.

## How to play it
1. Assign Aux = Hold and set the bed voicing (Decay, Pitch, Crush, Filter) first.
2. Set Lag low (short predelay), Multiply mid (~noon), Mix high.
3. Drop a banjo roll, then engage Aux to freeze into infinite trails.
4. Solo over the static bed.

## Notes
- Multiply at mid keeps it from tipping into self-oscillation.
- Same bone-dry-Hold caveat as the Freeze bed — new notes pass dry; use downstream verb for the lead.
- Store as a preset with Aux assigned to Hold.
