# 100%-wet bit-crushed drone

## Concept
A standalone "recorded-wrong" drone — no dry signal, just a bit-crushed reverb bed. "A bit-crushed drone on guitar." Mix is full wet, Crush sits below noon on the lighter side so you hear the sample-rate reduction without it dominating, and Volume comes up to make up the level lost at full-wet Mix.

## How to play it
1. Set Mix to 100% wet (full CW).
2. Set Crush below noon, kept toward the lighter side so the crush colors without dominating.
3. Tune Pitch first using the "a in Dark" unity trick before committing.
4. Raise Volume to make up the level lost at full-wet Mix.

## Notes
- Attribution flag: demonstrated on the **original mono** Dark Star in Crush mode; mapped here to V3 Crush-below-noon + Mix 100% wet.
- Lighter sample-rate reduction = musical; heavy = "spooky / sci-fi."
- Suggested onboard slot as a standalone drone recall.
