# Expression → Lag (sunbaked lo-fi tape modulation)

## Concept
The Pedal Zone demonstrator's favorite move: auto tape-style wow/flutter wobble on the predelay. Assign Expression to Lag and drive the expression input from a random LFO / ramper wave for "beautiful, slightly sunbaked lo-fi tape modulation." Manual Lag-wiggle also produces chorus.

## How to play it
1. Assign Expression = Lag — hold On/Off, sweep Lag heel→toe, then release.
2. Drive the expression input from a random LFO / ramper wave rather than a foot pedal.
3. Listen for the sunbaked tape wow/flutter on the predelay.

## Notes
- Other expression ideas from the same source: Expression → Filter = cinematic auto sweeps; Expression → Mix = tremolo-like (combine with Aux momentary pitch).
- Tape-flutter wobble is dead-on for the lo-fi lane.
- Expression assignment saves per preset.
