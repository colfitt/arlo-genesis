# Overdriven stereo reverb (Crush-CW "best sound on offer")

## Concept
The under-used CLOCKWISE half of Crush: a soft-clipping overdrive baked into the reverb tail, full-frequency in stereo. guitar.com calls it "the absolute best sound on offer here." Pair with high Decay for a saturated wall. The OD side is new versus the mono original, which only crushed.

## How to play it
1. Turn Crush CW (above noon) — soft-clipping overdrive into the tail, gain increasing as you turn.
2. Use Stereo routing for the full-frequency stereo blast.
3. Set Decay high to build a saturated wall.

## Notes
- The OD half of Crush is new vs the mono original (which only crushed).
- Fits the saturated-wall aesthetic; live mode or an onboard slot.
