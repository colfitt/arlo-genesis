# OBNE SOUND 1 — lo-fi octave-up soup

## Concept
OBNE's own demonstrated V3 build: a soupy, lo-fi, octave-up wash — roughly equivalent to chaining three mono Dark Stars (delay + pitch-up + crush). A little Multiply emulates the delay-mode soupiness, both shifters at +1 octave, and a little Crush below noon for the lo-fi quality.

## How to play it
1. Add a little Multiply to emulate the delay-mode soupiness.
2. Set Pitch 1 and Pitch 2 both to +1 octave.
3. Pull Crush a little below noon for the lo-fi quality.
4. Favor the LPF side of Filter if the octave-up content ice-picks over banjo.

## Notes
- This is OBNE's own published build — directional, not a saved factory preset and not numeric.
- Octave-up can ice-pick over banjo — favor LPF.
- Suggested onboard slot as an OBNE-blessed lo-fi recall.
