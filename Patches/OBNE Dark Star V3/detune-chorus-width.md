# Detune / chorus width (a smidge off-notch)

## Concept
Modulation and chorus width without committing to a musical interval, using the center Detune zone of the pitch knobs. Pitch 1 sits just a smidge either side of the center notch (in the wide, unquantized Detune zone), and Pitch 2 mirrors it then nudges a hair off for chorused width. Wiggling Lag also adds chorus.

## How to play it
1. Nudge Pitch 1 a smidge off the center notch into the Detune zone (MIDI CC ~42-85).
2. Mirror Pitch 2 to Pitch 1, then nudge Pitch 2 a hair off for chorused width.
3. Wiggle the Lag knob to add extra chorus motion (an OBNE official walkthrough move).

## Notes
- The Detune zone is unquantized and wide; the rest of the Pitch travel snaps in semitones.
- Lag-wiggle-as-chorus is from the OBNE official walkthrough.
- Chorused width is the rig's degraded-pad signature.
