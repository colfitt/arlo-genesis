# Lo-fi / crushed wash (fewer bits, broken)

## Concept
The "recorded-wrong" degraded reverb — the control that makes Dark Star read broken/lo-fi rather than hi-fi ambient. Crush below noon is the original Dark Star's sample-rate reduction mode; the more CCW, the more broken. Paired with LPF and long Decay for the classic V1-style lo-fi direction. AndyDemos adds dual detune for motion.

## How to play it
1. Set Crush CCW (below noon) — fewer bits the further you go.
2. Roll Filter to LPF (below noon) and set Decay long.
3. For motion, add dual detune on Pitch 1 and Pitch 2 (AndyDemos).
4. Push Crush further CCW for a more broken character.

## Notes
- Below noon = the original Dark Star's Crush mode (sample-rate reduction).
- The QI Etherealizer → Dark Star lo-fi terminus; squarely on-aesthetic.
