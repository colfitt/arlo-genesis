# Orchestral Halo Freeze Bed

## Concept
A static orchestral halo bed (the U.F.O.F. spectral side): build a wide opposed-octave pad, freeze a banjo roll / VG pad, then solo the banjo over it. DOUG-designed to chase the Bon Iver / Sufjan orchestral-electronic wash around one intimate voice.

## How to play it
1. Build a wide pad: Pitch 1 = +1 oct, Pitch 2 = -1 oct (opposed split = orchestral spread; or both detuned for a chorused cloud).
2. Set Decay high, Lag low, Multiply ~noon, Mix high, Crush ~11:00 (faint lo-fi).
3. Assign Aux = Hold.
4. Play a banjo roll / VG pad, freeze into a static halo bed, then solo over it.

## Notes
- Hold passes new notes BONE-DRY — fine here: the dry banjo lead sits in front (the close-voice-over-halo balance).
- Maps to Dark Star sheet patches Freeze bed / Frozen banjo bed.
- Store as a preset with Aux assigned to Hold.
