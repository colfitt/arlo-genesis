# Stacked-octave pad (shimmer + sub at once)

## Concept
The V3 headline move the mono original could not do: a shimmer voice AND a sub voice in a single pad. Pitch 1 (+1 oct) feeds the Left out and Pitch 2 (-1 oct) feeds the Right out, so the two octaves spread across the stereo field at once.

## How to play it
1. Set Pitch 1 to +1 octave and Pitch 2 to -1 octave simultaneously.
2. For chorused width instead of a hard interval, stay in the center Detune zone on either knob.
3. Dial Filter, Crush, and Decay to taste.
4. Hold a chord and let the two octaves bloom on opposite sides.

## Notes
- Pitch 1 = Left out, Pitch 2 = Right out — the octaves spread L/R.
- Either octave polarity works (see "Pad Factory" for the reversed split).
- A flagship "this is the pedal" recall; suggested onboard slot 1.
