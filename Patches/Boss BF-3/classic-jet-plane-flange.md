# Classic Jet-Plane Flange

## Concept
The textbook jet-plane flange — the dramatic talking whoosh that every flanger is judged on. All knobs sit at noon while RES is cranked to roughly 3–4:00 to push the resonance into that screaming, spectrum-sweeping rush. In this rig it's the literal jet-swell-on-the-wall target: engage it on a sustained fuzz wall for a dramatic sweep across the whole frequency range.

## How to play it
1. Set MODE to Standard, MANUAL/DEPTH/RATE all to 12:00, RES cranked to ~3–4:00.
2. Build the dirt stack into a sustained wall first.
3. Engage the BF-3 and let the comb sweep talk across the spectrum.

## Notes
- Watch output when feeding the Somersault — high RES + fuzz is additive gain. Trim downstream.
- The single most-copyable jet recipe on disk (spoken while playing by Messano).
