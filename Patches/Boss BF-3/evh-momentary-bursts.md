# EVH Momentary Bursts

## Concept
Flange that exists only while you hold the footswitch down — Van-Halen-style on-demand bursts for a single phrase, note, or the last chord of a song. There's no double-tap and no tap-tempo in this mode; the effect is purely momentary. Flanging starts in the low end (Standard voicing) and drops the instant you release.

## How to play it
1. Set MODE to Momentary, keep the Classic-Jet knobs (MANUAL 12:00, DEPTH 12:00, RATE 12:00, RES ~3–4:00).
2. Hold the pedal switch for the phrase you want flanged.
3. Release to drop the effect — it's on ONLY while depressed.

## Notes
- The EVH reference is the technique only — EVH used an MXR M117, NOT a BF-3; Momentary mode merely recreates the move. Not a BF-3 artist credit.
