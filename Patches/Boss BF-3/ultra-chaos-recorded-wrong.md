# Ultra Chaos (Recorded-Wrong)

## Concept
The "broken," metallic, ring-mod-adjacent scoop that sounds like the signal was captured on the wrong machine — self-oscillating against the fuzz feedback. Ultra mode with maxed RES and DEPTH full CW pushes the flanger into its deepest, most ferocious voice. The signature doom/art-rock degrade texture: pair it into the granular pedals downstream for full collapse.

## How to play it
1. Set MODE to Ultra, RATE 1–2:00, DEPTH full CW, RES maxed (5:00).
2. Ignore MANUAL — it has no function at max DEPTH (this is expected, not a fault).
3. Engage against a sustained fuzz wall so it self-oscillates and scoops; feed into the granular pedals to degrade further.

## Notes
- Designed — highest signature-fit (art-rock glitch + lo-fi "recorded-wrong" + post-punk brittle).
- MANUAL is dead at max DEPTH by design — not broken.
- Big additive level spike: trim hard into the Somersault/CE-2W.
- BOSS officially endorses Ultra as the deeper/more-ferocious voice.
