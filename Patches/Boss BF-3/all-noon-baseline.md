# All-Noon Baseline

## Concept
The closest thing the BF-3 has to a factory default (the pedal stores no presets). Standard mode with MANUAL, RES, DEPTH, and RATE all at 12:00 gives a balanced, moving comb filter on whatever's in front of it — a clean reference flange that announces itself but doesn't overwhelm. This is the patch you set the pedal to when you want to confirm it's working or find center before dialing somewhere else.

## How to play it
1. Set MODE to Standard, every knob to 12:00, GUITAR IN, OUTPUT A.
2. Tap-tempo off — let the RATE knob run free.
3. Play into it and listen for the moving comb sweep; tweak from here toward whatever patch you want.

## Notes
- Guitar Chalk: "sounds fantastic out of the box, with all settings at 12 o'clock."
- Use this as the home position; most other BF-3 recipes are described as moves away from all-noon.
