# Wes-Borland Laser Swell

## Concept
Maxed RES with a slow RATE yields a screaming, sustained resonant comb peak — the "laser" picking swell that pairs with tape delay. On the fuzz wall it turns into a vocal, talking resonant scream, squarely in art-rock/post-punk lead-texture territory.

## How to play it
1. Set MODE to Standard, MANUAL ~2:00, RES maxed (5:00), RATE ~9:00, DEPTH ~12:00.
2. Pick swells into it; the maxed resonance sustains a screaming comb peak.
3. Pair with tape delay downstream for the full laser lead.

## Notes
- Community recipe (cited), NOT an artist credit — no evidence Borland used a BF-3; the name is the cookbook author's flavor label. Trust the numbers, distrust the attribution.
- Maxed RES = strong additive gain on a sustained input — trim downstream.
- Knob values converted from Guitar Chalk's 1–10 scale (1≈7:00, 5≈12:00, 10≈5:00).
