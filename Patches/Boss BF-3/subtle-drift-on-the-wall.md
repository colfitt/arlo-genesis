# Subtle Drift on the Wall

## Concept
Slow, breathing movement that keeps a saturated wall intact instead of announcing itself — the always-on "flanging chorus" bloom for drone and shoegaze beds. Low RES, very slow RATE, and moderate DEPTH give a gentle, set-and-forget drift over a Hizumitas/Longsword wall. This is the BF-3's most defensible role in the rig: subtle motion on the saturated source, set once and left to breathe.

## How to play it
1. Set MODE to Standard, RES low (~8–9:00), RATE as slow as possible while still audibly moving (~7–8:00), DEPTH 10:00–12:00, MANUAL ~11:00.
2. Build the dirt stack into a sustained wall first.
3. Engage the BF-3 once the wall is already sustaining so it gently breathes underneath rather than sweeping.

## Notes
- Post-punk/shoegaze "flanger all the time" drift (Swans/Mogwai energy).
- MANUAL value (~11:00) is rig-inferred — BOSS leaves it unstated in the source Tape Warble recipe.
