# The Analog Man (Mike's Sound)

## Concept
The flagship always-on starter — Mike Piera's (Analog Man) own preferred King-of-Tone settings, shipped as factory Preset 1. The honest marquee "user" setting for this pedal: Channel 1 does transparent OVERDRIVE work as the always-on voice, while Channel 2 sits parked as a BOOST lift you kick on when you need more. A solid daily driver.

## How to play it
1. Leave Channel 1 (OVERDRIVE, HI GAIN 1 on) running as the always-on voice.
2. Kick on Channel 2 (BOOST) when you need a lift.
3. If popping the Ch2 kick jumps stage level, turn the MASTER dip ON so VOL 2 becomes the global master.

## Notes
- Modes and "HI GAIN 1 on" are quoted/documented; all clock-face numbers are inferred starting points (carried from dossier §13) — the Brothers AM manual prints panel diagrams and relative directions, not numeric clock values, so no exact numeric knob settings exist in any primary source.
- In this rig it's a fine starting point but not the rig-optimal role — see "2-IN-1" and the designed Post-Muff Tightener for the post-Muff job.
