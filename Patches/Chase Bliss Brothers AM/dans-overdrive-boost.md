# Dan's Overdrive Boost

## Concept
Dan Steinhardt's (That Pedal Show) decade-long, road-tested King-of-Tone setting: OVERDRIVE on Channel 1 always on, with Channel 2 in BOOST used purely to add harmonics plus a small mid lift when kicked in. A transparent, reliable always-on for guitar/baritone, and a great "honest user" baseline before tailoring for the modeled VG-800 source.

## How to play it
1. Run Channel 1 (OVERDRIVE) always on as the core voice.
2. Kick Channel 2 (BOOST) in for harmonics and a small mid lift — VOL 2 only ~1 o'clock; Dan keeps it small, not a big volume jump.
3. If the tone sounds closed-in or chewy, bring PRESENCE UP "quite considerably" — Dan's on-air "changed the game" move (raise via hold-footswitch + TONE).
4. MASTER dip ON recommended so the Ch2 kick stays level-controlled.
