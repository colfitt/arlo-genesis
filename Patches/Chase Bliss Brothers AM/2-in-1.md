# 2-IN-1 (always-on OD + footswitch push)

## Concept
The literal factory model for this rig's role: Channel 2 as an always-on overdrive tightener, with Channel 1 parked as a footswitchable BOOST to push it "beyond its limits" (and to clean-push the Longsword). This is the manual's "OVERLOADER" idea saved as a preset, and the closest factory match to the rig's always-on-tightener + footswitch-push template.

## How to play it
1. Run Channel 2 (OVERDRIVE) always on as the tightener; level-match VOL 2 to the Hizumitas-engaged signal.
2. Kick Channel 1 (BOOST) on for more bite — VOL 1 sets how hard it slams Ch2 / lifts level.
3. Keep MASTER dip ON so popping Ch1 in doesn't throw stage level (fixes the VOL-1 level-snap trap).
4. Nudge PRESENCE 2 up slightly (hold Ch2 footswitch + turn TONE 2) to recover banjo cut.
