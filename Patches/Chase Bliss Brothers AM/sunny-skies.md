# Sunny Skies (full 3-circuit stack)

## Concept
The "mixture of everything" factory preset — all three circuits in series for a bright, focused, charged voice: treble boost into DISTORTION (Ch1) into OVERDRIVE (Ch2). Shows off what the full `BOOST → CHANNEL 1 → CHANNEL 2` series stack does. Bright and forward; useful when you want the AM to actually be the dirt rather than just shape the Muff.

## How to play it
1. Engage both channels with the treble booster on — Ch1 feeds Ch2.
2. VOL 1 at ~11 sets the push into Channel 2.
3. Expect a level jump vs P1 (distortion clips differently) — rebalance with VOL 2 or set the MASTER dip ON.
4. If it sounds chewy, raise PRESENCE 2.

## Notes
- Modes are quoted; all knob clocks are inferred starting points (manual prints diagrams, not numbers).
- CB lists the treble boost as ON for this preset — pick DOWN/classic Rangemaster or UP/bright per taste, but not MIDDLE.
