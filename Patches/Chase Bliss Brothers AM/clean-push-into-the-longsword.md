# Clean Push into the Longsword

## Concept
A clean, footswitchable lift that drives the EAE Longsword CLEANLY — more cut and level, not more fuzz-on-fuzz. Built on the KOT principle that a clean boost AFTER a dirt pedal increases volume without adding much distortion. It layers on top of the always-on Post-Muff Tightener for solos and wall builds: patch 8 is your bed, this is the one-stomp lead/wall lift.

## How to play it
1. Keep Channel 2 (the Tightener: BOOST / low OD) on as your bed.
2. Park Channel 1 (BOOST, GAIN 1 ~9 clean) OFF; kick it in for the push, with VOL 1 ~1–2 setting the push amount into the Longsword.
3. Keep the MASTER dip ON so the Ch1 kick is a controlled lift, not a level explosion.
4. Keep the Longsword's OWN gain low downstream and let the AM supply cut + level.

## Notes
- Designed starting points, not found settings.
- Pairs with the Oxford treated as a one-button "brick everything" rather than a tone shaper.
- Watch the VOL-1 level-snap trap; MASTER on is what tames it.
- Slot: intended as a MIDI preset (suggested).
