# Bad Bros (max wall)

## Concept
"As big and brash as Brothers AM gets" — both channels stacked and cranked, both HI GAIN dips on. The overflowing-distortion factory preset, and a doom/wall candidate when you want max brash with no subtlety.

## How to play it
1. Engage both channels with everything high and the treble booster UP/bright to cut through the wall.
2. VOL 1 at ~1–2 is a heavy push into Channel 2.
3. Keep the MASTER dip ON so VOL 2 acts as master and level stays sane.

## Notes
- Modes/dips are quoted; all knob clocks are inferred starting points.
- RIG CAUTION — stacking this much AM distortion onto an already-squished Muff signal compounds compression fast and narrows cleanup. For the rig's wall, the designed "Recorded-Wrong Re-Clip" / "Ramped Drone Swell" patches are usually better-behaved. Keep this one for max-brash, no-subtlety moments.
