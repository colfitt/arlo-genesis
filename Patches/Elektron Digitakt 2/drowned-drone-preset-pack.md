# DROWNED! for Digitakt II — drone preset pack

## Concept
The single most on-target DT2 pack for this rig: 50 drone presets for drone/doom/shoegaze "recorded-wrong" walls, each shipping in a CLEAN (meditative, evolving) and a HEADCRUSHER ("like a guitar orchestra from the depths of hell") variant, plus 50 stereo source samples. DT2-native, built on the patch-9 creator method.

## How to play it
1. Load via Elektron Transfer → +Drive.
2. 50 drone presets, each in CLEAN (meditative evolving) + HEADCRUSHER variants + 50 stereo source samples.
3. DT2-native (3 LFOs + Comb); method = the patch-9 technique (DROWNED! Creator's Drone Method).
4. Note: OG version is samples only.
