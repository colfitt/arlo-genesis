# Save-Trig-as-Preset (capture a degraded state)

## Concept
The load-bearing move for the fuzz-wall workflow: promote a crushed/filtered locked step to a reusable preset. Hold the trig, hit PRESET/KIT, "Save new preset" — and it captures the sound with all its parameter locks baked in, recallable later via Preset Keyboard mode.

## How to play it
1. Hold the trig → press the `PRESET`/`KIT` button → "Save new preset."
2. This captures the sound with all its parameter locks baked in ("save a trig sound with all its plocks as a new Preset").
