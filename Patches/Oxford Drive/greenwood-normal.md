# Greenwood "Normal" (In Rainbows era)

## Concept
Jonny Greenwood's (Radiohead) documented ShredMaster "Normal" setting, c.2006 In Rainbows era, transferred 1:1 to the Oxford. The art-rock taste anchor for guitar or banjo. Per TKOG, back the Gain off a hair vs a real ShredMaster (the Oxford has extra gain on tap).

## How to play it
1. Dial the confirmed Greenwood values: '91 voice, Si clipping, Gain 3:00, Contour minimum (7:00) for max mids.
2. Back Gain off a hair vs a real ShredMaster.
3. Best used as Greenwood did it — a clean-ish front-of-chain role, or guitar-only into the End-board verbs.

## Notes
- CONFIRMED artist setting.
- RIG WARNING: Greenwood's Bass 2:00 assumes ONE pedal into a clean Fender 85. Stacked fifth here, run Bass 8–9:00 or it muds (Bass is logarithmic).
- Volume 5:00 is TKOG's documented value but "often set lower."
