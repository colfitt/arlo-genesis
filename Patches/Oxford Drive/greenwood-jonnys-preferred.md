# Greenwood "Jonny's Preferred" (dialed ON the Oxford)

## Concept
The setting Joe Edelmann dialed ON the Oxford itself in his A/B vs a real '90 ShredMaster — confirmed to nail "the Jonny Greenwood sound" on the Oxford (Fender 85 SS amp + Lace Sensors + SM58). The gold-standard Greenwood recipe here because it was dialed on the actual pedal, not just transferred.

## How to play it
1. Set Contour to minimum (7:00); Gain 3:00; Bass and Treble both at 1:00.
2. Volume maxed at 5:00 (level-matched on the Oxford's wider range).
3. To nudge toward the OG's brighter/more-open tone: push Treble/output a little and drop Gain a touch.

## Notes
- CONFIRMED to nail the Greenwood sound on the Oxford itself — gold-standard.
- Settings dialed ON the Oxford, not just transferred.
- Still single-pedal / high-Bass context — lower Bass if stacked.
