# Brittle Jangle Edge-of-Breakup

## Concept
Designed-to-emulate the Modest Mouse blues-driver-style bright edge-of-breakup jangle. Built on the factory Shoegaze/Low-Gain voicing, brightened and dried for the jangle. LED clipping cleans up with guitar volume — Brock's roll-back move (clean ↔ hair). No artist setting exists for this box.

## How to play it
1. Set LED clipping; Gain low (9–10:00) for edge of breakup.
2. Contour balanced/slightly bright (12:00); Treble bright/biting (2–3:00).
3. Ride the instrument volume for the dynamic clean↔hair Brock move.
4. Banjo-as-lead carries the brittle jangle line — keep it DRY + bright.

## Notes
- Designed-to-emulate, not an artist setting. No Modest Mouse Oxford setting exists for this box.
- Bass low/thin (8:00). LED clipping is the choice specifically because it cleans up with guitar volume.
