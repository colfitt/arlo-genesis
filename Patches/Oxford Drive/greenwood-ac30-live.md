# Greenwood AC30-live (2010)

## Concept
Jonny Greenwood's (Radiohead) live ShredMaster setting into an AC30-only rig from the 2010 Haiti Benefit. The low Volume (1:30) reflects pushing an already-loud, dirty-ish amp — not feeding a clean front end. A confirmed artist reference value.

## How to play it
1. Set Gain 3:00, Contour minimum (7:00).
2. The low Volume (1:30) is for pushing an already-loud amp — keep it low if replicating that context.
3. The extreme Bass/Treble (2:45) is amp-dependent — drop Bass hard and re-trim Treble to taste in this rig.

## Notes
- CONFIRMED artist setting.
- RIG WARNING: the extreme Bass/Treble (2:45) is amp-dependent — this is a reference value, not a literal stacked-rig recipe. Drop Bass hard (Bass is logarithmic) and re-trim Treble.
- Back Gain off a touch on the Oxford vs a real ShredMaster.
