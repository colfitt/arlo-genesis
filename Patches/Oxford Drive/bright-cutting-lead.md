# Bright Cutting Lead (community)

## Concept
A brighter, cutting take on the ShredMaster lead, sourced from community/aggregator recipes. A documented community alternative to the maker's lead voicings — anonymous, with no artist name attached.

## How to play it
1. Set Contour to minimum (7:00) for max mids; Gain 2:00.
2. Use '91 voice, or switch to '88 for extra bite.
3. Treble bright at 10:00 for the cutting edge.

## Notes
- COMMUNITY (anonymous — no artist name attached).
- The original recipe called "Bass full open," which is for a single pedal into a clean amp. On the log-taper Oxford that = mud, so Bass is corrected to 8:00 for this rig — especially stacked, RUN LOW.
