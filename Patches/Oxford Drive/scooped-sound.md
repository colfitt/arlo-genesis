# Scooped Sound

## Concept
The canonical mid-scoop reference for the Oxford — mid-scooped, trebly chunk that demonstrates the Contour-CW tilt (mids scooped + treble up). It's the essential "other end of Contour" counterpart to the mid-forward ShredMaster voice.

## How to play it
1. Set Contour fully CW-ish (3:00) to scoop the mids and lift treble.
2. Re-trim Treble to ~2:00 — Contour CW has already added treble, so back it off as needed.
3. Push Gain to 3:00 for the trebly chunk; Volume to taste.

## Notes
- Less taste-lens-relevant (scooped, not wall-building) but the canonical mid-scoop reference.
- Watch for fizz on single-coils at this Contour position.
- Re-trim Treble after every Contour move — Contour is a combined treble+mid tilt.
