# Blues Lead (LED, dynamic)

## Concept
A darker, mid-rich, touch-responsive lead built on LED clipping's open feel. The one factory patch on LED clipping — most open and dynamic, with the best guitar-volume cleanup. A good reference for how LED tracks the picking hand.

## How to play it
1. Switch Clipping to LED for the most open, dynamic response.
2. Contour at minimum (7:00) for max mids; Treble dark at ~8:00.
3. Ride guitar volume — LED cleans up best with the volume knob rolled back.

## Notes
- The one factory patch on LED clipping — best guitar-volume cleanup of the factory set.
- LED output runs hottest, so it'll slam the downstream BF-3 if left in the current slot — watch levels.
