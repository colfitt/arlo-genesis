# Traditional 90s Lead (ShredMaster honk)

## Concept
The straight ShredMaster mid-forward lead voice for guitar or banjo lead, mirroring Greenwood's documented voicing. Contour at minimum (7:00) gives the classic mid honk — the maximum-mids ShredMaster signature.

## How to play it
1. Set Contour to minimum (7:00) for the classic mid honk (max mids).
2. Gain 3:00; the mid-focus carries lead lines.
3. Banjo-as-lead: this mid-focused honk suits the banjo carrying a line, but tame Treble (banjo's 2–4 kHz spray) — see the Banjo Focus patch.

## Notes
- RIG WARNING: published Bass 2:00 is for a single pedal into a clean amp. Stacked fifth here, run Bass 8–9:00 or it muds (Bass is logarithmic).
- Mirrors Greenwood's documented ShredMaster lead voicing.
