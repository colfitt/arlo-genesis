# 108-PP1 — Westberg Grinding Bite (into the wall)

## Concept
The 108 cast as the grinding-bite pre-stage feeding the Hizumitas wall — the rig's equivalent of Westberg's Boss DS-1 grind into the sustaining Swans wall (Gira's wall + Westberg's grind = two dirt voices). Also the BC,NR JFET-boost-into-Maxon stacked move. A 🟣 designed-to-emulate patch: no 108-specific artist documentation exists, the ref is a circuit-reasoned analog, so verify by ear.

## How to play it
1. OUTPUT/VOLUME ~12:00 (to unity, feeding the Hizumitas).
2. FUZZ/INPUT ~12–1:00 (mid-gain grind, not maxed).
3. BUFFER ON (rig default).
4. Then Hizumitas HZ-PP1 smooths it into the wall.

## Notes
- **APPROXIMATION:** Westberg's bite is a Boss DS-1 (op-amp distortion), not a silicon Fuzz Face — same grinding-pre-stage role, different circuit.
- Ref: Swans — Westberg's Boss DS-1 "aggressive, grinding" bite feeding the sustaining wall.
