# Full-On Psych Fuzz

## Concept
A massive wall-of-fuzz for psych/shoegaze. FUZZ at 9/10 sits in the top quarter where the big gain jump lives, with output high for density. Works as standalone wall or as the bright bite feeding the Hizumitas. Guitar Chalk explicitly calls for BUFFER ON, consistent with the rig default.

## How to play it
1. OUTPUT/VOLUME ~3 o'clock (8/10).
2. FUZZ/INPUT ~4 o'clock (9/10) — into the top-quarter saturation jump.
3. BUFFER ON.
4. Play dense chords standalone, or feed the Hizumitas for a re-smoothed wall.

## Notes
- With a bright source this can get brittle (SuperFunAwesome: buffer-on + full fuzz = "almost too brittle") — **A/B BUFFER OFF** if so, and let the Hizumitas re-brighten downstream.
- FUZZ at 9/10, not maxed — the usable saturation already lives in the top quarter, so you rarely need full CW.
