# Modern High-Gain Doom

## Concept
Unrelenting, focused-mids, big-bottom fuzz for doom/sludge/metal — the heaviest community-cited setting and the highest taste-fit of the Guitar Chalk five. FUZZ at 3 o'clock sits right at the saturation-jump knee. Pairs naturally with the Hizumitas for a denser wall; on humbuckers (the SG) the 108 goes "creamy/saturated" here (gearmanndude).

## How to play it
1. OUTPUT/VOLUME ~4 o'clock (9/10).
2. FUZZ/INPUT ~3 o'clock (8/10) — at the saturation-jump knee.
3. BUFFER ON.
4. Run into the Hizumitas for a denser wall.

## Notes
- Watch brittleness with BUFFER ON + bright sources — flip to OFF and re-brighten downstream if needed.
- Humbuckers push it toward creamy/saturated; brighter pickups stay more aggressive.
