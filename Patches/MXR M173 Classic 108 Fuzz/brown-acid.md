# Brown Acid

## Concept
Balanced psych/stoner "brown" fuzz — the manual's default-character card. Both knobs sit past noon, giving a healthy fuzz with healthy output. Works as a standalone rhythm voice or as food for the Hizumitas doom wall. One of three Dunlop-named factory cards (Brown Acid / Black Iron / Kings of the Desert), all stoner/doom-adjacent — that naming tells you exactly what Dunlop voiced this pedal for, dead-center in the rig's heavy/desert space.

## How to play it
1. OUTPUT/VOLUME to ~1–2 o'clock, FUZZ/INPUT to ~1–2 o'clock (both past noon).
2. BUFFER ON (the rig default behind the Colour Box / VG-800 line).
3. Play rhythm standalone, or run it into the Hizumitas for a sustained wall.

## Notes
- Both knobs past noon = healthy fuzz + healthy output.
- Clock positions are read from the manual's tiny dial-card illustrations (no numeric callouts) — **approximate**.
- BUFFER ON is the rig default: the 108 is 5th on Board 1, fed by the JHS Colour Box V2 buffer + the GK-5→VG-800 modeled line, so it never sees a passive pickup.
