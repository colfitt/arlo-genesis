# Black Iron

## Concept
The darkest/heaviest of the three Dunlop factory cards — lowest output, fuzz at noon. The most doom-leaning factory voicing and the strongest taste-fit of the trio. A good rhythm bed to sit under the Hizumitas wall.

## How to play it
1. OUTPUT/VOLUME to ~10–11 o'clock (lower output), FUZZ/INPUT to ~11–12 o'clock (mid-gain).
2. BUFFER ON for the in-rig default.
3. **A/B BUFFER OFF** for an even darker/fuller voice (Premier Guitar: buffer-off = "rich, darker and fuller").

## Notes
- Buffer OFF here leans the brittle-vs-dark tradeoff toward "dark," which the Hizumitas can re-brighten downstream if it goes lifeless.
- Approximate clock positions read from the manual dial-cards — **no numeric callouts**.
