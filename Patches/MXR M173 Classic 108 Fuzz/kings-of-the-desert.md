# Kings of the Desert

## Concept
Desert/stoner-rock fuzz with fuzz up and healthy output — the most "lead/forward" of the three Dunlop factory cards. Stands alone or feeds the doom wall. Confirms the manual's own voicings cluster FUZZ noon-to-2, never maxed, matching the demo finding that the big gain jump lives in the top quarter.

## How to play it
1. OUTPUT/VOLUME to ~1 o'clock, FUZZ/INPUT to ~1–2 o'clock.
2. BUFFER ON (rig default).
3. Play forward/lead, or push it into the Hizumitas.

## Notes
- Fuzz up, output healthy — the forward voice of the factory trio.
- Approximate clock positions read from the manual dial-cards — **no numeric callouts**.
