# 108-PP2 — Joy Division / Swans Icy Brittle Stab

## Concept
Short, gated, splatty brittle high stabs — Swans' "icy stabs over warm hum" and Joy Division's separated behind-the-reverb guitar. The cold Dark Star DS-PP1 space then isolates them. A recorded-wrong texture: this re-aims the existing "Recorded-wrong static splat" at the post-punk icy-stab role. A 🟣 designed-to-emulate patch — verify by ear.

## How to play it
1. OUTPUT/VOLUME ~11–12:00.
2. FUZZ/INPUT ~2–3:00 (high, into the gate).
3. BUFFER OFF (behind the rig's buffers → the thin/gated/crackly misbehavior).
4. Feed the bright VG-800/banjo line for max ice; play short stabs and let it sputter on decay.
5. Send into the Dark Star (DS-PP1) cold space to isolate/separate the stabs.

## Notes
- **APPROXIMATION:** the 108's buffer-off gate manufactures the brittleness; the SPACE (Dark Star) does the isolation/separation.
- Ref: Swans "icy stabs" / Joy Division brittle separated guitar (recorded-wrong).
