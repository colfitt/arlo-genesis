# Dilla Feel — Quantize-Off, Hand-Played Micro-Timing

## Concept
Off-grid human boom-bap feel on chopped breaks and the rig's drones/banjo. Quantize off, finger-drum by hand, and apply deliberate asymmetric micro-timing. INFERRED mapping — Dilla never touched the AC50 (died 2006); the 30/20 ms figures are his documented micro-timing, the AC50 application is the mapping. The DIBIA$E Pad-Link/Offset half is REAL on this box.

## How to play it
1. Quantize OFF; finger-drum kick/snare/hat by hand (the Sample sequences quantized OR unquantized — mix both in one beat).
2. Deliberate micro-timing (Dilla: kick ~30 ms early, snare ~20 ms late — direction is reported inconsistently across sources; magnitude + asymmetry is the load-bearing fact, nudge by feel).
3. In Step Edit (`Shift+Seq`) move the fader to nudge a selected event ~20–30 ms; OR drag a pad late with Offset (0–100%, silence-in-front).
4. Combine with Pad Link to hand-build per-pad hat swing (DIBIA$E links pad 4→3, then varies the linked hat's velocity).
5. Vary velocity hard (accents vs ghosts) via poly-aftertouch.
