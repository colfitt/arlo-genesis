# Layered Snare — Crack + Tail via Pad Link

## Concept
A fat snare or clap built from a short "crack" layer and a longer body/ring layer, fired together via Pad Link. The community mpc-forums maxim here is "tune layers to agree" — both layers share a root so they reinforce rather than clash. It's a general layering principle, AC50-mapped, baked down with Resample.

## How to play it
1. Pad Link two pads (`Shift+K2` on Tune pg2 = partner pad).
2. Layer A = short-decay crack (use the Snappy One-Shot envelope, patch #1).
3. Layer B = longer tail/ring.
4. Tune both layers to agree (same root, Tune Semi).
5. Bake with Resample into one one-shot.
