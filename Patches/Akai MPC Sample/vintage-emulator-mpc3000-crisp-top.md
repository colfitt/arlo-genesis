# Vintage Emulator — MPC3000 Crisp Top

## Concept
Crisper top end plus snare crack — the brighter/cleaner vintage sibling. A/B-verified: crispier/cleaner top, snare "cracks" more, sample a little cleaner. The J Dilla / DJ Premier box but the brighter flavor — less on-aesthetic for doom than the MPC60/SP1200.

## How to play it
1. Knob FX → Vintage Emulator → K1 Type = MPC3000.
2. Character: crispier/cleaner top end, snare "cracks" more, sample a little cleaner.
3. Apply per-pad or whole-beat; bake via Resample.
