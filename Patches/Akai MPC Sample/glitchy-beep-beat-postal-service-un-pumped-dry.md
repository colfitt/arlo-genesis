# Glitchy Beep-Beat (Postal Service, un-pumped + dry)

## Concept
The Postal Service glitchy K2000 beat — fast, clattery beeps, NO compression/pump, dry-forward under a soft lead. The un-pumped counterpoint to MPC-E1/E2 (this is MPC-E3). Designed from the Postal Service "Such Great Heights" (Kurzweil K2000 beat, no sidechain) and Dntel's glitch ethos. The LoFi Bitcrush range is AC50-confirmed.

## How to play it
1. Resample a VG-800/OP-1 beep and chop it into pitched beeps; sequence a fast slightly-clattery beat.
2. NO compression/pump (Tamborello: "none of that on the album").
3. Add the broken element with the LoFi Bitcrush FX (24 → ~6-bit) on a couple of hits + loose 16th timing.
4. Send the bus to the Delay FX (the Line 6 / MIDIverb role).
5. Keep dry-forward.

## Notes
- MPC-E3.

- LoFi Bitcrush range AC50-confirmed.
