# Lazy Chop — Manual Real-Time Slice

## Concept
Hand-placed slices struck in real time while a break runs — DIBIA$E's (Steven Brandon, LA beat scene) stated favorite. A real artist on this exact box: *"my favorite is the lazy chop… if you hold shift and this encoder, you can zoom in on your actual chops so it can land right on the transient."*

## How to play it
1. Chop → mode = Manual.
2. Strike pads in real time where you want slices to fall (play along to the running break).
3. When done, Trim to refine each slice start/end.
4. `Shift` + encoder to zoom to the transient so the slice lands right.
