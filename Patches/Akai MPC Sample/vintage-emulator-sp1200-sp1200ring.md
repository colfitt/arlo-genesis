# Vintage Emulator — SP1200 / SP1200Ring

## Concept
Gritty, forward-snare crunch (SP1200) or a ring-oscillation snare mode (SP1200Ring). A/B-verified: SP1200 has low-end beef like the 60 but the snare is more forward/present, hats a touch shinier, slightly louder. SP1200Ring adds ring-oscillation on the snare — bit-crushy, modey — the most overtly degraded vintage option, a strong rig fit for obvious crunch.

## How to play it
1. Knob FX → Vintage Emulator → K1 Type = SP1200 (low-end beef like the 60 but snare more forward/present, hats a touch shinier, slightly louder) or SP1200Ring (SP1200 character + ring-oscillation on the snare — bit-crushy, modey).
2. Apply per-pad or whole-beat; bake via Resample.
