# Ambient / Shoegaze Clean (bright)

## Concept
The brighter, airier counterpart to the Round wash — a single-coil shoegaze clean that sits on top of a wall. Chime amp into the roomier AC30 fawn cab a, clean and chimey. The airy layer over the Punch Wall or under a Prismizer-style halo.

## How to play it
1. AMP chime, CAB a; DRIVE 2/10 (clean), BASS 4/10, MIDDLE 5/10, TREBLE 7/10.
2. LEVEL 6/10, ROOM 6/10 (SIZE medium).
3. INPUT LEVEL = Instrument, OUTPUT MODE = Normal, rear = STEREO.
4. Layer over the Punch Wall or under a Prismizer-style halo. Pick warm (Round) vs airy (Chime) per the layer you need.

## Notes
- Values are on the 0–10 community dial scale (Guitar Chalk editorial), not clock-face.
- Brighter Chime sibling of "Dreamy Ambient Wash" (#4).
- Roll the guitar tone back slightly on very bright pickups so the high TREBLE doesn't get brittle — on Chime, raise MIDDLE/high-cut to tame fizz rather than only pulling TREBLE.
