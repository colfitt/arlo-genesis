# Shiflett Deluxe (amp-replacement fly clean)

## Concept
Backline-free clean amp tone direct to FOH or interface — the documented "leave the amp at home" use. Any magnetic guitar gets a Deluxe-Reverb voice instead of a real amp, exactly as Chris Shiflett used the Iridium in place of his Deluxe Reverbs on his UK/Ireland solo tour. A clean Deluxe with just a hint of life.

## How to play it
1. AMP round, CAB a; DRIVE noon–1:00 (clean Deluxe with a hint of life), BASS noon, MIDDLE 11–noon, TREBLE 1:00.
2. ROOM ~11:00, SIZE medium.
3. INPUT LEVEL = Instrument, OUTPUT MODE = Normal, rear = MONO (OUT L) for a simple FOH send.
4. Monitor on the front 1/8" headphone jack (latency-free) while OUT feeds FOH.

## Notes
- Direction is artist-confirmed; the numeric values are the conventional Deluxe-clean starting point landing in his described tone, NOT his published dial. Shiflett names the amp/voice (Round = Deluxe Reverb), not exact knob values.
- Pairs with the Travel FAV (#11) for a full amp+verb gig.
