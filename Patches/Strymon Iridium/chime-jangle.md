# Chime Jangle (AC30 single-coil)

## Concept
The Jazzmaster bridge pickup (or any single-coil) into a Vox AC30 Top Boost Brilliant model and the AC30 fawn 2×12 cab. Airy jangle and chime-clean rhythm that bites when you dig in — the Jazzmaster's natural Iridium voice. Top of the DRIVE range engages a frequency-shaped front-end treble boost, so DRIVE acts as a built-in bridge booster on strums.

## How to play it
1. AMP chime, CAB a; DRIVE just below 11:00 (dial so lows stay full without flubbing), BASS noon, MIDDLE 9–10:00, TREBLE 2:00.
2. ROOM ~11:00, SIZE medium.
3. INPUT LEVEL = Instrument, OUTPUT MODE = Normal, rear = MONO or STEREO.
4. Pick lightly for air; dig in for bite. On very bright pickups, roll the guitar tone back slightly.
5. To tame fizz, RAISE MIDDLE (= high-cut), not just TREBLE.

## Notes
- On Chime, MIDDLE = HIGH-CUT is the tame-the-fizz control, not TREBLE — and it interacts with TREBLE.
- On Chime, BASS is a near tilt-EQ: min = all mids, max = all bass / no mids.
