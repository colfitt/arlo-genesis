# Dreamy Ambient Wash (warm/scooped)

## Concept
A warm, scooped, roomy clean bed for clean guitar (SG/Jazzmaster) under reverb/modulation — the dry-bloom feed into downstream verbs for the "Let Down"-style layered build. Round amp into the boxier Blues Jr cab b, with a scooped midrange and a touch of hair. The warm/scooped sibling of the brighter Chime version (Ambient / Shoegaze Clean).

## How to play it
1. AMP round, CAB b; DRIVE 4/10, BASS 5/10, MIDDLE 3/10 (scooped), TREBLE 6/10.
2. LEVEL 7/10, ROOM 7/10 (SIZE medium→large).
3. INPUT LEVEL = Instrument, OUTPUT MODE = Normal, rear = STEREO for the widest bed.
4. Feed the dry bloom into downstream verbs (H90 / Big Sky / Dark Star) for the layered build.

## Notes
- Values are on the 0–10 community dial scale (Guitar Chalk editorial), not clock-face.
- Keep the Iridium ROOM modest; the fixed room (256 ms IR + algorithmic tail) gets boxy if overdriven — let the End-board verbs do the long tails.
