# Gig Bank Template — 7 tunings × 4 voicings

## Concept

A live, MIDI-PC-switched organizational scheme — a real gigging player's Tone Exchange liveset architecture (Jared Gunston), the only documented user-shared VG-800 patch set. Architecture, not parameter values: 7 tunings done entirely in `ALT TUNE` (no physical retuning), 4 adjacent sounds per tuning so you click over live, all MIDI-PC-switched from an iPad via a CME WIDI wireless bridge.

## How to play it

1. Build 7 TUNINGS entirely in `ALT TUNE` (no physical retuning): Standard, -1, -2, -3, -4, Drop D, Drop C.
2. Build 4 SOUNDS per tuning, adjacent so you can click over live: Clean (chorus+delay+reverb) / Overdrive (dynamic, "bites back") / High Gain (built without IRs so it's shareable — layer your own IR locally) / Lead-Solo boost (+ wah engaged).
3. MIDI-PC-switch all of it from an iPad via a CME WIDI wireless bridge.
4. Organize User banks as tuning-banks (a Boss Tone Exchange liveset).

## Notes

- Real, cited community liveset (Jared Gunston). Adopt as an organizational template, not a sound recipe.
- High Gain built without IRs deliberately so the liveset is shareable — add your own IR locally.
