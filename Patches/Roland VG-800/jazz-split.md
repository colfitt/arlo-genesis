# Jazz Split (bass low / jazz-guitar high)

## Concept

A bass model under jazz voicings up top — mixing low-tuned chords with jazz guitar. A real factory preset demonstrating the bass-under-guitar split; CTL-1 engages chorus + delay.

## How to play it

1. Recall the factory bank. `INST TYPE = DUAL GUITAR` with a bass model on the LOW strings and a jazzy guitar model up top.
2. CTL-1 = chorus + delay engaged.
