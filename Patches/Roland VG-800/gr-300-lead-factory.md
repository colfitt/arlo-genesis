# GR-300 Lead (factory)

## Concept

The classic GR-300 poly-synth lead at the factory voicing — a quick recall before tweaking the parametrized build. Juca: "sounds really cool"; SoS: "brought back many happy memories"; Gear Sounds: "this voice is incredible." The named factory anchor for the signature banjo-as-lead build.

## How to play it

1. Recall the factory bank. `INST = SYNTH`, `SYNTH TYPE = GR-300`.
2. For the parametrized rebuild (V+D, DUET, PITCH/FINE, COMP, ENV MOD), see "GR-300 Thick-Synth Lead."
