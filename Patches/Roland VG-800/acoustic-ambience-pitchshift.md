# Acoustic Ambience — acoustic with CTL-1 pitch-shift blend

## Concept

The factory "Acoustic Ambience" preset Juca Nery tours — a spacious acoustic voice with a pitch-shifter blend on CTL-1 for shimmer/ambience. `INST = ACOUSTIC` gives the lush, modulated acoustic bed; CTL-1 blends in a PITCH SHIFT FX so the voice can bloom into a pitched-up haze on the footswitch. A long-sustained acoustic that lifts into an evolving ambient cloud when you stomp it.

## How to play it

1. Recall the factory "Acoustic Ambience" memory.
2. Play sustained open chords for the spacious acoustic bed.
3. CTL-1 blends in the pitch-shifter for a shimmery, ambient lift.
4. Into a smear reverb the pitched blend becomes an evolving acoustic haze.

## Notes

- Named factory preset (Juca Nery: "Acoustic Ambience (CTL-1 = pitch-shifter blend)"). PITCH SHIFT is one of the assignable FX (acoustic resonance / flanger / pitch shift / chorus seen in the FX blocks, Gear Sounds tutorial).
- Exact pitch interval, FX mix, and acoustic model are not published — dial from the factory memory.
