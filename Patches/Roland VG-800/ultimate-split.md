# Ultimate Split (clean low / dirty-ready high)

## Concept

The showcase first preset — a Dual Guitar string-split (clean model high, distortion model low) demoing the two-path architecture. A real named factory preset cited by two independent demos, and the template for the rig's "clean low / dirty-ready high" Dual Guitar move.

## How to play it

1. Recall the factory bank's first preset.
2. `INST = DUAL GUITAR` string-split via the DIV two-path architecture: clean guitar model on HIGH strings + distortion/different model on LOW strings.
3. For parametrized builds, see "Acoustic-Top / Dirty-Rock-Bottom Split" and "Dual Stereo Split."

- Real named factory preset, cited by two independent demos. Exact per-block values not captured — behavior only.
