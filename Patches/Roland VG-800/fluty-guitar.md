# Fluty Guitar (textural lead → distortion)

## Concept

A flute-voiced textural lead where CTL-1 adds distortion — degraded-lead territory the rig favors ("crazy tones but really inspiring" — Juca). A real named factory preset; flute-voice plus footswitchable distortion makes good textural drone food.

## How to play it

1. Select the flute-like voiced model.
2. Assign `DS`/distortion to CTL-1 so the footswitch dirties the flute.
3. Keep `NORMAL MIX` low.
4. Into a smear reverb or the fuzz.

## Notes

- Real named factory preset. No knob values captured on disk — behavior only.
