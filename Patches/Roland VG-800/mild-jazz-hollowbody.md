# Mild Jazz — warm hollowbody with CTL-1 chorus

## Concept

The factory "Mild Jazz" preset — a warm, rolled-off hollowbody jazz tone (ES-335/L-4 family) with chorus toggled by CTL-1. A mellow comping/chord-melody voice and the dark, smooth counterpart to the rig's brighter banjo/baritone sources.

## How to play it

1. Recall the factory "Mild Jazz" memory.
2. Confirm `INST` = a semi-hollow / hollowbody electric model with treble pulled for a warm jazz voice.
3. `CTL-1` toggles the chorus in/out per the factory convention.
4. Blend `NORMAL MIX` to taste for real-pickup body; keep onboard REV/DLY light.

## Notes

- Named factory preset; `CTL-1` = chorus toggle is documented behavior (Juca Nery). Exact model and EQ values not published — dial from the factory memory.
- The VG-800's E.GUITAR models include ES-335 and L-4 (Parameter Guide); pick whichever reads warmest.
