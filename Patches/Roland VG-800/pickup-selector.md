# Pickup Selector (modeled pickup toggle)

## Concept

A footswitchable neck/bridge pickup position on the modeled guitar. A real factory preset documenting the CTL-1 = modeled-pickup-position convention (also "Pickup Splitter," "Pickup Morphing").

## How to play it

1. Recall the factory bank.
2. CTL-1 switches the MODELED guitar's pickup position bridge (rear) ↔ neck (front).

- Real factory preset. No further values captured — behavior only.
