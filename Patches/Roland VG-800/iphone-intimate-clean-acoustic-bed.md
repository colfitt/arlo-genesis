# iPhone-Intimate Clean Acoustic Bed

## Concept

A pristine, present, close acoustic/banjo source — the clean source for the iPhone-degrade chain, or left dry for the Big Thief "Not" raw bed (Sufjan "Carrie & Lowell" fingerpicked guitar/banjo; Big Thief "Not" dry live-room source). A 🟣 designed-to-emulate build — the clean source the iPhone/cassette degrade wants to ruin, or the raw "Not" bed.

## How to play it

1. Set `INST = ACOUSTIC` (J-45 / D-28).
2. `ALT TUNE TYPE = OPEN D` or `OPEN G` (circular open tunings, Sufjan-style) OR standard.
3. Minimal/no dirt, `NORMAL MIX` low, onboard FX off.
4. Keep `ACOUSTIC RESONANCE` OFF if you want downstream per-string pan.
5. Feed into the Chroma CASSETTE degrade chain or leave dry.
