# Shiny Phase Clean — phased clean organ

## Concept

The factory "Shiny Phase Clean" organ preset Marcus Curtis tours — a clean, phased organ voice built from the drawbar ORGAN model with a phaser instead of a rotary. A shimmery, swirling sustained pad, distinct from the rotary organ-drone, and ideal as evolving looper/granular food. The drawbar ORGAN model with FEET 16/8/4 and a high SUSTAIN gives the continuous organ pad; a slow PHASER in an FX slot supplies the swirl rather than a Leslie ramp.

## How to play it

1. Recall the factory "Shiny Phase Clean" memory, or build it: `INST = SYNTH`, `SYNTH TYPE = ORGAN` with `FEET 16/8/4` up and `SUSTAIN` high.
2. Put a `PHASER` in an FX slot (slow rate) for the shimmery swirl.
3. Hold chords for the sustained organ pad.
4. Into Microcosm / Blooper the phased organ becomes evolving granular/looper food.

## Notes

- Named factory preset (Marcus Curtis: an organ — "Shiny Phase Clean"). The drawbar ORGAN model and FEET drawbars are manual-verified; the drone drawbar recipe (FEET 16+8+4, SUSTAIN high) is from the Parameter Guide MEMO.
- Distinct from the existing "Organ Drone + Rotary" (rotary swirl) and "ORGAN → Blooper Capture Drone" patches.
- Exact phaser rate/depth and the drawbar mix are not published — this is a dialable recipe; recall the factory memory and dial to taste.
