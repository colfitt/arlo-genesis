# Brittle Jangle Front-End (bright clean console)

## Concept
Modest Mouse bright/brittle clean jangle front-end. Brock's jangle is bright single-coils into a loud-clean Fender, with the pedals doing the dirt — the Colour Box's clean Neve color plus a bright EQ tilt is the rig's closest front-end to that. The bright, articulate single-coil-into-a-clean-Twin character; the banjo-as-lead carries the jangly single-note line.

## How to play it
1. INST input, **LO** on the verge of breakup (clean jangle). STEP 2 → PRE-VOL 11:00 (clean, slight hair) → MASTER to unity.
2. Lift TREBLE +slight with TREBLE SHIFT ~2:00 (≈8 kHz air) — keep it bright and biting.
3. Lift MIDDLE +1 with MID SHIFT ~12:00 for presence so single notes cut.
4. Keep BASS ~11:00 (thin/tight, the "Bass 4" feel).
5. HI-PASS **OFF** (jangle). Feed the Deco DEC-LF1 for the double-track chorus.

## Notes
- 🟣 **designed-to-emulate** — Brock's jangle, the rig's closest front-end.
- Keep it bright and dry — the clean console color is the whole job here; downstream pedals add the dirt.
