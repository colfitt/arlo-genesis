# Funk-Into-Console (clean scoop)

## Concept
Late-'70s/'80s funk-guitar-straight-into-the-console clean color (Chic / Grace Jones), and a thin Telecaster bridge thickening — two flavors in one. Cleaner here means MASTER high / PRE-VOL low. Lower signature-fit for this rig; included as an essential, classic console voice.

## How to play it
1. INST input, **LO** on the verge of breakup. STEP 2–3 → PRE-VOL 10:00 → MASTER high (~2–3:00).
2. **Thicken-the-thin-Tele variant** (PLAP, community-cited, frequencies named): boost low-mids — MIDDLE +2 (~1:00, MID SHIFT ≈300 Hz) + BASS +2 (~1:00, BASS SHIFT ~2:00 ≈300 Hz) + a little high-mid.
3. **Scoop variant** (Guitar Chalk, flagged low-confidence): MIDDLE − (~9–10:00, scooped) + TREBLE + (~2–3:00, bright) + BASS + (~1:00).
4. HI-PASS off or ~8:00.

## Notes
- The Guitar Chalk scoop is **FLAGGED low-confidence** — its 0–10 numbers are partly AI-assembled and use an impossible STEP scale; use as directional only.
- The PLAP thicken variant has named frequencies; the clock translation is mine.
