# Crunchy-Dry Direct-In (quiet source, boosted)

## Concept
Car Seat Headrest crunchy-dry, mid-forward direct-in crunch — a quiet source clipped and boosted. The Colour Box IS "boost a quiet source until it clips" in one box, the exact mechanic Toledo got from recording quiet then boosting in the mix. This chases the RESULT; no artist used this pedal.

## How to play it
1. INST input, **HI** mode. STEP 3 → PRE-VOL 1:00 (audible crunch, NOT full fuzz).
2. **Set MASTER LAST** — trim to unity; it jumps.
3. Boost MIDDLE +2 with MID SHIFT ~12:00 (≈1 kHz) for the mid-forward DS-2 spike.
4. Leave TREBLE ~12:00 (bright/dry) and BASS ~12:00 (don't fatten — it's dry).
5. Engage HI-PASS ~9–10:00 (≈140 Hz) to tighten.
6. Keep it DRY downstream — no long verb.

## Notes
- 🟣 **designed-to-emulate** — chases the recorded-quiet-then-boosted crunch result.
- The "boost a quiet source into clipping" mechanic is the whole point; resist fattening the lows.
