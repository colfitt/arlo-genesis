# MIX-at-Noon Freeze Pad

## Concept
Sustaining glitchy ambient pad-like chords that aren't overbearing, with the freeze moving along with your playing dynamics — the Freeze-side sweet spot. Per guitar.com: "MIX at noon gives sustaining glitchy ambient pad-like chords," and the differentiator is that "freeze [moves] along with your playing dynamics."

## How to play it
1. Set **MIX = noon** (50% dry/wet).
2. Set the channel to **FREEZE**.
3. Play — the pad-like chords sustain without overbearing, and the freeze moves with your dynamics.

## Notes
- Cited community value (MIX=noon). It's a one-knob anchor for any Freeze patch.
- The opposite end (MIX full) = mix-full-float-away-wall.
