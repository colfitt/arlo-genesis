# Living Pad

## Concept
The classic infinite freeze pad, but brought to life with motion so it never sits still — the shoegaze wall in one footswitch. Hold FREEZE to lock an infinite pad, then dial ERROR up and audition the ERROR TYPE toggle to introduce motion and variance into the held pad. The manual prints no numbers — just "hold Freeze + experiment with ERROR."

## How to play it
1. Set the channel to **FREEZE**.
2. **Hold the FREEZE footswitch** to lock an infinite pad — the classic freeze effect.
3. **Dial ERROR up** and experiment with the ERROR **TYPE** toggle to introduce motion and variance, bringing the pad to life.

## Notes
- Manual prints no numbers — only "hold Freeze + experiment with ERROR."
- The defining control is **ERROR variety** on a held freeze. This differs from Dancing Drone, which keys specifically on TYPE = Playback (see dancing-drone).
