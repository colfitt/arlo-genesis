# Frantic Company

## Concept
A wild burst of glitch energy on every note attack; in the gaps, the opposite — a soft, infinite Freeze pad. Dynamics taken to the extreme. Per the manual: "With everything routed only to Glitch, you will be greeted with a wild burst of energy whenever you play a note. In between you will have the opposite – a soft, infinite freeze."

## How to play it
1. Enter hidden routing (hold both footswitches) and route **everything ONLY to GLITCH** (all toggles toward Glitch, manual p.38).
2. Keep **SUSTAIN engaged** so the gaps fill with a soft infinite freeze.
3. Play with dynamics — hard attacks burst, gaps go soft.
4. Use **DUCK** if the GK-5 gets too hot on attack.

## Notes
- Previously known only via the Swain demo (she reads the name aloud); the manual confirms it as a real factory Idea with the routing-to-Glitch + SUSTAIN recipe.
