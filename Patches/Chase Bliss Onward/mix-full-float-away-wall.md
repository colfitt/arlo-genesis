# MIX-Full Float-Away Wall

## Concept
Float away completely into an all-wet drone wall with no dry signal. Per delaydude: "if you want to float away completely, you can turn the mix pot all the way up."

## How to play it
1. Turn **MIX all the way up** (full wet, no dry).
2. Play into the all-wet wall.

## Notes
- Same target as the Swain SIZE-3:00/MIX-full preset (sprawling-ambient-swain-demo) from a second independent source — the all-wet wall is double-sourced.
