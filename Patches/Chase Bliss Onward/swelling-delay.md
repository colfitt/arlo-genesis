# Swelling Delay

## Concept
A vaporous pseudo-echo whose repeats gradually swell in and crossfade into one another — the shoegaze swell wall without a dedicated reverb. Per the manual: "A vaporous pseudo-echo with repeats that gradually swell in and crossfade with one another as you play. These settings are just starters – adjust to taste."

## How to play it
1. Set the channel to **GLITCH**.
2. **SUSTAIN = down**, **SIZE = down** (short).
3. Set **FADE = Slow/left** — this is the load-bearing setting; the slow fade drives the crossfade-layering.
4. Play; banjo or baritone swells crossfade into a moving bed.
