# Banjo / Baritone Infinite Pad

## Concept
Sustain a banjo or baritone chord into an infinite pad it physically can't hold; OCTAVE-down gives a baritone sub-drone. The rig's "banjo made to sustain" move — the dossier's single most compelling source for this pedal in this rig. The banjo holds a roll into a wall you then play a lead over.

## How to play it
1. Set the channel to **FREEZE** and **SUSTAIN = max** for an infinite hold.
2. Set **LATCH dip ON** so the hold latches hands-free.
3. **Hold/latch the FREEZE footswitch** to lock the chord.
4. For a baritone sub-drone, set **OCTAVE down (left)**.
5. Play a lead over the held wall.

## Notes
- The underlying behavior (SUSTAIN-max = infinite, hold-to-lock, OCTAVE-down) is manual/Aaron-Rusch verified; the banjo/baritone framing is the rig inference.
