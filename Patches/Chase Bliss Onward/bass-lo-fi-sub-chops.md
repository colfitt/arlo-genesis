# Bass Lo-Fi Sub Chops

## Concept
Longer, lo-fi rhythmic bass chops from the Fender Pro Jazz — or, in the variant, octave-down sub drones. The HALF SPEED dip doubles the sample length to ~2s and makes it slightly lo-fi (half record quality), so glitch chops run longer and grainier. The lo-fi grain fits the cassette-degrade lens.

## How to play it
1. Set the channel to **GLITCH** with the **½ SPEED dip ON** (doubles sample length to ~2s, slightly lo-fi).
2. For drones instead: switch to **FREEZE + OCTAVE-down** for sub drones.

## Notes
- ½-SPEED behavior (half record quality → double sample length, lo-fi) is manual-verified; the bass framing is the rig inference.
- The lo-fi grain fits the cassette-degrade lens.
