# Speedy Starter

## Concept
A secondary harmonizing voice at 2x speed paired with the Glitch — dense rhythmic phrases that act as launching points. Directly serves banjo-as-lead. Per the manual: "Turn up OCTAVE to introduce a secondary, harmonizing voice that plays at 2x speed… play around for a while until you hear a phrase you like, then hold the Glitch footswitch to lock it in."

## How to play it
1. Set the channel to **GLITCH**.
2. **OCTAVE up (right)** for the double-speed/upper-octave secondary voice; **SIZE up**, **SUSTAIN up**.
3. Play until you hear a phrase you like.
4. **Hold the GLITCH footswitch** to lock it in — it repeats while you keep playing on top.

## Notes
- Maps onto ALPFV's "glockenspiel-like melodic voices" target (bell-ish OCTAVE-up top) and the rig's banjo-as-lead move.
- Lock-to-follow lets the banjo drive a 2x harmony top while you keep playing.
