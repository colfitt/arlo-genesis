# Route TEXTURE Drive to Glitch Only

## Concept
Keep TEXTURE drive/grit on the Glitch stutter while the Freeze pad stays clean — avoid unwanted distortion on one channel. Isolating the Effects section (OCTAVE/TEXTURE/ANIMATE/EQ) to the GLITCH channel only means cranking TEXTURE adds drive to Glitch while Freeze stays clean.

## How to play it
1. Enter hidden ROUTING: **hold both footswitches**.
2. Flip the **ANIMATE/Effect toggle** to isolate the Effects section (OCTAVE/TEXTURE/ANIMATE/EQ) to the **GLITCH channel only.**
3. Crank **TEXTURE** to add drive to Glitch — Freeze stays clean.

## Notes
- delaydude calls out routing specifically to avoid distortion bleeding onto one channel; the routing mechanic is manual-verified (p.16–17).
- Can be baked into a Complex Couple / Two-Personality preset.
