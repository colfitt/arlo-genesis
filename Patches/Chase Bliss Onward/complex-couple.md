# Complex Couple

## Concept
The official template for the rig's signature two-personality patch — a clean, evolving Freeze bed + a chaotic Glitch top that move as one but sound completely different. Per the manual: "The various effects give energy and nuance to the Freeze channel, while the errors create motion and variation on the Glitch side… the two channels move as one, but make very different, complementary effects with loads of depth."

## How to play it
1. Enter hidden per-section ROUTING: **hold both footswitches**, then move the TYPE/FADE/ANIMATE toggles toward a channel (manual p.38).
2. Route the **Effects (OCTAVE/TEXTURE/ANIMATE) → FREEZE channel**.
3. Route the **Errors → GLITCH channel**.
4. Dial knob amounts to taste — the routing assignment is the fixed part.
