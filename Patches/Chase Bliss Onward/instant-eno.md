# Instant Eno

## Concept
Turn anything you play into a sprawling ambient composition — the manual's flagship full-pedal patch and a Brian Eno homage; oceanic and Freeze-forward. It's the only patch shown with a full 9-knob icon grid (SIZE, MIX, OCTAVE, ERROR, SUSTAIN, TEXTURE all active, plus TYPE/FADE/ANIMATE), each knob turned to a moderate/upper position. The manual's only verbal direction: "Play slowly and be patient; it will take awhile for your new samples to fade in."

## How to play it
1. Set every knob to its moderate/upper position per the grid above.
2. **Play slowly and be patient** — it takes a while for your new samples to fade in.
3. Pair into Dark Star / H90 (reviewers loved Onward → BigSky/H90).

## Notes
- The clock values are the dossier author's reading of the icon grid, **not** factory-printed numbers — a plausible starting point only.
