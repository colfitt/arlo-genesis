# Getting-Started Guided Tour

## Concept
The manual's step-by-step first patch — a soft floating Freeze pad, then a repeating Glitch that follows you, then ERROR pulling it apart. The canonical "lock Freeze, play Glitch over, embrace ERROR" move the pedal is built around. Run this first to internalize the two-channel feel.

## How to play it
1. Turn **FREEZE on**, play a note — a soft floating pad that follows along.
2. Turn **TEXTURE DOWN (left)** for digital seasoning.
3. Turn **OCTAVE UP** to blend a harmonizing voice.
4. Flick **ANIMATE** for motion.
5. Turn on **GLITCH**, leaving pauses between phrases.
6. Once you hear something you like, **HOLD the Glitch footswitch to lock it** — Glitch repeats while Freeze keeps following you.
7. Finally turn up **ERROR** "and watch the beautiful scenery fall apart."
