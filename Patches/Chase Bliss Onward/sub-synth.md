# Sub Synth

## Concept
A doomy lower-register synth-pad that tracks your playing and fills out the low end — the baritone-Jazzmaster / bass doom bed. Built on the FREEZE channel with OCTAVE pinned to its minimum (the half-speed octave-down voice), so a held chord becomes an infinite sub-octave drone. The manual makes only two controls load-bearing here: OCTAVE-min and SUSTAIN-up. Everything else is open by design.

## How to play it
1. Set the channel to **FREEZE**.
2. Turn **OCTAVE fully left / minimum** — this is the one load-bearing control (the octave-down, half-speed voice).
3. Bring **SUSTAIN up / high**.
4. **Hold the FREEZE footswitch** to lock an infinite low drone.
5. Per the manual: "As long as OCTAVE is at the minimum position, the rest is up to you. Experiment with FADE, TEXTURE, ANIMATE."

## Notes
- Only two controls are load-bearing: OCTAVE-min + SUSTAIN-up. Everything else (FADE, TEXTURE, ANIMATE) is open by design — experiment freely.
- Best on the baritone JM or Jazz bass off the GK-5.
- Feeds Dark Star / H90 beautifully as a sub bed.
