# Freeze-Lock + Play-Over (core trick)

## Concept
The single most-cited Onward move: lock a Freeze drone, then play glitchy or lead material over the top with the dry signal passing through unaffected. This is the literal banjo-lead-over-a-wall workflow. Per delaydude: "Hold down the freeze footswitch while triggering glitches to play over the freeze sound."

## How to play it
1. Set **LATCH dip ON.**
2. **Hold/latch the FREEZE footswitch** to lock the current sample into an infinite drone.
3. Play over it — incoming audio passes through, optionally hitting **GLITCH**.
