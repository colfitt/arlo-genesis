# Haunting Backward Texture (Long Reverse)

## Concept
Sustained backwards swells that bloom into reverb — Parting's "destroy it beautifully" reverse voice. With Dissolve well above noon you get the longest, most degraded reverse; Smear at noon-to-1:00 blooms the reverse trails into reverb. Banjo rolls and held baritone chords become stretched, degrading backwards arpeggios. This is the core ambient-ceiling sound and hands the Microcosm ideal granular fodder.

## How to play it
1. Set global defaults: Routing Stereo, Trails ON, Aux = Tap Tempo.
2. Push Dissolve to 3:00 (well above noon) for the longest, most degraded reverse.
3. Keep Chance at noon so the reverse stays steady and constant; Glitch at 11:00 keeps the clock fixed.
4. Bloom the reverse into reverb with Smear at noon–1:00; roll off reverse-trail fizz with Filter LP at 1:00.
5. Play held chords or banjo rolls and let them swell backwards; feed the result into the Microcosm.
6. Save to Preset 2.
