# Tape Wow & Flutter Delay

## Concept
Wow-and-fluttered tape-reel delay tones — Russo Music's (Eric Lapp) "tape emulation" voice: analog graininess and reel slip-and-slide. A gently degraded, pitch-warbling delay that sits between clean and broken. Vibrato is the documented "most like EQD Aqueduct" mode, and Dissolve just below noon adds gentle sample-rate grit (not full crush). Maps onto the rig's tape aesthetic alongside Generation Loss downstream.

## How to play it
1. Set global defaults: Routing Stereo, Trails ON.
2. Switch Mod to **Vibrato** but keep Depth low (9:00) — vibrato is extreme, so the warble should stay subtle.
3. Set Rate slow (10:00) with a Sine shape for a gentle pitch warble.
4. Nudge Dissolve to 11:00 (just below noon) for gentle grit, NOT full crush.
5. A few diffuse repeats from Smear at 9:00–11:00; moderate Time at noon.
