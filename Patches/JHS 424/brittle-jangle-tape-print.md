# Brittle Jangle Tape Print

## Concept
Modest Mouse's brittle single-coil jangle printed to the cassette terminus without losing the attack. Keep the gain low so the jangly attack survives the print; Treble is the make-or-break clarity control that claws the brittle articulation back through the cassette murk. The lo-fi-printed brittle jangle.

## How to play it
1. Keep Gain 1 low (10:00) so the jangle survives — just print it, don't dirty it.
2. Keep Gain 2 modest (11:00), no gate.
3. Tighten/thin the bottom with Bass (11); use Treble (1:30) to claw the brittle articulation back.
4. Run the XLR to the Apollo, mono.

## Notes
- 🟣 designed-to-emulate. Treble is the make-or-break clarity control.
- Low gain keeps the jangly attack alive through the print. No artist setting for this box.
