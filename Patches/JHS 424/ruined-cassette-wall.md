# Ruined-Cassette Wall

## Concept
The drone/doom "recorded-wrong" wall: speaker-ripping, gooey, gated cassette destruction stamped onto an already-textured bus and sustained as a wall. Post-punk / Swans terminus color. Gain 1 is heavy at 2:00 for input saturation; Gain 2 is pushed high to 2:30 but deliberately kept just under the ~3:00 gate so a sustained drone rings instead of choking.

## How to play it
1. Keep the upstream MXNHLT PORTA424 CLEAN so only this one stage does the destroying.
2. Run the XLR to the Apollo, mono.
3. Pull Bass back hard (10) — it flubs at this gain.
4. Use Treble (1:30) to claw articulation back through the murk.

## Notes
- Dossier §13(c) lists Gain 2 near max for full gating; this designed variant deliberately backs it to ~2:30 because the TASTE goal is a SUSTAINED wall — full gating would chop the drone.
- For an intentionally STUTTERED/choked texture, push Gain 2 past 3:00 instead (see Gated-Fuzz Monster).
- Designed/inferred — NOT an artist setting. No factory clocks exist.
