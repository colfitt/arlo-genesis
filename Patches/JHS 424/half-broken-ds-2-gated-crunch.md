# Half-Broken DS-2 Gated Crunch

## Concept
Car Seat Headrest's half-broken / gated DS-2 spit: brittle demo-crunch stabs from a Boss DS-2 dirt box that "keeps breaking." Gain 2 deliberately crosses the documented ~3:00 gate threshold (at 4:00) to get the spitty, gated, half-failing texture. Pick attack and note-offs do the gating — that brittleness is the point for demo-crunch stabs.

## How to play it
1. Set Gain 1 at 1:00 and push Gain 2 to 4:00 — past the gate threshold.
2. Let pick attack and note-offs do the gating = the half-failing dirt box.
3. Pull Bass back (10). Run mono, XLR.

## Notes
- 🟣 designed-to-emulate. Deliberately crosses the documented ~3:00 gate threshold to get the spit/choke = the "keeps breaking" DS-2 character.
- NOT for sustained drones (it chokes sustain — that brittleness is the point for demo-crunch stabs).
