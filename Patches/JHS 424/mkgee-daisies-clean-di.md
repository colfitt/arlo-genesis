# Mk.gee "Daisies" Clean-DI

## Concept
The low-key, almost-just-DI Mk.gee tone from "Daisies" (Justin Bieber x Mk.gee): clean signal coming through, gain only slightly pushed, treble cut a touch for that dark intimacy. An indie-folk-adjacent close, dry-forward voice. The trick is low Gain 1 with a higher Gain 2 — that combination keeps it clean while still adding character.

## How to play it
1. Run the XLR to the Apollo, no cab sim; judge on monitors.
2. Keep Gain 1 low (9:00–10:00) and push Gain 2 up (1:00–2:00) — the clean-keeping move.
3. Cut the Treble a touch (10:00–11:00) for the dark intimacy, but keep the clean tone coming through.
