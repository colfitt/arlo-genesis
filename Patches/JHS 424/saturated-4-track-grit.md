# Saturated 4-Track Grit

## Concept
Audible-but-musical cassette dirt printed on the bus: chords smear, single notes bloom. This is the "obviously recorded to a four-track" finish for the lo-fi cluster — Car Seat Headrest / Modest Mouse demo-crunch, in the Tame Impala / Mac DeMarco 8-track lineage. The grit is born on Gain 1 at noon; Gain 2 stays at noon, below the gate threshold so it doesn't choke.

## How to play it
1. Run the XLR to the Apollo, mono.
2. Push Gain 1 for more dirt at equal loudness, then trim Volume back to compensate.
3. Pull Bass back slightly (11) to stop the flub at this gain.

## Notes
- Grit is born on Gain 1 — that is your dirt control, not Volume.
- Stays below the ~3:00 Gain-2 gate threshold so it doesn't choke.
- No factory clocks exist; positions are community/directional.
