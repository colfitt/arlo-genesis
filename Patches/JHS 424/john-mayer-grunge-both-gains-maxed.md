# John Mayer "Grunge" (Both Gains Maxed)

## Concept
The most aggressive, full-breakup voice the 424 offers — Pearl Jam / Stone Gossard "Good Love Is on the Way" territory that John Mayer dialed when Josh Scott asked him for grunge in the official JHS guest demo. Both gains all the way up puts it deep past the gate, so it spits and chokes by design.

## How to play it
1. Run mono, XLR to the Apollo.
2. Trim all the way up (Gain 1 = 5:00), Channel all the way up (Gain 2 = 5:00) — Mayer's exact spoken move.
3. Set Volume (10–11) for sane output; watch Bass for flub at full gain.
