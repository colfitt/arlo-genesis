# John Mayer "Rubbery"

## Concept
The elastic, "every note falls off the cliff" / mercury-rolling dynamic tone Mayer dialed in the official JHS demo. Set near the clipping threshold: notes blow out on accents and clean up when you ease off. The dynamic-trigger TECHNIQUE — dig in to detonate, back off to clean up — is load-bearing here, more than the static knob values.

## How to play it
1. Set Gain 1 at noon and move Gain 2 up to ~2:00 so you sit right near the clipping threshold.
2. Dial Bass up ("nice and bassy", ~1:00); play fingerstyle for dynamic control.
3. TRIGGER the blow-out by digging in on accents; ease off for clean.
