# VG-800 De-Perfecter

## Concept
Making clean COSM models believable: run a modeled amp/cab or a pad/synth COSM patch through the worn cassette channel so the digital perfection turns gooey, blown-out, and "recorded-wrong." The line-level caveat is the whole craft — the VG-800 hits Gain 1 hotter than a passive guitar, so Gain 1 starts noticeably lower. This is the rig's main tool for de-glossing the VG-800.

## How to play it
1. Start Gain 1 LOW (11:00) — the VG-800 is near line level and hits Gain 1 hotter than a passive guitar; raise only to taste.
2. Keep Gain 2 at 1:00 for grain without gating.
3. For pad/drone COSM patches, nudge Gain 1 up to ~1:00 to turn them into gooey blown-out drones.
4. Run mono, XLR to the Apollo.

## Notes
- The line-level caveat is the whole craft — Gain 1 starts noticeably lower than for a guitar.
- Lean into the gooey-drone variant (Gain 1 up to ~1:00) for the taste lens.
