# Demo-Is-The-Song 4-Track Grit

## Concept
The lo-fi cluster cassette-grit terminus print where the demo IS the song. This is the "obviously recorded to a four-track" finish voiced for Car Seat Headrest / Modest Mouse bedroom-tape demo crunch left audible: chords smear, single notes bloom. Grit is born on Gain 1 at noon; Gain 2 stays at noon, well under the ~3:00 gate so it doesn't choke. Mirrors the Saturated 4-Track Grit patch, voiced here for CSH / Modest Mouse.

## How to play it
1. Push Gain 1 for more dirt at equal loudness, then trim Volume back to compensate.
2. Pull Bass back slightly (11) to stop the flub.
3. Run the XLR to the Apollo, mono-commit.
