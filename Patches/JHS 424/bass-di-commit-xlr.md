# Bass / DI Commit (XLR)

## Concept
A one-box bass recording chain: warm, tape-like saturation as a character DI, high-headroom and compressed, straight into the Apollo via XLR. This is the documented bass favorite — clean-ish character rather than overt dirt. Volume high with both gains low is the high-headroom commit move.

## How to play it
1. Take the XLR out into the Apollo; engage the ground-lift dip switch if the rack hums. Mono.
2. Keep Gain 1 at 10:00 (warm, harmonically rich preamp) and Gain 2 low at 9:00 (color without overt dirt).
3. Sculpt Bass to the room; raise Treble to de-haze if stacking effects after.
4. For a degraded bass instead, push Gain 1 and watch Bass for flub.

## Notes
- Utility/essential rather than taste-leaning — clean-ish character DI, hence not a signature ⭐.
- Volume high + both gains low = high-headroom commit.
- Clocks are community/directional; no factory values exist.
