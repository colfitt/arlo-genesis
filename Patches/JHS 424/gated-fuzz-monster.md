# Gated-Fuzz Monster

## Concept
Tight, aggressive, spitting gated fuzz — for when you WANT the choke/stutter as a rhythmic texture (brittle post-punk attack, glitchy art-rock stabs) rather than a sustained wall. Gain 2 deliberately crosses the documented ~3:00 gate threshold (at 4:00), so pick attack and note-offs do the gating.

## How to play it
1. Run mono, XLR to the Apollo.
2. Let pick attack and note-offs do the gating — dig in for the spit, let off to choke.
3. Banjo/baritone transients into this get spitty and gated; that brittleness IS the point for post-punk texture.

## Notes
- The ONE drone-finish patch where you deliberately cross the gate line — NOT for sustained drones, it chokes sustain.
- Per dossier §6, banjo/baritone transients into this get spitty and gated by design.
- Gain 2 = 4:00 crosses the quoted threshold; surrounding clocks are community/directional.
