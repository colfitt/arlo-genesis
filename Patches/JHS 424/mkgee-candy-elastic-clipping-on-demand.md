# Mk.gee "Candy" Elastic (Clipping on Demand)

## Concept
The general blown-out Mk.gee "elastic / slimy midrange-saturation" voice. It sits near the clipping threshold so accents crack lo-fi while soft passages stay clean — the signature shoegaze-adjacent dynamic crunch. Gain 1 (at noon) is the grit control; Gain 2 sits up at 1:00–2:00. The TECHNIQUE — near-threshold plus dig-in — is the load-bearing part, not the exact clocks.

## How to play it
1. Set Gain 1 at noon (grit) and Gain 2 up to 1:00–2:00.
2. Play fingerstyle on baritone / lower tuning (C standard) and trigger the blow-out dynamically.
3. For the full tone, follow with a gated/ducking distorted reverb (rig: dark short verb).

## Notes
- HONESTY: Mk.gee is the inspiration, never a documented pedal user (he uses real Tascam hardware) — this is a community-synthesized recipe chasing that tone, not his settings.
- The load-bearing finding is the TECHNIQUE (near-threshold + dig-in); clocks are inferred (demos use words, not numbers).
