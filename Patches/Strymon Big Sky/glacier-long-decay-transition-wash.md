# "Glacier" — long-decay transition wash (Outrun the Sunlight)

## Concept
The End-board transition function: a long ambient wash that carries the listener between sections. Decay sits at 20 seconds with a heavy, near-full-wet mix. Pair it with Freeze for an infinite hold underneath a section change.

## How to play it
1. Let the wash build with a heavy mix as one section ends.
2. Hold/freeze under the transition so the wall persists into the next section.
3. Release into the new part once the change has landed.
