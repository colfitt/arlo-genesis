# Eerie Chorale drone (factory bank)

## Concept
A factory bank of five Chorale slots for eerie vowel/choir drone color. **"Ghosts"** (OOOHOH vowel, high resonance) is the most drone-eerie of the set and the pick for the rig.

## How to play it
1. Pick a slot for the vowel/resonance character you want.
2. Swell or hold chords for the choir-pad wall.
3. Reach for Ghosts when you want the most unsettling drone color.

## Notes
- Vowel and Resonance are captured where stated; Decay is stated only for Epiphany. Other knobs are dial-art (not captured).
- Source data is sparse for this bank.
