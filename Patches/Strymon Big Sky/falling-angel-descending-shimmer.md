# "Falling Angel" — descending shimmer (REGEN)

## Concept
The inverse of the ascending cathedral: a long, super-spacey Shimmer where the pitch slowly falls. In REGEN mode the shimmer lives on the regen tank only (IN+REG would go even bigger). The voices are shifted down ~10 cents, and because REGEN re-feeds the tank, the pitch drops a little further each cycle — a falling shimmer. On-aesthetic for degraded/uneasy textures.

## How to play it
1. Hold a drone chord; the shimmer swells in after the attack (REGEN) and sinks in pitch.
2. Use the expression pedal on MIX for dynamic wet control — it can swell excessively, so ride it.
3. Back the Tone off if it gets shrill (it's cranked to max by default).

## Notes
- PARAM1 = Amount, PARAM2 = Mode.
- REGEN swell-after-attack is ideal for held drone chords.
- EXP → MIX is the stated technique for taming the excessive swell.
