# Drone Wall (Cloud, freeze)

## Concept
The textbook on-aesthetic drone anchor: a Cloud reverb with very long decay and high diffusion, frozen into a sustained oceanic wall. Freeze a chord and play over the top — the rig's drone/doom workhorse.

## How to play it
1. Strike or swell in a chord on baritone/banjo/VG-800.
2. Freeze it (HOLD = FREEZE) to lock the wall.
3. Play the lead over the held bed.
4. For banjo-as-lead, pull MIX back to ~12:00 so the wall sits behind the line.

## Notes
- Fully numeric but DOUG-designed (inferred from the dossier), not a found/cited setting — treat the clock positions as a starting point, not an artist's dials.
- High Diffusion smooths the Cloud grain into a pad.
