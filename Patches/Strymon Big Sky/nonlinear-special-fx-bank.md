# Nonlinear special-FX bank (reverse / gate / swoosh)

## Concept
A factory bank of eight Nonlinear slots for gated/reverse/swoosh special-FX textures, reverse pedal-steel swells, and degraded drone-wall starting points. The REVERSE slots (Creeping Up, Phase Rev) give backwards pedal-steel swells; **15B "Undulate"** is the same bank Antoine re-shaped to RAMP for the maxed drone wall (trippy-frozen-drone-wall).

## How to play it
1. Pick a slot for the Shape you want (REVERSE / SWOOSH / BOUNCE / GATE).
2. For backwards pedal-steel swells, use the REVERSE slots.
3. To build the doom drone wall, start from 15B and re-shape to RAMP.

## Notes
- On Nonlinear the front knobs remap: DECAY = Time, PRE-DELAY = Feedback.
- Shape + Decay are exact; the rest is dial-art (not captured).
