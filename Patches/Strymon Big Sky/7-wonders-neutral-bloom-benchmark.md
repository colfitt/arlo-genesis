# "7 Wonders" — neutral all-noon Bloom benchmark

## Concept
A balanced, neutral Bloom meant as a baseline to tweak from. The swell rises and grows after the note, sitting just behind the dry. Unusually for a TWP, every knob is stated in text: Length just over 50%, Feedback at noon ("medium bloomed"), and Pre-Delay/Tone/Mod all at noon.

## How to play it
1. Use it as the clean Bloom starting point.
2. Push Feedback + Decay + Mix toward a drone wall when you want more.

## Notes
- Not an aesthetic target — it's the clean baseline, hence untagged for signature.
- Every knob stated in text (rare for a TWP).
