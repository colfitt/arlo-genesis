# Chorale "Wide & Deep Ambient Swell" (HA vowel)

## Concept
The usable, fully-specified Chorale patch: an eerie synthy-choir drone color sitting behind swells, ideal for banjo-as-lead or a VG-800 pad. The HA vowel is the softer voice, with medium resonance and decay around 6 seconds. Every clock position here was verified from video.

## How to play it
1. Swell chords in; the choir color blooms behind them.
2. Drop DECAY to ~3 s for faster chord changes.
3. For a wide stereo swell, pair with a TimeLine Pattern-1 ping-pong (Time ~500 ms, Repeats 2:00, Mix 2:00, Filter 11:00, Speed 10:00, Depth 1:00).
