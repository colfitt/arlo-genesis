# Shimmer self-build / freeze-a-fifth (technique)

## Concept
A technique for held drone chords: in REGEN the shimmer lives on the regen tank, so it builds AFTER the attack rather than rising instantly. Voices are tunable from an octave down to two octaves up; small ±10-cent detunes give a lush detuned reverb instead of an obvious octave. Freeze a tuned fifth as the drone bed under a banjo lead.

## How to play it
1. Set Mode = REGEN so the shimmer builds after the attack.
2. Use PARAM1 to scroll the interval (m2/M2/m3/M3/tritone/oct/oct+5th/2 oct up/oct down).
3. Rabea's demo voicing: voice 1 octave DOWN, voice 2 octave-and-a-fifth UP, Amount full, INPUT.
4. Freeze a tuned fifth and play the lead over it.

## Notes
- REGEN "finds the harmonies you play" (community + Rabea corroboration) — be specific about the part: fixed harmonies clash if you wander off key.
- Built on the Stardust/REGEN voicing (reuse 14C) as the starting slot.
