# "Bloombient" — electronic-ocean wash

## Concept
A wash of noise "like a wave in an electronic ocean": single-note rings swept up into a wall. In Bloom, Tone is the cutoff of a synth-like resonant filter, so sweeping it (via EXP) is the signature move. Length around 20 s decay gives a huge wash; Feedback at extremes has pitch-shifting potential.

## How to play it
1. Pick single notes and let them ring.
2. Sweep Tone with the expression pedal (the resonant-filter cutoff) and nudge Length.
3. Drop DECAY for a fade-out.

## Notes
- PARAM1 = Length, PARAM2 = Feedback.
- MOD modulates both the bloom delay lines and the tank.
- Input-dependent — adjust live.
