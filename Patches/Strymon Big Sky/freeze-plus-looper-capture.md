# Freeze + Looper Capture (wash-on-wash fix #2)

## Concept
A technique recipe that pairs directly with the Big Time centerpiece/looper workflow. Freeze a chord into a pad, record a short loop of that frozen pad on a looper placed AFTER the Big Sky, then close the loop. Once you hear only the loop, you're free to switch the Big Sky to any other patch and play over the captured bed with full reverb.

## How to play it
1. Set HOLD = FREEZE (or INFINITE) and freeze a chord.
2. Record a short loop of the frozen pad on a looper placed *after* the Big Sky.
3. Close the loop and release the footswitch — you now hear only the loop.
4. Switch to any Big Sky patch and play over it with full reverb.
5. Fade/stop the loop when the section ends.

## Notes
- Looper position (AFTER the Big Sky) is what makes this work — it captures the wet wall as audio.
- Technique recipe, no knob values.
