# "For Days" — max-decay Cloud (instant drone)

## Concept
The standout factory slot for the rig: Cloud at the pedal's maximum 50-second decay with high diffusion, freeze-capable. Strike one chord and the wall sustains essentially forever — an instant drone.

## How to play it
1. Play near-zero-dry (MIX toward full wet) so the source becomes a pad.
2. Strike a chord; the 50s tail holds the wall.
3. Freeze (HOLD = FREEZE) to lock it indefinitely and play on top.

## Notes
- Decay (50.0 S), Diffusion (high), and HOLD (FREEZE) are exact from the factory list.
- MIX / TONE / PRE-DELAY / MOD are dial-art in the factory PDF (not captured) — grab the free .syx for the exact dials rather than inventing them.
