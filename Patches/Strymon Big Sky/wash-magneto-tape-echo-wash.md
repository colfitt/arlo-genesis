# "WASH" — Magneto tape-echo wash (full community patch)

## Concept
A rare full control-by-control community patch: a washed-out mix of delay and reverb on the Magneto multi-head tape echo. With Heads at 6, Even spacing, Time (Decay) maxed for the widest head spacing, and Feedback (Pre-Delay) at 2:00 for a long/sustained tail, the multi-head echo smears into a reverb wash. Dark Tone plus rolled-down Low End tame the baritone/VG-800 long-tail buildup.

## How to play it
1. Set Heads = 6, Spacing = Even.
2. Max DECAY (= Time) for the widest head spacing; Feedback (Pre-Delay) at 2:00 for sustain (not maxed).
3. Pair with a footswitched delay in front for broken-tape textures.

## Notes
- On Magneto the front knobs remap: PRE-DELAY → Feedback, DECAY → Time.
- MIX 12:00 = 50/50.
- All knobs stated. Thread body is paywalled; values came through a search-indexed quote, verified as written.
