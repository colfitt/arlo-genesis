# Magneto factory tape bank (rhythmic + textural)

## Concept
A factory bank of eight Magneto tape-echo slots — the rig's degraded/recorded-wrong-adjacent machine, spanning rhythmic and textural repeats. Uneven spacing (6 Marblet, What 4, Cerberus) is textural; Even spacing is rhythmic.

## How to play it
1. Pick a slot for the head count / spacing / delay-time feel you want.
2. Pair with a footswitched delay (TimeLine / Big Time) per Rabea, tapping on/off for set repeats.

## Notes
- DECAY = last-head delay time; PRE-DELAY = Feedback.
- Heads/Spacing/Decay/HOLD are exact; Low End/Diffusion/Mod are dial-art (not captured).
