# Shimmer drone-fifth / octave beds (factory bank)

## Concept
A factory bank of six Shimmer slots, all with high Amount, for tune-your-own-harmony cathedral and drone-fifth/octave beds. The drone-fifth/octave picks are **5th Wheel** (-P5/+P5) and **Low Blow** (-OCT/+OCT) — freeze one under a banjo lead for a tuned harmony bed.

## How to play it
1. Pick a slot for the interval you want (fifth, octave, second, etc.).
2. Strike or swell a chord; the shifted voices stack the harmony.
3. Freeze (or INFINITE on 5th Wheel) to lock it as a banjo-lead bed and play over the top.

## Notes
- Shift/Mode/Decay/HOLD are exact from the factory list; other knobs are dial-art (not captured).
- 5th Wheel and Low Blow are the drone-fifth/octave workhorses for the rig.
