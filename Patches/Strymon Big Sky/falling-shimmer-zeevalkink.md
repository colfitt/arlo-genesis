# Falling Shimmer (Evert Zeevalkink — both voices down)

## Concept
A named-artist recipe: both Shimmer voices nudged down ~10 cents (no up-octave at all), Amount maxed, in REGEN so the detune compounds and sinks each cycle. The result is a continuously descending, queasy detuned wall that swells after you stop playing — the recorded-wrong/degraded character without the bright octave-up worship sheen.

## How to play it
1. Sit it under a sustained baritone or banjo drone.
2. Let the wall build and sink as REGEN re-feeds the detuned voices.
3. Freeze to lock the sinking wall and play a lead over it.

## Notes
- Concrete Shift/Amount/Mode/Mix from a named artist; DECAY/TONE/MOD/PRE-DELAY were not stated.
- Distinct from "Detune Verb" (INPUT, both-direction): here BOTH voices are DOWN and the mode is REGEN, so the detune compounds and sinks rather than staying static.
