# Shimmer Cathedral (ascending, IN+REG)

## Concept
A textbook ascending pitched cathedral: Shimmer with voice 1 up an octave and voice 2 up an octave-and-a-fifth, in IN+REG mode so the shift applies to both the input and the regen tank. Makes a shoegaze lead bed or an ascending wall.

## How to play it
1. Play or swell a chord; the shimmer voices rise above it.
2. For banjo-as-lead, pull MIX to 11:00 so the shimmer sits behind the line, not on top.
3. Let the ~8s decay sustain the cathedral.

## Notes
- Fully numeric but DOUG-designed (inferred from the dossier), not a found/cited setting — treat clock positions as a starting point.
- The Mix-11:00 variant is the rig-specific move for keeping the banjo readable.
