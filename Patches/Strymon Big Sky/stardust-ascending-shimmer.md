# "Stardust" — ascending shimmer (REGEN, freeze)

## Concept
The factory "Stardust" slot: a Shimmer cathedral with voice 1 at OCT-5th and voice 2 up an octave, high Amount, in REGEN mode. REGEN builds the shimmer after the attack rather than rising instantly, which suits held chords. Long 13.7s decay, freeze-capable.

## How to play it
1. Strike or swell a chord; the shimmer builds in after the attack (REGEN).
2. Freeze it (HOLD = FREEZE) to lock the wall as a banjo-lead bed.
3. Play the lead over the held shimmer.
