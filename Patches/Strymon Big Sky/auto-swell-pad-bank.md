# Auto-swell pad bank (factory, banjo-friendly)

## Concept
A factory bank of six Swell slots — auto volume-swell into verb that hides the percussive attack, ideal for banjo-as-lead pads. The SWLDRY members (Slumber, Wet Swells, Dry Off) erase the EBM-5 banjo pluck entirely into an instant pad; "Stay With U" is the longest at 20 s.

## How to play it
1. Pick a SWLDRY slot to erase the banjo pluck into a pad, or a SWLWET slot to keep the dry attack with a swelling reverb behind it.
2. Pick/play; the auto-swell hides (or accompanies) the attack.
3. Hold/freeze per the slot for a sustained bed.

## Notes
- Decay/Rise/Mode/HOLD are exact from the factory list.
- SWLDRY = erases the attack; SWLWET = keeps the attack, swells the reverb.
