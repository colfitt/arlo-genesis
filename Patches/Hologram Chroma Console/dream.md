# Dream

## Concept
A soft, pad-like, darkened tone. TEXTURE FILTER in the LPF zone (~7–10 o'clock) removes a lot of the highs — "like turning the tone knob down on the guitar" — for a soft pad.

## How to play it
1. TEXTURE **FILTER** in the LPF zone (~7–10 o'clock).
2. Roll off the highs until it reads as a soft pad.
