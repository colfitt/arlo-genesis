# Gestural Motion Swells

## Concept
Turns staccato banjo/baritone Blooper loops into evolving, bowed-drone swells. The GESTURE is the "manual LFO" doing the work — record a slow sweep, then play it back fast via clock so it moves faster than your hand could.

## How to play it
1. CHARACTER **SWELL**, AMOUNT **12** (clock-face), SENSITIVITY to taste.
2. DIFFUSION **SPACE**, TIME **1 o'clock**, AMOUNT **11**.
3. Record a **GESTURE (C+D)** on **MIX** or **TILT**, sync it to tap tempo, then speed it up.
4. Set **MIX ~60%**.
