# Banjo-as-Lead, Recorded-Wrong

## Concept
The structural intent the whole board is built around — banjo (GK-5 → VG-800) as a degraded lead voice. Roll off the bright top, turn staccato banjo loops into bowed-sounding drones, and smear them into the signature texture.

## How to play it
1. TEXTURE **CASSETTE + FILTER (LPF, ~7–10 o'clock)** to roll off the bright top.
2. CHARACTER **SWELL** (or a recorded GESTURE volume swell) turns staccato banjo loops into bowed-sounding drones.
3. DIFFUSION **COLLAGE** or **REVERSE** on a banjo Blooper loop = the "banjo-as-lead, recorded-wrong" texture.
4. Calibrate at MEDIUM/HIGH headroom.

## Notes
- Rig recipe — named effects, no numerics. This is the structural intent the whole board is designed around.
