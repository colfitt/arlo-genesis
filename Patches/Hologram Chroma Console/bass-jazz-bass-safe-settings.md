# Bass / Jazz-Bass Safe Settings

## Concept
A source-specific recipe for using the Chroma sparingly on bass so the fundamental survives. Keep MIX low and lean on width/size rather than degradation.

## How to play it
1. Keep **MIX** low.
2. Lean on MOVEMENT **DOUBLER** and DIFFUSION **SPACE** for width/size.
3. Keep TEXTURE **BROKEN** and **SQUASH** modest.
4. Set calibration to the appropriate lower-distortion headroom.
