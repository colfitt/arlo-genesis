# Subtle Tape Glue

## Concept
The whisper-level tape voice in the rig — a hair of CASSETTE plus a DRIFT-warbled vibrato that reads as "recorded a while ago" without announcing itself. Meant to sit under a loop for cohesion only when Gen Loss is dialed back, so you don't double the wow/flutter.

## How to play it
1. TEXTURE **CASSETTE**, AMOUNT **9** (clock-face) — just a hint of tape.
2. MOVEMENT **VIBRATO**, RATE **9**, AMOUNT **8**, secondary **DRIFT 9** — subtle warble.
3. Bypass the other modules (hold their button).
4. Set **MIX ~70%** and leave it nearly always-on under a loop.

## Notes
- One of only four fully-numeric patches on disk — but the dials are DOUG-inferred, not found or artist-attributed. Treat the clock-face values as a designed starting point.
- Use ONLY when Gen Loss is dialed back, or you double the wow/flutter.
