# Artifacting Delay

## Concept
A degraded/recorded-wrong loop texture — the delay pitch-bends and glitches on a TIME move, then loops the artifact. DIFFUSION can be CASCADE, REELS, or COLLAGE; COLLAGE self-edits destructively (half/double-speed, pitch bends) as TIME moves.

## How to play it
1. Set DIFFUSION to **CASCADE, REELS, or COLLAGE**.
2. Record a **GESTURE (C+D)** moving the **TIME** knob.
3. The delay pitch-bends on the TIME move, then loops the artifact.
