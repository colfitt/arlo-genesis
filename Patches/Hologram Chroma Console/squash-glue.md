# SQUASH Glue

## Concept
A tiny bit of compression glue — or a way to fatten a filter-thinned sound back up. TEXTURE SQUASH is a console-style heavy compressor that runs into overdrive; just a tiny bit of AMOUNT glues (KVNDRA), or push it to re-fatten a thinned source ("would work well with a bit of drive" — Robin Vincent).

## How to play it
1. TEXTURE **SQUASH**.
2. For glue: just a tiny bit of AMOUNT (KVNDRA).
3. For re-fattening: push AMOUNT to thicken a filter-thinned sound back up.

## Notes
- Watch the lows on bass/baritone (can flub the fundamental).
