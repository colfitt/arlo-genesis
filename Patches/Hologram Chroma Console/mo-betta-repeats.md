# Mo Betta Repeats

## Concept
A sustained, squashed swelling-echo bloom — repeats with no hard attack. Delay comes first, then SWELL removes the attack from the repeats, then SQUASH compresses/overdrives them into a sustained wall.

## How to play it
1. Set the chain (module order): **DIFFUSION CASCADE *or* REELS → CHARACTER SWELL → TEXTURE SQUASH.**
2. Delay first, swell the repeats (removes the hard attack), then compress/overdrive them.
3. Dial per-knob amounts to taste.

## Notes
- Sustained-wall fit. Concrete module order is published; no numerics.
