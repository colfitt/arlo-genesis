# Microcosm Pairing Labor-Split

## Concept
A pairing recipe (not a single patch) for running the Chroma alongside the Microcosm without redundancy. Let one pedal own SPACE/reverb, keep INTERFERENCE distinct from the granular glitch, and layer so Chroma shapes character/movement/texture while granular space sits on top.

## How to play it
1. Let ONE pedal own SPACE/reverb (KVNDRA skips SPACE on Chroma and uses the Microcosm for space instead).
2. Keep Chroma's **INTERFERENCE** distinct from the Microcosm's granular glitch (they overlap tonally).
3. Layering logic: Chroma shapes character/movement/texture; granular space sits on top.

## Notes
- This is a pairing recipe applied across the bank, not a single dial-in patch.
