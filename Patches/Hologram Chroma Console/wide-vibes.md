# Wide Vibes

## Concept
A huge wide stereo image with a touch of chorus — re-widens a mono signal (the rig's re-expander job). Built on VIBRATO with its secondary DRIFT turned up to engage stereo width: "immediately it pulls everything to the sides, sounds super wide, super huge," with a touch of chorusing.

## How to play it
1. MOVEMENT **VIBRATO** on its stereo-spread side.
2. Turn the secondary **DRIFT up** to engage the stereo width.
3. Result: everything pulls to the sides, super wide and huge with light chorus.
