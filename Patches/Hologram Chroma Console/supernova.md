# Supernova

## Concept
Scattered multi-octave delay that shoots up and down "like a spaceship," resolving into an ambient soundscape generator. COLLAGE is a looping delay that destructively re-pitches as TIME moves, spitting signal back in different octaves; a recorded GESTURE sweeps those octaves, and a ton of reverb glues it into a wash.

## How to play it
1. DIFFUSION **COLLAGE** (looping delay that re-pitches destructively as TIME moves).
2. Record a **GESTURE (C+D)** that sweeps octaves up and down.
3. "Throw a ton of reverb on it" — heavy SPACE here or downstream (e.g. H90).
