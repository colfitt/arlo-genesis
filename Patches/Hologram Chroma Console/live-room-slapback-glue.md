# Live-Room Slapback Glue

## Concept
A vintage Space-Echo slapback as vocal/lead glue — modeled on Andrew Sarlo's documented EchoBoy-on-lead-vocal move on Big Thief "Not." A short REELS slapback with low feedback and aged DRIFT flutter, glued under a close voice rather than washed across it.

## How to play it
1. DIFFUSION **REELS** (tape echo) at a short **SLAPBACK TIME**, AMOUNT (feedback) **low** (one-two repeats), secondary **DRIFT ~50** (aged/flutter — the Space-Echo character Sarlo chased).
2. CHARACTER **SWEETEN** light glue.
3. Keep **MIX** modest so it's a glue texture on the close voice, not a wash.
4. Run Buffered+Trails, both outs connected (mono→stereo re-expansion).

## Notes
- 🟣⭐ Glue texture, not a wash — the restraint/live-room lane. Maps to Chroma sheet #1 + DRIFT cheat-sheet. Suggested slot A.
