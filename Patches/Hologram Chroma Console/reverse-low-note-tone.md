# Reverse Low-Note Tone

## Concept
A low reversed note on a lead or pad source. DIFFUSION REVERSE's top TIME/pitch knob sweeps the reverse pitch from very high to very low — set it low for a low reversed tone, with AMOUNT pulled back to blend. REVERSE works on leads/pads, not staccato arps.

## How to play it
1. DIFFUSION **REVERSE** on a low note.
2. Use the top **TIME**/pitch knob to set the reverse pitch (very high → very low).
3. Pull **AMOUNT** back to blend.

## Notes
- REVERSE's own DRIFT is effectively inert (community-confirmed) — animate with a GESTURE on TIME instead.
- Use on lead/pad sources, not staccato arps.
