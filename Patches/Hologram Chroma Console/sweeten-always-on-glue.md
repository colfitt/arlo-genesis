# SWEETEN Always-On Glue

## Concept
Keep one module always-on as a preamp/EQ/compression glue while the rest of the chain footswitches off — the in-pedal analogue of the always-on CB Clean. SWEETEN (preamp EQ + compression + gentle saturation) is set to stay engaged via Dual Bypass even when the pedal is "bypassed."

## How to play it
1. CHARACTER **SWEETEN** (preamp EQ + compression + gentle saturation).
2. In **FX Setup → Dual Bypass**, hold the BYPASS footswitch and make SWEETEN **solid yellow** (stays on) while the other modules blink yellow (bypass).
3. SWEETEN now stays engaged even when the pedal is "bypassed" = always-on glue, rest footswitchable.

## Notes
- SWEETEN is the gentle clean lift/thickener.
- SENSITIVITY ~7 o'clock = cleanest / highest fidelity.
