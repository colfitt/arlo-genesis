# Baritone Movement / Cut

## Concept
A source-specific recipe for processing the baritone Jazzmaster / low-end mass without flubbing the lows. Avoid the effects that smear bass, and lean on width, movement, and resonant stabs that cut through a baritone drone.

## How to play it
1. AVOID heavy **CASSETTE** and **SQUASH** (both flub the lows — leave heavy tape destruction to Gen Loss).
2. Use instead DIFFUSION **SPACE** (reverb), MOVEMENT **PHASER/VIBRATO** (movement), or CHARACTER **HOWL** (resonant stabs that cut through a baritone drone).
