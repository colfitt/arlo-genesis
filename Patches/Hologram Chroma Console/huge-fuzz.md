# Huge Fuzz

## Concept
A big, wide, full fuzz that's still controlled enough to be a driving lead voice. HOWL provides a resonant filter fuzz, DOUBLER spreads it wide in stereo, and a touch of SPACE adds room — "big, wide, full, controlled enough for a big driving lead guitar part." Translates well to banjo-as-lead.

## How to play it
1. CHARACTER **HOWL** (resonant filter fuzz).
2. MOVEMENT **DOUBLER** (stereo width).
3. DIFFUSION **SPACE** (a little reverb).
