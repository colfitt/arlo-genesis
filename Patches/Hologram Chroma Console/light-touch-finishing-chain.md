# Light-Touch Finishing Chain

## Concept
Use all four modules together as a stacked light-touch color/glue stage to "finish" a source — and to make a clean source sound like it's falling apart / malfunctioning (the recorded-wrong target). The idea is "adding 10% of this, 5% of that, 20% of something else" across CHARACTER / MOVEMENT / DIFFUSION / TEXTURE rather than one cranked effect. Rival Consoles' path: Sequential Prophet Rev2 → Chroma.

## How to play it
1. Engage all four modules at **small AMOUNTs**.
2. Distribute tiny amounts — 10% of one, 5% of another, 20% of a third — across CHARACTER / MOVEMENT / DIFFUSION / TEXTURE.
3. Aim for "finished" glue, or push toward the "malfunctioning" recorded-wrong target.

## Notes
- **APPROACH ONLY** — Rival Consoles published NO specific module/effect/knob values; the "malfunctioning effect" on *Nocturne* is the cited target, not a recipe. The gold-standard taste reference for this rig, but it carries no dial-ins.
