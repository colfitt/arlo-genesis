# VG-800 Pad → Evolving Cloud

## Concept
Turn degraded modeled VG-800 pad/COSM patches into deep, evolving ambient clouds. Secondary DRIFT (on MOVEMENT and/or DIFFUSION) plus DIFFUSION COLLAGE keeps the texture morphing and re-pitching into a cloud.

## How to play it
1. Turn secondary **DRIFT up** (on MOVEMENT and/or DIFFUSION).
2. Engage DIFFUSION **COLLAGE** — together they make evolving clouds.
3. Calibrate at **"Very High" headroom** (VG-800 runs near line level — the manual lists line-level/modular there).
