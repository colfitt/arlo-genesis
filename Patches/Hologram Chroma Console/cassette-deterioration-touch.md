# CASSETTE Deterioration Touch

## Concept
TEXTURE CASSETTE is wow & flutter, slowing/speeding, and magnetic-tape degrade that worsens as AMOUNT climbs — "like running your sound through a Portastudio." Use a little for a subtle sense of deterioration; crank it for heavy degrade.

## How to play it
1. TEXTURE **CASSETTE** — start with a little AMOUNT for subtle deterioration.
2. Climb AMOUNT for heavier wow/flutter and tape degrade.

## Notes
- Robin Vincent's favorite Texture effect.
- Watch the lows on baritone/bass — CASSETTE flubs bass-heavy material.
- Don't stack it heavy against Gen Loss (the "don't double the tape" rig rule).
