# CAPTURE — Drone vs Looper

## Concept
A mechanism recipe (not a single patch) — the 30-second sustainer/looper. A short note becomes a seamless overlapping ambient pad/drone; a longer phrase becomes a traditional looper with a harder cutoff. Sustained-wall building.

## How to play it
1. Hold the **LEFT (TAP/CAPTURE)** footswitch to record up to **30s**; release to loop.
2. SHORT note → seamless overlapping ambient pad/drone (blue LED).
3. LONGER phrase → traditional looper with a harder cutoff (green LED).
4. It records parameter moves while looping; a NEW recording overwrites (no take-layering).
5. Route PRE or POST FX in FX Setup via the MIX knob (far left = PRE, far right = POST); it prints at unity dry volume.

## Notes
- This is a technique recipe applied across the bank, not a single dial-in patch.
- **CAPTURE recordings are NOT saved in presets.**
- SOS flagged the sustainer-pad mode as finicky / not exactly matching the docs.
