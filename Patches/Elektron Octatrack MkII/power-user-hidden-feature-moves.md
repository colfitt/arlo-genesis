# Power-User Hidden-Feature Moves (dice, retrig-on-fader, scenes-as-keys)

## Concept
A technique grab-bag of endless-variation and happy-accident moves you layer on top of any patch — generative scrubbing, instant randomization, pitched stutters. This is the crossfader/retrig slice of the Elektronauts trick thread (it overlaps the STRT-LFO Generative Scrub patch by design). The retrig-on-fader-as-pitch tool and slide-trig RATE tape-feel are the on-aesthetic picks.

## How to play it
1. **Crossfader → RETRIG:** assign retrig to the fader; **RTIM steps are tuned in accurate chromatic semitones** (retrig as a pitch/comb tool).
2. **Scenes as keyboard:** program scenes with different filter/pitch/STRT, hold [SCENE B]+tap scene slots like keys.
3. **The dice:** [TRACK PARAM]+[YES] randomizes the current param page (+[NO] reloads saved); pair with PART RELOAD ([FUNC]+[CUE] or [FUNC]+up/RELOAD) to tweak destructively then snap back.
4. **Slide trigs on RATE** = tape-inertia PIPO / pitch glides.
5. **STRT-LFO scrub** for endless variation.
6. Remove a lock surgically: hold [NO]+the PARAM knob while the sequencer plays over the step.
