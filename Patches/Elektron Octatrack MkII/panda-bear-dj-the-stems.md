# Panda Bear DJ-the-Stems (7 stems + Track 8 master)

## Concept
Laptop-free song performance and song-to-song transitions — "Ableton in a box." Bounce a song to ~7 stems, one per Track 1-7, with Track 8 as a master comp+reverb glue channel, and perform mutes/levels/FX per stem live with the crossfader. A clean stem-playback Project template, broadly useful even though it's not the rig's drone/mangle aesthetic.

## How to play it
1. Bounce a song to **~7 stems → one per Track 1-7**.
2. **Track 8 = master channel** (comp + reverb glue).
3. Perform mutes/levels/FX per stem live with the crossfader.
4. *Two-OT variant:* two identical units holding the SAME content on both cards = cue the next song on OT B while OT A plays, crossfade between them = gapless song-to-song stem-DJing without a laptop.
