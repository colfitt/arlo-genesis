# EZBOT Performance Mixer / Ultimate FX Template (64 FX, 4 Parts)

## Concept
The best off-the-shelf match for OT-as-live-FX-mixer for the pedalboard — 64 effects across 4 Parts themed Space / Build / Crush / Party, pre-built so you don't have to construct 64 scenes by hand. Two independent loopers, two compressors (one per input pair), sidechain pumping on every pattern, and a freeze-delay pre-placed on Tracks 1 and 5. Works on MkI and MkII.

## How to play it
1. Patch pedalboard → A/B (+2nd source → C/D).
2. Pick a Part: **Space** = ambient drone, **Crush** = degraded walls (also Build, Party).
3. Ride the crossfader between two scenes (each Part = 16 scenes; named scene-FX include "Hallway Riser," "EZ Drummer," "Tape Stop," "Daft Punk Effect Alt").
4. Use the 2 loopers + the tracks-1/5 freeze-delay to sustain.
5. Concrete defaults: **Tempo 120 BPM; pattern length 64 steps (4 bars); recording starts at step 1 of 64; ALL recorder captures at UNITY GAIN.**

## Notes
