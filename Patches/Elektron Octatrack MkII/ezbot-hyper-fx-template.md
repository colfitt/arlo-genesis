# EZBOT Hyper FX Template (punch-in FX as an instrument)

## Concept
Glitchy/lo-fi punch-in FX played by hand like an instrument — beat-repeat, stutter, stretch, reverse, and tape-stop on demand. 64 scenes across 4 Parts (FX blocks named Hyper / Corpo / Zone / Dream), with a Freeze Delay pre-placed on Track 1. The tape-stop/stutter/freeze palette suits degraded-tape and glitch-lead work. $22.

## How to play it
1. Pick an FX block: **Hyper / Corpo / Zone / Dream** (64 scenes / 4 Parts, 16 each).
2. Punch in FX by hand: **Beat Repeat ×4, Stutter ×4, Stretch ×4, Filters ×3, Master Looper, Tape-stop & speed-up (Dream block), Reverse** (needs a pre-recorded 4-bar loop), **Freeze Delay on Track 1**.
3. Default scale = INFINITE; PATTERN CHANGE = 64 steps (4 bars).
4. Sample into the free Flex track (ships as a duplicate project with a free Flex track).
5. Enable **RECORD QUICK MODE** in system settings first (required).
