# Slip Pseudo-Sitar & Glitch Trails

## Concept
Slip's continuous auto-sampling and pitch-shifting, run **live (not frozen)**, makes pseudo-sitar drones and glitchy reverse-flavored trails — whimsical harmonies that follow behind your playing. **TIME = sampling size**: high produces harmonized phrases that trail your notes (the drone/sitar feel); low gives an instant pitch-shifter response with glitchier, reverse-flavored stutters. **MODIFY = playback speed/direction in semitone steps** (CCW of noon = reverse). Great as interstitial/ambient filler between songs or under a solo. Distinct from the frozen-Slip glitch-synth and the doom Slip-reverb-smear: here Slip runs live, not frozen.

## How to play it
1. Set Wet MODE = **Slip**.
2. Use a higher **TIME** so harmonized phrases follow behind your notes (drone/sitar feel).
3. Set **MODIFY** off neutral for sustained pitch-shifted harmonies (semitone steps).
4. Lower **TIME** for an instant pitch-shifter response and glitchier, reverse-flavored stutters.
5. Optionally engage **SPREAD** for auto-panned drama; use as ambient filler between songs or under a solo.
