# TONE — wet hi-cut to darken / V1 voicing

## Concept
Tame the MkII's brighter, more-present wet voicing with a hidden high-cut filter, pushing the wash back toward the darker/mellower original MOOD sound or simply sitting it under a mix. It's a simple but drastic-at-max low-pass on the Wet Channel, set with the Wet MODIFY knob inside the hidden options menu — the documented gentle way to keep MOOD's degraded wash from getting harsh before downstream stages.

## How to play it
1. Hold both footswitches until the LEDs turn green (hidden-options menu).
2. Turn the Wet MODIFY knob counter-clockwise to roll off highs (CW = brightest/default).
3. Stop where the wash sits under your mix or matches the darker original-MOOD voicing.
4. Exit by releasing/tapping out; the Wet MODIFY knob returns to its normal smear/feedback duty.

## Notes
- No published clock-face / numeric value for the cut — this is a dialable recipe set by ear. The cut is gentle in the lower range but drastic near max, so listen, don't set it by eye.
- Default is fully CW (brightest); on-camera the cut at max was drastic ("took all the highs out").
- This is the documented gentle way to keep MOOD's degraded wash from getting harsh before downstream stages.
