# Replace Trick — clear space & punch in glitches

## Concept
The Micro-Looper's bypass doubles as a **replace** function. The instant you bypass the channel it starts erasing the existing loop and recording your input in its place — so bypassing isn't just "off," it's a live overwrite. That makes it a fast way to clear a section or punch in glitch stabs. It's distinct from **overdub** (which layers new material onto the loop) because **bypass replaces** what was there.

## How to play it
1. Have a micro-loop playing.
2. **Bypass the Micro-Looper Channel** (RIGHT footswitch) — it immediately starts erasing the loop and recording your live input in its place.
3. **Hold it bypassed** to clear space, or **punch quickly** for glitch stabs.
4. **Re-engage playback** to lock in the new/replaced material.
