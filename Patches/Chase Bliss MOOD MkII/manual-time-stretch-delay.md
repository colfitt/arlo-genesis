# Manual Time-Stretch (Delay)

## Concept
Tape-style manual time-stretch with no pitch artifacts. Turn **MIX and MODIFY up**, then **sweep the TIME knob by hand** — you get clean transitions with no pitch-bends in the existing echoes. This is the Delay mode's headline trick: the TIME knob is safe to sweep live.

## How to play it
1. Set Wet MODE = **Delay**.
2. Turn **MIX** up and **MODIFY** up.
3. Play, then **sweep the TIME knob by hand** — the echoes stretch cleanly without detuning.

## Notes
- The Delay TIME knob is the only one designed to be swept live for this trick — widely cited across the on-disk notes.
- Turn **CLASSIC** on if you *want* rubbery pitch-bend in the stretch instead of clean transitions.
