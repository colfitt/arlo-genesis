# Balance Beam — degraded-but-in-tune Tape loop

## Concept
A "recorded-wrong" tape loop that stays at its **original pitch/speed** but gains aliasing vibe — exactly the degrade-without-detuning trick this rig wants. Set Looper MODE = **Tape**, **lower CLOCK** to introduce grit/character, then **raise MODIFY** to speed the loop back up to its recorded speed → "same speed as you recorded it, but more vibe."

## How to play it
1. Set Looper MODE = **Tape** and capture a loop.
2. **Lower CLOCK** to introduce grit and aliasing character.
3. **Raise MODIFY** to bring the loop back up to its recorded speed — degraded but in-tune.
4. Set LENGTH to taste.

## Notes
- Inverse move = slow CLOCK + 2×/4× MODIFY for the gritty-Tape sound (gritty-tape-loop).
- Perfect for banjo/baritone beds. Turn **CLASSIC** on for deteriorating loops.
