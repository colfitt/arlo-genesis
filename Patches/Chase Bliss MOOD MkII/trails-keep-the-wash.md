# TRAILS — keep the wash on bypass

## Concept
Let the Wet Channel smoothly fade out after you bypass the pedal instead of cutting dead, so toggling the effect mid-performance doesn't kill the vibe. The recommended default-on dip for ambient/drone work where an abrupt cut would break the spell. Behavior is identical to MkI TRAILS.

## How to play it
1. Flick the **TRAILS** dip on.
2. Build a wet wash / freeze.
3. Bypass the pedal mid-passage — the wet tail fades out smoothly instead of cutting off.
4. Leave **TRAILS** on as your default for ambient sets so toggling never kills the wash.

## Notes
- No knob values — TRAILS is a behavior toggle, not a dialed setting. Dial the Wet Channel itself to taste.
- Power-user preference: "I like to have trails engaged… so it keeps a smoother effect… I don't want it to kill the vibe."
- Same behavior as MkI TRAILS.
- The looper is separate — TRAILS governs the **Wet Channel** fade; in true-bypass the looper doesn't run at all.
