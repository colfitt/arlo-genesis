# EXP/CV single-pedal texture morph

## Concept
One expression pedal sweeping several knobs at once to "morph the whole texture." Plug into the EXP/CV jack (TRS expression, or floating-ring TS for CV) and assign it to any ONE of the five knobs — or several simultaneously (TIME, LENGTH, MODIFY, CLOCK, the other MODIFY) — via the back dips. e.g. sweep **CLOCK + MODIFY + LENGTH** together for a full-texture morph.

## How to play it
1. Connect an expression pedal (TRS) or CV source (floating-ring TS) to the EXP/CV jack.
2. Use the back dips to assign it to one or several knobs (TIME / LENGTH / MODIFY / CLOCK / other MODIFY).
3. Choose BOUNCE (modulate) or ramp-and-hold (rise/fall) behavior.
4. Sweep — e.g. CLOCK + MODIFY + LENGTH together morph the whole texture. With no knob engaged, EXP controls MIX.

## Notes
- ⚠️ **0–5V only — any negative/over-voltage can damage the pedal.**
