# FADE (slowly evolving loops / looper-as-delay)

## Concept
Loops that gradually fade while overdubbing — the ambient slow-build move, or a way to treat the Micro-Looper like a delay. This is a hidden option: hold **both footswitches** (LEDs green), then turn **looper LENGTH** down/CCW to set FADE-while-overdubbing. New overdubs slowly replace old material instead of stacking forever.

## How to play it
1. **Hold both footswitches** until the LEDs go green (hidden-options mode).
2. Turn **looper LENGTH** down/CCW to engage FADE-while-overdubbing.
3. Overdub into the loop — older layers gradually fade out as you add new ones.

## Notes
- ⚠️ Mutually exclusive with **NO DUB** ("no dub, no fade").
