# Slip-Reverb Smear (rig patch b)

## Concept
Pitch-stepped harmonized haze; doom drift when frozen. Slip just off neutral (±a few semitones) for a harmonized smear, with SPREAD scattering it across the field. For doom drift, freeze Slip into its glitchy synth and play under it.

## How to play it
1. Set Wet MODE = **Slip**, TIME 11:00 (instant-ish), MODIFY just off neutral (±a few semitones). Micro-Looper off (or **Env** at low sensitivity). CLOCK noon, MIX noon.
2. Engage **SPREAD** for scatter.
3. Play for a pitch-stepped harmonized haze.
4. For doom drift: **freeze** Slip into its glitchy synth (freeze-idea-slip-solo) and play under it.
