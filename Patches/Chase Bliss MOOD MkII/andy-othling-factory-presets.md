# Andy Othling factory presets — copy-by-ear

## Concept
The official onboard preset suggestions — the ambient/drone voices that defined the pedal, authored by Andy Othling (Lowercase Noises). Named presets: *Nude Tayne · Tittleman's Crest · Krunkle Kick · Sweet Berry Wine · Uncle Muscles · Glow Bones · All the Food is Poison · Where's My Chippy? · Business Hugs.*

## How to play it
1. Listen to the named presets on the Chase Bliss SoundCloud (soundcloud.com/chaseblissaudio/sets/m-o-o-d).
2. Copy the sound **by ear** — there is no published knob table.
3. If you transcribe one, store it to **Slot L or R** (dips save with it).

## Notes
- ⚠️ **NO STORED KNOB VALUES ARE PUBLISHED ANYWHERE — audio only on the CB SoundCloud; copy by ear.**
- Attribution is real and cited (Othling authored them); values deliberately **not fabricated** — no knob table exists on disk.
