# SMOOTH-Clock continuous pitch-bend

## Concept
De-stepping the CLOCK into continuous pitch-bends — a strong drone/SFX move. Engage the **SMOOTH** dip and CLOCK sweeps continuously instead of jumping in harmonized 4th/5th steps. Sweep CLOCK by hand, or via ramp/expression, for fluid downsample bends. Demoed as "gnarly … so crazy."

## How to play it
1. Engage the **SMOOTH** dip.
2. Sweep **CLOCK** by hand for a continuous pitch-bend (no stepping).
3. For hands-free bends, drive CLOCK with an expression pedal or internal ramp.

## Notes
- Combine with EXP (exp-cv-texture-morph) or RAMPING (ramping-waveform-select) for hands-free generative bends.
