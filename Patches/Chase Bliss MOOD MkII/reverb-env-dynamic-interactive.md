# Reverb + Env — dynamic / interactive

## Concept
Reverb wash where your **attack carves into the drone** — your dynamics stutter and freeze slices into the bed. A **mode pairing only** (no published knob values) — set the Env looper per Manual-Stretch-by-Touch and layer Reverb over it so your touch shapes the wash.

## How to play it
1. Set Wet MODE = **Reverb**.
2. Set Looper MODE = **Env** and use the Manual-Stretch-by-Touch gesture (env-manual-stretch-by-touch).
3. Play dynamically — softer attacks slow/freeze slices into the reverb bed.

## Notes
- No knob values published — pairing only.
