# Kid-A Mistreated Micro-Loop (CLASSIC + Tape)

## Concept
Kid A's "loops mistreated, stretched, pulled out of phase — everything breathes wrong on purpose." A tape micro-loop that deteriorates and detunes as it runs, made playable: capture a phrase with **CLASSIC** on, then use the **Balance Beam** move (lower CLOCK for grit/aliasing, then raise MODIFY back to recorded speed) → "same speed, but more vibe" — degraded but in-tune, the mistreated-loop sound without falling out of key.

## How to play it
1. Set Looper MODE = **Tape** and turn the **CLASSIC** dip ON (the single most on-aesthetic switch — loops gradually deteriorate/distort over time).
2. Capture a phrase.
3. Apply Balance Beam: **lower CLOCK** for grit/aliasing, then **raise MODIFY** to bring the loop back up to its recorded speed (degraded but in-tune).
4. For "pulled out of phase," let CLASSIC + low CLOCK drift it. LENGTH to taste.
5. Layer the Wet **Reverb** over it for the wash.

## Notes
- This is the Kid A method made playable: Ed cut up the Rhodes in real time over loops — here you capture, then mistreat the loop live while playing fresh over the top.
- Commit the result to Blooper (SYNC + downstream) for a kept layer.
