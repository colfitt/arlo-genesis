# Freeze Pad (Wet hold)

## Concept
An infinite sustained bed to play baritone/banjo over — the core drone move. Set the Wet Channel to taste, then **HOLD the LEFT (Wet) footswitch** and the current Wet sound repeats infinitely; you can move knobs while frozen. Per mode: **Reverb → ambient pad · Delay → looping echo · Slip → repeating tone.**

## How to play it
1. Dial the Wet Channel to taste in your chosen mode.
2. Play, then **HOLD the LEFT (Wet) footswitch** to freeze the current sound into an infinite bed.
3. With **LATCH** on, the hold latches so you don't have to keep your foot down.
4. Play baritone/banjo over the frozen wall; move Wet knobs while frozen to morph it.

## Notes
- Combine with a looper mode for the stored-slot moves (frozen-grain-wall / frozen-micro-loop-drone — see Slot L/R strategy).
- With **TRAILS** on, toggling the effect mid-performance won't kill the wash. THE everyday move.
