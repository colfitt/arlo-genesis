# DIRECT MICRO-LOOP clean blend

## Concept
Keep a captured loop partly clean and legible under the 100%-wet path — so a banjo/baritone loop stays readable inside a drone. This is a hidden option: hold **both footswitches** (LEDs green), then turn **looper MODIFY** CW from NONE toward UNITY to blend the clean micro-loop into the wet path.

## How to play it
1. Capture a micro-loop.
2. **Hold both footswitches** until the LEDs go green (hidden-options mode).
3. Turn **looper MODIFY** CW from NONE toward UNITY to dial the clean loop blend.

## Notes
- ⚠️ Causes an audible volume bump — recheck **LEVEL BALANCE** (level-balance) after.
