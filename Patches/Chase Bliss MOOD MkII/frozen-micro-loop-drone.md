# Frozen Micro-Loop Drone (rig patch a)

## Concept
A sustained wall under baritone/banjo — capture, freeze, play over. Reverb with heavy smear over a half-speed (octave-down) Tape micro-loop, frozen into a pad. The baritone/banjo source is rig-specific (via GK-5 → VG-800).

## How to play it
1. Set Wet MODE = **Reverb**, TIME 2:00, MODIFY 1:00 (heavy smear). Set Looper MODE = **Tape**, LENGTH noon, MODIFY at half-speed (octave-down). CLOCK 11:00, MIX 1:00, ROUTING = **IN+micro-loop**.
2. Play a phrase.
3. Tap the **Micro-Looper** (RIGHT footswitch) to capture.
4. **HOLD the LEFT footswitch** to freeze the reverb into a pad.
5. Play over it.
