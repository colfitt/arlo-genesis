# Ruined Cassette Wall

## Concept
Full trashy "ruined cassette" distortion as the final word over a sustained drone/doom climax — the whole bus collapses into one blown-out lo-fi bounce. Built for post-punk wall and Swans-style brittle density. This is the maximum-degraded tie-breaker setting.

## How to play it
1. Max Input Trim and run the Channel Fader to ~90%.
2. Treble 1 o'clock, Bass at noon.
3. Bring Master Volume well down — this setting is loud.
4. Run at 9V for maximum trash and compression.
5. MONO-SUM the H90 bus into it; place at the end of chain over a sustained drone/doom climax.

## Notes
- One-stage-destroys discipline keeps it musical: pair with a LIGHT JHS 424 (just for level/XLR) so only one stage destroys — OR stack both into the JHS's grit for total collapse on the final climax.
- All clock/fader/voltage values are designed-to-spec for the ruined-cassette-wall aesthetic.
