# De-Perfect the Modeler

## Concept
Strips the sterile, too-perfect quality off a VG-800 modeled cab / COSM patch — printing it through the cassette channel gives it the analog "recorded-wrong" patina the whole rig is built around. Turns pad/synth COSM patches into convincing found-tape drones. A direct hit on the "degraded/recorded-wrong" making aesthetic, the reason a cassette print sits at the rig terminus at all.

## How to play it
1. Set Input Trim to 11–12 o'clock.
2. Shelve Treble down to 10 o'clock to de-sterilize the modeled top; Bass at 1 o'clock.
3. Channel Fader ~75%, Master at unity.
4. Run 18V for clean de-perfecting — or drop to 9V to make the modeled signal itself bloom into found-tape grit.
5. Mono-sum the bus; place at the end of chain.

## Notes
- Drop to 9V to make the modeled signal itself bloom into tape grit.
