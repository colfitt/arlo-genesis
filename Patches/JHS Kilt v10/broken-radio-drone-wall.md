# Broken-Radio Drone Wall

## Concept
Designed against the taste lens (degraded/"recorded-wrong" + sustained-walls aesthetic; Post-punk/Swans + Lo-fi clusters). A deliberate aesthetic-filtered variant of Forbidden mode (G1 up / G2 up) — vs the Karlsson reference recipe, this one engages Low-Cut **cut** for brittleness and is explicitly a TEXTURE-into-smear source, not a lead voice. A degraded, "recorded-wrong" sustained texture wall feeding granular/smear/freeze pedals.

## How to play it
- Set the grid to **Forbidden**: G1 up / G2 up.
- Drive Gain deep into the gated last-25% (3:00–4:00); Volume 2:00–3:00.
- Push Tone bright/spitty (~3:00) for the brittle wall.
- Engage Low-Cut **up/cut** to thin it toward the broken/lo-fi character — Eirik's "almost lo-fi-sounding" cut is a FEATURE here.
- Does NOT clean up with guitar-volume (by design — embrace the uncontrollable).
- Mono → place early/pre-stereo, BEFORE the Middle board's granular/smear pedals (Hologram Microcosm, OBNE Dark Star, CB Blooper).

## Notes
- DESIGNED — a texture-into-smear variant of Forbidden. Strongest fit for the rig's signature degraded-drone-wall identity.
