# Stu G Live — OD-into-gated-fuzz

## Concept
Stu G (Stuart Garrard, Delirious?) — Kilt co-developer / signature artist. The single best-documented real-use workflow for the pedal: G1 always-on for overdrive, with G2 controlled by the separate Red Remote latching footswitch so a tap jumps G1+G2 into a raging gated fuzz hands-free, mid-song. Stu runs it as a SECOND drive on top of an always-on slightly-crunchy pedal (a JHS Morning Glory): bed → Kilt OD → Kilt fuzz.

## How to play it
1. Set G1 **up** (always-on overdrive); leave G2 to the Red Remote.
2. Knobs at noon-ish: Volume 12:00–1:00 (unity-to-slight-boost over the bed), Gain 12:00, Tone 12:00.
3. Kilt alone at G1-up = overdrive.
4. **Tap the Red Remote** → G1+G2 = gated fuzz, hands-free mid-song.
5. Consider Tone ~1:00 to compensate — Eirik flags the G2 flip reads darker.
