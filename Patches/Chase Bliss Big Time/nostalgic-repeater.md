# Nostalgic Repeater

## Concept
Factory preset #8 — Chase Bliss's official starter for "malfunctioning echoes and Frippertronics looping," quoted verbatim as *"a cozy looping delay that crumbles and quivers, with no dry signal."* Pure degraded repeats with no dry — ideal as a wet-only parallel/aux source so the dry stays clean upstream of the tape print. On-aesthetic out of the box.

## How to play it
1. Recall **PC 8**; faders fly to saved positions.
2. Lay down sustained notes/chords and let the loop crumble and quiver around them.
3. DRY KILL is already ON — route it as a wet-only aux send if you want the dry preserved upstream.

## Notes
- Comes pre-configured with DRY KILL ON — pure wet out of the box.
- Numeric faders are a designed interpretation of the factory intent; flying faders override on recall.
