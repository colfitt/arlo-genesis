# Sagging Echoes

## Concept
Factory preset #3 — Chase Bliss's official starter for "dynamic delays," quoted verbatim as *"immersive echoes with long, compressed trails that react to your playing."* The Compressed STATE is the "safe" self-oscillation state (it holds itself together); echoes duck out from under your attack, then bloom. More of a clean-musical delay than the core degraded aesthetic — hence not signature-tagged.

## How to play it
1. Recall **PC 3**; faders fly to saved positions.
2. Tap TIME to the song; play a lead/vocal line over it.
3. The trails ride your dynamics — they duck under the attack, then bloom in the gaps.
4. TEXTURE ~55% sets the compression amount (the ducking sag).
