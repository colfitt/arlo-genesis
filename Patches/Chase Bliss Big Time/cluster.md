# Cluster$%&!

## Concept
Factory preset #6 — Chase Bliss's official starter for "exploring strange ambience," quoted verbatim as *"a misbiased wash of swirling clusters – start with sparse playing and see where it takes you."* The misbias STATE makes it playing-reactive: soft playing stays mostly clean, big hits trigger fast dropout. This is where the "punch-in-clean-then-destroy" Basinski move lives — strange/chaotic ambience, banjo-cascade fuel, "recorded-wrong" swirling wash.

## How to play it
1. Recall **PC 6**; faders fly to saved positions.
2. Start with sparse playing — soft notes stay mostly clean.
3. Dig in / hit big to trigger the fast misbias dropout and swirling clusters.
4. CLUSTER ~75% is the swirl; SPREAD widen → ping-pong scatters it stereo.

## Notes
- Playing-reactive: gentle = clean, dig in = texture.
- The Basinski "punch-in-clean-then-destroy" gesture is native here.
- Numeric faders are a designed interpretation of factory intent; flying faders override on recall.
