# Cold Wire — Chords (Am)

## Verse
```
| Am     | Am     | C      | Dm     |
| Am     | Am     | C      | Dm     |
```
*2 bars per chord, 16 bars total. Repeat.*

Am is the anchor — let it breathe for 4 bars before moving. The C–Dm motion gives that classic post-punk pull without resolving. Single-note guitar line: A–C–D motif over the Am bars.

## Chorus
```
| F      | G      | Am     | Am     |
| F      | G      | Am     | Am     |
```
*2 bars per chord, 8 bars per pass (repeat on Chorus 2/3).*

F to G creates upward momentum before crashing back into Am. Strum these as power chords (Brothers AM on) — eighth-note downstrokes.

## Bridge
```
| Dm     | Dm     | F      | E      |
```
*2 bars per chord, 8 bars total. Sustained, half-time feel.*

The E major is the tension chord — it's borrowed from A harmonic minor (V of Am). Let it hang with the BF-3 flanger sweeping underneath. Resolves back to Am on Chorus 3.

## Notes

- Verse voicings: use the barre shapes at 5th fret (Am) and slide up for C and Dm. Keeps the hand in one zone — more angular, less open/folky.
