# Cold Wire — Song Plan

## Vibe

Taut, angular post-punk. Think Interpol's rhythm section meets Preoccupations' atmosphere. Dm tonality, mostly minor pentatonic with chromatic passing tones. Everything tight and forward — drums and bass drive, guitar slashes on top.

## Signal Chain (Guitar)

1. **Chase Bliss Brothers AM** — Channel A boost into Channel B overdrive. Gritty, mid-forward bark. Not fuzzy — clipped and percussive.
2. **Boss CE-2W** — CE-2 mode (vintage), rate slow, depth moderate. Classic cold chorus on clean passages; bypass during driven sections.
3. **Boss BF-3** — Standard mode, low rate, for the bridge. Metallic sweep under sustained chords.
4. **Strymon TimeLine** — dBucket machine, dotted-eighth pattern at 138 BPM. Mix low — rhythmic ghost, not wash.
5. **Strymon Deco V2** — Saturation side only, light tape compression to glue the tone.
6. **Strymon Big Sky** — Room algorithm, short decay (~1.2s), low mix. Just enough space.
7. **Strymon Iridium** — Chime (AC30) amp model. Treble up, bass rolled back. Jangly but aggressive when pushed.

## Drums (Digitakt 2)

Program all drums on the Digitakt 2. Load tight, punchy one-shot samples:

- **Kick:** Short, dead — 808-style but dry. Hits on 1 and the "and" of 3.
- **Snare:** Snappy, gated. Backbeat on 2 and 4, plus ghost notes on the "e" of 2.
- **Hi-hat:** Driving eighth notes, accented offbeats. Open hat on beat 4 in the chorus.
- **Toms/perc:** Low tom fill every 4 bars. Add a sidestick layer in verse 2.

Use Digitakt 2's parameter locks for subtle filter sweeps on the hi-hat pattern — darkens on verse, opens on chorus.

| Tag      | 8    | 2:34–3:00  | Single clean guitar note (D) ringing into Big Sky room reverb. Fade or hard stop. |

## Chord Progression

- **Verse:** Dm – Dm – F – Gm (2 bars each, repeat)
- **Chorus:** Bb – C – Dm – Dm (2 bars each)
- **Bridge:** Gm – Gm – Bb – A (sustained, 2 bars each — the A creates tension before resolving back to Dm)

## Bass Approach

Root notes, eighth-note pulse. Follow the kick pattern closely. Occasional octave leap on the "and" of 4 heading into a new chord. Run bass through the Deco V2 for tape warmth, or DI clean and add saturation in the box.

## Production Notes

- Record guitar via Iridium stereo out into interface — no mic needed.
- Digitakt 2 stereo out direct to interface. Use its internal compressor on the master.
- Keep the mix dry and upfront. Post-punk lives in the mids.
- Pan: drums center, bass center, rhythm guitar 60L, delay guitar 60R, chorus guitar parts hard-panned.
- If layering, use Deco's doubletracker side for ADT on the vocal or lead guitar.
