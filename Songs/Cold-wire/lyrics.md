## Delivery Notes

- Clipped, spoken-sung. Don't over-sing — think Ian Curtis or Paul Banks. Let the words land on the beat, not float over it.
- Lines 1-2 sit over the 4-bar Am anchor. Keep the melody narrow — two or three notes max, mostly A and C.
- Lines 3-4 shift with the chord motion (C to Dm). The melody can rise slightly here, following the harmonic lift.
- Second stanza: same rhythmic phrasing, new words. Verse 2 in the arrangement would use this same structure with a fresh set of lines.
