# Digitakt II — Community Cheat Sheet (downloadable reference)

A widely-shared, community-made DT2 quick-reference. The forum thread announces it; the actual content is the downloadable PDF.

- **Download:** https://shop.davemech.live — offered "pay what you want," including free (enter 0 at checkout). Two versions: one optimized for on-screen viewing, one for printing.
- **Front side:** "The most important shortcuts neatly segmented to make it easy to find what you need."
- **Back side:** copy/paste shortcuts, **LFO speed calculation help** (LFO rate → musical timing), and **filter frequency estimates** (cutoff value → approx Hz), plus a "big list of tips and tricks" and a legend.

Useful as the printed reference next to the unit for shortcut recall (the granular button-combos live in the PDF, not the thread). Third-party/community resource — not official Elektron — but well-regarded in the thread.
