# Elektron official Sound Packs for Digitakt II

Elektron's own store packs that are DT2-targeted. All are WAV-based and load to the +Drive via Elektron Transfer; the paid ones include emailed install instructions. Packs marked stereo exploit DT2's stereo engine; on mono Elektrons (OG Digitakt, Model:Samples) the samples auto-convert to mono and any DT2 presets won't carry.

## Most relevant to a drone/doom/degraded rig

- **Reduktion** — $12. By **Keinseier** (Hamburg sound artist). **110 stereo samples**. Kicks/snares/hats/rimshots/glitches/loops PLUS pads, leads, basses, chords. Character: glitch + texture, "blurry boundaries of glitch, structure, and contrasting textures," "sonic fragments, both grounded yet abstract." An exercise in self-imposed limitation. The most texture/abstract-leaning official pack. Also loads on OT MkI/II, OG Digitakt, Rytm, Model:Samples.

- **Drench Case** — $19. By **Mick Harris** (Scorn, Lull; ex-Napalm Death). **261 mono samples** (153 drums, 67 basses, 41 FX), 16-bit/48k. Harsh, industrial, gritty — "the scars of having weathered the most unforgiving of elements." Textural grit, not pads. **Works on BOTH OG Digitakt and DT2** (mono pack). Strong fit for "recorded-wrong"/industrial doom percussion.

- **HexCells** — $12. By **Dissonant Witchcraft** (Jeanne Artemis / Catenation). **203 stereo samples** = 28 chains + 175 one-shots (51 loopable) + **86 dedicated DT2 presets**. Grainy, organic, granular-processed percussion from Elektron HW, desktop synths, Eurorack + granular FX. Load samples FIRST for correct folder structure. Stereo → auto-mono on OG.

## Other DT2 packs (less on-aesthetic, still useful drum sources)

- **EuroKlang** — FREE. By **Mythologen** (Gothenburg). **97 MONO samples**, 16-bit/48k. Found-sound (station announcements, road/rail field recordings) + kicks/snares/chords/leads/basses/glitches. Techno/house/electro/DnB w/ European flavor. Used on Mythologen's Year0001 album "Eurovision." Free = good for harvesting field-recording texture.

- **Mega Mechs** — $12. By **User Friendly**. **95 samples** (stereo + mono versions). Kicks/snares/hats/foley/rolls + "micro chains." Mechanical/industrial, metallic. Micro-chains and rolls optimized for DT2 (chains let one track hold many hits).

NOTE: prices/contents per Elektron product pages at capture; sample counts are the vendor's. "Chains" = multiple samples concatenated into one file so a single DT2 track + Slice/start-locks can access many hits — handy given 1024 slots but monophonic tracks.
