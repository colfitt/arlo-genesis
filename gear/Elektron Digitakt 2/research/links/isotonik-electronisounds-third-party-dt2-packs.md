# Third-party DT2 sample/sound packs (Isotonik + ELECTRONISOUNDS)

Commercial DT2 packs beyond Elektron's own store. Most ship WAVs (load via Transfer) and some include DT2 patterns/kits.

## Isotonik Studios — Yves Big City / Jessica Saves
- **Broken FM** (Yves Big City) — stereo FM sources for DT2. **~1 GB stereo one-shots from a DX7 + Digitone 2**, **400 glitch-processed FM loops**, custom drumkits, and **16 pre-built DT2 patterns**. "Retro DX7 character with futuristic edge," glitch-treated. Royalty-free. WAVs work on Digitakt 1 + any WAV sampler; the **16 patterns are DT2**. Demo: youtube.com/watch?v=J8gA3TEtQgk. Good FM/metallic source material for detuned drones.
- Other Yves Big City / Jessica Saves Digitakt packs exist (Berlin Dreams, Big City Dreams Anthology, Vintage Breaks, Deep Dub Techno, Mega Pack) — most are OG-Digitakt-era WAV packs that load on DT2 as samples; check each for whether kits/patterns are DT2-native.

## ELECTRONISOUNDS — Digitakt 2 sound banks (stereo)
All **$20 each**, or **7-pack bundle $50** (reg $140). Marketed as stereo, DT2-targeted:
- **Chill Chord Chronicles** — chords/pads (most useful for ambient beds).
- **Dark Arcade Drums**, **Glitchcraft Percussion**, **Dean's Drum Stash – Gold Edition** — drums/percussion.
- **Darkstar Infusion** — dark/textural.
- **Low Frequency Lab** — sub/bass material.
- **Melodic Superhero** — melodic/lead.
(ELECTRONISOUNDS pages don't publish per-pack sample counts; "feature stereo samples." Treat counts as unverified.)

## How to load any of these
Elektron **Transfer** → drag WAVs to +Drive; load DT2 patterns/projects (where included) from the project/pattern menu. Stereo packs auto-convert to mono on OG Digitakt / Model:Samples.

NOTE: contents per vendor pages / demos; sample counts unverified where the vendor doesn't list them. "Broken FM" is the standout for this rig (stereo FM = rich detunable drone/metallic source).
