# DROWNED! — Drone Preset Pack for Digitakt II

The single most aesthetically on-target DT2 pack found for this rig (drone/doom/shoegaze, "recorded-wrong" walls).

## Contents
- **50 drone presets**, each shipped in TWO variants:
  - **Clean** versions — "meditative, mesmerizing" evolving drones.
  - **Headcrusher** versions — heavily processed, "gritty… like a guitar orchestra playing from the depths of hell."
- **50 source samples** included (stereo, with spatial content).

## Sound-design approach (DT2-specific, per the author)
- **3 LFOs per track**, set to **slow** modulation on **Filter cutoff** and **Pitch** for evolving movement.
- **Random LFOs with slow Slew** for unpredictable, non-repeating motion.
- **Comb Filter** processing (DT2-exclusive filter machine) for metallic/resonant drone color.
- **Stereo source samples** for width.
- The Headcrusher tones were created by re-processing the sources through the **free HEADCRUSHER Ableton Live Rack by Primal Guitar** before sampling back in — a useful "degrade outside the box, then resample" recipe that maps directly onto running prints through the pedalboard.

## How to load
- Distributed as a Gumroad download: brucemenace.gumroad.com/l/drowned (price not stated in thread).
- Presets + samples load via **Elektron Transfer** to the DT2 +Drive.

## DT2 vs OG compatibility
- **DT2:** full support (current firmware).
- **OG Digitakt:** PARTIAL — the source samples load, but OG lacks the 3 LFOs and Comb filter, so the preset *behavior* does not transfer; Transfer also down-converts the stereo samples to mono. Treat as a DT2 pack; samples-only on OG.
