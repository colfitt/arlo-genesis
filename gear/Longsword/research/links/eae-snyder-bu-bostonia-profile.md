Independent academic-side corroboration of Snyder's background — useful for verifying the maker story against a non-music-press source.

**Verified biographical timeline:**
- Snyder earned a **BU CAS degree in 2014** and his **doctorate in electrical engineering (ENG) in 2020**.
- PhD work was in **BU's Photonics Center basement** — "long hours babysitting experiments in a clean room." (This is the literal basement the legend grew out of.)
- "A gearhead since high school, Snyder melded his two interests, designing an effects pedal to use with the band he was playing in." — confirms the first pedal was built as a personal tool for his own band, consistent with the Longsword origin.

**Growth (verbatim):** "Those early adopters were very excited about what I was doing, and suddenly 15 turned into 30, that 30 turned into 50, that 50 turned into 100."

**His own band:** Snyder plays in the **noise-rock band Weird Machine** — i.e. EAE's founder is an active player in the same loud/experimental scene as his artist roster.

**HONEST FLAGS:**
- The BU profile does **NOT** confirm the "2015 snowpocalypse" origin event and does **NOT** identify which pedal was first (it lists Longsword, Model feT, Limelight, Bard, Prismatic Wall without ordering). The snowpocalypse + Longsword-first claim rests on EAE's own manual and the 2016 Pedal of the Day interview, not on this independent source — so present "first pedal" as EAE's account, corroborated in spirit (basement, side-project-during-PhD) but not independently confirmed by BU.
- No additional artists named here beyond Weird Machine. No Longsword-specific endorser.
