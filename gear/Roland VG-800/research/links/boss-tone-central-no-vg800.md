State of the OFFICIAL Boss-curated patch library for the VG-800: NONE.

- Boss Tone Central is the official, Boss-authored "Tone Collection" / liveset library
  (the curated banks Boss itself publishes, distinct from user uploads).
- The VG-800 does NOT appear anywhere on Tone Central — not in the product list, news,
  or content categories. There is no /liveset/category/vg-800/ page (returns 404).
- Tone Central DOES host curated collections for the VG-800's siblings — notably the
  GM-800 ("Extra Collection" series: e.g. "Extra Collection3 - Spatial", "Extra
  Collection5 - Mono Lead") and the SY-1000 — so the pattern of Boss publishing extra
  official banks exists; it just hasn't reached the VG-800 yet as of this research.

IMPLICATION: do not expect downloadable official "bonus banks" for the VG-800. The
factory content is whatever ships ON the unit (see soundonsound-vg800 link). Community
sharing is via Tone EXCHANGE (separate site), not Tone Central.
