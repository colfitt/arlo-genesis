Official BOSS deep-dive on V-Guitar (GM-800/VG-800/GK-5) advanced features. The one source
with a FULLY-PARAMETRIZED named factory preset.

NAMED FACTORY PRESET (concrete, official):
- "B-Bender" — "the CTL 1 switch (located on the top panel) applies a one-tone pitch glide
  to the guitar's B-string to emulate pedal steel."
  → Build: STR BEND SW ON, BEND DEPTH (B string) = +2 semitones (one tone), BEND CONTROL
    assigned to CTL1 (0→100). CTL1 held = +2 glide, released = standard. (Matches the SoS
    "super-smooth B-Bender, hold CTL1" note.)

STRING-SPLIT EXAMPLE (concrete model assignment):
- "clean acoustic guitar on the top three strings and an overdriven rock guitar on the
  bottom three strings" — a DUAL GUITAR split by string group (high 3 = ACOUSTIC model,
  low 3 = E.GUITAR into a dirty amp). Confirms per-string-group model assignment in DUAL.

ALT-TUNINGS named officially: baritone, Nashville, custom open-chord tunings.
SYNTH named officially: Roland GR-300 Polyphonic Guitar Synthesizer emulation.
EFFECTS named officially in examples: Slow Gear, Slicer, rotary, BD-2 Blues Driver,
  DS-1 Distortion.

CONTROL JACK MAP (official):
- CTL 1 = top panel (the per-patch performance toggle, e.g. B-Bender)
- CTL 2, 3 / EXP 1 = rear panel
- CTL 4, 5 / EXP 2 = left side panel

Confidence: HIGH — official BOSS source; B-Bender preset fully parametrized; string-split
model assignment confirmed. (Resolves the SoS "B-Bender activated by holding CTL1" into an
actual STR BEND / BEND DEPTH +2 recipe.)
