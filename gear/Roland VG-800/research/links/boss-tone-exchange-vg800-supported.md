CORRECTION TO DOSSIER: the VG-800 IS a supported product on the patch-sharing platform.
The dossier (§§10–13) implied patch sharing lives at "Boss Tone Central / Tone Exchange"
and was uncertain. The reality, confirmed:

- The sharing platform for the VG-800 is **BOSS TONE EXCHANGE** (bosstoneexchange.com),
  NOT Boss Tone Central (bosstonecentral.com). Tone Central does NOT list the VG-800 at all
  (its product list is GM-800, GT-1000, GX-100, SY-1000, ME-90, KATANA, etc.).
- BOSS Tone Exchange's supported-model list explicitly includes **VG-800 (Guitar)** and
  **VG-800 (Bass)** as two separate gear entries. (Also: GX-10, GT-1000, GT-1000CORE,
  Pocket GT, GT-1/1B, GT-100, GT-001, ME-90/90B/80/25, GM-800, SY-1000, GP-10, SY-300,
  KATANA family, WAZA-AIR.)
- Live gear pages exist at:
  - https://bosstoneexchange.com/gear/vg-800_guitar/
  - https://bosstoneexchange.com/gear/vg-800_bass/
- Mechanism (from Roland "How do I download from Tone Exchange"): you browse/search
  Tone Exchange INSIDE the Boss Tone Studio for VG-800 app (logged into a Boss account),
  download a shared **memory** (single patch) or **liveset** (array of memories), then
  must explicitly transfer/export it to the hardware — downloads are NOT auto-pushed to
  the unit. A VG-800 "memory" stores the full block combination: Instrument + Effects +
  Guitar-to-MIDI + every parameter.

HONEST LIMITATION: the Tone Exchange site is JavaScript-rendered; the actual count of
user-uploaded VG-800 livesets could not be read by automated fetch (pages return only
loading spinners). The platform supports the VG-800; how MANY community patches actually
exist there for it (as of mid-2026) is UNVERIFIED here — likely sparse given the box is
a 2025 release. To confirm, open the two gear URLs above in a real browser.

Distinction to carry into the guide: Tone Central = Boss-curated official collections
(no VG-800 yet). Tone Exchange = user-to-user sharing (VG-800 supported, populated by
community uploads). Both are reached through Boss Tone Studio.
