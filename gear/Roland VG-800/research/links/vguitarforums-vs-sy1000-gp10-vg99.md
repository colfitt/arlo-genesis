Owner verdicts: VG-800 vs SY-1000 / GP-10 / VG-99 / GT-1000. (Reddit/gearspace 403'd; this is the most substantive owner comparison that loads.)

## vs GT-1000 (the lineage that matters most for tone)
- **jwhitcomb3:** *"amps and distortion block are straight out of the GT1000 — a major step up."* The VG-800's amp/dist engine = GT-1000 generation. This is the unit's strongest tonal asset.
- BUT the GT-1000 has far more DSP headroom. Owner-tabulated deltas:
  - Distortion blocks: **VG-800 = 1** vs GT-1000 = 5.
  - Parallel paths: **VG-800 = 1** vs GT-1000 = 3 (stephenc: *"(Ouch)"*).
  - EQ: **2 (parametric only)** vs 4. Delays: **2** vs 4+master, missing ANALOG/TAPE/REVERSE/SHIMMER. Reverb lacks SHIMMER/DUAL/TERA ECHO.
  - Sample rate: **48 kHz** vs 96 kHz — jwhitcomb3: *"48KHz perfectly sufficient"* for guitar.

## vs VG-99
- **Scratch17:** patch portability only **"50% like for like,"** ~30% **"impossible without dual units"** because the VG-800 has fewer FX blocks. So it's not a clean VG-99 replacement for complex patches.
- loopman/bosetuno: VG-800 **drops** HRM (harmony) algorithms and the Vari-guitar that the VG-99 had.

## vs SY-1000
- Owner consensus (guest posters, admin_shawnb): the VG-800 is **guitar-modeling-focused, NOT a synth box.** For a dedicated synth user it's *"basically redundant"* next to the SY-1000, which keeps three simultaneous synth engines, internal sequencers, LFOs/control waveforms.
- loopman: *"Same effects as SY-1000… big disappointment,"* the **only** real advance being **tracking** (Serial GK / GK-5).

## vs GP-10
- Framed by **pasha811** as a *"hybrid between VG (models and synths) and GP (size and focus on guitar)"* — i.e. GP-10's compact guitar-focus + VG-line modeling, on the SY-1000 effects foundation. Universally treated as a clear upgrade over the GP-10 on tracking, effects, and modeling.

## Net for this rig
The verdict that matters: the amp/dist block is GT-1000-grade (great, and the downstream fuzz wall covers the single-block limit anyway), tracking is the best in the V-Guitar line (the actual reason it's here), and the synth/FX-depth shortfalls vs SY-1000 are irrelevant because the rig wants *modeling + alt-tuning + a clean source to mangle*, not deep synthesis. The SY-1000 would only win if the rig were synth-centric — it isn't.
