Survey of where to BUY ready-made TimeLine preset packs (all delivered as .syx for Nixie import). Mostly worship-oriented but with ambient/swell/reverse content.

## GuitarForHisGlory — "Strymon Timeline Library"
- Covers **175+ songs** with ready-made tones. ~$59.99–$149.99 (one-time vs. lifetime updates).
- "Core Pack" = **21 foundation templates** organized by delay type:
  - Standard: 8th Tape, 16th Digital, Quarter Tape, Quarter Digital, Analog Dot
  - Tape: Dot, Dot+, DMM, Tape Warble
  - Specialty: Dual Delay, **Reverse Ambient**, **Ambient Tape**
- On-aesthetic named presets: **"Ambient Reverse"** (reverse delay for swells) and **"Ambient Tape"** (tape delay for swells).
- Delivery: .syx files + a "Read Me" with import/organization instructions. Imported "via a midi cable and Strymon's visual editing program, NIXIE."

## Other paid sources found (not deep-dived)
- **SoundShop — "TimeLine Presets by Tommy Gerlach"** (https://www.soundshop.co/product-page/timeline-presets-by-tommy-gerlach) — individual creator pack.
- **MultiTracks.com — "Strymon Presets Vol. 1" by Peter Burton** (https://www.multitracks.com/sounds/peter-burton/strymon-presets-vol.-1-strymon/) — TimeLine + BigSky bundle, worship-leaning.

## Honest assessment for THIS rig
These packs are heavily worship/U2-dotted-eighth oriented — useful as raw .syx templates and for the few ambient/reverse/swell entries, but NOT a drone/doom/shoegaze library. For this aesthetic the better free sources are Strymon's own "This Week's Preset" Lo-Fi/dTape entries (Corrupted Flowers, Bokeh Bells) and the factory degraded/Lo-Fi/Ice/Swell presets — see separate captured files. No large *free* community .syx repository (Discord/forum) surfaced; TimeLine preset trading is far less of an ecosystem than synth-patch trading.
