## "This Week's Preset" — how it works
- Strymon's blog series sharing new presets for TimeLine, BigSky, Mobius. Each TimeLine post = one downloadable .syx (load via Nixie or legacy Librarian) plus a knob-position image.
- Index/category page links every entry. Most on-aesthetic TimeLine entries found:
  - **"Corrupted Flowers"** — Lo-Fi, 4kHz / 10-bit / Intercom filter (separate captured file)
  - **"Bokeh Bells"** — dTape, wow & flutter maxed (separate captured file)
  - **"Synthy Triplets"** — Filter-delay machine (https://www.strymon.net/weeks-preset-timeline-synthy-triplets/)
  - **"Orobas Presets"** — artist preset set (https://www.strymon.net/this-weeks-preset-timeline-orobas-presets/)
  - **"Envelop Me" / Native Suns**, **"Waiting for the Wind" / David Helpling** — artist presets (separate captured files)
