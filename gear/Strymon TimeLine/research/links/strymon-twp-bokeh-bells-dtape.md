Official free Strymon dTape preset, downloadable as a .syx. The "machine in desperate need of service" — directly matches this rig's degraded-tape-wall goal.

## Preset: "Bokeh Bells" — dTape machine
- Machine: dTAPE
- Wow & Flutter (DEPTH): **maximum**
- Tape Crinkle (SPEED): **near maximum**
- Tape Age (FILTER): **~2 o'clock**
- Grit/Filter set for a worn-tape character

## Sound / quote
Built to emulate "a machine that is in desperate need of service, with a warbled wash of echoes." In the demo a bell-like Kawai K5 synth patch is layered through the dTape to add warmth/depth to a thin source — i.e. dTape used as a thickening, smearing layer, not a rhythmic delay.

## Rig fit
This is the dossier's "Degraded tape wall" recipe pushed to the wow/flutter extreme. Drop a banjo's fast decay or a baritone chord into this and the warble turns it into a seasick sustained pad. Download via Nixie / Strymon Librarian (.syx). Settings image on the page shows exact knob positions.
