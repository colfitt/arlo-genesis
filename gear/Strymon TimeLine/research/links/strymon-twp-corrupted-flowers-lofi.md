Official free Strymon Lo-Fi preset, downloadable as a .syx. Squarely on-aesthetic for the "recorded-wrong"/broken-transmission goal of this rig.

## Preset: "Corrupted Flowers" — Lo-Fi machine
- Machine: LO-FI
- Sample Rate: **4 kHz** (heavy aliasing)
- Bit Depth: **10-bit**
- Filter shape: **Filter 8 — "Apartment Intercom"**
- Plus Filter + Grit + a slow-moving modulation
- MIX: the full-resolution (clean) signal is blended back in to soften/round the degraded repeats

## Technique / quote
Tufts: "I've created a jagged, lo-fi sound by setting the sample rate way down to 4KhZ, and set the bit depth at 10-bits." The trick is blending the full-res signal back under the corrupted repeats so it's textured rather than pure noise.

## Rig fit
This is essentially the dossier's "Broken transmission" starting point (160-ms-ish Lo-Fi, intercom/cell-phone filter) but tuned harsher. Pair after VG-800 pad patches → Dark Star for drone. Download via Nixie / legacy Librarian (.syx).
