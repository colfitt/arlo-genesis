NEW set of official Filter-machine presets — the rig's "a delay that isn't a delay" / resonant-motion territory, complementing the factory Filter-sweep presets (Soft Saw, Sweeper, EchoChamber) already on disk. The Filter machine puts an LFO-swept filter on the repeats; Speed is set as a RATIO of the delay time. Free .syx in each post. Verbatim numbers below; remaining knob positions are in the settings image / .syx.

## "Synthy Triplets" — Filter machine (Ethan Tufts, 2014-11-13)
A synth-like filtered delay where the LFO plays triplets against the delay time.
- TYPE: FILTER
- LFO SHAPE: SAW
- LFO SPEED: 3/1 (ratio of delay time → "3/1 speed to have the LFO play triplets off of my delay time")
- GRIT: 0 ("left the Grit at zero to keep the sound mostly clean")
- MOD: "a bit of slow, mellow modulation to add a bit more dimension"
- (TIME / MIX / REPEATS / FILT-Q / location numbers in image/.syx)

## "Float Around" — Filter machine (Ethan Tufts, 2014-07-03)
A short-time, fast-LFO filtered shimmer — "a delay that's not a delay."
- TYPE: FILTER
- TIME: 60 ms (minimum)
- LFO SPEED: 32/1 (very fast, 32× the delay time)
- FILTER + GRIT: "a small amount of filter and grit"
- MOD: "a good amount of modulation"
- (FILT-Q / LFO shape / location numbers in image/.syx)

## "Oil Can Delay" — Filter machine, NO filter modulation (Matt Piper, 2017-02-16)
Emulates a vintage oil-can delay's natural pitch wobble — Filter machine but the LFO is OFF; the wobble comes from the delay's own pitch mod.
- TYPE: FILTER
- LFO DEPTH: OFF ("no modulation of the filter cutoff frequency")
- SPEED & DEPTH (delay pitch vibrato): "set to approximate the natural pitch vibrato inherent in oil can delays"
- (TIME / MIX / REPEATS / FILTER cutoff numbers in image/.syx)

Rig fit: Float Around (60 ms, 32/1) and Synthy Triplets (saw, 3/1) are off-aesthetic-but-useful motion delays; Oil Can is the on-aesthetic one — a degraded, pitch-warbling vintage-delay voice (sits near dTape Wow&Flutter, but on the Filter engine) good under banjo/baritone for a "wrong machine" wobble.
