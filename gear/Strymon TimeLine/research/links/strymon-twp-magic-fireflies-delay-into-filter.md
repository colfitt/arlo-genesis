## "MF RIFF" — delay repeating through filters (main melodic riff)
- Lemes: joined "the two effects" (delay + filter) because "the Timeline is a complete delay machine" — i.e. uses the Filter machine so the repeats are filtered ("delays repeating in filters").
- Recorded "in stereo mode to be wider."

## "MF SOLO" — dTape effect (for solos)
- TYPE: dTAPE — the solo voice; tape-style repeats under lead lines.
- Recorded in stereo for width.
