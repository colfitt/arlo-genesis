Complete factory preset bank (Banks 00–49, A/B = 100 presets) with exact settings as printed on Strymon's official factory-preset PDF. Knob values are the literal text shown on the card (TIME in ms, machine-specific PARAMS spelled out; SMEAR/BLEND/SENS/DEPTH shown as bar-graphs on the card and noted here as low/mid/high where a number isn't given). TAPDIV is QRTR and BOOST 0.0dB on nearly all factory presets unless noted. This is the on-aesthetic subset plus full reference.

## How factory presets are organized
- 100 banks total but the FACTORY card only fills Banks 00–49 (the second 50 banks ship as duplicates/empty for the user). Each bank has an A and a B preset.
- The user templates at the end of the PDF (blank TYPE/NAME cards) are meant to be photocopied and filled in by hand.
