VERIFICATION TARGET: does Boss ever call any BF-3 feature "Momentum"? Verdict below.

## "Momentum" verdict — NOT an official Boss term
Direct fetch of the official US product page: the word **"Momentum" does NOT appear anywhere.** Boss's actual copy:
- Tap tempo: **"Tap tempo adjustable via pedal"** (their exact phrase) — done on the unit's own footswitch, no external jack.
- **"Momentary mode turns flanging on instantly"** — a SEPARATE, distinct mode (flange only while the switch is held).
- Modes named officially: **"New Ultra and Gate/Pan modes for ultra-fat flanging and futuristic slicer-type sounds, respectively."**

So there are two real, separately-named things — **tap tempo** and **Momentary mode** — and "Momentum" is neither. A targeted web search for `Boss BF-3 "Momentum"` surfaced only retailer/marketplace listings (Sweetwater, Reverb, etc.) using "Momentary Mode & Tap Tempo"; none use "Momentum."

## Conclusion
"Momentum" = **community / marketing shorthand**, most likely a slurring of "Momentary" + "tap tempo," with zero presence in official Boss documentation. Do not present it as a BF-3 feature name. Use "tap tempo (via the footswitch)" and "Momentary mode" as the correct, distinct terms.
