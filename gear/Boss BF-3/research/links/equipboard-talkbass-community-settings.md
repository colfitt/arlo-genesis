## Concrete community recipes (quoted)
- **"Good flange" general-purpose** — RES at 12 o'clock, MANUAL "full on" (max CW), DEPTH at 3 o'clock, RATE at about 11:30. STANDARD mode.
- **Subtle coloring / chorus-ish** — Resonance at minimum (full CCW), MANUAL at noon, DEPTH at 10 o'clock, RATE at 2 o'clock. STANDARD mode. ("most at home as a subtle modulation layer over a clean signal.")
- **Tremolo / ring-mod-ish in mono** — Gate/Pan mode, RATE around 2 o'clock → "tremolo-style sounds and almost ring modulator tones" in mono.
- **Chorus/phaser trick** — "Setting the 'Res' knob low and the 'Rate' knob high can easily get chorus sounds, and you can even get phaser-like tones."

## Shoegaze/post-rock community context (TalkBass shoegaze-pedalboard thread)
- BF-3 cited in a board aimed at "Mogwai, Isis, and Explosions in the Sky type sounds"; the recommendation for those was "flanger to taste, or all the time."
- BF-3 "set up as a flanging chorus" makes the guitar "bloom in a very musical way." → community support for an always-on subtle drift (relevant to the drone-wall lean).
