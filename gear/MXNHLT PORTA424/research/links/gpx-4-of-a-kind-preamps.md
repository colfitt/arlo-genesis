guitarpedalx.com · Stefan Karlsson · "4 of a Kind: Tascam 424 Portastudio-style Preamps"

The single most useful third-party piece — puts the MXNHLT Porta 424 head-to-head with the JHS 424, Benson Portable Distortion 424, and Neutrino Labs MX 424.

MXNHLT PORTA 424 in the comparison:
- Price: $170 USD.
- Controls (5): In (Input Gain), EQ Hi, EQ Lo, Out (Output Gain), + a Level SLIDER for volume. Matches the JHS and Neutrino's 5-control layout; the Benson has only 4.
- Author's take (quoted): "a highly commendable choice," praising "excellent aesthetic choices — including the Knob types, and the fact that there is a Slider Control for the Level / Volume." Says MXNHLT has "long delivered interesting preamp pedals," and: "The Porta 424 is a highly commendable choice here — and had I better access to that — it may have been the one I went for."
- On choosing between the four: "you could really go with any of these," noting a "$100+ price differential between highest and lowest cost options."

SETTINGS: NONE. The article gives NO specific dial-in recommendations, EQ settings, or gain-staging numbers for the MXNHLT. It's a layout/price/character comparison, not a settings guide.
