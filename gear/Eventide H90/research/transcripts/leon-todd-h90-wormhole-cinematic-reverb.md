Deep dive on the Wormhole reverb algorithm (one of the H90's ten then-new algorithms). Cleaned to prose; mostly playing demos, so this is a parameter rundown plus the two factory presets used.

Wormhole is described as "if the Blackhole algorithm went to a whole other level" — a massive ambient reverb with reverb-pitch-shifting/warping built in.

## Controls
- **Mix** — wet/dry blend.
- **Length** and **Diameter** — the reverb decay and size (Length = decay, Diameter = size).
- **Entry Field** — a diffusion control (heard at min vs max it changes the reverb's grain/character).
- **Pre-delay**.
- **Low Decay** and **High Decay** — separate decay tilt for lows vs highs.
- **Stability** and **Stability Rate** — modulation: higher Stability = LESS modulation in the reverb; lower Stability = more pitch-unstable, wobbling tail.
- **Warp** section — the signature move. Warp XL sets the time/attack of the pitch-shifted reverb sweep; Warp Factor, Warp Mix, plus Base and Travel for the warp parameter. Engaging Warp produces a rising/falling pitch-shifted reverb swell ("imagine the Millennium Falcon jumping to hyperspace").

## Presets / settings demonstrated
- **Wormhole Serpent** (factory) — Todd assigned a footswitch to control Warp as a LATCHING switch so he can hold it and let the reverb ring out. Workflow: noodle ambient, then kick in Warp for the hyperspace swell.
- A longer-warp variant: Warp Factor cranked to 10, Warp Mix 100, Bass and Treble (of warp) at 100; Stability brought DOWN for more modulation; overall reverb Length up; Low Decay down, High Decay up slightly; ~65 ms pre-delay added.
- **New Junk City** (factory) — a lower-time setting: Diameter, Length, and Entry Field all quite low; he suggests raising Stability and Low Decay; "a lot of mix going on in this one."

Note: also shown working well on synth (Hydrasynth) as a keys reverb. He demos these on the H9 hardware editor on-screen, but Wormhole is the same algorithm as on the H90.

Rig note: Wormhole's Warp-on-a-latching-footswitch and low-Stability (more modulation) settings are exactly the unstable-pitch sustained-wall behavior this rig wants — assign Warp to an H90 HotSwitch or Perform footswitch over a baritone/fuzz drone.
