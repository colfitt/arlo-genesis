# On-aesthetic free H90 Programs on Patchstorage (ambient / drone / granular / pad)

Curated for the End-board drone/doom/shoegaze role: sustained walls, pads, shimmer, granular
"recorded-wrong" texture. All free; `.pgm90` (single) or `.lst90` (List). Import via H90 Control.
The two most prolific on-aesthetic uploaders are **apalazzolo** and **brock**.

## apalazzolo — the ambient/pad workhorse
- **"Ten H90 Ambient Pads/Soundscapes"** (`.lst90`, 10 programs)
  https://patchstorage.com/nine-h90-ambient-pad-soundscape-programs/
  Inspired by Eventide's **UltraSwell** factory program. Built around **UltraTap in gate mode** + a
  different reverb per program. Key technique quoted: *"PLAY DISTINCT RESTS BETWEEN NOTES to reset
  the UltraTap gate. Note length is loosely related to swell time."* Stereo insert mode; uses
  hotswitches, expression pedal, and aux switches. This is the closest free analog to the rig's
  "swelling pad wall" goal. (Per-program names/algorithms live inside the file.)
  One user note: programs were silent until they removed an external insert effect from the chain —
  relevant since this rig may use inserts.

- **"Eight H90 Bowed Instruments"** (`.lst90`, 8 programs)
  https://patchstorage.com/nine-h90-bowed-stringed-instruments-with-reverb/
  Same UltraTap-gate swell engine + 8 different reverbs; *"aspire to emulate a solo bowed string
  instrument in a small hall."* Technique: *"Use wide vibrato"*, *"note length is loosely related to
  swell time"*, "play the effect." Excellent for turning the baritone Jazzmaster or banjo into a
  bowed/cello-like drone voice.

- **"GRANULATE (All The Kids Do It)"** (18 programs, insert mode, Dec 2025)
  Uses **3 of Eventide's 4 granular algorithms** (Cosmic Web, Glitch, GrainMod, Stutter — see below).
  Directly on-aesthetic for the degraded/glitch/"recorded-wrong" thesis. Requires recent firmware
  (granular algos are post-v1.1.4, free via H90 Control).

- **"H90 Polysynth Organs"** / "Twenty Five H90 Leslies and Cathedral/Tonewheel Organs"
  https://patchstorage.com/h90-polysynth-organs/ — 25 organ/Leslie programs. Less core to drone,
  but the cathedral/tonewheel pads are usable sustained-wall material on baritone.

## The four GRANULAR algorithms (free firmware add, post-v1.1.4) — what they do
Source: Eventide "Eventide Goes Granular" (https://www.eventideaudio.com/eventide-goes-granular/)
- **Cosmic Web** — blends reverse-pitched grains with classic Eventide reverb → deep delay + pad-like
  modulated space. (The single most on-aesthetic granular algo: reverse grains into reverb.)
- **GrainMod** — modulated grain params; 3 independent LFOs on Trigger Rate / Grain Duration / Filter
  for textures that *evolve rather than repeat*. (Evolving drone beds.)
- **Glitch** — slices/distorts/reconstructs audio; 3 glitch types, stackable. (Degraded "broken" fx.)
- **Stutter** — burst patterns, precise subdivision; tight rolls to scattered patterns.
All four free for H90 owners via H90 Control. *Flag: keep firmware current — these are the most
rig-relevant algorithms and they postdate the bundled v1.1.4 manual.*
