# Firmware lore: the granular update (Cosmic Web, Glitch, GrainMod, Stutter)

The H90's "mod path" is free firmware. The most on-aesthetic addition for a drone/doom/shoegaze rig is the late-2025 **granular update** — four new algorithms, free via H90 Control, also shipped to the H9000:

- **Cosmic Web** — shimmering granular delays + ambient reverb that "stretch and bloom"; blends **reverse-pitched grains with classic Eventide reverb** for deep delay and pad-like modulated space. (The single most rig-relevant new algorithm — reverse-pitched granular pads into reverb is exactly the wall-builder this rig wants.)
- **Stutter** — tight controlled burst patterns, or scatter them loosely across time.
- **Glitch** — aliasing, bursts, and pitch jumps for rhythmic, reactive textures.
- **GrainMod** — evolving modulation across every grain (movement in the mix).

Walkthroughs: "Walkthrough of the H90 (v1.12.5) Granular & Glitch Factory Presets" (The Pedal Collaborative); Eventide's own YouTube granular walkthrough (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0jEvzZPuzbU).

## Firmware-as-mod-path takeaways
- These algorithms **postdate the bundled v1.1.4 PDF** in this folder — they are NOT in the printed manual. Keep H90 Control updated to get them.
- The shipped count was 62; firmware has added VocalShiftMIDI, Quadravox+, and the four granular algorithms, putting the current build's algorithm set well past 62.
- Granular processors are directly on-aesthetic: Cosmic Web/GrainMod for evolving pads, Glitch/Stutter for the "recorded-wrong" rhythmic destruction the tape stage echoes.

## Rig relevance
For the End board: try **Cosmic Web** as Preset A (granular reverse-pitch pad) feeding a Blackhole/Plate in Preset B, then print the whole thing to the PORTA424/JHS 424 tape stage. **Glitch/Stutter** can pre-mangle a sustained baritone drone before it hits the reverb. Update firmware first — none of this is in the bundled manual.
