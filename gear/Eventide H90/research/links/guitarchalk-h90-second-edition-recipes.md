# Guitar Chalk (2nd ed.) — 5 named Programs: algorithm pairs + Mix values (partial)

Five more named two-Preset Programs. Algorithm pairing, routing, and the two Mix levels are concrete;
the four tone controls are only labeled "Param 1–4" on this page (generic 1–10), so these are
structural starting points, not full knob tables. Provenance = community/blog.

## 1. "Clean Studio FX" — Bluesy Clean
- Algo A **Spring** → (series) → Algo B **Mod EchoVerb**
- Mix A **4**, Mix B **5** · Param 1 **2**, 2 **5**, 3 **6**, 4 **6**

## 2. "Heaven Pad" — Ambient Swells [ON-AESTHETIC]
- Algo A **ShimmerVerb** + (parallel) + Algo B **Digital Delay**
- Mix A **7**, Mix B **6** · Param 1 **3**, 2 **4**, 3 **5**, 4 **7**

## 3. "Surgical Chug" — Modern High Gain
- Algo A **PitchFuzz** ← (series, B into A) ← Algo B **H910 Harmonizer**
- Mix A **5**, Mix B **4** · Param 1 **9**, 2 **6**, 3 **3**, 4 **7**

## 4. "Liquid Lead" — Fusion Lead
- Algo A **CrushStation** → (series) → Algo B **MicroPitch**
- Mix A **6**, Mix B **5** · Param 1 **7**, 2 **4**, 3 **7**, 4 **5**

## 5. "Bit Glitch" — Experimental Glitch [ON-AESTHETIC]
- Algo A **UltraTap** + (parallel) + Algo B **SP2016 Reverb**
- Mix A **8**, Mix B **9** · Param 1 **4**, 2 **5**, 3 **4**, 4 **6**

## Why it matters for this rig
**"Heaven Pad"** (ShimmerVerb ∥ Digital Delay) and **"Bit Glitch"** (UltraTap ∥ SP2016) are the two
on-thesis ones — useful as algorithm-pairing + routing + balance templates even though the four tone
params aren't individually named on the page. Treat as structure-only starting points.
