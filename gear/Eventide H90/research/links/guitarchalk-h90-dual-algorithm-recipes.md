# Guitar Chalk — 7 dual-algorithm H90 Program recipes (full control-by-control values)

Seven named two-Preset Program recipes, each with both algorithms, routing, and exact control
values. (The "Shimmer Pad Wash," "Chorus Verb Dreamscape," and "Lo-fi Vinyl Space" recipes were
already captured in h90-ambient-pad-settings-recipes.md — repeated here verbatim for completeness;
the other four are NEW.) Starting points — dial by ear.

## 1. Shimmer Pad Wash (Blackhole + PitchFuzz, PARALLEL) — ambient/drone [already on disk]
- Preset A — Blackhole: Size **90**, Gravity **Inverse**, Feedback **40%**, Mix **70%**
- Preset B — PitchFuzz: Pitch 1 **+12 (one octave)**, Gain **Low**, Tone **Mellow**, Mix **50%**

## 2. Modulated Lead Delay (Mod Delay + CrushStation, SERIES) — NEW
- Preset A — Mod Delay: Delay Time **500 ms**, Feedback **35%**, Mod Depth **30%**, Mix **45%**
- Preset B — CrushStation: Drive **45%**, Octave Blend **+1 Oct 25%**, Sag **20%**, Mix **40%**

## 3. Chorus Verb Dreamscape (TriceraChorus + Spring, PARALLEL) — ambient/shoegaze [already on disk]
- Preset A — TriceraChorus: Detune **60%**, LFO Shape **Sine**, Depth **50%**, Mix **50%**
- Preset B — Spring Reverb: Decay **4.5**, Drip **80%**, Mix **60%**

## 4. Pitchy Rhythm Sync (Micropitch + Synthonizer, SERIES) — NEW
- Preset A — Micropitch: Detune Left **-9 cents**, Detune Right **+9 cents**, Delay Spread **25 ms**, Mix **50%**
- Preset B — Synthonizer: Voice 1 **Saw**, Glide **Medium**, Env Mod **35%**, Mix **60%**

## 5. Lo-fi Vinyl Space (LoFi + Blackhole, SERIES) — strongly on-aesthetic [already on disk]
- Preset A — LoFi: Bit Depth **12-bit**, Noise **Tape Hiss 40%**, Flutter **60%**, Mix **70%**
- Preset B — Blackhole: Gravity **-25**, Feedback **70%**, Size **90**, Mix **65%**

## 6. Envelope Pitch Chaos (PitchFuzz + ModFilter, SERIES) — NEW, glitchy/degraded
- Preset A — PitchFuzz: Pitch 1 **+7 semitones**, Fuzz Gain **40%**, Env Sensitivity **High**, Mix **55%**
- Preset B — ModFilter: Filter Type **Bandpass**, LFO Rate **1.2 Hz**, Depth **75%**, Mix **50%**

## 7. Sunset Trails — Dual Delays (Digital Delay + Reverse Delay, PARALLEL) — NEW, ambient/drone
- Preset A — Digital Delay: Time **400 ms**, Feedback **45%**, Mix **40%**
- Preset B — Reverse Delay: Time **650 ms**, Mix **60%**, Crossfade **80%**

## Why it matters for this rig
The NEW ones that fit the End-board drone/degraded thesis: **Sunset Trails** (parallel forward+reverse
delays = self-blurring ambient bed over a drone), **Envelope Pitch Chaos** (PitchFuzz→ModFilter =
"recorded-wrong" envelope-swept pitch grime), and **Pitchy Rhythm Sync** (Micropitch ±9c into a
Synthonizer saw = detuned synth-pad layer under the baritone). Provenance = community/blog
(single-author, not Eventide-official) — treat as dial-by-ear starting points.
