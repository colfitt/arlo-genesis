# Eventide-staff technique settings: Shimmer / volume-swell / ModEchoVerb (concrete-where-given)

Three forum threads. Staff gave technique-level settings rather than full knob tables, but the
specific control targets + the few numeric values below are NEW and actionable. Provenance =
community/manufacturer-staff.

## Shimmer reverb (staff "brock") — expression-pedal control of Pitch Decay
- Shimmer's pitch loops around & re-shifts, so it overloads if left running. The fix (staff-recommended):
  **map an expression pedal to Pitch Decay** — **Start ≈ 80–100**, **End point set THROUGH Pitch Freeze
  / Pitch+Verb Freeze** (past 100). Sweep up to bring shimmer tails in, then push past 100 to freeze
  before the clash. Key controls: **Decay** broken into **Low Band / Mid Band / High Band Decay**, and
  **Pitch Decay** (0→100, then Pitch Freeze, then Pitch+Verb Freeze).
- Alternative that froze cleanly for one user: **DualVerb** (instead of Shimmer).

## Volume-swell / violining (staff "brock" + "tbskoglund")
- **UltraTap** with **Chop = a Swell option**; adjust the swell **threshold** (trigger level), **Rise**
  (time to full volume), and **Release** time. Run **UltraTap parallel with DynaVerb** (set DynaVerb
  **Threshold** + **Release**) for a richer swell.
- **PolySynth** swell: start from the **"PWM Strings"** preset, **turn Attack UP** for a slower swell,
  adjust filter **cutoff/resonance** to taste.
- **HotSawz** for a violin tone: **map Tri Spd & LFO (Pitch) Amount to an expression pedal**; adjust
  **Attack & Decay together via the [P] HotKnob**.

## ModEchoVerb (staff "ndeshpande") — shoegaze/ambient direction
- **"Keep mod rate low and use mod depth to smooth out the top end. Stick to the Hall 2 setting, keep
  the room size control large. Experiment with diffusion, especially on the lower end of the range."**
  → low Mod Rate, higher Mod Depth, **Hall 2**, large Size, low Diffusion.

## Why it matters for this rig
The Shimmer exp→Pitch-Decay 80–100→Freeze move is the reliable way to ring out shimmer drones without
the documented overload; UltraTap Chop=Swell + the "distinct rests" gate (see apalazzolo file) is the
playable-swell recipe; ModEchoVerb Hall 2 + low-mod-rate/high-depth + large size is the staff's own
shoegaze-wall direction. All staff-sourced, but technique-level (no full knob tables).
