# H90 ambient / pad / drone Program recipes (copyable starting values)

Concrete two-Preset Program recipes for sustained pads and ambient walls. Numbers are starting points
from Guitar Chalk's H90 settings guide and the Eventide forum pad-soloing thread — dial by ear. All
are on-aesthetic for the End-board drone/shoegaze role.

## "Shimmer Pad Wash" (Blackhole + PitchFuzz, parallel)
- **Preset A — Blackhole**: Size **90**, Gravity **Inverse**, Feedback **40%**, Mix **70%**
- **Preset B — PitchFuzz**: pitch **+1 octave**, low gain, mellow tone, Mix **50%**
- Routing: **Parallel**; map an **expression pedal to reverb Mix (30–80%)** for swelling the wall in/out.

## "Lo-fi Vinyl Space" (LoFi → Blackhole, series) — strongly on-aesthetic for this rig
- **Preset A — LoFi**: Bit Depth **12-bit**, Noise **Tape Hiss 40%**, Flutter **60%**, Mix **70%**
- **Preset B — Blackhole**: Gravity **−25**, Feedback **70%**, Size **90**, Mix **65%**
- Routing: **Series** (LoFi → Blackhole); expression pedal controls both. This is essentially a
  pre-degraded ambient wall — overlaps the rig's tape/lo-fi thesis even before the PORTA424 stage.

## "Chorus Verb Dreamscape" (TriceraChorus + Spring, parallel)
- **Preset A — TriceraChorus**: Detune **60%**, LFO Sine, Depth **50%**, Mix **50%**
- **Preset B — Spring Reverb**: Decay **4.5**, Drip **80%**, Mix **60%**
- Routing: **Parallel** — lush, shimmery clean ambience for arpeggios/intros.

## Pads-to-solo-over — algorithm + technique menu (Eventide forum)
For a sustained pad you can solo on top of:
- **PolySynth** (newer algo) has a **Freeze** — *"freeze/sustain the synth sound and then solo on top
  of it."* Best for a synthy pad.
- **Polyphony** — same idea, *"less of a synth tone"* (more natural/organ pad). SIFT algos are
  *"fantastic for pads."* (Chord-capable — relevant to banjo, per the dossier.)
- **UltraTap** — factory **UltraSwell** preset is *"a great starting point for pads"*; add a reverb
  after UltraTap *"to smooth out the release."* (This is the engine behind apalazzolo's ambient-pad
  and bowed-instrument Patchstorage lists.)
- **Any Reverb** with **INF / FREEZE / extremely long Decay** = instant pad; fade in/out with an
  **expression pedal or MIDI**.
- **Delay** algorithms can mimic pads with a healthy amount of **SLUR / SLURM** applied.
- Looper workflow: layer a pad with the looper, then swap in another Preset to play along with the loop.
- Hardware reality check (quoted): *"you'll only need one H90 for this; perhaps just 1/2 of one."*

## Build-order tip for live (from the dossier + these recipes)
Map **HotSwitch 1 → Freeze/Infinite** on the reverb (Blackhole/Shimmer/Hall) so one footswitch turns
any held chord into an infinite pad to solo over — the H90 way to cover scene changes the benched Big
Sky's presets would otherwise handle.
