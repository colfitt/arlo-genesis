# Paid / commercial H90 preset packs (polished, ambient-leaning)

Most commercial H90 packs come from the worship-guitar world — which overlaps heavily with this rig's
aesthetic (big ambient swells, shimmer, pads, atmospheric delays). Listed for completeness; the free
Patchstorage well (separate file) is deeper, but these are curated and labeled. None of these are
"signature artist" presets — they're producer/educator packs.

## Worship Tutorials — "Eventide H90 Presets Pack" — $29.99
https://worshiptutorials.com/product/eventide-h90-presets-pack/
- **50+ presets across 33 Programs.** Delays, reverbs, modulation, pitch, synth, drive, fuzz.
- Works in Insert and Dual modes. Design note (quoted): *"some of our programs are designed so both
  presets are used together. Others have two different variations of the same sound (like a subtle
  reverb and a bigger reverb of the same type)."* Good model for how to build A/B Program pairs.

## Jake Fauber / "TeddyTones" — "H90 Presets V2" — $70
https://www.jakefaubermusic.com/teddytones/p/teddytones-h90-presets-v2
- **36 presets.** Delays, modulation, ambience, reverb. A "Chasing Bliss" section is explicitly
  *"ambient textures and cinematic sound design"* — the on-aesthetic part. Named captures incl.
  CLEANWATER PLUS, MAVERICK, GRAND CANYON (PLUS + STANDARD versions). "Wide, atmospheric textures."

## Gideon Boley — 26 H90 worship multi-FX patches
https://www.soundshop.co/product-page/h90-presets-by-gideon-boley
- 26 programs; "the exact patches the worship guitarist plays live."

## Jay Parmar (guitarist/YouTuber)
https://www.patreon.com/jayparmarguitarist
- **Free**: a synth-strings preset (turns guitar into ambient synth-string keyboard) — good free demo
  of the H90's synth/pad capability. https://www.patreon.com/posts/finally-h90-free-129596477
- **Paid pack**: 14 premium presets incl. "JAY CLEAN DELAY + SHIM" (clean delay + shimmering
  harmonics). Ambient/lead-leaning.

## Verdict for this rig
Skip the paid packs unless you want a fast labeled starting set; the free Patchstorage ambient/
granular programs (apalazzolo, brock, rustydutch) are more on-aesthetic for drone/doom/shoegaze.
If buying one, Jake Fauber V2's "Chasing Bliss" cinematic section is the closest fit.
