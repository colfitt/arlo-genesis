# Dark Star Stereo — Russo Music review (directional recipes)

Directional, not full copyable patches. Distilled guidance:

## Shimmer
- "Turn a knob to the right and get a heightened pitch reverb, reminiscent of shimmer-style reverbs." → Pitch 1/Pitch 2 **clockwise** = upward pitch / shimmer.

## Octave-down drone / pad
- "Double down on lower pitches and hear the interstellar grumbles..." → Pitch knobs **counter-clockwise** for downward pitch. Pairs with high Decay + Multiply for dense evolving textures. (This is the doom-pad recipe direction.)

## Lo-fi / Crush
- "The more you move counterclockwise, the fewer bits are used... the more broken and distorted the reverb sounds." → Crush **counter-clockwise** for escalating bit-crush degradation.

## Hold / frozen
- Aux footswitch in Hold mode "acts as a sustain control that holds out the tone of the reverb infinitely." (The freeze-drone move.)

## Self-oscillation warning
- "Be mindful of the Dark Star Stereo's pension [penchant] for self-oscillation at higher settings." → raise **Multiply** carefully; high Multiply + high Decay tips into runaway feedback. (Confirms dossier §4.)
