gearnews.com · "The Lo-Fi-iest Reverb Pedal of them all?" · 2024 (launch coverage)

Tips-flavored feature piece. Useful for the "use in moderation" notes and routing-toggle mechanics.

POWER-USER NOTES
- MULTIPLY: "used in moderation" it produces "wonderfully yowling and wobbly sounds." Implication: a LITTLE Multiply = chorused/wobble character; a LOT = self-oscillation. The sweet spot for wobble is just before runaway.
- PITCH QUANTIZATION: the two shifters (±2 oct) are quantized to "pure intervals" so you can build "reverb CHORDS" instead of dissonant noise — a deliberate songwriting tool, not just detune.
- CRUSH: left reduces samples (bitcrush), right "transforms it into a nasty overdrive."
- VOLUME knob exists specifically so output "doesn't become too quiet when the Mix is set to maximum" (full-wet drops level; use Volume to make up gain).
- SPREAD = stereo width control.

ROUTING TOGGLE MECHANIC (verbatim)
- "Hold the Bypass footswitch and press Preset to switch between mono-to-mono, mono-to-stereo, or stereo-to-stereo configurations." (Global routing; matches dossier.)

CHARACTER VERDICT
- Reverb is "epic" / "cathedral-like." Notably ABSENT: "crisp plate reverbs" or spring sounds — this is not a utility reverb, it's the extreme/lo-fi end. Price listed €349.
