patchstorage.com · "Neutron Star" by user "wz" · community ZOIA patch

NOT a Dark Star pedal preset — it's an Empress/Poly-Effects ZOIA / Euroburo patch that EMULATES the Dark Star. Included as evidence of the Dark Star's community reputation (people clone it) and as a cross-reference of which "modes" players consider essential.

WHAT IT IS
- Author "wz" describes it as "A unique, haunting, crushed and broken reverb based on the OBNE Dark Star."
- Recreates "modes 1 and 3 of the ORIGINAL Dark Star" in full stereo — i.e. the community shorthand for the original mono Dark Star's Pitch mode and Delay mode, the two most-loved of its three toggle modes.
- Core = a GhostVerb module, with optional pitch-shift and bit-crush toggled via stomp switches, plus CV control over decay, modulation rate, and blend.
- Stereo in / stereo out (or mono-in/stereo-out). ~84% CPU on ZOIA firmware 2.0. License: WTFPL.

TAKEAWAY FOR US
- The two original-Dark-Star modes players bother to clone are PITCH + DELAY (over Crush alone) — useful signal on what the "essential" Dark Star voice is in community memory. On the V3 these run simultaneously rather than as a toggle, so the V3 inherently does what this patch reassembles.
