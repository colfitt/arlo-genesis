# The Tone Shepherd — Hologram Microcosm preset pack (commercial)

The only notable *commercial* preset pack found for the Microcosm. Because the pedal has no
preset file format (see microcosm-presets-banks-and-management.md), this is **sold as a
settings/recipe sheet you dial in by hand**, not a downloadable patch file.

## What it is
- **Price:** $19.99. Includes "lifetime updates"; buyer must "set up an account upon checkout
  to retain lifetime access."
- **6 presets**, named:
  - **Soft Ambience**
  - **Under Cover**
  - **Faux Fast Picking**
  - **Drowned Pad**
  - **Star Bed**
  - **Instant Fast Pick**
- Seller framing: the Microcosm is exceptionally suited for "lush ambient textures," it "dices,
  stretches, warps, and mangles your signal." Recommends pairing with Strymon / Meris / POG2 /
  H90 for fuller results.

## Honest assessment for this rig
- The names map cleanly onto the rig's needs: **"Drowned Pad" / "Star Bed" / "Soft Ambience"**
  are the frozen-drone / ambient-bed sounds; **"Faux Fast Picking" / "Instant Fast Pick"** are
  the onset-chaining arp/strum sounds that turn banjo plucks into cascading leads.
- **Actual knob values are NOT published on the page** — they're the paid product. So this is a
  pointer, not a recipe. Given the pedal's depth, the on-aesthetic presets here are reachable
  by hand from the dossier's §13 starting points (Tunnel B / Haze A for "Star Bed"-type beds;
  Arp A/B for "Fast Pick"). Buying the pack is optional; it mainly saves dialing time.
- **Not verified by listening** — preset descriptions are the seller's; treat sound claims as
  marketing until auditioned.
