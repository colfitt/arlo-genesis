Equipboard · TC Electronic PolyTune 3 Tuner - Demo & Review · 2019-06-12

A short demo/review by Giulio of Equipboard.com, who runs the PolyTune 3 on his own board. Concise mode walkthrough.

Build/controls: soft-click footswitch on the front. True bypass normally, but he has his set to BUFFERED bypass with display ALWAYS-ON — done by unscrewing the back and flipping a couple of DIP switches. Big bright screen whose brightness auto-adjusts to ambient lighting. On top: tuning button, display button, 9V power input, a power OUTPUT (this pedal can power a few other pedals from its DC out), and a micro-USB port for firmware updates only.

Modes — cycling the display button steps through:

- Several alternate / capo tunings (capo modes up through fret 7) on the tuning side.
- Guitar Needle, Guitar Strobe, Bass Needle, Bass Strobe on the display side.

Polyphonic mode — "which these PolyTune tuners made famous": strum all strings at once and tune them all simultaneously in one glance, rather than string-by-string. Demonstrated as crisp and fast.

Verdict: his favorite guitar pedal tuner; loves it. The piece is brief and confirms the buffered + always-on DIP configuration as a practical real-world choice (so the readout stays visible mid-play without muting).
