iOS/Loopy-Pro-leaning owner discussion. Useful cross-checks and a couple of fresh gotchas.

## Workflow positives
- **AndyPlankton:** the on-board sequencer cuts "screen swapping" when an iPad is the sound source — the iPad just synthesizes/records while the SL drives. Same logic applies to a DAW-less rig: the SL holds the sequencing so the gear stays in playing mode.
- **Keyboard splits (Zones)** remove the need for separate routing AUs/utilities — one keybed addresses multiple synths at once.
- **Scale highlighting** on the key LEDs doubles as a performance tool and learning aid.

## Gotchas confirmed / added
- **No MPE support** — called a dealbreaker by one user. (The keybed is channel-aftertouch only; the *pads* have poly-AT, but there is no MPE for soft synths that want it.)
- **No motorized faders** — flagged as an oversight at the price (ties to the fader-pickup file).
- **"DAW/plug-in control is a massive PITA"** — echoes the Automap-gone + Components-only-templates friction.
- **iOS integration "largely ignored"** — not relevant to this Mac-based rig, but explains why iPad users grumble.
- **CV tuning** is demonstrated in the Novation deep-dive video (~25:00 mark) — confirms the A2/A4 calibration flow in the CV file.

Net: the community verdict is "deep and capable but fiddly, not revolutionary" — which matches every other source.
