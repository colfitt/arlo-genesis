# Micro-steps — 6 per step (off-grid placement, triplets, glitch repeats)

Added in **firmware 1.2**. Each of the 16 sequencer steps subdivides into **6 micro-steps** for higher resolution (off-grid placement, triplet feels, glitch/ratchet repeats, swung notes).

## How to access & edit
1. In **Steps view**, press **Options**.
2. **Choose a sequence step** by pressing its pad.
3. The **six micro-steps appear lit on the top row of buttons** (the row above the faders).
4. **Press and hold a micro-step button** + **play key(s)** → adds those notes to that micro-step.
5. To remove: press and hold the micro-step button + press the note to remove.
6. Press a **different step** → the six micro-step buttons update to show that step's micro-steps: **bright = note present, dim = empty.**

## Why it matters (practical)
- A step = a 1/16 by default; its 6 micro-steps land at 1/16 + (1/6 of a step) increments. Placing one note on micro-step 1 and another on micro-step 3 or 5 gives **triplet** / off-grid timing without changing the pattern Sync Rate.
- Stack notes on consecutive micro-steps of one step = **glitch repeat / ratchet** burst.
- **Non-quantised live record lands notes on the nearest 1/6 tick** — micro-steps view is where you then *see and nudge* those recorded notes (see seq-manual-recording.md).
