# SL MkIII firmware 1.4 — the "generative & constantly evolving instrument" upgrade (NEW, this wave)

Captured during the patch-list wave to confirm the generative-engine framing the patches lean on. This is Novation's OWN positioning of 1.4 and the single cleanest official statement that the SL is meant to run as a generative instrument — directly supports the drone/aleatoric patches below.

## Distilled (verbatim / near-verbatim from the article)
- **Per-arpeggiator probability** — *"brings an element of randomness to each arpeggiator, turning SL MkIII into a generative and constantly evolving instrument."* Each of the **8 Parts** has its own independent arpeggiator, and each arp now has its own probability → polyrhythmic, never-repeating layers from held chords.
- **Step probability** — the probability of a programmed sequencer step can be altered, *"bringing creative irregularity and extending the musical palette."* (This is the per-step Chance already documented in the manual; 1.4 surfaced it more.)
- **Sequencer transpose (with pads)** — the 8×2 pads become a performable transposition keyboard; *"makes sequencer transposition a powerful performance tool."* Range 0..+11 semis (Down arrow → −11..0), per `seq-manual-scales.md`.
- **Pattern octave transpose** — shift a pattern across octaves for live pitch exploration.
- **Toggle swing per-Part** — independent groove per track of the 8 → tracks at the same rate can be made to breathe against each other (the phasing-drift trick).
- **Pattern shift** — *"quickly offset the start and end points of a pattern together to explore variations."*
- **Pitch-bend → CV** (the CV-bend feature) is also a 1.4 addition (corroborates the dossier correction).

## Honest notes
- The article gives **no numeric default values or ranges** for probability — those come from the V2 manual (Chance 0–100% per step; arp Chance 0–100%; see `seq-manual-step-editing.md` + `seq-manual-arpeggiator.md`).
- **No artist is named.** A Bandcamp track ("Things in Motion — Session III") surfaced in the same search but I could NOT verify any SL-MkIII attribution for that artist → not cited as a found setup. No verified famous user exists for this controller (normal for the category).
