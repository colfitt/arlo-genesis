# Scales — 16 types + chromatic, root, modes, sequence transpose

Scales apply to **both the keys AND the sequencer.**

## Enable / behaviour
- **Enable Scales view:** hold **Shift + Scale.** With Scales on, notes adhere to the chosen scale whether played on keys or from the Sequencer.
- **Key LED behaviour:** root notes = bright; in-scale notes = dim; out-of-scale = off (unlit). So any lit key (dim or bright) is guaranteed in-scale.

## Scale Settings (press Scale)
- **Root Note** (left-most rotary): C, C#, D, Eb, E, F, F#, G, Ab, A, Bb, B.
- **Scale Type** (Type rotary), 16 types + chromatic:
  Natural Minor, Major, Dorian, Phrygian, Mixolydian, Melodic Minor, Harmonic Minor, Bebop Dorian, Blues, Minor Pentatonic, Hungarian Minor, Ukrainian Minor, Marva, Todi, Whole Tone, Chromatic.
- **Scale Mode** (how out-of-scale notes behave):
  - **Snap** — out-of-scale notes snap up/down to nearest scale note.
  - **Filter** — out-of-scale notes don't play at all.
  - **Display Only** — notes pass unmodified; LEDs are just a *guide* (free to play non-diatonic notes).
- **Turn Scale on/off per Part:** soft buttons above the pads. White background = scales enabled for that Part; black = disabled. (Useful: disable scale on a drum Part, keep it on for synth/bass/strings.)

## Sequence Transpose (independent of scale)
- **Sequence Transpose** rotary applies a transpose value to notes coming from the Sequencer (e.g. +5 semitones). **Independent of Root and Type** — shifts sequenced Parts without changing the scale.
- Range: **±11 semitones (half-steps).**
- **Sequence Transpose with pads** (firmware 1.4): in Scale Settings the 8×2 pads become a chromatic keyboard for **performable** transposition. Default range 0–11 semitones up; **Down arrow** (left of pads) accesses negative −11 to 0.
