What Strymon say about it: the Swell machine **brings in the reverb gradually behind the dry signal** — so you can play your part and the reverb swells in around the notes. It's for subtle, evolving textures, like having a volume pedal on the wet signal. **Alternatively, you can choose to have the dry signal itself swelled into the reverb, for maximum ambience and atmosphere** (this is the "Dry" mode — it hides the picking attack entirely). It's a very cool, creative reverb and good for writing music.
