Aggregated survey of community/paid Big Sky preset packs · various stores · current

The biggest organized source of shareable Big Sky presets is the **worship-guitar**
ecosystem — a handful of creators sell .syx packs (load via Nixie 2 / Strymon Librarian
over MIDI). These skew toward "modern worship ambient" (Bethel/Hillsong/Elevation tones)
but the underlying machines (Cloud, Bloom, Shimmer, Swell, Chorale, Magneto) and the
swell/pad-wall techniques are directly transferable to drone/doom/shoegaze. None publish
full settings on the sales page (you get the .syx + a master list after purchase), so
treat these as "where to buy ready-made walls," not as free-settings sources.

## Notable packs / sources
- **GuitarforHISGLORY — "Big Sky Presets (Artist and General)"** · $39.99 ·
  https://guitarforhisglory.com/product/strymon-big-sky-presets-artist-and-general/
  - Categories: ~5 Bethel, 4 Hillsong, 5 Elevation, 2 Carson Tomlin, plus general
    Cloud/Plate/Chorale/Spring/Bloom/Magneto/Shimmer presets.
  - Named examples: "Cloud Swell", "Bloom Swell", "Swell Cloud", "Brighty Washy".
  - Delivery: "imported into your pedal using a MIDI cable and Strymon's visual editing
    program, NIXIE." (i.e. .syx via Nixie 2 + MIDI interface — same workflow as everything.)
- **The Tone Shepherd — "Strymon BigSky Presets"** · $19.99 ·
  https://www.thetoneshepherd.com/store/p/strymon-bigsky-presets
  - "Premium tones for passionate praise"; author's own on-stage/studio presets, includes
    a "MASTER PRESET LIST" doc + **lifetime updates**. Worship focus.
- **Sam Wittek Music — Strymon BigSky bundle** ·
  https://www.samwittekmusic.com/shop/p/strymon-bigsky-bundle
  - Worship-guitar resource shop; bundles of fine-tuned presets (subtle→ambient), with
    machine variants noted (Bloom, Cloud, Plate, "Float" = a Cloud variant for leads).
- **Nigel Hendroff & Peter James (Hillsong) — "BigSky Presets"** · MultiTracks.com ·
  https://www.multitracks.com/sounds/nigel-hendroff/bigsky-presets-by-nigel-hendroff-and-peter-james-bigsky/
  - Presets from actual Hillsong touring guitarists — the most "real artist" pack of the
    group. Sold through MultiTracks' Sounds store.
- **The Church Collective** · https://thechurchcollective.com/resources/strymon-bigsky-presets/
  - Landing/community page (gated behind a free network signup); not a direct .syx list.

## Free / DIY routes (no purchase)
- **Strymon "This Week's Preset"** — free official .syx downloads (see
  strymon-bigsky-this-weeks-preset-series.md). Best free named presets with intent docs.
- **Factory presets** — already on the pedal; re-derive/edit in Nixie 2 (see
  strymon-bigsky-factory-presets.md).
- **Forums/Discord** — TGP, SevenString.org, Strymon's own forum, and the Strymon Discord
  trade .syx files informally. (Flag: ad-hoc, unverified quality; no canonical archive.)

## Bottom line for the rig
For drone/doom/shoegaze you don't need to buy a worship pack — the **factory Cloud/Shimmer/
Bloom/Swell/Magneto presets + the free "This Week's Preset" .syx files** cover the
aesthetic. Buy a pack only if you want a large ready-made library of swell/pad walls fast.
The Hillsong (Hendroff/James) pack is the one with genuine touring-artist provenance.
