A settings roundup with five named starting-point patches. **QC FLAG:** values are given
on a vague **0–10 knob scale** (not clock positions or seconds), the "Value" knob on the
Big Sky is actually fine-Decay/preset-scroll (not a standalone 0–10 param), and the prose
reads like generic/SEO content. Treat these as rough directional starting points, NOT
verified factory or artist settings. Useful only as "which machine for which vibe" + a
loose Mix/Decay/Mod ballpark.

| Patch (their name) | Machine | Decay | Pre-Delay | Mix | Mod | Intent |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Bluesy Clean | **Cloud** | 5/10 | 3 | 4 | 2 | warm, articulate |
| Vintage Ambient | **Bloom** | 8/10 | 2 | 6 | 7 | spacious/ethereal lead lines |
| High-Gain Ambient Metal | **Shimmer** | 9/10 | 6 | 5 | 6 | massive trails over distortion |
| Experimental Soundscape | **Nonlinear** | 10/10 | 5 | 8 | 9 | unpredictable evolving textures, extreme mod |
| Synth-Like Clean Swell | **Swell** | 9/10 | 7 | 7 | 8 | lush pad-style swells |

On-aesthetic reads for the rig: the "Experimental Soundscape" (Nonlinear, max decay + high
mod + high mix) and "Synth-Like Clean Swell" (Swell, long decay, high pre-delay, high mod)
are the doom/shoegaze-leaning ones — but build them by ear from the factory presets
(see strymon-bigsky-factory-presets.md), not from this 0–10 table.
