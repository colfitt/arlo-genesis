# Official Big Sky ambient/drone preset recipes (Cloud / Bloom / Shimmer)

Concrete, Strymon-authored patches for exactly the drone/shoegaze territory this rig wants. Exact numeric knob values live in the per-preset images and the downloadable .syx files; the **techniques and the values stated in prose** are captured below.

## "Star Cloud" — Cloud (huge ethereal wall)
URL: /this-weeks-preset-bigsky-star-cloud/
- **Mod: 2:00** = "maximum modulation depth but at the slowest speed possible" (the slow-deep mod that makes Cloud shimmer without obvious wobble).
- Signal: guitar straight into Big Sky, **zero dry signal**, direct out — proves Cloud can be a standalone pad source.
- Live fade-out trick: roll **Tone all the way down, then Decay to minimum** for a fast, smooth tail kill.
- Use: with a prevalent dry signal it's "a rich, ethereal wall of sound behind your guitar"; with zero dry it becomes an organic synth-pad substitute.

## "Bloombient" — Bloom (electronic-ocean wash)
URL: /this-weeks-preset-bigsky-bloombient/
- **PARAM 1 → Length**, **PARAM 2 → Feedback** (the two deep Bloom controls put on the front knobs).
- **~20-second decay** for "a huge wash."
- **Expression pedal → Tone** (in Bloom, Tone is the cutoff of a synth-like resonant filter — sweeping it is the move).
- Mod modulates BOTH the bloom-generating delay lines and the tank delay lines.
- Technique: pick single notes, let them ring, sweep Tone + nudge Length → "a wash of noise like a wave in an electronic ocean."

## "Late Bloomer" — Bloom (delayed onset)
URL: /weeks-preset-bigsky-late-bloomer/
- **Long Bloom Length + high Pre-Delay** so the reverb arrives well after the note.
- **Mix ~3:00** (high) — yet the dry signal is not overpowered because the wet arrives late.

## "7 Wonders" — Bloom (balanced starting point)
URL: /bigsky-bloom-reverb-this-weeks-preset/
- **Bloom Length just over 50%, Feedback 50%, Pre-Delay/Tone/Mod all ~50% (noon).** A good neutral Bloom to tweak from.

## "Falling Angel" — Shimmer (descending pitch)
URL: /this-weeks-preset-bigsky-falling-angel/
- **Mode = REGEN** (shimmer applied to the regen tank, not the input). IN+REG goes even bigger.
- **Tone cranked to max; Mod ~halfway.**
- **PARAM 1 → Amount, PARAM 2 → Mode.**
- Voices shifted **down ~10 cents**, and the pitch drops further with each regeneration → a slowly *falling* shimmer (inverse of the usual ascending cathedral). The way shimmer hits the regen chamber makes the reverb **swell after the initial attack**.

## Rig notes
- Cloud-with-zero-dry and Bloom-with-late-onset are ideal for banjo/baritone drone walls. Put **Length + Feedback (Bloom)** or **Amount + Mode (Shimmer)** on the PARAM knobs and **Tone on the EXP pedal** for live morphing.
