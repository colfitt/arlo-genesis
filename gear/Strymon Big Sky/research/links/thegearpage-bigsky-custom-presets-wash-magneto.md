## "WASH" — MAGNETO (community, full control-by-control)
Posted as "a washed out mix of delay and reverb." All values quoted as written:
- **Machine:** Magneto
- **Mix:** 12:00 (50/50 — delay-and-reverb blend sits with the dry, not over it)
- **Pre-Delay knob:** 2:00  ⚠️ on Magneto Pre-Delay = **Feedback**, so this is ~2:00 of feedback (long, sustained, not maxed)
- **Decay knob:** Max  (on Magneto, Decay = delay TIME → longest head spacing)
- **Mod:** 1:00
- **Tone:** 11:00 (slightly dark)
- **Lo End:** "7 clicks from the far left (−)" — i.e. low end rolled well down (thin/controlled bottom)
- **Diffusion:** "3 clicks from the far right (+)" — high diffusion → smears the multi-head echo toward reverb
- **Heads:** 6
- **Spacing:** Even (rhythmic, not textural)
- (Hold mode not stated.)

## Why it fits the rig
- This is the documented community version of the on-disk "Magneto drone" recipe:
  6 heads + Even + high Diffusion + high Feedback (Pre-Delay 2:00) = the echo smears
  into a sustained reverb-wash. Maxed Decay (delay time) widens the head spacing for
  the slowest/longest stutter before it blurs.
- Rolled-off Lo End + Tone 11:00 = the "dark/controlled" character the baritone +
  VG-800 needs to stop the long Magneto tail from building into mud.
- Pairs with a footswitched delay in front (Big Time / TimeLine) exactly as Rabea
  recommends for Magneto — broken/degraded tape textures.
