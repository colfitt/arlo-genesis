Source: official Strymon factory-preset settings chart. Page links to the PDF
`https://www.strymon.net/documents/BIGSKY_Factory_Presets.pdf` (13 pages, 33 banks ×
A/B/C = 99 documented slots + blank user templates). Each card shows machine TYPE,
Decay (in seconds/mS), the machine's menu params, and HOLD mode (INFINITE vs FREEZE).
Knob positions are shown as dial graphics in the PDF, not numbers — so Decay + the
listed menu parameters are the only exact values; Tone/Mix/Pre-Delay/Mod must be read
off the dial art (approximate). Below is the full machine + decay + menu-param table,
with the drone/doom/shoegaze-relevant ones called out.

## Useful notes
- Factory ships banks 00–33 (banks 33B–99C are duplicates/blank from the factory; the
  dossier notes re-init via power-up holding A+C).
- HOLD mode is per-preset: **FREEZE** holds the wash and lets you play over it without
  adding (the drone keeper); **INFINITE** sums every new note into the wash.
- "For Days" (10A) is the standout for the rig: Cloud at the full **50-second** decay.
