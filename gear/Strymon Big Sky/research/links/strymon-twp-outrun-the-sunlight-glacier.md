## "RedBird" — (machine not stated; mid+high balanced verb) — artist, concept only

high-range frequencies," built to support an Ebow lead in the song "Red Bird."

  designed to sit under a sustained Ebow lead.

## Why it fits the rig
- "Glacier" (20-second decay + heavy/near-full mix, used for transitions) is a clean
  artist-grade template for a long ambient wash that carries between sections — the same
  function the Big Sky serves at the End board. Pair with Freeze for an infinite transition.
- "RedBird" supporting an Ebow lead maps directly onto the banjo-as-lead / sustained-drone
  use: a full-range verb bed under a singing lead voice.
