**Five footswitch commands total** (kept small so it's easy to remember): a tap command for each switch, a hold command for each switch, and one command for pressing both at once.

**Tap commands:**
- **Left tap** = record. Press again to set the measure (loop length) and go into playback. From then on, left tap toggles between record (overdub) and play.
- **Right tap** = stop the loop.
- **Both at once** = delete the loop.

**External footswitch:** plug a switch into the **EXT RECORD jack** and it takes over the left-footswitch function — no dip switch needed. (KNOBs uses this throughout to play hands-free.)

**Left HOLD = One-Shot Recording.** This is the clean way to "print" effects. Hold the left switch: the record LEDs blink red, then turn green. Set up your effects (he demos a dark/murky Filter + Stability), hold the left switch for exactly one pass, then turn the modifiers/Stability OFF — and **you still hear their effect because it's been recorded into the loop.** One-shot recording lets you paste effects right over the loop for exactly one pass without worrying about timing.
- Perk: after printing effects this way, you can overdub clean guitar on top that *won't* be affected by those (now-baked-in) effects.

**Right HOLD = undo/redo submenu** (the final footswitch function — a little submenu; identical to the LAYERS knob's undo/redo).
