## The critical caveat that shapes everything
**Blooper saves AUDIO, not control settings.** Unlike most Chase Bliss pedals, it has NO knob/dip "preset" recall. It stores **16 LOOPS** (audio + full layer stacks), recalled via SAVE/LOAD and via **MIDI Program Changes 0–15** (so loops sit in line with BOSS ES / MS-series controller banks and CB "Faves"). Confirmed by Andy Othling on stream (*"you can save the whole layer stack… it'll save all of it"*) and by CB docs.
- Consequence: there is **no community ecosystem of shareable knob-preset files** the way there is for, say, an H90 or a synth. A "Blooper recipe" = a written description of mode + dip + modifier + knob positions, OR a saved-loop audio file — not a transferable preset blob.
- What CAN be shared/customized in software: your **modifier arrangement** (which 12 of the roster live in the two banks) via the **BLIP** firmware tool — but that's a layout, not a "sound patch." (BLIP/MIDI ecosystem is owned by the parallel `cb-stack-` agent; don't duplicate.)

## Best sources for actual recipes/settings (ranked)
1. **Official modifier cheat sheet** — 4-page PDF with 2 named recipes + a tip PER modifier (TAPE STOP, GLACIER, CRUMBLE, DRONE PATTERN, ROLLING CLOUDS, STEP SEQUENCE, etc.). The single most copyable recipe doc. → captured in `chasebliss-blooper-modifier-cheat-sheet.md` (this folder). (PDF: https://assets.ctfassets.net/mixoa90md6d2/zf28ER5UdB8CuelEXog7d/2e7811de2c0862084be6d006ceb1c2f8/Chase_Bliss_blooper_-_modifier_cheat_sheet.pdf)
2. **The Blooper manual's named patches** — "Blippertronics," "Tape Machine," "Delooping," "Repair Me," "Noise Generator," "Loop Capsules," "Accumulator," "Carryover." These are documented in the in-folder 2023 manual and summarized in the dossier (`Blooper-DeepResearch.md` §3, §13). The manual is the authoritative recipe book.
3. **KNOBs "better bloops" official video manual** (Modifiers / Additive / Stability episodes) — captured by the parallel agent in `transcripts/knobs-better-bloops-*.md`. Co-designer Scott Harper demonstrating each control. Don't duplicate.
4. **Andy Othling "Office Hours" teaching streams** — Andy walking through real recipes live (filter-fade delay, rainbow-machine pitch cascade, reverse fifths, momentary-record sampling). → `transcripts/andy-othling-office-hours-blooper.md`.
5. **modifier resource pages** — https://blooper.chasebliss.com/modifiers (living modifier docs) and the BLIP resource bank (downloadable modifier sets).

## Community channels (honest: sparse on copy-paste recipes)
- **r/blooper** (Reddit) — the official-ish community hub; Andy points people there for sound-clip requests. Activity is mostly clips/Q&A, **not** a structured recipe library. (Linked by Andy in the Office Hours stream.)
- **Elektronauts "Chase Bliss Blooper" thread** (https://www.elektronauts.com/t/chase-bliss-blooper/76484) — long thread; mostly comparison/chatter (vs Octatrack/Mood/ZOIA), **few concrete settings.** Surveyed — no extractable recipes.
- **TalkBass "accessing extra modifiers" thread** (https://www.talkbass.com/threads/chase-bliss-audio-blooper-users-accessing-extra-modifiers.1526902/) — about reaching alt-bank modifiers via dips/BLIP; returned 403 to automated fetch but title/topic logged for manual follow-up.
- **Morningstar forum** (https://forum.morningstar.io/t/issue-cba-blooper-toggle-modifier-settings/3598) — MIDI/controller toggle issues, not sound recipes.
- **Get Offset podcast demo** (https://getoffsetpodcast.com/demo-chase-bliss-blooper/) and **guitarjawn "Ambient Explorations Vol.1: Mood & Blooper"** (guitarjawn.com; site had a TLS cert mismatch on automated fetch — flagged for manual visit) — demo-context, pairs Blooper with Mood for ambient.

## Honest verdict
For COPYABLE recipes, rely on (1) the official cheat sheet and (2) the manual's named patches — both captured/summarized in this folder. The "community recipe share" scene for Blooper is genuinely thin because the pedal can't export knob presets; the canon is the official docs + the KNOBs/Andy Othling demo body of work, not user-submitted patch files.
