A feature-by-feature operational tutorial covering every basic function except MIDI. This is the single best step-by-step "how do I actually drive this thing" reference found. Button combos below are exact.

PRO TIP (physical): the unit slides on a desk — he added cheap stick-on rubber feet so it stays put during hands-on tweaking.

CALIBRATION (do this first, out of the box):
- Hold BOTH footswitches until you see purple lights across the board.
- A white light shows the current headroom preset, from LOW to VERY HIGH; you can set it manually with a button.
- Better method: let it calibrate to your input. Plug in your source, play a note/sample; lights turn green while it listens, then it auto-picks the headroom and returns to the main menu. Correct calibration gets the most out of the effects.

CONTROL LAYOUT:
- Four effect channels: RED (Character), YELLOW (Movement), GREEN (Diffusion), BLUE (Texture). Each channel selects 1 of 5 effects via the button beneath it. You cannot stack two effects from the same channel, but you can chain across the four channels.
- Top encoders by default: RED = TILT, YELLOW = RATE, GREEN = TIME. Bottom encoders by default = AMOUNT. Top-right gray encoder = MIX (left = dry, right = wet).
- SECONDARY layer: press A+B together. Now top encoders become RED = SENSITIVITY, YELLOW = DRIFT, GREEN = DRIFT; gray = OUTPUT LEVEL; bottom encoders = EFFECT VOLUME. Press A+B again to exit.
- Bypass one channel: long-press its button (light goes off). Cycling the button through all 5 effects also lands on an "off" state.
- Bypass whole pedal: single quick press of the bottom-right footswitch (light off, channel LEDs dim). Press again to re-enable.

REORDER EFFECTS (A+D):
- Press A+D. The chain lights up in current order (default left→right: Character → Movement → Diffusion → Texture). When the lights flash blue you can tap the modules in a NEW order (e.g. Diffusion first, then Character, then Texture, then Movement). It saves and returns to main. Press A+D again to view/reset.

TAP TEMPO: tap the LEFT footswitch to set tempo. Time-based effects (e.g. REELS) lock to it. Demonstrated speeding up / slowing down the delay by re-tapping.

CAPTURE / LOOPER (30 seconds):
- Two flavors. Record a SHORT note → it adds a soft edge and seamlessly overlaps (drone-like); the LED lights BLUE during playback. Record a LONGER phrase → behaves like a traditional looper with a harder cutoff at the loop point; LED lights GREEN.
- Activate by LONG-pressing the LEFT footswitch.
- It records parameter changes you make while looping, too.
- Starting a new recording overwrites the previous one.

GESTURE (knob-motion recorder, C+D): "one of the coolest features."
- Press C+D together → a row of WHITE lights (all white = nothing recorded).
- Start moving any encoder → that step lights RED (recording). A little timer shows how long you can record.
- Press C+D to stop → the step lights GREEN (motion captured and looping). It plays back like DAW automation.
- You can record multiple encoders/parameters at once.
- DELETE one knob's gesture: move that encoder a VERY SMALL amount until its light turns white — careful, move it too far and it starts re-recording.
- DELETE all gestures: press and HOLD C+D until it flashes.
- You can change the TAP TEMPO after recording to retime gestures — record at 120 BPM, drop the box to 70 BPM, hear what it does.

PRESETS (80 user slots, NO factory presets):
- Important correction: there are NO built-in presets — only 80 empty user slots. (He notes this isn't clear in the manual; forums are full of people asking why they can't hear factory presets. Audition turns up nothing on a fresh unit because the slots are empty.)
- SAVE: set up your sound, press B+C together → enters preset browser → scroll to a slot (all-white LEDs = empty; colored = occupied) → press again to save (teal/green light runs through).
- RECALL: long-press the right footswitch to open the browser, scroll to a saved (colored) preset.

DUAL BYPASS (assign specific modules to the footswitch):
- Press A+D (same menu as reorder). While in it, hold the BYPASS footswitch → blinking yellow lights. A BLINKING yellow module = will be bypassed (default = all blinking). Click a module to make it SOLID yellow = stays ON. Exit with A+D. Now pressing bypass leaves the solid module engaged and bypasses the rest.

FILTER TYPE (Texture module): press A+D, wait for blue flashing, then move the BLUE encoder: middle/green = TILT filter; full right/orange = HIGH PASS; full left/blue = LOW PASS.

CAPTURE ROUTING (pre/post FX): press A+D, then with blue flashing turn the MIX (gray) encoder: far left = PRE-effects, far right = POST-effects.

GLOBAL SETTINGS (hold ALL FOUR buttons → orange):
- RED amount encoder = LED brightness.
- YELLOW amount encoder = preset AUDITION on/off (default on/white-left; one step over = audition OFF — useful for live so the current preset persists while scrolling).
- GREEN amount encoder = bypass mode: far left = BUFFERED, one step = BUFFERED + TRAILS, third = TRUE BYPASS.
- Global settings reset: hold all four until it flashes. FULL FACTORY RESET: enter global settings (all four), then hold A+D plus both footswitches simultaneously (gives a countdown warning).

He explicitly skipped MIDI ("not a MIDI guy"). Concludes the pedal looks simple but is a surprisingly capable sound-design box under the hood.
