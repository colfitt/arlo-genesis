# EZBOT (Matthew Piecora) — OT-as-performance-mixer for live techno + the template author

VERIFIED. EZBOT is a Seattle hardware-electronic artist/educator (Bitwig certified trainer); '90s alt-rock / deep house / techno / jazz influences. He is BOTH a documented live OT user AND the author of the most-used downloadable OT performance templates (so his templates are "how he actually plays"). Same person as forum handle "mpiecora."

## How he uses the OT live (verified)
- **OT as a performance MIXER**, not a sampler-first instrument: it's the **master clock** and mixer for a stack of Elektron boxes (Digitakt, Digitone, Syntakt) and synths. (Directly applicable: OT as clock+mixer for this rig's Digitakt 2 / VG-800 / etc.)
- **"Punch-in" FX as an instrument**: effects are triggered by hand like a played instrument (Part button + arrow keys to jump FX sets), not left running.
- **8-bar auto-modulated FX** + **8-bar loops** as structural building blocks; **side-chain ducking** for pump.
- A **"unique resampling technique" to build a rumbling bass foundation** — resamples to create a sub/rumble bed under the mix.
