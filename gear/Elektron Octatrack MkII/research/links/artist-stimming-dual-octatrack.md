# Stimming — the famous TWO-Octatrack-MkII dawless live rig (Ableton/APC40 killer)

VERIFIED that Stimming runs a dual-OT-MkII laptop-free live show (visible in the Cercle "Phare de Cordouan" lighthouse set and multiple Elektronauts threads). He stated in a 2015 DJ Tech Tools interview that he wanted to ditch Ableton + APC40, and by the dual-OT setup he had.

## The two-OT architecture (corroborated across threads; the deep routing he said he'd detail "later" with DJ Tech Tools)
- **Left OT = "mothership"** — plays the *tracks without groove*. He uses essentially **bank 1, pattern 1**, and just **walks down the list of tracks** (the song stems live on the 8 tracks). This OT also acts as the **mixer / master**.
- **Right OT = "groove sampler"** — holds the grooves; he reportedly uses up to **~25 banks** of grooves here.
- **Routing**: the right OT's **main outs feed the left OT's inputs**; a **Roland SE-02** synth runs into the **other input pair** of the left OT. So the left (mothership) OT mixes its own 8 tracks + the groove OT + the SE-02 — either **through the whole engine including FX, or just summed to the output** on top of its tracks.

Caveat/honest flag: the exact bank counts and "mothership/groove" split come from his own forum/interview comments and community recaps; the single fully-authoritative routing diagram (the promised in-depth DJ Tech Tools walkthrough) is the best confirmation if you want primary detail. The general architecture (2× OT MkII, one as mixer/master, SE-02 into inputs) is well attested. Later setups (e.g. Boiler Room Chemnitz) swapped to Microfreak/Volca Kick/OT/Model:Samples, so the dual-OT rig is era-specific.

## Technique takeaways for this rig
- **OT-as-mixer-with-FX**: one OT can be the central live mixer — external sources land on input pairs A/B and C/D and you choose per-source whether they pass through the FX engine (Thru/DIR=0) or just to the output (DIR=127). This is exactly the "OT as the desk's mangling brain" role: pedalboard print → A/B, a second source (Digitakt/VG-800) → C/D, mix + process both inside one OT.
- **Split roles across devices**: stems/drones on one device "without groove," rhythmic/groove material on another — clean conceptual model even with one OT (use Parts/banks for the split).
