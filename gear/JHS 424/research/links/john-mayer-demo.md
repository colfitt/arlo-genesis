# John Mayer DEMOED the 424 — he is not a confirmed user

Honesty flag for the famous-users section: Mayer made one demo video; nothing indicates he uses the pedal live or on records.

## Why a demo, not an endorsement
- Mayer stepped in because JHS founder **Josh Scott had a serious mountain bike accident** and couldn't film. Scott: "John texted me after my mountain bike accident and just said he wanted to do a video because he wanted to make sure that things kept moving forward."
- He is described as making a one-off demo video during Scott's recovery — NOT as an ongoing user. Treat as "Mayer demoed ≠ Mayer uses."

## Mayer's actual point (useful framing, not a settings tip)
- "A revolution [is] taking place right now in guitar playing, and it has to do with dynamics." Modern players are "plugging into things that aren't tube amplifiers" (USB interfaces), which respond differently and change how you play.
- "This is a whole new dynamic approach. What you do with this pedal is up to you; it is not so much a paint color, but a whole different brush." (i.e., the 424 is a touch-/dynamics-reactive device, not a fixed tone — play into it.)
- Guitar used: not specified in the article (other coverage suggests a new PRS "Dead Spec" Silver Sky — unconfirmed detail).
