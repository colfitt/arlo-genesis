## Control mapping (corroborates manual/JHS)
- Three gain controls: **Gain 1 = trim**, **Gain 2 = channel**, **Volume = master**.
- **Low / High EQ** (Bass / Treble) for further tone shaping.
- "all work in tandem for dialing up your desired tone."
