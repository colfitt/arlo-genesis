# Guitar Chalk — original Boss CE-2 chorus settings (named presets)

Companion to the CE-2W settings page already on disk. These are for the *original CE-2* (mono, Standard-voicing only), so they apply directly to the CE-2W's **Standard (CE-2) mode**. All values on a **0–10 numeric scale**, NOT clock positions (per the article: "approximate and subject to the rest of your gear"). Rough translation: 2 ≈ 7–8 o'clock, 4 ≈ 10 o'clock, 5 ≈ noon, 6 ≈ 1 o'clock, 8 ≈ 3 o'clock, 9 ≈ 4 o'clock.

## Named presets (quoted RATE / DEPTH, 0–10 scale)

| Preset | RATE | DEPTH | Use (quoted) |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Bluesy Clean Wash** | 3 | 4 | "Warm, subtle shimmer that enhances single-coil tones with a gentle modulation" (blues/R&B) |
| **Dreamy Ambient Clean** | 2 | 8 | "Spacious, lush chorus effect perfect for ambient textures, layered pads and clean delay-based soundscapes" |
| **'80s Jangle Pop** | 6 | 6 | "Bright, pronounced modulation ideal for rhythmic arpeggios and cutting through a mix" |
| **Mid-Gain Grunge/Alt Rock Swirl** | 4 | 7 | "Thicker chorus effect with enough movement to sit well under gain" ('90s alt) |
| **High-Gain Chorus Lead** | 5 | 9 | "Aggressive, wide stereo-like chorus that thickens high-gain leads and solos" |

## Relevance to the rig
- **Dreamy Ambient Clean (RATE 2 / DEPTH 8)** = the closest CE-2-mode match to the slow oceanic / drone-bed goal; very slow, very deep, made to sit under delay/reverb (i.e. feeding the Deco → Board 2). Corroborates the CE-2W page's "Dreamy Ambient Delay Stack" but in Standard mode.
- **Mid-Gain Swirl (RATE 4 / DEPTH 7)** is the lo-fi/alt-rock "movement under dirt" recipe — useful with the Hizumitas/Colour Box wall upstream (Car Seat Headrest / Modest Mouse lo-fi cluster lean).
- Note: original-CE-2 presets only cover the Standard voicing; CE-1 Chorus / CE-1 Vibrato presets are on the CE-2W page (guitarchalk-settings.md).
