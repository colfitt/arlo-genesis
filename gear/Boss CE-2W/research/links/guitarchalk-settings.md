# Guitar Chalk — CE-2W settings & tone tips (named presets, mode-paired)

Five named presets, each with RATE/DEPTH on a **0–10 numeric scale** (NOT clock positions) plus the mode. Useful for the mode-pairing logic; translate the 0–10 values to clock face roughly (e.g. 3 ≈ 9 o'clock, 5 ≈ noon, 7 ≈ 2 o'clock, 9 ≈ 4–5 o'clock).

## Named presets (quoted RATE/DEPTH + MODE)
1. **Bluesy Clean** — RATE 4, DEPTH 5, Mode: CE-2 (Standard). Mild, classic clean chorus.
2. **'80s Stereo Chorus Clean** — RATE 3, DEPTH 7, Mode: CE-1 Chorus. Slow rate, deeper depth — the lush stereo '80s wash.
3. **Dreamy Ambient Delay Stack** — RATE 2, DEPTH 8, Mode: CE-1 Chorus. Very slow, very deep — ambient/oceanic, made to sit under delay/reverb.
4. **Gritty Indie Gain** — RATE 5, DEPTH 6, Mode: CE-2 (Standard). Moderate everything into dirt.
5. **Heavy Chorus Metal** — RATE 6, DEPTH 9, Mode: CE-1 Vibrato. Fast + very deep vibrato for dramatic warble.

## General guidance (quoted)
- "Set RATE low (9–11 o'clock) for subtle, vintage-style movement suited to rhythm tones."
- At the lowest DEPTH the chorus is so subtle you may barely notice it (matches Premier Guitar's "subliminal" / lowest-third note).

## Rig translation
- The **Dreamy Ambient (RATE 2 / DEPTH 8 / CE-1 Chorus)** preset is the closest match here to the drone/shoegaze "slow oceanic" goal — very slow, very deep, CE-1 mode, made to live under reverb/delay (i.e., feeding the Deco → Board 2).
- The **'80s Stereo Clean (RATE 3 / DEPTH 7 / CE-1 Chorus)** is the lush stereo-widener variant with more depth than Boss's own "wall of sound" numbers.
- All values are QUOTED from the article but on a 0–10 scale, not clock positions — treat as relative, not exact.
