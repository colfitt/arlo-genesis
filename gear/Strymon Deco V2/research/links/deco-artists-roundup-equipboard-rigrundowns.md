The Deco is a studio-flavor utility, not a signature-artist pedal — its user base is broad but diffuse. Below: who's documented with one, with honesty flags on source strength. (Equipboard sightings are user-submitted; treat as "spotted on the board," not "signature tone.")

## Most on-aesthetic / best-documented
- **Hammock (Marc Byrd, Andrew Thompson)** — ambient/cinematic post-rock. **CONFIRMED via Strymon's own pedalboard feature** (Deco in their chain, after the dirt). See `hammock-deco-ambient-technique.md`. The reference user for this rig.

## Documented in rig rundowns (confirmed on board, no Deco-specific settings published)
- **Myles Kennedy (Alter Bridge / Slash)** — named the Strymon Deco in his **2023 Premier Guitar Rig Rundown** (Alter Bridge, filmed Memphis 2023). Genre is hard rock, not on-aesthetic; no Deco settings given. Source: Premier Guitar rig rundown.
  - https://www.premierguitar.com/gear/rig-rundown-alter-bridges-mark-tremonti-and-myles-kennedy
- **Ben Howard** — folk/indie; Deco listed on his board (Equipboard + setups). No documented settings.

## Equipboard-only sightings (weaker — user-submitted, verify before citing)
- **Yvette Young (Covet)** — math-rock/post-rock; tapping/ambient. Equipboard lists a Deco. QC FLAG: her detailed 2023 Guitar World pedalboard walkthrough does **NOT** list the Deco (her board: EQD Warden → Beetronics Fatbee → ZVEX Mastotron → GCA Noodles → Boss OC-5 → **EAE Longsword** → **Caroline Somersault** → Walrus Julianna → delays → Meris Mercury7 + Hologram Microcosm). So the Deco may be off her current board / studio-only. Interesting overlap: she runs the same **EAE Longsword** and **Caroline Somersault** that are on Pedalxly Board 1, and organizes the whole board "by stereo… so it won't reduce things to mono" — same stereo-discipline philosophy this rig needs around the Deco.
- **Toro y Moi (Chaz Bear)** — chillwave/lo-fi; per the dossier's Equipboard list.

## Honest summary
No artist claims the Deco as a defining voice (unlike Wata/Hizumitas). For this rig, **Hammock is the only artist worth modeling**, and the modeling is about *method* (ride the knobs live, tape-on-source, feed the downstream decay) — there are no published "signature Deco settings" from any artist.
