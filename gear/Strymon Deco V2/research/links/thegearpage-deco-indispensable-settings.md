## Power-user tips from the thread
- **Slapback level trick:** "bring the VOLUME down very low and then increase the SATURATION slightly so it doesn't sound weak" — keeps a doubletracker slapback from overpowering the dry signal while preserving body. Counters the Deco's tendency to be louder when engaged (it's a gain device).
- **Expression over LAG TIME:** assign an expression pedal to the Lag Time parameter to "sweep through different effects on the fly — flanger, chorus, and delays" in one continuous foot motion. The single most-recommended live trick: one pedal walks the whole tape arc (flange→chorus→slapback→echo) under your foot.
- General sentiment: the pedal becomes "indispensable" as an always-on tape-saturation/glue voice rather than a featured effect — the doubletracker is the bonus.
