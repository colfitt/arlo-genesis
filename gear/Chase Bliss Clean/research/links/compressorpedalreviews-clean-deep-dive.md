Compression-nerd deep dive. The most concrete tips-and-gotchas source found outside the manual. Distilled:

## Concrete settings / behaviors
- **Two-stage:** stage 1 = your dialed comp; stage 2 = an always-on "black box" variable limiter catching peaks the first stage misses.
- **Dynamics sweep:** CCW–noon = comp (1:1 → ∞:1); noon–3:00 = limiter (feedback→feedforward blend); **past 3:00 = Sag** ("simulates tube overload with quirky sputtering — works better with guitar than bass").
- **PRO TIP (quoted intent):** *"Start around 10 o'clock for approximately a 4:1 ratio."*
- **Sensitivity is per-instrument-critical:** "dialing in the Sensitivity control is critical" because it drives compression AND the modulation/envelope behaviors. Watch the compression LED.
- **Attack:** 0.5 ms fastest "allows transient duck/swell with high ratios." **Custom release** (hold both footswitches → rotate Attack) covers 50–650 ms.

## Hidden / advanced (specialist notes)
- **Envelope Balance (hidden, under EQ knob):** "functions like a sidechain high-pass filter" — filters lows from the *control signal only* (audio unaffected). **Explicitly recommended for 5-string bass to prevent the low B from choking the engine.**
- **Physics:** L subtle / MID stable (default) / R twitchy. "Sag intensity tracks Dynamics knob position; Sensitivity heavily impacts response."
- **Dusty:** turns stage-2 limiter into a clipping circuit. *"It's definitely a little tricky to get dialed in"* — requires balancing Sensitivity, Wet, and Dry; trial-and-error.
- **Motion:** modulates comp amount (Dynamics = depth, Attack = rate); fades when input drops below Sensitivity threshold.
- **Noise Gate:** hidden option under Sensitivity = threshold; under Dynamics = release time.

## Parallel compression
"Blend uncompressed (Dry) with compressed (Wet) to retain articulation under extreme settings. Both controls offer significant boost." For pure compression, leave Dry at zero.

## Preset save/reset (concrete sequence)
- Save right slot: hold RIGHT switch 3 s, then LEFT 3 s. Save left slot: hold LEFT 3 s, then RIGHT 3 s.
- Reset all hidden options: toggle preset L/center repeatedly until lights blink, confirm with both switches.

## Gotchas / pitfalls (quoted)
1. "Not for the faint of heart" — needs comfort with experimentation; "many features aren't immediately obvious; manual study essential."
2. **Dusty mode balancing** is finicky (Sensitivity/Wet/Dry interaction).
3. **Single compression LED** — less precise than multi-LED competitors.
4. **Power: 9V DC center-negative, minimum 300 mA — "critical for noise performance."**
5. **CV: higher voltage or ANY negative voltage could damage the pedal.**
6. Avoid Sag on bass — "reacts better with higher frequencies."

## Verdict
"Warm, full-bodied, punchy compressor tone," works equally on bass + guitar, **"extremely quiet even with Wet/Dry boosted"** (headroom not an issue). "Not a 'need' pedal but an 'enjoy' pedal." Best as a "tonal playground."
