Aaron Rusch Guitar · Chase Bliss Onward Demo 2: Freeze · 2024-08-22

Part 2 of Aaron Rusch's series — a focused deep-dive on the FREEZE channel with the mix fully up so each control's effect is audible. (Mentions dip switches but defers them.)

Concept: you play a note, the pedal samples it; play another note and it samples that new note and forgets the previous one. There's a dip switch (MANUAL) that puts this into a manual mode — covered separately. Here the pedal is basically default except MIX is fully up so you can hear what each setting does to the Freeze.

SIZE: on the Freeze channel, the Size knob seems to control the fade-in envelope. It has an almost global impact on how the Freeze channel operates. Left at noon for this walkthrough.

ERROR (on Freeze): TIMING changes the sample size. CONDITION introduces mutes and sample-rate shifts (makes it glitchy). PLAYBACK destabilizes the playback speed and direction.

SHAPE: SUSTAIN determines how long sounds hold before fading away — significant on Freeze. Lower settings create short blips, echoes, and synthy effects; at max the sustain is infinite, holding until you trigger a resample.

Important: it really reacts to your dynamics. It's hard to articulate, but as you play it, you feel it — it reminds me of the (EHX) Super Ego a little, in that you have to learn when and how to hit a note to get it to do what you want. It's subtle, a feel thing.

EFFECT (with mix full): OCTAVE gives a high or low octave. At full mix you don't hear your actual played note; dial it back to hear some of the original attack, but the sustained piece is the octave-down. The knob is not a hard on/off — set around 9 o'clock it's partially octave-down and partially your original note; same going up.

TEXTURE: the grit / digital rate-reduction distortion. Cranked to the left (rate reduction) you get some really synthy sounds. The overdrive (right side) is one of my favorite settings on the pedal — it gives a warmer, darker sound.

ANIMATE toggle: right = chorus (a slow chorus), left = vibrato. Vibrato is impacted by the Size knob — if Size is big, the vibrato slows down.
