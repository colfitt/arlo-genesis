# Onward — signature artists & how they use it (honest roundup)

Coverage is genuinely thin: the Onward shipped June 2024, so there is **no artist who "owns" it as a signature voice** (no Wata/Hizumitas equivalent). Most sightings are photo/promo-sourced. Ranked by how *instructive* the use is for this rig, not by fame.

## Tier 1 — actual documented technique
- **Courtney Swain (Bent Knee)** — CB's own vocal demo, the most instructive use captured. Freeze = sustained base, Glitch = rhythmic top voice; MANUAL mode for control; reads named factory presets. Full breakdown: `courtney-swain-onward-vocals-demo.md`. https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gzP8yXCznRk
- **A Last Picture From Voyager (ALPFV)** — ambient project that wrote a whole Onward track, "Beautiful Errors," embracing the ERROR section as the engine. Closest aesthetic match to this rig. Full breakdown: `alpfv-onward-beautiful-errors.md`. https://alpfv.bandcamp.com/album/chase-bliss-journey
- **CB design team (Tom Majeski / Joel Korte / Zach Bramble)** — the official walkthrough is effectively the canonical "how the designer uses it" reference (Error Blend secret mode, lock-to-follow, fade/sustain → synth shaping, per-channel stereo errors). Transcript: `research/transcripts/chasebliss-onward-walkthrough-official.md`. https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ez2TyzuWOLs

## Tier 2 — confirmed on board, technique not documented (photo/promo-sourced)
- **James Blake** — Onward seen in a Vault.fm studio post (per Equipboard); identifiable by the enclosure + faceplate art. Fits his ambient/degraded-texture production language, but **no interview or settings** — visual sighting only. ⚠️ technique unverified. https://equipboard.com/pros/james-blake
- **Chris (Christopher) Vanderkooy (Peach Pit)** — Onward on his board per Equipboard; also runs CB Thermae. Indie/jangle context, not ambient — shows the pedal's "for all styles" reach but less rig-relevant. ⚠️ no settings published. https://equipboard.com/pros/christopher-vanderkooy
- **Balsac the Jaws of Death (GWAR)** — Onward visible in a Premier Guitar rig rundown (~27:50). Metal/theatrical context; notable mostly for breadth. ⚠️ no settings.
- **Austin Hull** — Onward on his board in a "My DREAM Home Studio Tour" video (~19:32). ⚠️ no settings.

## Takeaways for this rig
1. The two genuinely instructive uses (Swain, ALPFV) both converge on the **Freeze-bed + Glitch-top, errors-embraced** approach — which is exactly the drone/shoegaze + "recorded-wrong" target. Lean on those, not the celebrity sightings.
2. No artist has published a copyable dip/knob recipe. The closest copyable values come from Swain reading factory presets (SIZE ~3:00 / MIX full = sprawling ambient) — see the recipe-sources file.
3. ⚠️ Whole list is indicative, not exhaustive; Tier 2 is photo/promo-sourced. Treat as "who's been seen with it," not "who has a signature Onward sound."
