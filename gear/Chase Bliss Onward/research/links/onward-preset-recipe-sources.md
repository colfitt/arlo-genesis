# Where to get Onward settings recipes & shared presets

The honest headline: there is **no large public library of named Onward dip/knob "recipes" with copyable values** the way some older pedals have. Coverage is thin and young (pedal shipped June 2024). The recipe material that DOES exist lives in three places, ranked by usefulness:

## 1. "Presets for Chase Bliss" iOS app — the real community preset hub
- **Unofficial, fan-made** (developer **Laurens Lamberts Software**) — its own copy: *"Unofficial fan-made app. Not affiliated with or endorsed by Chase Bliss."* Despite that, it is the de-facto community preset exchange and CB has tacitly tolerated/promoted it.
- Supports the Onward (incl. the "Onward Prism Edition" variant added in v1.1.3). Sends settings to the pedal over **Bluetooth** (needs a CB MIDIbox / Bluetooth bridge in the chain).
- **Community library is the payoff:** *"Share your presets with the world. Explore new sounds and possibilities… by viewing presets others have shared."* You can favourite presets, follow creators, and read public notes attached to each preset. This is where copyable Onward dial-settings actually accumulate.
- Workflow for this rig: browse Onward presets in-app → audition → push to the pedal → save to one of the 2 onboard slots / 122 MIDI slots. Pairs with the CB-stack MIDI scene-recall already documented (`Gear/Chase Bliss Blooper/research/links/cb-stack-preset-scene-recall.md`).
- ⚠️ Third-party app; preset quality is user-submitted and uneven. Treat shared presets as starting points, not gospel.

## 2. Chase Bliss's own published starting points (the manual)
The most authoritative "recipes" are the manual's named starting-point patches — already transcribed in the dossier §13 (Instant Eno, Sub Synth, Auto-Repeater, Living Pad/Dancing Drone). Those are CB-authored and the highest-confidence values to copy. Not re-derived here; see `Onward-DeepResearch.md` §13.

## 3. Named factory/demo presets surfaced in CB's own videos
Courtney Swain's vocal demo (see `courtney-swain-onward-vocals-demo.md`) reads several **named factory presets** off the pedal/app while playing — concrete, CB-authored, and copyable in spirit even if exact knob graphics aren't shown:
- **(a sprawling-ambient setting)** — *"size to 3:00, mix all the way… this setting will turn any sounds that you play into a sprawling ambient composition."* That is a copyable two-knob starting point: **SIZE ~3 o'clock, MIX full wet.**
- **"frantic company"** — *"you will be greeted with a wild burst of energy whenever you play a note; in between you will have the opposite, a soft infinite freeze."* (Dynamics-extreme: loud glitch on attack, infinite freeze pad in the gaps.)
- **"a classic repeater effect"** — *"simple and infinitely loop-able"* (straight Glitch repeater).
- A fourth **"special one"** — two contrasting textures at once: *"so much rhythm coming out of one side and then one side's just kind of giving you something to stand on"* (Glitch rhythmic + Freeze bed).

## Forums (real, but mostly not copyable recipes)
- **The Gear Page** has the largest Onward thread (`threads/chase-bliss-onward-pedal.2559936`, 39+ pages) — but it's now behind a paywall/anti-bot wall (tollbit redirect, 402). Lots of impressions, few transcribable dial-settings.
- **The Gear Forum** (thegearforum.com) "NEW CHASE BLISS ONWARD PEDAL" thread also exists but 403s to scraping.
- **Patchstorage** has no Onward *preset* files, but hosts **WAYWARD**, a ZOIA recreation of the Onward glitch section (see `wayward-zoia-onward-glitch-clone.md`) — useful as a control map, not a recipe.

## Getting-started dip-switch advice (community/CB consensus)
- **Start with all dip switches OFF except LATCH** if you find the dynamic re-triggering unpredictable. Add SPREAD for stereo width, ½ SPEED for longer/lo-fi glitch, DUCK/SIDECHAIN for pumping. (Aligns with manual; dossier §2.)
- For the hot GK-5 / VG-800 line source in this rig: tune the hidden **Sensitivity** (on SIZE) toward LESS so the banjo/per-string transients don't over-trigger (dossier §6).

⚠️ Unverified-value flag: the Courtney-demo preset names are confirmed by audio/voice in CB's own video, but the *exact* dip/knob graphic for each was not on screen — treat the quoted SIZE/MIX values as the only hard numbers.
