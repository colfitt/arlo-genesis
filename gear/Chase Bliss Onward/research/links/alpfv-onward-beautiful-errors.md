## The release
- CB **generously supplied ALPFV the Lossy and Onward**; combined with the artist's already-owned **MOOD and Blooper**, this became a 4-track EP, **"Chase Bliss Journey" (June 26, 2025)**, with **one track built around each pedal** and a behind-the-scenes video for each:
  - **"Beautiful Errors" → Onward** (2:40)
  - "Transmissions" → Lossy
  - "Golden Memories" → Blooper
  - "Autumn Light" → MOOD
- "Beautiful Errors" contains a **Carl Sagan voice sample**; EP mastered by Hugo Paris.
- **Track title is the tell:** "Beautiful Errors" = built on the Onward's **ERROR section** — embracing the random timing/condition/playback glitches as the compositional engine rather than fighting them. This is precisely the "degraded / recorded-wrong" aesthetic on the rig sheet.
