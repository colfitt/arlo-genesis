# Blissprint (Synlyfe) — a second Chase Bliss preset-documentation tool

A web/Windows **visual pedal-parameter editor** for Chase Bliss pedals (in development,
v1.5). Pick a pedal (Onward is on the list), match the on-screen graphic to your physical
settings — *"tweak the knobs, latches, dipswitches, and customize the Hidden Settings as
well"* — then save/share it as an **image preset**. Free, unofficial, not affiliated with CB.
A documentation/sharing layer rather than a Bluetooth-push tool (that's the other app,
"Presets for Chase Bliss," already on disk).

## Honest status — no copyable Onward recipes here yet
- The dedicated **Onward community thread** (`itch.io/t/3752101/onward`, opened by Synlyfe
  2024-05-14) is **empty of submissions** — *"Share your presets for Onward below!"* with no
  posted presets and ~250 views. No knob values to capture.
- A "Wombtone (Billy Strings)" community post exists but that's a **different pedal** (the
  Billy Strings Wombtone phaser), NOT an Onward preset — false lead.
- ⚠️ Note: Blissprint does **not run correctly on mobile** currently.

## Why it's still worth a file
It's the second community Onward-preset hub (alongside the "Presets for Chase Bliss" iOS app).
The iOS app remains the de-facto exchange; Blissprint is a clean way to *render/share* a found
recipe as an image and supports the Hidden Settings explicitly. Worth re-checking later — if the
Onward thread fills up, it becomes a live source of copyable community dial-settings.
