Chicago Music Exchange · Yvette Young Plays The Somersault Lo-Fi Modulator | CME Gear Demo · 2019-07-19

[Music / Applause]

Hi, my name is Yvette, I play in the band Covet, and I am checking out the Somersault Lo-Fi Modulator by Caroline.

I'm new to square wave, so I think I definitely go with the more traditional way of setting it. [The square wave] was cool though — while I was playing with it I got like this cool delay thing that kind of sounded like a ticking clock.

My favorite design on the pedal is — I guess I like the little running guy. I don't know what he's running from, but I relate to him, or her.

[Music]

I like subtlety, but sometimes I like to just go ham with the OFFSET. I really do like that kind of modulated seasick sound when it's offset, so I really enjoy how you can tweak that — you can make it like kind of a subtle warble, but you can also make it like you're on a boat about to throw up. I like both the settings for different reasons; I guess whatever fits the part.

I really enjoyed playing through this pedal — it kind of transported me. I feel like when I write music I always kind of want to take someone somewhere, and this was full of nice retro tones. I was kind of reminded of my childhood, watching TV shows and hearing the theme songs and whatnot.

[Music]
