NI makes the polished, "official-feeling" MPC Expansions. Two are dead-on for the drone/doom/shoegaze-adjacent lo-fi/ambient aesthetic — **BUT they are MPC OS 3 expansions and DO NOT list the MPC Sample as compatible.** Capturing them as both a recommendation (great sounds) and a compatibility flag.

## Drift Theory (LA beat scene / experimental lo-fi)
- "Boundary-pushing, lo-fi edge to hip hop, inspired by the experimental energy of the LA beat scene. Saturated drums, dusty synths, warped keys, and vocal textures... analog hardware grit and cassette character... full of swing, texture, and attitude."
- Contents: **50 MPC Kits, 42 instrument presets, 3 MPC Projects, 303 drum samples, 34 loops, 400+ one-shots.**
- **Compatibility: "designed specifically for standalone Akai MPC workflows and requires MPC OS 3.5 or above."** → **This is the AC50's blocker.** The MPC Sample does NOT run MPC OS 3.5; it runs its own firmware. The structured kits/presets/projects will not load natively on the Sample. (URL: native-instruments.com/.../expansions/drift-theory/)

## Faded Reels (soulful / ambient lo-fi, w/ MSXII)
- "Fuzzy chords, cozy keys, dusty drums, and pastel textures... recorded using a mix of live instruments, classic hardware and boutique outboard effects, all sounds resampled to tape or hardware samplers for maximum warmth and soul... soulful hip hop, chilled-out beats, or ambient lo-fi soundscapes."
- **Compatibility (listed hardware): MPC Key 37, MPC One+, MPC X Special Edition, MPC Key 61, MPC One, MPC Live III, MPC X, MPC Live.** **The MPC Sample is NOT in the list.** → won't load as a kit on the AC50. (URL: native-instruments.com/.../expansions/faded-reels/)

## Verdict for the AC50
- **Do not buy NI MPC Expansions expecting them to load as kits on the MPC Sample** — they target MPC OS 3.x hardware/software.
- **Workaround if you already own them on a desktop MPC license:** the underlying one-shots/loops are WAVs; you could copy the raw audio (drums/one-shots/loops) onto the Sample's microSD and rebuild kits by hand. You lose the prebuilt kit mappings, presets and projects, but the *sounds* (Faded Reels' tape-resampled warmth especially) are exactly the rig's flavor.
- For a turnkey AC50-ready alternative in the same lane, use the **MPC-Samples "MPC Sample Edition"** packs (Dirty Drummer, Underground Crates) instead — see mpc-samples-sample-edition-expansions-catalog.md.

NOTE: NI may add Sample-compatible editions later (young product). Re-verify the compatibility list on the product page before purchase.
