Pins down the factory-preset claim for the patch list (the box's own kits = the "factory" tier).

## What's confirmed (vendor/press, not a per-kit list)
- **"Over 100 factory kits"** included (Chuck Levin's, Mixdown).
- **~2 GB of samples**, "spanning genres including **hip-hop, funk, house, jazz** and more"
  (Mixdown, MusicTech). One-shots + loops + "stock drum kits and loops."
- Hardware backing the kits: **60 onboard effect types across 4 FX engines**, 32 stereo voices,
  16 poly-aftertouch pads.
- Boots into a **demo project** you can finger-drum immediately (manual, Tutorial chapter:
  "a sample demo project loads automatically").
- Manual confirms the box ships with **"hundreds of different samples, from individual drum and
  instrument hits to full drum breaks"** browsable via SAMPLE SELECT → ENCODER.

## Honest gap
No public per-kit name list exists yet (March-2026 product). The "factory" patches in this gear's
patch sheet are therefore the **factory FX/processing presets and the demo-project structure**,
not named kit recipes — Akai hasn't published a kit index. If a named-kit list surfaces in a later
firmware/manual, add it here.
