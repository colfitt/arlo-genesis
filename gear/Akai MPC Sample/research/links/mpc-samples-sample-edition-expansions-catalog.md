## How they install on the AC50
Use the **"MPC Sample Edition"** folder in the download → SD Card Access → copy to a folder on the microSD → load from the SD location. (See mpc-samples-mpc-sample-expansion-install.md.)

## FLAGS
- **Always confirm a pack lists an "MPC Sample Edition."** Packs labeled only "for all modern standalone MPCs, Force & MPC Software" target **MPC OS 3** and ship `.xpm`/`.xpn` programs that the AC50's separate firmware does not browse the same way — they won't load as kits. Worst case you can still load their raw WAVs manually.
- Storage: park these on the microSD, not the (non-USB-mountable) 8 GB internal.
- Counts/edition availability change as MPC-Samples adds Sample Editions to its catalog — re-check at purchase time.
