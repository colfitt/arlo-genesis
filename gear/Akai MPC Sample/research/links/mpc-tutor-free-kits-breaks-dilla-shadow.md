## AC50 compatibility
These are raw WAV samples / generic MPC program kits. **The WAV breaks and one-shots load on the AC50 with zero issues** (drop on microSD, load to pads). The "MPC program kit" (`.xpm`-based) layouts are built for MPC OS 3 and won't auto-load as kits on the Sample's separate firmware — but you can still import the underlying WAVs and rebuild the kit by hand, or just chop the breaks (which is what you'd do on the AC50 anyway).

## Why it matters for the rig
The Dilla-style and Shadow-style free kits + royalty-free vintage breaks are the cheapest way to (a) get authentic boom-bap source material onto the box and (b) practice the lineage technique (unquantized swing, micro-chopping) the dossier §7 and the artist files describe.
