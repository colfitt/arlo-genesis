AC50-SPECIFIC but LOW SIGNAL. The Gearspace MPC Sample thread is mostly first-impressions and banter rather than hands-on tips — flagging that honestly. The few substantive points:

- Several users want it precisely BECAUSE it's a stripped-down "drum sampler, less DAW-in-a-box" alternative to the full MPC. One Live II owner: "it's just way too much machine for the simple sample-based stuff I like to make. Will definitely be trying this." (Confirms the AC50's niche = simplicity, which is exactly its role in this rig.)
- Hopes expressed early on for "a graphical waveform editor and a quite capable sequencer" — both of which the shipping unit delivers (waveform editing + the genuine MPC 960-PPQ sequencer).
- A user noted gen-1 / non-touch-slider MPC devices "can't even use the crossfader function in the OS" — i.e. no DJ-mode crossfader on hardware like this without a touch slider. Consistent with the AC50 having a fader but no crossfader/DJ mode.
- Cosmetic ribbing: the "palm rest" is actually a thumb rest; several called the look "Fisher Price."

For real power-user tips/gotchas, the dedicated MPC-Forums.com "MPC Sample" subforum (f=50) is far higher signal than this thread; the keygroup/no-chords complaints that surfaced here are captured in detail in the mpcforums-ac50-vs-alternatives-limitations.md file.
