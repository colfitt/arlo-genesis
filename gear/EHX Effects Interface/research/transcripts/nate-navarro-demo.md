Nate Navarro · Electro-Harmonix Effects Interface Hardware Plugin | Demo by Nate Navarro · 2026-04-16

Nate Navarro's short demo, bass-focused, covering both directions of the bridge.

CONCEPT. Bridges the gap between computer plugins and outboard gear: bring your pedals and other gear into your DAW and use them just like a plugin, OR bring your computer plugins seamlessly to your live rig "to achieve your favorite studio tones real time in a live setting."

PEDALBOARD-INTO-SESSION (Hardware Plugin Mode) WORKFLOW. Starts with a simple bassline for an electronic track and wants to turn it into "a big synthy texture." Loop the physical pedalboard into the session: connect the EHX Effects Interface to a computer over USB, then connect its INPUT to the pedalboard OUTPUT, and its OUTPUT to the pedalboard INPUT. "The pedalboard is in." Adds POG 3 (octaves), an EHX Battalion (bass preamp/dirt), and Bass Clone chorus to the bass part — real pedals processing the DAW track.

PLUGIN-AS-PEDAL TO LIVE RIG (Pedalboard Mode). Has been enjoying heavy bass-distortion plugins like Abyssal Bass from Aurora DSP. "With the EHX Effects Interface I can use the Abyssal plugin just like a pedal and send it to my live rig." (The plugin becomes a stompbox on the physical chain/amp.)

WHY IT MATTERS — AUTOMATION. His favorite aspect: combining all his favorite pedals and outboard gear with digital plugins AND tapping into the power of computer automation, which "brings a whole new dimension to my live performances." Specifically he can add intricate noise gating, automatically engage and bypass effects, and precisely control delays, reverbs, modulations, and pitch shifting "in ways that tap dancing across my pedalboard could never come close to."

VERDICT. "Incredibly useful device — it really improves my workflow and opens up so many new capabilities for my live sound." As crossover between outboard gear and digital plugins grows in both studio and live environments, the device brings utility and convenience. (Short demo; ends with isolated bass parts.)
