WATA'S CANONICAL STACK:
- "Her typical approach is to hit the Hizumitas with a BOSS DS-1." (DS-1 distortion stacked WITH the fuzz = the documented chaos recipe.)

STACKING RECIPES TESTED (drives IN FRONT of the Hizumitas):
- JHS PackRat, Empress Para EQ MkII, EQD Special Cranker, EQD Plumes, Boss SD-1 Waza Craft — each pushing the Hizumitas into "extreme high-gain distortion that can only be described as brutal and savage."
- Gain-staging logic: a lower-gain OD/boost (Plumes, SD-1, Special Cranker) in front tightens and slams the Sustain stage; a RAT-voiced drive (PackRat) adds grind; a para-EQ (Empress) in front lets you pre-shape what the fuzz saturates.

DOCUMENTED WEAKNESS (important gotcha):
- "the Hizumitas would need some sort of EQ or mid-hump pedal in the mix to help expand its versatility." I.e. pair with an EQ/mid pedal AFTER to restore/sculpt mids the dense wall can mask. (Maps directly onto Brothers AM-after in the user's chain.)
