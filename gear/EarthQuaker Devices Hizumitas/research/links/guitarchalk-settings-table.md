- Art-fuzz / experimental texture — Fuzz 8, Tone 2, Level 5

- Caution noted: high Fuzz + high Tone simultaneously may oscillate/hiss.

INTERPRETATION CAVEAT: Note this page's Tone numbering does NOT clearly account for the Hizumitas's INVERTED tone knob — on the real pedal, low Tone numbers = treble/mid-forward (CCW) and high = bass (CW). Their "doom = Tone 4" and "metal = Tone 7" only make sense if "higher number = more bass." Cross-reference EQD's own quoted positions (eqd-noodlers-settings.md) over this table when they conflict.
