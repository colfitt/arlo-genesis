- Confirms unity gain ~10 o'clock; cleans up with guitar volume (esp. treble-bleed guitars) — unusual for a Muff.

ANDY BASSIST — BASS (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DXC2dfQlwPI · 2023-03-03):
