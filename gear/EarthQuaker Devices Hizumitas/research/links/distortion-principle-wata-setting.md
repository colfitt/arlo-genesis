A demo that lands on a concrete, copyable "best" setting and attributes it to Wata. (Full transcript: ../transcripts/distortion-principle-most-aggressive-big-muff.md)

RECOMMENDED SETTING (the video's takeaway, quoted near the end):
- "Dial it to nine/ten o'clock on the Volume, put everything else at one o'clock."
  → Volume 9–10, Sustain 1 o'clock, Tone 1 o'clock.
- Attribution: "It's really not too far from where I saw Wata had hers set in a video — she had hers at noon but mine's at one o'clock, so it's basically her setting." So the WATA-DOCUMENTED reference is ~noon on Sustain and Tone (slightly less bass than 1 o'clock), Volume at unity ~9–10.

OTHER POSITIONS SHOWN:
- Tone fully CCW (treble) = "scooped, lots-of-high-end, more like a Big Muff." Tone CW ~3/4 = big low end. Tone "like a RAT knob, in reverse."
- Sustain min = "fuzzy overdrive" / crunch; Sustain max = much more low end.

STACKING:
- EQD Plumes IN FRONT of Hizumitas = his "go-to metal setting." Demoed clean (EVH 5150 clean ch.) and broken-up (Orange TH30).
