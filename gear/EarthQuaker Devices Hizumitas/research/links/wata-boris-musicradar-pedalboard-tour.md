Wata's touring pedalboard, from EQD's own video tour. Best source for board context around the Hizumitas.

PEDALS ON HER BOARD (verified list):
- Fuzz/dist: Hizumitas (EQD signature), MASF Watafuzz (her OTHER signature fuzz, Foxx-derived), Dwarfcraft Shiva, Mattoverse AirTrash, Boss DS-1 distortion.
- Mod/time/glitch: EQD Hummingbird tremolo, Aqueduct Tremolo, EQD Avalanche Run (stereo reverb+delay), Bananana Mandala glitch pedal, EHX Freeze.
- Roland Space Echo (vintage tape echo) mounted shoulder-height on her right so she can tweak it mid-song.

NUANCE / CORRECTION TO LORE: This article says the Hizumitas was "painstakingly recreated from Wata's original Elk BM" and that she "still uses an original unit" — slightly softer than the Guitar World "replaced it on the board" framing. Honest read: she tours the Hizumitas as her main, keeps the Elk as a reference/backup. Both true depending on date.

BOARD LAYOUT: Volume + wah on the right side; EHX Freeze on the left (used to cue bandmate Takeshi). No per-pedal settings published.

PRICE: $149 / £159 (US street).

USE NOTES: She carries MULTIPLE fuzzes and swaps per song — Hizumitas is the main Big-Muff-wall voice; Watafuzz is the gnarlier/octave-ish texture; DS-1 is the stacking distortion for chaos.
