# EarthQuaker Devices Hizumitas

**Summary** — Fuzz sustainar inspired by the Elk BM Sustainar — the rare Japanese muff variant used by Wata of Boris. Big, sustaining, doomy wall-style fuzz with a Filter knob that re-shapes mid character from scooped to thick.

## Why I have it
Sustain manufacturer for banjo on Board 1. Banjo decays in 1.5 seconds — Hizumitas completes the note. Pairs with the MXR 108 (raw vintage fuzz) by covering the long-sustain wall territory that 108 can't reach on its own. Also doubles as a doom-wall fuzz for the shoegaze scenes.
