# Factory presets + official stacking recipes (the dip-recipe core)

Chase Bliss's own preset descriptions and stacking guidance — these are the concrete "what to set, why" recipes.

## The 4 factory presets (what each demonstrates)
- **THE ANALOG MAN** ("Mike's Sound") — a recreation of **Mike Piera's own preferred King of Tone settings**, **HI GAIN 1 on**. A solid always-on starter. (chasebliss.com: "ships with our favorites, including a recreation of Analog Man's own preferred settings.")
- **SUNNY SKIES** — "a mixture of everything Brothers AM has to offer — **treble boost into distortion into overdrive**" — bright, focused, charged. Shows the full 3-circuit series stack.
- **2-IN-1** (Bank 2) — a performance combo: **Channel 2 is the always-on overdrive; Channel 1 is parked to boost it beyond its limits.** Kick Channel 1 on when you want more bite. (This is exactly the rig's "always-on OD + footswitchable push" template.)
- **BAD BROS** (Bank 2) — **HI GAIN 1 + HI GAIN 2**, everything stacked and cranked: "as big and brash as Brothers AM can get," an overflowing distortion.

Bank 2 (2-IN-1 / BAD BROS) is exposed via the **BANK** dip.

## Official stacking recipe — boost into boost
"A versatile, loud, and open-sounding drive can be created by setting **both channels to BOOST** and using **Channel 1 to push Channel 2**." Chase Bliss recommends **turning on the MASTER dip while exploring boost-stacking**, which lets you turn up **VOL 1** (the push amount) without disruptive volume jumps. This is the canonical fix for the VOL-1 level-snap trap.

## Manual stacking modes (named)
The manual's "Stacking Ideas" are **OVERLOADER / EXPANDER / COMBO** — all progressively crank **VOL 1** to push Channel 2 past where it goes alone (series push, not a parallel blend — the AM has no blend knob).

## Treble booster (bonus circuit)
"A treble booster, inspired by the **Beano Boost** by Analog Man. Push your amp to new heights, enjoy biting textures that will cut through any mix." Stacking all three circuits = "Brash, bright, and charged." Has a **huge low-cut** and is **fixed at the front of the circuit** (linked to Channel 1).

## Rig relevance
2-IN-1 is the literal model for "always-on tightener + footswitchable push into the Longsword." Both-channels-BOOST + MASTER dip is the clean re-leveling recipe after the Hizumitas.
