TKOG's own usage guide for the Oxford Drive — the authority on controls, voicings, and clipping modes.

## What it clones
- "replicates the circuit of a certain shred-tastic high-gain distortion pedal from the 1990s" (= Marshall ShredMaster, 1991), "but with more gain, more volume, bonus clipping options, extra voicings, flexible soft-touch true bypass, and top-mount jacks, all in a small 1590B enclosure."
- Inspirations also drawn from the "Gubernatorial" pedal of 1988 (= Marshall Guv'nor) and a shield-shaped-enclosure ShredMaster variant used by 2000s NYC garage-rock bands.

## Voice switch (NOTE: official now lists THREE voices)
- **'91** — "darkest, offering a focused distortion sound with a powerful mid-range punch"; accurately replicates the 1991 ShredMaster.
- **'88** — "crisp tone, perfect for rhythm playing," with input filtering to reduce the harsh upper-mids of the original Guv'nor.
- **'01** — "brightest by far, offering an aggressive, cutting distortion sound" (the 2000s NYC garage variant).

> VERSION FLAG: site shows V2.2 with the '01 voice. The repo's physical unit is a late-2021 two-voice V2 whose manual documents only '91/'88. Confirm against the pedal's toggle: 2-position = two-voice; 3-position = '01-equipped build.

## Clipping modes
- **Si (silicone)** — stock; "compressed and focused tone with plenty of overtones." Tightest.
- **LED** — "more 'open' sound, well-suited to rhythm playing," higher output, more opamp clipping; "at lower gain settings, it may even sound more like a boost." (Inspired by LED clipping in the Guv'nor / Drivemaster.)
- **Asymmetrical** — "silicone/LED hybrid, which has plenty of dynamic range while still providing the warm, fat sound associated with asymmetrical clipping."

## Contour (the key gotcha)
- "Contour controls the overall EQ shape... adjusting both the treble and mid frequencies. More mids and less treble to the left, less mids and more treble to the right." It is NOT a mid knob.
- "The amount of treble is highly dependent on the setting of the contour control, so the treble control will likely need to be readjusted when the contour control is adjusted."

## Bass
- Logarithmic taper; bass becomes much more noticeable past a certain point — run it LOW for normal-OD bass cut.

## Bypass / footswitch
- Soft-touch true bypass: footswitch with no resistance and no click; HOLD = momentary, TAP = latching. Default on/off state selectable at power-up.

## Format / availability
- 1590B enclosure, top-mount jacks. Sold in small numbered batches via store.thekingofgear.com.
