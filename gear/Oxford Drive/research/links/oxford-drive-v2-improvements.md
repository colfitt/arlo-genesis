Distills the V2-vs-V1 changes — the gotchas/known-issues that the dossier flags as FIXED on this rig's unit.

## What V2 fixed / added
- "The circuit board was totally redesigned for lower noise."
- "Upgraded high quality C0G capacitors... prevent the occasional microphonic issues that some Oxford Drive V1 pedals experienced." (V1 used ceramic caps that could be microphonic.)
- Added **flexible soft-touch bypass**: footswitch with no resistance and no audible click; HOLD = momentary, choice of latching/momentary by how long you hold.
- Added the **voice switch** (V1 had none).
- Same "soft touch" knobs as found on vintage UK gear (cosmetic nod to the ShredMaster lineage).

## Gotcha context for this rig
- The repo's V2 is PAST the V1 microphonic/oscillation problems — those were V1-era issues, not relevant here.
- Output is hottest in LED mode; large available volume boost — easy to slam a downstream pedal (e.g. the BF-3) too hard.
