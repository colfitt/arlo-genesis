Okay, so this is The King of Gear at the Brooklyn Stompbox [Exhibit] with a collector//emitter, and we're going to demo our two new prototypes. One is a filter pedal and one is a drive — let's start with the filter.

It's an envelope filter with modulation, so when you dig in the filter opens up, and then when you let it ring out the modulation swells and gives you a nice reviving/evolving sound. You have controls for the base frequency of the filter, for resonance (so you can get pretty freaky), you've got sensitivity for the envelope, and then you have three controls that set the LFO rate, depth, and shape.

[Filter musical demo.] If you cancel the sensitivity you just kind of have a [static filter sweep].
