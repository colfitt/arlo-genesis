# Where Big Time recipes / settings shares actually live (coverage map, June 2026)

Honest state of the world for a pedal that's **weeks old**: there is **no community recipe ecosystem yet** — no Reddit/TGP/Gearspace patch threads, no recipe blogs, no Discord recipe shares, no third-party preset packs. Searches for user patches return only the official docs and launch-news rewrites. So the "shared recipes" are, for now, **first-party only**:

## Where the real recipes are (ranked)
1. **The 10 factory presets** — the official starting-point library, names + intent quoted in `cb-big-time-factory-presets-recipes.md`. Because Automatone faders fly to saved positions, **loading a preset and reading the sliders/buttons is the recipe.** This is the single best source.
2. **Official manual "State and Limiter" + "Color and Preamp" + "Modes" sections** — the dial-in mechanics (STATE×TEXTURE, COLOR/FEEDBACK gain structure, MODE "try this" tips). Captured in the factory-presets file.
3. **Superbooth 26 walkthrough w/ John Snyder** — per-control function + live tour moves; captured at `research/transcripts/sonicstate-superbooth-john-snyder-eae-interview.md` (incl. VOICING filter frequencies and STATE×TEXTURE technical detail).
4. **EAE lineage** — how prior EAE delays (Sending, etc.) inform Big Time's voicings/states; see `eae-lineage-artists-and-design.md`.
5. **Owner discussion (thin, no recipes yet):**
   - Elektronauts thread: https://www.elektronauts.com/t/chase-bliss-big-time-analog-digital-delay/249961 (started ~Apr 30 2026; early hype, no settings shares captured — page returned no recipe content).
   - The Gear Forum: https://thegearforum.com/threads/chase-bliss-big-time-collab-with-eae.11307/ (collab discussion).
   Watch both for the first real owner patch shares as units ship.

## Design-intent quotes that frame how to USE it (manual Foreword/Overview, John Snyder)
- "Big Time is a strange entanglement of analog and digital circuit blocks. I don't think it could have come from either of us on our own."
- The thesis: "the sounds are shaped by how filtering, compression, and carefully tailored preamps evolve feedback loops over time" — i.e., **the recipe IS the feedback-loop evolution**, not a static patch. Dial COLOR/FEEDBACK/STATE/TEXTURE for how repeats *change*, not just how they start.
- "The processor in Big Time delays and diffuses audio while also dynamically adjusting the surrounding analog circuitry in response to that same audio… a strange loop of causality." → many states (Compressed, #!&%) are **playing-reactive**; the recipe includes *how hard you play*.
- "The anything-is-possible of digital / The everything-sounds-good of analog." · "Big Time is our foolish return to doing things the hard way, so that you can sound very good, and do whatever you want."
- "more wild and dangerous than any Chase Bliss pedal" (re: the misbias/runaway behavior — mind WET).

> Bottom line: until owner shares appear, build from the 10 factory presets + the STATE/TEXTURE + COLOR/FEEDBACK rules. Re-check the two forum threads in a few weeks for the first community recipes.
