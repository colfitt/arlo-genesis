## Framing
"It's as intricate as a tape delay, with the fidelity of a studio rack delay, and the complexity of a pedalboard full of small Chase Bliss pedals. ## Navigation (player's quick version)
- Six sliders control six things; **hold the far-left (shift) button** and they control six more — "like having 12 sliders" — and they fly right back when you exit, so it's not confusing.
- Each arcade button has four modes, labeled right above it (VOICING changes the delay's voice; MOTION changes how the modulation moves).
- A footswitch changes presets and the sliders jump to where they belong. "That's pretty much it, except for MIDI, aux, and expression — from there you just explore."
