# Chase Bliss Big Time

**Summary** — Chase Bliss × EAE Automatone stereo delay (motorized faders) — hybrid analog/digital, models early-'80s rackmount delays; 4 mode ranges (mod → short → long → loop), MIDI clock. High draw (~1 A; ships with a current-doubler cable).

## Why I have it
The Chase Bliss × EAE Automatone — a motorized-fader stereo delay that models the mixed-up analog/digital circuitry of early-'80s rackmount units, with an analog preamp at the input and a clipping limiter inside the feedback loop. It's the primary delay on the End board (2nd on Board 3, after Generation Loss, into the MOOD/Blooper stack), and its four mode ranges (Mod → Short → Long → Loop) let one box cover chorus/flange texture, lo-fi tape echo, ambient diffusion, and looping. It earns its place because the degradation is *performed* — play gently and repeats stay clean, dig in and they crumble — which is exactly the dynamics-reactive, stereo-diffuse character the ambient/lo-fi/post-rock work wants, without duplicating the dedicated reverb (H90) already on the board.

## Signature uses
- **MOD mode (3–46ms) as the chorus/flange/resonator zone** — not a delay but a modulation engine: SCALE Off + Sine MOTION = chorus/flange/tape warble; pull DRY to zero for true vibrato (factory #1 COMPRESSED CHORUS territory).
- **Broken Dynaflange (MOD + #!&% misbias + Env MOTION)** — a flanger that starves and sweeps with playing dynamics, only crumbling when you dig in (factory #5 territory).
- **COMPRESSED / "Blue Mode" state** — the mis-calibrated-limiter character borrowed from the Lexicon PCM42: high COLOR bonks the echoes into the limiter for ducking, self-oscillating walls.
- **CLUSTER thickening** — blends in extra taps + diffusion (spiritual nod to the Ursa Major Space Station) as a stereo widener and "reverb but not quite" ambient space from delay taps.
- **LOOP-mode overdub performance** — frozen playback bed, then re-engage OVERDUB to write progressive disintegration into new layers (Saturated/#!&% bite only on the overdub pass; VOICING Warm/Analog carves always-live).
- **Self-oscillation via the in-loop limiter, steered by TILT EQ** — ride FEEDBACK with Saturated/misbias and the analog limiter saturates the runaway into musical oscillation; TILT inside the feedback loop governs decay (boost lows = longer tail), hold MODE ~2s to panic-reset.
