# Notable / verified Iridium users (HONEST)

The Iridium is a ubiquitous studio/fly-rig DI, not a signature-artist pedal — but unlike the dossier's "no marquee user surfaced" note, there ARE several VERIFIED named touring users. None has a tone "named after" the Iridium; all use it as a direct-amp/FOH tool. Captured honestly:

**Chris Shiflett (Foo Fighters) — CONFIRMED.** Used the Iridium *instead of an amp* on his UK/Ireland SOLO tour (lighter rig, no backline). His own quote: "I have it set to the Deluxe Reverb because usually I'd be doing my solo gigs with my Deluxe Reverbs… they aimed that right at me because [those] are the three things that I go to; the AC30, a Marshall or an old Fender combo amp." Clean fly-rig endorsement; he leans on the Round (Deluxe) voice.
Source: https://www.musicradar.com/news/chris-shiflett-was-using-a-strymon-iridium-pedal-on-his-uk-tour-instead-of-an-amp

**Mark Bowen (IDLES) — CONFIRMED, and the most rig-relevant.** Premier Guitar Rig Rundowns (2023 + 2024) show **TWO Strymon Iridiums** handling all cab emulation on his experimental/noise board. In the 2024 rundown the pair is "hidden under the Moog units" and cabs **everything — including signal from the drummer's kit** (a real-world AMP-DISABLE-style non-guitar convolution use). Board also runs EHX 95000 looper, POG2, OBNE Minim, Vongon Paragraphs, Sequential Prophet-5, Akai MPC One+. Noise-rock/experimental context closest to this drone/shoegaze rig.
Source: https://www.premierguitar.com/videos/rig-rundown/idles-2024

**Rebecca & Megan Lovell (Larkin Poe) — CONFIRMED.** Near-identical boards (Line 6 HX Effects + Iridium + Rodenberg TB Drive). Rebecca: "As a redundancy, and to provide sonic options for our front-of-house engineer, I also have a Strymon Iridium wired into the chain… sounds absolutely amazing." Used as FOH-DI redundancy alongside their real Fender amps. Exactly the dossier's "failover + direct-to-FOH" role.
Source: https://www.guitarworld.com/features/larkin-poe-rebecca-lovell-pedalboard

**Russian Circles' Mike Sullivan — UNVERIFIED / LIKELY FALSE.** An early search hit linked his post-theft rig rebuild to the Iridium, but his actual rig rundowns show **Verellen Meat Smoke / Loucks tube heads**, not an Iridium. Do NOT attribute. Flagged as not credible.

**User profile (honest):** heavy in worship/modern-church silent-stage rigs and gigging players flying direct-to-FOH. Larkin Poe's "redundancy + FOH options" framing is the canonical pro use. No "this artist's sound IS the Iridium" claim exists.
