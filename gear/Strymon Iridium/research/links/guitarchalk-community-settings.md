# Guitar Chalk — community starting-point settings (1–10 scale)

Two Guitar Chalk articles each give five copyable preset configs. Values are on
a **0–10 dial scale** (NOT clock-face), amp = Round/Chime/Punch, "Cab/IR 1/2/3" =
the a/b/c cab toggle. Useful as community reference starting points; not artist
attributions. Honest flag: these are generic editorial presets, not gig-tested
or artist-sourced — treat as conventional voicings to deviate from.

## Notes / caveats from the articles
- "Drive control can introduce brittle highs when pushed too far" → on Chime,
  remember MIDDLE = high-cut, so raise MIDDLE to tame the fizz rather than only
  pulling TREBLE.
- The two "ambient" presets disagree on amp (Round-b-Room7 vs Chime-a-Room7):
  both are valid — Round = warmer/scooped pad, Chime = brighter/airier pad.
- Doom preset here is conservative (Drive 9/10, conventional balanced cab c) vs
  the rig's hot-rodded "Punch wall" (DRIVE past 2 o'c, bright 8x12, LARGE room).

## Relevance to this rig
- The "Dreamy Ambient Wash" (Round + cab b + DRIVE low + ROOM 7) and
  "Ambient/Shoegaze Clean" (Chime + cab a + TREBLE 7 + ROOM 6) are the two most
  rig-aligned community presets — both feed the dry-bloom-into-downstream-verb
  approach for the SG/Jazzmaster clean-DI role.
- All Punch presets confirm the high-gain doom direction; the rig's own Punch
  wall pushes further (past the real-Plexi 2 o'c ceiling into hot-rodded gain).
