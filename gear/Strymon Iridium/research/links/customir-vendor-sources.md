# Where Iridium owners actually get custom IRs

## THE practical constraint (repeat for emphasis)
Iridium loads ONLY **24-bit / 96 kHz** WAV, mono/stereo, ≤500 ms. Most IR packs ship 24/48 or 44.1 → must be SRC'd to 96 k or you buy the 96 k variant. Some vendors offer "96k" or "UltraRes/long" sets; check before buying.

## Factory partners (the 9 onboard IRs come from these — all sell more)
- **OwnHammer** — community calls them "a bit more up front / less polished but balanced," huge range of speakers/cabs. The workhorse vendor.
- **Celestion** (official store, digital "speaker shop" IRs) — buy the exact speaker (Blue AlNiCo, G12M Greenback, Vintage 30, etc.). Sold in multiple sample rates incl. 96k.
- **cabIR.eu** — German vendor, "worldclass authentic" cab IRs; loved on axefx.de/Fractal forums. Ships both ~170 ms and 500 ms versions and RAW format — the 500 ms RAW WAVs suit Iridium (after 96k SRC if needed). Praised: vintage Orange/pre-Rola cabs "amazingly balanced, crispy cleans, smooth crunch, no tweaking needed."
- **Valhallir.at** — and the key freebie: **proof of Iridium purchase → one free V2-cab pack from Valhallir** (Strymon's IR page states this directly). Valhallir's *ambient* cab IRs are notable: users report "even the ambient cabs add to the feel… mixing a little ambient with a close-mic'd IR is doing nice things, especially with panning" — directly useful for the rig's wide/drone aesthetic.

## Other community favorites (paid)
- **York Audio** — repeatedly called the industry standard / "best sounding IRs out there"; "balanced, full-range, smooth, polished, like a finished recording." Best for clean/jangle/ambient (the rig's clean DI use). Loved packs: MES 212 V30, KW 412 M25-SH (warm lows, vocal Greenback mids, articulate top). Confirm 96k availability per pack.
- **ML Sound Lab** — darker-sounding; better for high-gain than for clean/jangle. Offers a **free premium IR** as a sampler. Less ideal for this clean/shoegaze rig.

## Free packs (good for auditioning before buying)
- **Redwirez** free Marshall 1960A (Celestion G12M-25) pack — many mics/positions.
- **Overdriven.fr** — free Celestion cab IRs (V30, G12-65, G12-T75) incl. multiple sample rates.
- **GuitarHacks** free packs — frequently preferred over paid by hobbyists.
- **ambienttrash** (Bandcamp/Patreon) — Avatar 2x12 w/ Creamback & Greenback, explicitly ambient-leaning (paid/Patreon).

## Picking for THIS rig (clean/jangle/doom direct)
- Clean DI (SG/Jazzmaster → Round): York Audio Fender-style or a Celestion clean cab; keep it balanced/smooth.
- Jangle (Chime/AC30): a Celestion Blue AlNiCo 1x12 or York AC-style; bright, airy.
- Doom/wall (Punch): a dark 4x12 V30/G12M or an 8x12 — cabIR vintage Orange or an OwnHammer Marshall; pull TREBLE in the per-cab Info editor if it spikes.
- Stereo bloom trick: load a darker IR L + brighter IR R in one slot for a wide pad (see tips-soundonsound-poweruser.md).
