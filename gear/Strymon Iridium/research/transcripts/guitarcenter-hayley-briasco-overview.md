Guitar Center · Strymon Iridium Amp & IR Cab Effects Pedal | Demo and Overview with Hayley Briasco · 2023-11-15

Concise, clean official-retailer overview/DI demo (Hayley Briasco, guitarist/MD for Clairo). ~6 min. Recorded as a straight DI out of the Iridium — a good plain-language confirmation of the cab list, per-amp cab preferences, and the compact travel/DI pitch.

## Format
- Demoed DI STRAIGHT OUT of the Iridium (no real amp) — the headline use case. Three amps, each with three cab IRs, plus dial-in room tone.

## Per-amp + cab notes (her preferences, as a quick reference)
- ROUND (Deluxe Reverb): nice mid scoop. Cabs — a) 1x12 Deluxe Reverb, b) 1x12 Blues Junior, c) 2x10 Vibrolux. She prefers CAB C (the 2x10) here, then cranks DRIVE a bit and adds room.
- CHIME (AC30): cabs — a) 2x12 AC30 Alnico, b) 1x12 Alnico, c) (Mesa half-back). She prefers CAB A for the nice drive; adds a little room.
- PUNCH (Plexi): "incredibly powerful for its [size]." Cabs — a) 4x12 (GNR/Marshall), b) 2x12 Vintage 30s, c) 8x12. She prefers CAB A; adds room tone.

## Wrap-up / why it's a travel + DI box
- Loves the compact size — fits in a backpack, take it anywhere.
- Includes a HEADPHONE jack (silent practice / latency-free monitoring).
- MIDI/connectivity capabilities called out as a plus.

## Relevance to this rig
- A clean confirmation of the factory cab roster and the "DI straight out, no amp" workflow that defines the Iridium's role here. Her per-amp cab picks (Round=c, Chime=a, Punch=a) are reasonable default starting points; note these differ from the doom "Punch wall" recipe (Punch + cab c, the bright 8x12, + large room) — her picks favor a more conventional balanced voicing.
