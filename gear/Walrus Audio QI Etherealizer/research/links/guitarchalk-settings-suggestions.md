Five named copyable patches for the Qi Etherealizer. Knob values are on a 0–10 scale (0 = full CCW / off, 10 = full CW / max). Note: "Mix" = the Mix/Dry master blend knob; "Dry" appears as a separate listed value (their interpretation of the Mix/Dry control). "Delay" listed alongside Time/Feedback appears to mean the Delay *Mix* knob. These are the author's suggested starting points, not factory presets — flag as third-party-suggested, not official. No Red/Green/Blue factory descriptions on this page.

1. **Dreamy Granular Wash** — "Smooth ambient pad with drifting grains and soft chorus"
   - Mix 7 | Dry 6 | Grain Mix 8 | Tone 4 | Space 8
   - Playback: REVERSE | Delay Mix 3 | Time 4 | Feedback 2
   - Chorus Rate 3 | Depth 6 | Freeze Off

2. **Warped Tape Echo** — "Vintage, drifting repeats with subtle grain smear"
   - Mix 5 | Dry 5 | Grain Mix 4 | Tone 5 | Space 3
   - Playback: x0.5 | Delay Mix 6 | Time 5 | Feedback 5
   - Chorus Rate 4 | Depth 7 | Freeze Off | Delay subdivision: Quarter

3. **Watery Chorus Drift** — "Clean, floating chorus with subtle granular wandering"
   - Mix 6 | Dry 6 | Grain Mix 3 | Tone 6 | Space 4
   - Playback: RNDM | Delay Mix 2 | Time 3 | Feedback 1
   - Chorus Rate 5 | Depth 8 | Freeze Off

4. **Glitch Pulse Delay** — "More aggressive, rhythmic grain stutters with synced delay pulses"
   - Mix 8 | Dry 7 | Grain Mix 7 | Tone 6 | Space 5
   - Playback: x2 | Delay Mix 7 | Time 5 | Feedback 4
   - Chorus Rate 6 | Depth 4 | Freeze Off | Delay subdivision: Eighth

5. **Freeze Pad Texture** — "Long, lush pads using the Freeze switch with dense space and smooth grain clouds"
   - Mix 9 | Dry 4 | Grain Mix 7 | Tone 4 | Space 10
   - Playback: REVERSE | Delay Mix 2 | Time 2 | Feedback 1
   - Chorus Rate 2 | Depth 5 | **Freeze: On**
