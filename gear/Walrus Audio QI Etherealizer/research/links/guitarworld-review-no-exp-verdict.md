## Confirmed limitations (community-relevant gotchas)
- **NO expression pedal input** — stated outright. Continuous/sweep control is MIDI CC only. (Corroborates the dossier's call to fix GearProfile `expression: false` — and there's NO jack at all.)
- Grain is "one effect too far for some players" — i.e. plenty of buyers use it as a chorus/delay/reverb box and ignore the grain. Validates running the Qi conservatively in this rig if the Microcosm already owns the glitch.
- "Significant financial investment" ($449 / £449) — the price is repeatedly flagged as the main barrier.

## Routing nuance (IMPORTANT for stacking — new detail)
- In BOTH Series and Parallel, **reverb is the FINAL stage.** In Parallel, chorus/delay/grain are processed independently off the dry input, **summed, and THEN hit the reverb.** So Parallel tames how the engines bleed into *each other*, but the Space reverb still sits across the whole summed wet bus at the end. Practical consequence for Qi → Dark Star: Parallel keeps grain/delay discrete, but you STILL must keep Space modest because it's downstream of everything regardless of Flow mode.
