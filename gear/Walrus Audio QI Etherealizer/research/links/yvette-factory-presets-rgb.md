**RED PRESET — "Great for freezing & making granular pads"** (directly quoted)
Freeze LED shows red in the diagram. This is the freeze/granular-pad voicing — the headline workflow.
Knob positions read from diagram (approximate clock face):
- Flow: **PARALLEL** (parallel LED lit)
- Mix/Dry: ~10–11 o'clock
- Grain Mix: ~1 o'clock | X: ~1 o'clock | Playback: ~x1/x2 region
- Grain mode toggle: **GRAIN CLOUD**
- Delay Mix: ~noon | Time: ~1 o'clock | Feedback: low (~8) | subdivision **QUARTER**
- Chorus Mix: ~9–10 (subtle) | Rate: ~10 | Depth: low | mode **TRI**
- Tone: low (~7–8 o'clock, darkened) | Space: low (~7 o'clock)
- Use: dial a chord, short-press Freeze to capture a granular pad, play over it.

**GREEN PRESET — "THE Chorus tone."** (directly quoted — note the emphatic "THE")
Walrus-audio LED shows green. This is Yvette's flagship chorus voicing — the one she signs off on as the chorus.
Knob positions read from diagram:
- Flow: **PARALLEL** (parallel LED lit)
- Mix/Dry: ~noon–1 o'clock
- Chorus Mix: HIGH, ~2 o'clock (the dominant engine) | Rate: ~2 | Depth: ~1
- Grain Mix: minimum/off (~7 o'clock) | Delay Mix: low-moderate (~1) 
- Tone: ~10 | Space: low (~7 o'clock)
- Chorus mode toggle: TRI/STEREO (diagram ambiguous; the lush "THE chorus" descriptor points to Tri)
- Use: the down-to-earth, always-useful lush chorus. Grain essentially off.

**BLUE PRESET — "Great for randomized moments to freeze and play over."** (directly quoted)
Both Freeze LED and Walrus LED show blue (grain-freeze + reverb-pad armed). The randomized-playback freeze voicing.
Knob positions read from diagram:
- Flow: **PARALLEL** (parallel LED lit)
- Mix/Dry: higher than Red, ~noon
- Grain Mix: ~1 o'clock | X: mid | Playback: randomized region (RNDM) — matches the "randomized moments" description
- Delay Mix: ~1 | Time: ~1 | Feedback: low | subdivision QUARTER
- Chorus Mix: ~1 | Rate/Depth: mid
- Tone: ~9 | Space: low-moderate
- Use: freeze a randomized grain moment and improvise over the unpredictable result.

Reset note: factory reset (hold Bypass+Freeze while applying power) restores these three Yvette presets and Trails mode.
