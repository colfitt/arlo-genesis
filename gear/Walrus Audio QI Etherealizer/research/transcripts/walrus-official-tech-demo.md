Distilled notes (what this video covers, cross-confirmed against the Mad Steex and Pedal Collaborative transcripts and the manual):
- Walks the four engines left-to-right (Chorus, Delay, Grain, Reverb/Space) plus the global Mix/Dry, Tone, and Flow controls.
- Demonstrates Flow button: Series ("Forward") = Chorus -> Delay -> Grain -> Reverb -> Tone -> Dry; Parallel = the three effects run off the dry input in parallel and sum into the always-last Reverb.
- Shows the Freeze footswitch: short-press = freeze grain buffer to loop (blue LED); long-press = ramp Space reverb to 100% (green LED).
- Shows Tap/Osc hold = delay self-oscillation (feedback to max).
- Demonstrates Grain Cloud vs Phrase Sample and the Playback rotary (x1 / x2+oct / x.5-oct / REV / RNDM).
- Stereo demo throughout (the pedal is true stereo in/out).
