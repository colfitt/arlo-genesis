## Controls, verbatim gist (the load-bearing part)
- Reverb modes: **Spring / Plate / Hall.** The **Reverb knob controls the mix** of the reverb.
- Delay modes: **Tape / Analog / Precision** (Precision = a clean digital delay).
- **The Color and Mod knob functions depend on the selected delay mode:**
  - **Tape mode:** Color = **tape record level**. Mod = **tape type** — left = NEW tape, right = USED tape.
  - **Analog mode:** Color = **input gain**. Mod = below noon = **vibrato** modulation; above noon = **chorus**.
  - **Precision mode:** Color = **tone control**. Mod = below noon = **flanger** modulation; above noon = **chorus**.
- **The UA app gives you different *voicings* for each effect** — "this is where you can access a wide range of sounds including pitch-shifted delay effects and reverbs with different decay, EQ and pre-delay settings."

## Takeaway for settings work
This confirms the manual's control map exactly (Color/Mod are bipolar and mode-dependent; noon = mod off).
