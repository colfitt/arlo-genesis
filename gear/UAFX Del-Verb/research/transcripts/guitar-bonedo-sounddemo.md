## Spoken overview gist
- "Combination of the effect algorithms from the UAFX Echo Station and the Golden Reverberator with a simple interface and fewer controls."
- "Like all UA pedals you control the Del-Verb via the smartphone app, which offers a lot more so-called *voicings* for each of the three reverb and delay types."
- "It sounds very good right out of the box, **although you can't edit the voicings in detail** — but everyone will find a matching variant of the three algorithms." (Important nuance: voicings are pick-from-factory only; there is no per-parameter voicing editing in the app.)

## The footswitch critique (corroborates the mode tradeoff)
- "What I don't like is that you can't control just the delay bypass AND the tap-tempo option at the same time — so if you want reverb always on and just need delay for a solo… [you must trade something]."
- "The available footswitch configuration (adjustable in the app) is not well designed. I'd like to see a HOLD option on one footswitch to enter tap-tempo mode instead." (A real, repeated owner complaint: the three fixed modes don't let you have independent reverb toggle + dedicated tap simultaneously.)
- Verdict: "a great-sounding unit which maybe can even replace two pedals on your board."

## Takeaway
Secondary confirmation of the voicing system (assign-only, not edit) and the footswitch-mode limitation. No spoken clock-face numbers.
