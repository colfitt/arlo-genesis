## Del-Verb walkthrough (the useful part)
- Framing: "the love child of the Golden and the Starlight." Delay side = all five knobs (Time/Feedback/Mix/Color/Mod), three modes: **Tape Echo (EP-III), Memory-Man-inspired Analog (DMM), and Precision** (a very faithful/accurate digital delay). Reverb side = single **Reverb** knob + 3-way toggle: **Spring / Plate / Hall** ("Lexicon-ish, very beautiful"). "All the knobs do what they say."

## The voicings system — concrete facts (most valuable takeaways)
- **"~126 different voicings"** total on the unit (counted on the beta; "may vary depending on firmware/updates"). This is the hard number behind the dossier's "~12 per effect" — it's a large pool, organized per mode.
- **Per-mode banks:** "per bank you can select… right now I'm in Tape, so I can select between all these different voicings for Tape." Each of the 3 delay modes and each of the 3 reverb modes has its own voicing list.
- **Named voicing examples shown:** *"EP with octave pitched buckets"* and *"EP with a fifth pitched"* (Tape EP-III voicings that add pitched repeats — i.e. octave/5th shimmer-style delays). These are the pitched-delay voicings that turn a plain tape echo into a shimmer/harmonized echo.
- **Assignment is sticky and STORED IN THE PEDAL, not the app:** "you can select whatever voicing you prefer for each of the three modes on both delay and reverb and keep them… this is stored in the pedal, not in the app. Once you select one it stays there… it's going to be there for that mini-toggle position for good until you change it." (Critical: you can pre-load 6 voicings — one per toggle position — and they persist without the phone. This is what makes it usable on a laptop-/phone-free travel board once configured.)
- **What voicings change:** "if you want a longer reverb, longer trails, a different dwell, or change something about the delay sounds — that's what you do with the app." Confirms decay/dwell/trails-length/pre-delay/pitched-repeats live in voicings, not on the hardware. "This pedal has the most intense app feature set."

## Footswitch modes (corroborated)
- Out of the box: **Delay | Reverb** (left = delay on/off, right = reverb on/off).
- **Effects | Tap** mode: left toggles both delay+reverb, right = tap tempo.
- "Nothing keeping you from having reverb always on and the left footswitch doing just delay + tap tempo on the right" (= the **Delay | Tap** mode; reverb always on, leveled by the knob).

## Buyer framing
"UA quality, not cutting corners where it matters (sound), at a more affordable price point than the original Golden/Starlight pair" — i.e. the value pitch is two flagship pedals' worth of sound in one simpler box. Reinforces the travel/grab-and-go role.
