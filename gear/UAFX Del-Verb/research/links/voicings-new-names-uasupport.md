# Del-Verb — additional named voicings (decay/pre-delay specs)

## PLATE 140 (the missing variants flagged in voicing-list-official.md)
- **Default Plate 140** — Studio standard Plate A, ~3.0 ms (factory baseline; already in main list as "Studio standard Plate A").
- **Fast and Fat Plate** — 140 Plate A with a bit of pre-delay and pulled-back bass AND treble (darker, thicker, slightly held-back plate — good for taming a bright source like banjo while staying lush).
- **Lush Recall** — 140 Plate A with SHORT decay and **250 ms pre-delay** (the gap-then-bloom plate; pre-delay keeps the attack clear, then a contained tail — articulate ambience).
- **Present Plate** — 140 Plate A with **almost 6-second decay** and NO pre-delay (long, immediate, enveloping plate wall — the biggest plate option; strong drone/sustained-wall candidate).
- **Plate B Thick Predelay** — Plate B, **3.3 s decay, 180 Hz input filter (high-pass), added treble** (long decay but the 180 Hz HPF keeps the low end from mudding up under a baritone or bass — a cleaner big plate).

## SPRING '65 (additional variants)
- **Spring Slap Show B** — Spring Tank B with **100 ms slapback-style pre-delay** (a built-in slapback gap before the spring drip — rockabilly/surf slap reverb in one toggle; pairs with a short Precision slap for a doubled vibe).
- **Spring Ghostly C Vibrato** — Ambient Tank C with custom **vibrato AFTER the reverb** (a moving, detuned, ambient spring — the modulated/spooky spring, distinct from the pitch-modulated "Symphonic Reverb"; surf-ambient hybrid).

## ANALOG DMM (note)
- Page confirms DMM voicings include **higher-octave repeats on the DMM** (an octave-up pitched BBD variant), and that Color = input gain is editable.
