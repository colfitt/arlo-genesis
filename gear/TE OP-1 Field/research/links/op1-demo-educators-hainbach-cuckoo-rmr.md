# OP-1 Field demo/educator artists + their signature techniques & patch packs

These three are the loudest documented OP-1 / OP-1 Field voices and the people whose patches/techniques actually circulate. (Shane Becker deliberately excluded — owned by another agent.)

## Hainbach (Berlin; tape/test-gear artist)
- Calls the OP-1 a **"super Casio SK-1"** — i.e. he prizes its lo-fi sampler character, not fidelity.
- Workflow: samples are recorded to **tape machines + mics FIRST**, then sampled into the OP-1 — pre-degrading the source before it ever hits the box.
- Tape-loop composing rule: he **writes parts imagining how they'll sound at half-speed**, because "it always sounds different… often better." → compose for the varispeed/half-speed result, not the original pitch.
- Sampled a **24-piece orchestra** and treats those fragments in the OP-1. Did the Perfect Circuit OP-1 Field demo/review.
- Directly relevant to this rig's "recorded-wrong"/generation-loss aesthetic: degrade before sampling, then degrade again on tape.

## Cuckoo (Mr. Cuckoo / cuckoomusic; YouTube + Patreon)
- The OP-1's longtime explainer; widely recommended patch source (op1.fun user `cuckoomusic`, Patreon).
- Signature packs (concrete, named):
  - **"OP-6"** — 40 synth patches **sampled from a Sequential/DSI OB-6** (analog poly) into the OP-1 sampler engine. The whole pack is "play an OB-6 through the OP-1."
  - **"Opines Collection"** — 15 patches, almost all on the **DR Wave engine** with a chiptune/"Nintendoish" character (see dedicated capture `op1fun-cuckoo-opines-collection.md` for per-patch engine/octave/FX/LFO).
  - **NES / 8-bit** preset sets.
- Technique signature: heavy use of the **sampler engine to clone other synths**, plus quirky chiptune voicing.

## Red Means Recording — Jeremy Blake (YouTube)
- Top OP-1 content creator; makes whole songs start-to-finish on the box and detailed tutorials.
- Sells his personal patch library as **"RMR OP-1 Pack: Volume 1"** (Gumroad) — the contents he actually used on his channel + albums (see `op1fun-rmr-pack-vol1.md`).
- Also sells **"Dreams"**, a dedicated OP-1 **Field** preset pack (pads/ambient).
- Technique signature: full-arrangement-on-tape, layering synth + sampler + drum into a finished sketch in one sitting — the canonical demonstration of the OP-1 "couch instrument" workflow.

## Why this matters for the rig
Three different blueprints: Hainbach = degrade-before-and-after (drone/texture); Cuckoo = sampler-as-synth-cloner (turn the VG-800/banjo into a playable engine); RMR = finish-a-whole-sketch-on-tape. All three are directly applicable to the OP-1's "front of the funnel" role here.
