Distilled interview. NOTE: this interview does NOT name the OP-1 Field or specific synths — it's background/philosophy, useful for understanding the person behind the workflow. No gear quotes available.

## Background
- Songwriter / musician / producer from **Egg Harbor Township, NJ**. Primary instrument is **drums**; also plays guitar, bass, keyboard.
- Audio engineer at Fresh Start Church (Egg Harbor Twp) for ~5 years.
- Self-taught engineer since 7th grade — started recording himself in **Audacity** to avoid paying engineers, then expanded.

## Indian Run (the solo project)
- His personal creative outlet. Quote: "I don't write these songs for really anyone but myself and that's why I keep it a solo project."

## Cedar Room Studio
- Built by converting his parents' loft into a recording studio.
- Stated mission: "The goal of the studio is to provide high quality recordings for musicians on a small budget, like myself."

## Relevance to OP-1 workflow
- Explains his DIY, self-contained, do-everything-myself ethos — which maps directly onto the later "OP-1 Field as the entire DAW" approach: one person, one box, finish the track.
- "Cedar Room Studio" is the room you see in his OP-1 reels.
