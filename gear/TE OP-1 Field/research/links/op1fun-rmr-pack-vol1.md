# RMR OP-1 Pack: Volume 1 — contents (a major shared preset pack)

Jeremy Blake's (Red Means Recording) personal OP-1 patch library, "used in the creation of the songs on his channel, as well as multiple albums." Paid (Gumroad). Detailed load instructions included.

## What's in it
- **24 drum kits**
- **14 pitched drum-kit instruments** (drum-sampler kits tuned to be played melodically across the keys)
- **124 synth-engine presets**, organized by: bass, fx, keys, leads, pads, synths
- **34 sampler-engine presets**, organized by: bass, keys, leads, pads, pocket-operator sounds, samples, synths

## OP-1 Field compatibility flag (important)
- The pack predates / targets the original OP-1. Community reports **up to ~7 of the synth patches do not load / are not compatible with the OP-1 Field** — they use engine/parameter combinations the Field changed. Everything else loads. This is the single clearest real-world example of original-OP-1 → Field patch incompatibility.

## Also from RMR
- **"Dreams"** — a dedicated OP-1 **Field** preset pack (ambient/pad focused) sold separately on the same Gumroad.

## Note
Per-patch parameter values aren't published on the sales page; like all .aif patches you'd dump the JSON locally to read `knobs`/`adsr`/`fx_params`. The value here is the **category breakdown** (pads/leads/bass/fx split, pitched drum instruments) and the **Field-incompatibility data point**.
