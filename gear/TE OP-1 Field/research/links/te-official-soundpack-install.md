# Official TE sound packs + verbatim install steps

Teenage Engineering hosts free official **sound packs** for the OP-1 Field on this page. The page also gives the canonical install procedure, which applies to ANY patch/pack (op1.fun, RMR, etc.), not just TE's own.

## Verbatim install steps (quoted from the page)
1. "connect OP–1 to your computer using usb."
2. "hold shift and press com, then press T4 to enable an mtp connection." (this puts the Field in content/disk mode and mounts it as a drive)
3. "download the sound pack to your computer and unpack it."
4. "drag and drop the sound pack folder from your computer on to either the drum or synth folder in OP–1 field." (drum kits → `drum/`; synth/sampler presets → `synth/`)
5. "when the transfer is complete press T4 again to eject OP–1 field."
6. "go to synth or drum and load your new sounds."

## Notes
- The Field exposes exactly two patch destinations: the **`synth/`** folder and the **`drum/`** folder. Choosing the wrong folder = the patch won't show up under the right mode.
- No SD/expandable storage — everything is internal; you manage it over USB in this MTP/content mode.
- `shift`+`com`+`T4` is the magic chord to enter disk mode; `T4` again ejects.
