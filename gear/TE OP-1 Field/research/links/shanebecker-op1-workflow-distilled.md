## The one-line thesis
He uses the **OP-1 Field as the DAW** — the central arranger/sequencer/sampler that the whole track is built on and through — while a small set of external mics, synths, and boutique pedals feed sounds INTO it. He says "OP1 field as the daw" almost every post; it's his signature framing.

3. **Mic real instruments straight into the rig.** Two mic approaches:

4. **Play external synths for chords/bass/keys**, sampled/recorded in:
