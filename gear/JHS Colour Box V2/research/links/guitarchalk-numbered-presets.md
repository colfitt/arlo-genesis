Guitar Chalk · "JHS Colour Box V2 Pedal Settings Suggestions" · (no date given)

LOW-CONFIDENCE / USE AS DIRECTIONAL ONLY. Five numbered presets on a 0–10 dial scale (NOT clock-face). Caveat: the page labels the input switch "Line/Mic" — the actual hardware switch is INST/XLR, and "Phantom Power: Off" is listed as a control (it's a pass-through, not a switch). These inaccuracies suggest the presets are partly generic/AI-assembled. Treat the relative directions (which way to push gain vs. EQ for each character) as the useful signal; treat the exact numbers as approximate. STEP listed as 1–5 in #1/#2/#4/#5 but as "8" in #3 (impossible — STEP only has 5 positions), reinforcing the caveat.

1. **Clean Console Sparkle** — Master 7 · Pre-Vol 4 · Step 2 · Bass 6 · Mid 5 · Treble 7 · Hi-Pass 2 · Pad Off · input "Mic"
2. **Vintage Console Overdrive** — Master 4 · Pre-Vol 8 · Step 5 · Bass 6 · Mid 7 · Treble 5 · Hi-Pass 1 · Pad On · input "Mic"
3. **Lo-Fi Chill Texture** — Master 5 · Pre-Vol 6 · Step "8" (→ read as 5/max) · Bass 3 · Mid 4 · Treble 3 · Hi-Pass 6 · Pad On · input "Line"
4. **Studio-Style Scoop (Funk/Pop)** — Master 6 · Pre-Vol 5 · Step 3 · Bass 7 · Mid 3 (scooped) · Treble 8 · Hi-Pass 2 · Pad Off · input "Mic"
5. **Gritty Direct-In Bass** — Master 3 · Pre-Vol 9 · Step 6 (→ read as 5/max) · Bass 6 · Mid 8 · Treble 5 · Hi-Pass 0 (off) · Pad On · input "Line"

USEFUL DIRECTIONAL TAKEAWAYS (these match what the video demos show):
- Clean/sparkle = low STEP (2), low PRE-VOL (4), MASTER high (7), gentle treble lift.
- Console overdrive = high STEP + high PRE-VOL + MASTER trimmed back, mids pushed.
- Funk/pop = scooped mids (3) + bright treble (8) + bass lift.
- Gritty bass DI = high PRE-VOL (9), MASTER low (3), mid-forward (8), no high-pass.
