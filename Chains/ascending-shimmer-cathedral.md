# Ascending Shimmer Cathedral — banjo lead over a frozen fifth ⭐

A heavenly octave-up shimmer wall on the End board — the classic pitched-cathedral bed, frozen so the banjo reads as the lead in front of it. This is a 🟣 DOUG-designed integration; no artist used this exact pedal chain, the Taste-ref names the sound it chases.

## Signal path

Banjo (GK-5) → **VG-800** (clean banjo-as-lead) → **CB Clean** → [Board 1] → Board 2 bypassed/light → [Board 3] → **Big Sky** (Shimmer, frozen) **OR** **H90** (Shimmer, Pitch-Freeze) → tape.

## Per-unit

- **VG-800 — #10 Open G Five-String** (5th muted, banjo-style) or the EBM5-BANJO GK profile (#1) into a clean E.GUITAR model — keep it bright and trackable so the shimmer's pitch engine locks.
- **CB Clean — #5 Banjo Transient Tamer** (slow attack so the pluck spikes through then clamps; Envelope Balance high to track the bright register).
- **Big Sky — #8 "Stardust" (ascending shimmer, REGEN, FREEZE)** (Shift1 OCT 5th, Shift2 +OCT, Decay 13.7 s) — or **#7 Shimmer Cathedral** with the **MIX-11:00 banjo-as-lead tweak** so the wall sits behind the line. Use **#25 Swell "Dry Mode"** as a feeder to erase the banjo pluck if you want pure pad.
- **H90 (alternative engine) — #7 Shimmer / Pitch Wall** with **#9 exp-pedal → Pitch Decay → Freeze** (the staff move that rings shimmer drones out reliably), or **#8 Shimmer Subtle Octave Wash** (+1 AND −1 oct) for a gentler version.

## Routing

Stereo. Pick **one** shimmer engine per song (Big Sky is the bench failover, H90 the everyday — don't stack two shimmer verbs). With Big Sky **FREEZE**, notes on top come through dry — so place a reverb/delay **before** the Big Sky (the H90 or Big Time) to give the banjo lead its own space (Big Sky wash-on-wash fix #1, sheet #4). Or **freeze + looper-capture** after the Big Sky (#5) and switch patches over the captured bed.

## Sound

A rising, glassy octave-up cathedral that hangs forever, with the banjo's bright fingerpicked line cutting through in front of it.

## Play

Play a chord → freeze the shimmer (Big Sky HOLD = FREEZE / H90 Pitch+verb Freeze on a footswitch) → solo the banjo over the locked fifth. For a backwards entrance, swap to **H90 #13 Reverse Reverb** with a volume-swell attack.

**Taste-ref:** post-punk-texture-dynamics / shoegaze pitched walls; the Big Sky sheet's banjo-lead shimmer-bed brief.
