# Frozen Grain Wall — MOOD Stretch + Reverb, played-over ⭐

One frozen grain held into an infinite drone you build a whole intro/outro on — the sustained-wall workhorse, all from one pedal's two channels, then widened. This is a 🟣 DOUG-designed integration; no artist used this exact pedal chain, the Taste-ref names the sound it chases.

## Signal path

Baritone / banjo → **CB Clean** → [Boards 1–2 light] → [Board 3] → **MOOD MkII** (Stretch grain + Reverb freeze) → **Chroma Console** (re-expand + light tape) → **H90 / Big Sky** (hall, low Mix) → tape.

## Per-unit

- **VG-800 — #29 ORGAN → Blooper Capture Drone** (steady waveform, no rotary) — a dead-steady drone is the best stretch/grain source.
- **CB Clean — #6 Baritone Drone Sustainer** (limiting ceiling + slow release = a sustained bed).
- **MOOD MkII — #18 Stretching 101 + Frozen Grain Wall** (Stretch: both MODIFY+LENGTH max, rotate MODIFY toward **noon to FREEZE** a single grain; LENGTH sizes the held grain) layered with **#12 Freeze Pad** on the Wet Reverb channel. Save it to **Slot L "Frozen Grain Wall"** with **#29 CLASSIC** dip on for grit. This is the **Perry Frank Reverb+Stretch "sprawling epic"** (#32).
- **Chroma Console — #13 Ambient Diffusion Bed** (the re-expander) + a light **#2 Subtle Tape Glue** (keep CASSETTE light — don't double the tape if Gen Loss is running).
- **H90 — #6 Blackhole Freeze Drone** at low Mix, or **Big Sky #3 "For Days"** (max-decay Cloud) as the failover — one diffuser only.

## Routing

MOOD's **SPREAD width collapses into the mono Blooper/Chroma path** — keep wide intent for after the Chroma re-expansion. Use **MOOD LATCH** dip so the Freeze holds hands-free. Re-check MOOD **LEVEL BALANCE (#26)** between the two channels (they're not auto-balanced).

## Sound

A grainy, smeared, infinitely-held wall — granular fuzz at the edges, a steady organ drone at the core — that you play a live melody over.

## Play

Hold a chord → tap MOOD's Micro-Looper to capture → **HOLD LEFT FS** to freeze the reverb into a pad → rotate MODIFY to noon to lock a single stretched grain → play over the held wall. Slowly turn **CLOCK down (#2)** for a degrading, detuning drift as it sustains.

**Taste-ref:** post-punk-texture-dynamics — sustained-crescendo drone walls (Swans-adjacent); the MOOD sheet's "sprawling epic" / sustained-wall workhorse.
