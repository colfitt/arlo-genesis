# Prismizer Halo Bed — pitched harmony cloud around one voice ⭐

The rig's approximation of the Bon Iver / Prismizer vocal halo — a pitched harmony cloud hovering around a single held banjo/voice, with a played diatonic voice underneath. A halo bed, flagged honestly as the result, not the method. This is a 🟣 DOUG-designed integration; no artist used this exact pedal chain.

## Signal path

Banjo (GK-5) → **VG-800** (one played diatonic harmony voice) → **CB Clean** (present) → [Board 1] → **QI Etherealizer** (Prismizer-halo grain-freeze cloud) → **Dark Star V3** (dual-pitch shimmer pad) → [Board 3] → **H90** light hall → tape.

## Per-unit

- **VG-800 — #31 Banjo Halo Harmony Drone** (`ALT TUNE MODE = HARMONY`, `+1 oct` or a USER-SCALE diatonic 3rd/5th in the song's key; `NORMAL MIX ~30%` keeps the dry banjo under the harmony) — the closest the rig gets to the Prismizer's *played* harmony (one fixed interval).
- **CB Clean — #5 Banjo Transient Tamer** — keep the source present and articulate (the "dry voice" in front of the cloud).
- **QI Etherealizer — #27 Prismizer Halo Freeze Cloud** (Flow Parallel, Grain Cloud, Playback **x2** +1 oct bright prism-up layer, Chorus Tri Rate 9 Depth 11 = the long-glide slur stand-in, Space 10–11:00). Play a chord → **short-press Freeze** = the grain cloud loops as a pitched cloud.
- **Dark Star V3 — #29 Halo Shimmer Pad (dual-pitch glide)** (Pitch 1 +1 oct L / Pitch 2 detune just off unity R via the "a in Dark" trick; Crush ~12 clean, Filter ~1 HPF glassy, Mix mostly wet but UNDER the dry voice) — two fixed pitched voices echoing the halo's pitched copies.
- **H90 — #8 Shimmer Subtle Octave Wash** (+1 AND −1 oct, low Mix) to seat the cloud, optional.

## Routing

Stereo, QI **Parallel** (live banjo present on top, like the dry vocal). Pad/reamp the VG-800 line into the QI. Space modest on the QI; Dark Star owns the width. For the "715 hit-it-harder" distortion, push the VG-800 / Board-1 source hot before the (padded) input.

## Sound

A held banjo note surrounded by a pitched-up harmony cloud and two chorused octave voices — a "choir of one banjo," with the dry played line and a fixed diatonic harmony underneath.

## Play

Play the harmony-voiced banjo chord → short-press QI **Freeze** to lock the pitched cloud → solo a banjo lead over it; the Dark Star adds the two fixed pitched copies. For the genuine article, run the in-the-box **Antares Harmony Engine (PRZ-TB1, index-only)** on the banjo instead — the pedals only approximate it live.

**Approximation flag:** the real Prismizer is a MIDI-played, formant-shifted, polyphonic 4-note harmony cloud; the QI gives a fixed/drifting pitched cloud, the Dark Star two fixed voices, the VG-800 one played interval — all chase the RESULT, not the METHOD. No artist used this pedal chain.
