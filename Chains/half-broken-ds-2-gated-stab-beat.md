# Half-Broken DS-2 Gated Stab Beat

The deliberately spitty, gated, "keeps-breaking" demo-stab texture — used as a rhythmic element where the choke IS the groove, locked to a glitch beat. A 🟣 DOUG-designed integration. This is the chain that deliberately crosses the JHS 424 Gain-2 ~3:00 gate (the one documented threshold).

## Signal path

banjo/baritone/VG-800 → CB Clean → JHS Colour Box V2 → JHS 424 (gated) → PORTA424 → tape. (Octatrack/MPC glitch beat clocks it.)

## Per-unit

- **CB Clean** — **#3 Tight & Lively** (fast attack/release) so the stabs stay tight before the gate.
- **JHS Colour Box V2** — **#4 Honk / Telephone (mid-stacked radio voice)** (all three SHIFTs to midrange, boosted) for the brittle telephone stab, OR **#2 Console Crunch** to pre-shape.
- **JHS 424** — **#14 Half-Broken DS-2 Gated Crunch (424-LF2)** — Gain 1 1:00, **Gain 2 4:00 (PAST the 3:00 gate)**, Bass 10. Pick attack and note-offs do the gating = the half-failing dirt box. **NOT for sustained drones** — that brittleness is the whole point here.
- **PORTA424** — **#1 Always-On Cassette Print** (light, 18V) — because the 424 is the stage destroying, keep the PORTA424 clean (the one-stage-destroys rule).

## Routing / sync

**This is the chain that deliberately crosses the JHS 424 Gain-2 ~3:00 gate** (the one documented threshold — cited on the 424 sheet). Because the 424 gates hard, **keep the PORTA424 clean** so only one stage destroys. Mono. Octatrack/MPC runs the glitch beat as clock master; the gated stabs are played or sequenced in time so the choke lands on the grid.

## Sound

Tight, aggressive, spitting gated crunch — single notes and chord stabs crack lo-fi then choke off, brittle and percussive, sitting like a broken-drum-machine element inside the beat.

## Play

Program or play stabs on the grid; the note-offs gate them. Pair under the Octatrack **#34 / OT-E3 Postal-Service-style un-pumped glitch beat** or the MPC **#31 Glitchy Beep-Beat** for the clattery, dry-forward bed. Use as a section element, not a whole song (it chokes sustain).
