# Washed-Out Ambient Guitar

Guitar -> **Chase Bliss MOOD MkII** -> **Hologram Microcosm** -> **OBNE Dark Star V3** -> **Strymon Big Sky**

## Signal Flow Rationale

1. **Chase Bliss MOOD MkII** (granular texture source)
   - Wet channel on Slip mode to smear incoming notes into drifting micro-loops
   - Drolo side on Stretch for time-warped grain clouds
   - Clock slow; Mix mostly wet — strips attack and creates the initial wash

2. **Hologram Microcosm** (granular layering)
   - Mosaic or Haze engine to scatter the already-softened signal into shimmering fragments
   - Activity knob moderate so grains overlap without chaos
   - Adds melodic movement and spectral shimmer on top of the MOOD's smear

3. **OBNE Dark Star V3** (pad reverb + pitch)
   - Pitch knobs to +1 octave / -1 octave for organ-like depth
   - Crush low, Filter slightly rolled off high-end for lo-fi haze
   - Freeze footswitch to capture and sustain a pad underneath live playing

4. **Strymon Big Sky** (final space)
   - Cloud or Shimmer algorithm, Decay long, Mix ~60-70%
   - Pre-delay short to keep things diffuse rather than distinct
   - Acts as the master "room" that glues everything into one enormous wash

## Tips

- Roll your guitar's tone knob down to ~4-5 for extra softness at the source.
- Use volume swells (volume knob or expression pedal) to remove all pick attack.
- MOOD's freeze/loop can hold a bed while you layer new phrases through the rest of the chain.
