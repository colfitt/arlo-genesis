# Crap-Amp Demo Grind

A dry, mid-forward, demo-grade rhythm grind — old-pedal-into-a-practice-amp character on baritone/VG-800, under a groovebox kick, printed to four-track. A 🟣 DOUG-designed integration (EAE Stormbringer voice re-pointed at the cluster; no artist used these boxes).

## Signal path

baritone/VG-800 → CB Clean → Longsword → JHS Kilt v10 → [End] CB Generation Loss → PORTA424 → JHS 424 → tape. (Digitakt/MPC kick clocks the part underneath.)

## Per-unit

- **CB Clean** — **#7 Always-On Huge Fuzz Feeder** so even quiet picking triggers the downstream grind evenly (or **#6 Baritone Drone Sustainer** if the baritone needs taming first).
- **Longsword** — **#LSW-LF1 Crap-Amp Vintage Grind (Stormbringer-lean)** (Level/Drive ~1–2:00, Mid boosted at 1 kHz, DIODE silicon, SHIFT up). EAE's own "old distortion pedal into a practice amp" voice. Keep it DRY.
- **JHS Kilt v10** — **#KLT-LF1 Telephone Demo-Crunch (thin/console-overload)** (Gain 10–11:00, Tone 1:00, Low-Cut up = deliberately thin/telephone). Pushes the warts audible.
- **CB Generation Loss** — **#30 Brittle Mid-Forward AM-Jangle Bed (GL-LF2)** (Model 7–9, thin/narrow) for the small-speaker demo character, or **#1 Subtle Tape Age**.
- **PORTA424** — **#12 Bedroom 4-Track Demo Print** (9V, trashy) — the 4-track grit terminus.
- **JHS 424** — **#13 Demo-Is-The-Song 4-Track Grit (424-LF1)** (Gain 1/2 12:00, under the gate) → Apollo.

## Routing / sync

Mono throughout (baritone/VG → mono board → mono tape). **Don't crank both tape boxes** — split the destruction: if PORTA424 is at 9V/trashy, keep 424 light, or vice versa. Groovebox is clock master (MPC or DT2); the grind part is played live over it, not sequenced.

## Sound

Thin, honking, mid-forward demo crunch on the baritone — the sound of a half-broken dirt box into a small amp, captured to cassette. Sits as a rhythm bed, not a lead.

## Play

Set the grind, play the rhythm part dry over the groovebox kick. For a brittle stab section, swap Kilt for the gated path in the Half-Broken DS-2 Gated Stab Beat chain. Ride instrument volume for the clean↔hair dynamic (the Kilt cleans up beautifully with the guitar volume).
