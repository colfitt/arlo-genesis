# Holy-Trinity Granular Pad — Microcosm → QI → Dark Star ⭐

The rig's headline lush ambient bed — a glacial granular wash that blooms into a wide stereo reverb-pad you solo a banjo lead over. The Board-2 texture stage at full song. This is a 🟣 DOUG-designed integration; no artist used this exact pedal chain, the Taste-ref names the sound it chases.

## Signal path

Banjo (GK-5) → **VG-800** (Organ/Crystal pad) → **CB Clean** (always-on) → [Board 1 stereo send] → **Microcosm** (Haze grain-wash) → **QI Etherealizer** (frozen grain cloud) → **Dark Star V3** (stacked-octave reverb terminus) → [stereo to Board 3] → **H90** light hall → tape.

## Per-unit

- **VG-800 — #6 CRYSTAL Shimmer Pad** (bell drone, `SUSTAIN 90–100`, `NORMAL MIX OFF`) or **#11 Organ Drone + Rotary** (`SUSTAIN 100`) — a steady continuous-waveform source is the best grain fodder. Onboard REV/DLY off; pad the line out before Board 2.
- **CB Clean — #6 Baritone Drone Sustainer** (or **#1 Home Base** for banjo) — set SENSITIVITY by the left LED first; just glue/level, EQ at noon (tone is the Colour Box's job).
- **Microcosm — #11 Haze Grain-Wash Map (variation B/D)** with **#27 Ambient Smear Wash** clock-face as the dial-in (Activity ~1:00, Shape ~1, Filter 11–12, Space ~3:00, Mix ~1:00). The grain event-generator; keep Filter back to tame the banjo top.
- **QI Etherealizer — #4 Frozen Banjo/Baritone Pad → Dark Star** (Flow **Parallel**, Grain Cloud, Space **10–11:00 only** so the Dark Star owns the space, Tone ~1:00 to roll the ice-pick). Hit a chord → **short-press Freeze** (#18 gesture) → grain cloud loops.
- **Dark Star V3 — #5 Pad Factory (opposed-octave split)** or **#4 Stacked-octave pad** (Pitch 1 −1 oct / Pitch 2 +1 oct, Decay high, Crush just below noon for light degrade, Spread full CW). Use the **#16 "a in Dark"** trick to seat unity. Board-2 terminus config **#28** (Stereo, trails on).
- **H90 — #4 Blackhole Low Orbit Resonance** at low Mix (a sub-heavy hall to seat the bloom), or bypass and let Dark Star be the whole space.

## Routing

Stereo from Board 1 onward. Run QI **Parallel** so the live banjo stays present on top of the frozen cloud. **Don't max two reverbs** — QI Space modest, Dark Star owns the wide space, H90 only a low-Mix seat. No clock needed (this is a free-running pad). Pad/reamp the VG-800 into the QI (no level switch — clips hot).

## Sound

A diffuse granular shimmer that widens into an orchestral octave-split reverb pad — huge, slow, glassy, with a defined banjo lead floating on top.

## Play

Strum/hold the VG pad → let the Microcosm grain-wash build → short-press QI **Freeze** to lock the grain cloud → solo a banjo lead over the held bed. Sweep QI **Tone** down by hand (#20) for movement (the QI has no EXP jack). To go bigger, **long-press QI Freeze** (#13) to ramp Space to 100% — but then pull Dark Star back so you don't double the verb.
