# Crescendo Drone Arc

The Swans crescendo as a **whole-board performance** — rise from near-silence to a pulverising sustained wall via repetition + layering + a late dynamic slab. Pairs a dark low wall (baritone) with brittle icy stabs over a frozen drone bed, building to "four church choirs." The arc IS the patch; resist set-and-forget. (Gira uses *no pedals* — volume + sustain + overtones + layering — so the whole rig chases the *result* of physics, not a setting.)

This is a **designed integration** — no artist ever used this exact chain. Real method = Gira's no-pedals loud-amp wall (Gibson Lucille + GK 1000 bass head + Mesa 4x12s) + Westberg's Ibanez AS-200 → DS-1 → Dyna Comp → Orange Thunderverb — none of this rig. ⚠️ the wall is the *result* of physics (sustain + bass weight + freeze-layering + a dynamics arc), not a single patch; "four church choirs" = freeze/Multiply + freeze pads, not live instruments. 🟣 designed.

## Signal path

baritone/banjo (GK-5 → **VG-800** baritone + banjo lead) → **CB Clean** (dynamic swell from near-silence) → **MXR 108** (grinding bite) → **Hizumitas** (sustaining chordal wall) → **Longsword** (drone-bed feeder / late op-amp slab) → Board 2 → **Dark Star V3** (near-silence frozen drone bed, Board-2 terminus) → **QI Etherealizer** (galaxy-wash crescendo freeze + oscillate) → Board 3 → **Big Time** (oceanic freeze + one-stomp climax) → **H90** (cavernous Blackhole freeze) → tape.

*Under it:* Digitakt/MPC motorik 16ths via the 61SL clock.

## Per-unit (staged as the arc)

- **(1) Near-silence —** **Dark Star V3 #32 DS-PP2 Swans Near-Silence Frozen Drone Bed** (Mix 1, Decay 12, Filter 11–12 dark warm, Crush 11 barely-there, **Aux = Hold**: strum a baritone/banjo drone, freeze it into a barely-audible static bed) + **QI #30 QI-PP1 Swans Galaxy-Wash Crescendo** short-press Freeze (the barely-audible grain start). **VG-800 #7 Doom Baritone Wall (−7 STEP)** or **#5 VIO Bowed Swell Drone** as the source; **CB Clean #12 Dynamic Volume Swell** to bring it up from silence (Wet = Swell In ~noon–1:00). ⚠️ Dark Star Hold passes new notes bone-dry — swell in via the dry path / expression.
- **(2) Pulse —** clock-locked motorik 16ths from **Digitakt/MPC** under **Longsword LS-PP3 Swans Crescendo Drone-Bed Feeder** (DIODE center · Drive ~10:00 smooth bloom · Low/High ~11:00 softened · Mid SHIFT down 300 Hz ~1:00 body · Boost OFF — a *stable* bed you swell up). **61SL = clock master** drives the kraut pulse.
- **(3) Grind + stabs —** **MXR 108 #13 108-PP1 Westberg Grinding Bite** (OUTPUT ~12:00 to unity · FUZZ ~12–1:00 mid-gain grind · BUFFER ON) feeding **Hizumitas #13 HZ-PP1 Swans Sustaining Chordal Wall** (Volume ~1:00 · Sustain ~2:00 just shy of max so a chord still reads *as a chord* · Tone ~1:00 bass side = the "warm guitar/bass hum"). Brittle icy stabs over the warm bed = **108 #14 108-PP2 Icy Brittle Stab** (FUZZ 2–3:00 into the gate · **BUFFER OFF** = thin/gated/crackly) on the bright VG/banjo line, OR **Hizumitas #15 HZ-PP3 Joy Division Dark Treble-Cut Rhythm** for the darker option. Gira's "icy stabs over warm hum."
- **(4) Crescendo —** kick the **Hizumitas wall** to full + the **VG-800 baritone**, then **QI #30 QI-PP1** long-press **Freeze** (Space → 100%) + hold **Tap/Osc** (delay self-oscillates into a saturated snowball over several seconds) = the overwhelming wall. Add **Big Time #4 Oceanic Drone Bed** (FREEZE a chord into a non-degrading pad) and/or **Big Time #10 One-Stomp Climax (OVERLOAD ramp)** (hold RIGHT in Long → ramps COLOR+FEEDBACK to a wall, release to collapse). **H90 #6 Blackhole Freeze Drone** locks the cavernous bed under it = "four church choirs."
- **(5) Collapse/fade —** release the freezes; **Gen Loss #6 Tape-Stop Doom Cadence** (Aux = Stop) can halt the whole wet voice into a dying repeat that Big Time catches.
- **(Late slab variant) —** for the BC,NR-adjacent "kick the door in," **Longsword LS-PP1 Op-Amp Sabbath Slab** (Boost footswitch ON as the door-kick) and/or **Hizumitas HZ-PP2 Big-Muff Sabbath Slab** — park off until the climax.

## Routing / sync

The arc is a **dynamics performance, never a static patch.** **61SL = clock master** drives the motorik 16ths (Digitakt/MPC). Board 2 stays **Stereo, trails on** (Dark Star Board-2 terminus). ⚠️ **RIG-CRITICAL: pad/reamp the VG-800 line level before the Qi** (no level switch — it clips hot). ⚠️ **Dark Star Hold + QI Freeze pass new notes bone-dry** — swell the live wall up *over* the frozen beds via CB Clean's dynamic swell / a volume pedal, not by re-triggering into the freeze. Let ONE box own the big space per moment (Dark Star early, then QI + H90 at the peak) — don't max three reverbs (mud). The crescendo build is **WET/FEEDBACK riding up**, not added notes. Watch Big Time WET before OVERLOAD (it's "scary loud"); hold MODE to panic-reset.

## Sound

A barely-audible frozen baritone drone that a kraut pulse emerges under, warm sustaining chordal walls layering up with icy brittle stabs on top, building over minutes via repetition + freeze-layering into an overwhelming, self-oscillating, cavernous wall — four church choirs — then collapsing. Cold and crushing.

## Play

Strum a baritone drone → **Dark Star Aux = Hold** + **QI short-press Freeze** to seat the barely-audible bed. **CB Clean dynamic-swell** the live source up from silence. Start the motorik pulse (61SL/Digitakt). Layer the 108-grind → Hizumitas wall, drop brittle stabs on top. At the peak: **QI long-press Freeze → hold Tap/Osc**, **Big Time OVERLOAD hold**, **H90 Blackhole Freeze** — let the wall consume. Then release everything (or **Gen Loss Aux=Stop** tape-halt) for the collapse. Five to seven minutes of one chord, repeated, growing.
