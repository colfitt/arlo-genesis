# QI Galaxy-Wash Crescendo + Dark Star Shimmer Wall  ⭐

The "let the Qi take over" pole — capture a baritone drone, freeze it, ramp Space to 100%, oscillate the delay into a saturated snowball, and seat it in a Dark Star shimmer wall. The most texture-forward wall here; pure Middle-board granular crescendo into the End board. Designed integration; Swans build the crescendo over minutes on a loud stage — the freeze + oscillate manufactures the rise-and-overwhelm in the box.

## Signal path

Baritone JM (GK-5 → **VG-800 #6 CRYSTAL Shimmer Pad** or #29 ORGAN drone) → CB Clean #6 (sustain the bed) → Hizumitas #10 Synth-Filter Decay Sweep (a morphing source) → (Middle board) → **EHX Effects Interface** (pad/reamp the VG line — RIG-CRITICAL) → **Walrus QI #30 (QI-PP1) Swans Galaxy-Wash Crescendo** → **OBNE Dark Star V3 #25 Infinite Shimmer Drone** (Aux = Hold) → (End board) → Big Time bypassed → tape.

## Per-unit

- **QI QI-PP1 (#30):** Flow **Series**, Grain Mix ~2:00 **Phrase Sample**, **Playback x.5 (−1 oct, baritone weight)**, Delay Mix noon / Feedback ~2:00, Space ~2–3:00, full wet — the performance is short-press **Freeze** (grain loop) → long-press **Freeze** (Space → 100%) → **hold Tap/Osc** (saturated snowball crescendo); **Tone-by-hand** is the expression substitute (no EXP jack).
- **Dark Star #25:** **Mix 2–3 · Decay 4–5 (near infinite) · Multiply 1–2 · Filter 1 (HPF, glassy) · Pitch 1/2 +1 oct · Crush 12 (clean) · Lag 11**, **Aux = Hold**, Stereo/trails.

## Routing

Stereo from the QI onward (true stereo). **Two rig-critical cautions, both documented:** (1) the QI has **no level switch and clips hot** — pad/reamp the VG-800 line via the EHX Effects Interface BEFORE the QI (QI #30 / #23). (2) **Don't stack two maxed reverbs** — keep the QI's Space crescendo as the event and the Dark Star as the seating space, or run the Dark Star LPF-side if the octave-up shimmer ice-picks the banjo.

## Sound

A baritone drone that rises from a frozen grain loop into a galaxy-sized oscillating wash, seated in an infinite glassy shimmer wall. Taste-ref: Swans "Bring the Sun" / "The Seer" — rise-from-quiet pulverising crescendo (shimmer-wall lean).

## Play

Capture the drone → short-press QI Freeze (barely audible) → long-press Freeze to ramp the reverb → hold Tap/Osc to oscillate the delay into the crescendo; ride the QI Tone by hand. Freeze the Dark Star bed under it for the infinite shimmer tail.
