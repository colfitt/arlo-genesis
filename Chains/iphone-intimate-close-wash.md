# iPhone-Intimate Close Wash — banjo bed, recorded-wrong-but-warm ⭐

The opposite of a wall — a faint, close, in-the-room wash sitting *behind* a present, dry fingerpicked banjo. Cassette warble and a halo, never a hall. The intimate-wash brief. This is a 🟣 DOUG-designed integration; no artist used this exact pedal chain, the Taste-ref names the sound it chases.

## Signal path

Banjo (GK-5) → **VG-800** (clean fingerpicked banjo) → **CB Clean** → [Board 1] → **Microcosm** (faint glacial Haze) → **QI Etherealizer** (faint wash, mostly dry) → [Board 3] → **Chroma Console** (CASSETTE intimate degrade) → tape (PORTA424).

## Per-unit

- **VG-800 — #32 Sufjan Fingerpicked Banjo Lead** (5th excluded = "4-string guitar," bright/clean, `NORMAL MIX` moderate so the real pluck/finger noise stays) or **#33 iPhone-Intimate Clean Acoustic Bed**.
- **CB Clean — #1 Home Base** / **#5 Banjo Transient Tamer** — gentle glue, keep the source present and dry-forward (this chain is *not* about squashing it into a wall).
- **Microcosm — #29 Glacial Halo Bed (Haze)** (Mix **~11:00**, sits BEHIND the dry voice — the halo-not-hall move; Filter 11–12 to tame the banjo top).
- **QI Etherealizer — #29 iPhone-Intimate Faint Wash** (Flow Parallel, **Mix/Dry HIGH** mostly dry, Grain Mix ~10:00 faint, Space 9:00 a halo not a hall, Tone ~11:00).
- **Chroma Console — #31 iPhone Voice-Memo Cassette Capture** (TEXTURE CASSETTE ~10–11:00, SWEETEN ~9–10 so the source still reads present, VIBRATO + DRIFT subtle warble, **MIX ~65–70%**). Pick **this OR Gen Loss** as "the tape" — don't double it.

## Routing

Stereo, but everything stays **mostly dry** — Mix knobs low across the board so the banjo stays close and present. Chroma Buffered+Trails, both outs. No freeze, no big space. This is the restraint chain.

## Sound

A close-mic'd banjo with a faint pitched haze and a hint of cassette wow/flutter behind it — like an intimate take captured on the wrong, slightly worn machine.

## Play

Fingerpick rolling banjo arpeggios; keep the dry forward and let the haze/cassette sit *under* it. Resist the urge to freeze — the point is intimacy, not a wall. If you want a touch of glue, add **Chroma #32 Live-Room Slapback** (EchoBoy-style, one-two repeats) instead of more wash.
